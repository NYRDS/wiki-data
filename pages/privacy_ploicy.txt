====== Privacy Policy ======

===== Advertising =====


Nyrds products ask to personalize your advertising experience using Ad Providers (Admob and Appodeal).

If you agree to the collection and use of personal information in our products, Ad Providers and theirs partners may collect 4 categories of personal data (device data, app data, ad data and user data).

You can read more about what this means in the corresponding Privacy Policies:

  * [[https://www.appodeal.com/privacy-policy|Appodeal privacy policy]]
  * [[https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en|Admob privacy policy]]

If you don't want to share your personal data, it is possible for you to opt out of all data collection at first launch of our products.

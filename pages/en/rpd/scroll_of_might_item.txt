====== Scroll of Might ======

{{ rpd:images:scroll_of_might_item.png|Scroll of Might }}

**Scroll of Might** is a powerful upgrade scroll in Remixed Dungeon that permanently enhances the hero's attributes.

==== Description ====
The Scroll of Might permanently increases the hero's Strength and Health, making it one of the most valuable scrolls in the game. It provides permanent attribute boosts that improve character capabilities significantly.

==== Effects ====
  * **Permanent Strength Increase**: Adds +1 to the hero's base strength
  * **Maximum Health Increase**: Increases maximum HP by 5 points permanently
  * **Single Use**: Consumed upon reading
  * **Immediate Effect**: Stats are increased immediately when read

==== Stats ====
  * **Type**: Upgrade Scroll
  * **Rarity**: Rare
  * **Weight**: 5 units
  * **Identified**: Always identified
  * **Upgradable**: No

==== Usage ====
  * **Single use**: Consumed upon reading, no going back
  * **Permanent**: Effects last for the entire game
  * **Timing**: Best used when strength gain will be most beneficial
  * **Strategy**: Often saved for difficult sections of the dungeon

==== Strategy ====
  * **Timing**: Best used after some equipment has been found to take full advantage of the strength boost
  * **Equipment**: Higher strength allows equipping better armor and weapons
  * **Synergy**: Works well with equipment that requires high strength
  * **Risk**: Don't use when cursed, as the effect is still permanent

==== Acquisition ====
  * **Found in**: High-level treasure chests
  * **Dropped by**: Powerful enemies and bosses
  * **Special locations**: Sometimes found in secret rooms or special areas
  * **Shop availability**: May appear in high-end shops

====== Debuff ======

==== Description ====
**Debuff** is a negative status effect that hinders the character's abilities or causes harm. Debuffs are a subset of [[en:rpd:buff|buffs]] that have negative effects on the character.

==== Common Debuffs ====
  * [[en:rpd:poison_buff|Poison]] - Causes periodic damage over time
  * [[en:rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] - Fire damage over time
  * [[en:rpd:paralysis_buff|Paralysis]] - Prevents the character from taking actions
  * [[en:rpd:blindness_buff|Blindness]] - Reduces accuracy in combat
  * [[en:rpd:weakness_buff|Weakness]] - Reduces damage dealt and physical attributes
  * [[en:rpd:slow_buff|Slow]] - Reduces movement speed
  * [[en:rpd:terror_buff|Terror]] - Forces the character to flee
  * [[en:rpd:charm_buff|Charm]] - Makes enemies fight for the player temporarily
  * [[en:rpd:amok_buff|Amok]] - Makes enemies attack each other
  * [[en:rpd:corruption_buff|Corruption]] - Gradually turns allies against the player

==== Sources of Debuffs ====
  * Enemy attacks (e.g., poison, fire damage)
  * Traps (e.g., weakening gas, paralytic gas)
  * Cursed items
  * Environmental hazards (e.g., fire, toxic gas)

==== Countering Debuffs ====
  * [[en:rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] can remove harmful effects
  * Some armor and rings provide resistance to specific debuffs
  * [[en:rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]] can remove curse-related debuffs

====== Mystery Meat ======

{{ rpd:images:mystery_meat_item.png|Mystery Meat }}

**Mystery Meat** is a food item in Remixed Dungeon. When eaten, it has a random beneficial or harmful effect. It can be safely cooked at a cauldron to create [[en:rpd:chargrilled_meat_item|Chargrilled Meat]], eliminating the risk of negative effects.

==== Description ====
According to the in-game description: "A mysterious piece of meat. Eating it might be risky, but there's a chance it could have positive effects."

  * **Java Class:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/food/MysteryMeat.java|MysteryMeat.java]]
  * **Inherits From:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/food/Food.java|Food.java]]

==== Properties ====
  * **Energy Provided:** 2,400 units (Hunger.STARVING - Hunger.HUNGRY)
  * **Price:** 5 gold per item
  * **Type:** Food item
  * **Stackable:** Yes (up to 10 per stack)
  * **Special Properties:** Random effect when consumed

==== Effects When Eaten ====
When consumed, Mystery Meat randomly applies one of the following effects:

  * **Burning** - Sets the hero on fire (20% chance)
  * **Roots** - Immobilizes the hero (20% chance) 
  * **Poison** - Poisons the hero (20% chance)
  * **Slow** - Slows the hero's movement and actions (20% chance)
  * **No Effect** - Simply satisfies hunger (20% chance)

==== Obtaining ====
  * **Mobs drop:** Dropped by certain mobs including [[en:rpd:crab_mob|Crab]], [[en:rpd:spinner_mob|Spinner]], [[en:rpd:scorpio_mob|Scorpio]], and various spider types
  * **Butchering:** Can be obtained from defeated animals and other meat-based enemies
  * **Other food:** Can result from certain food transformations

==== Strategy ====
  * **Risk vs Reward:** The risk of negative effects must be weighed against potential benefits (no effect means safe hunger satisfaction)
  * **Cooking:** Generally safer to cook Mystery Meat at a cauldron to make [[en:rpd:chargrilled_meat_item|Chargrilled Meat]] instead, which provides nutrition without risk
  * **Emergency Use:** May be necessary to consume when other food sources are unavailable
  * **Hunger State:** Riskier to consume when nearly starving compared to when moderately hungry

==== Transformations ====
Mystery Meat can transform into other items under certain conditions:
  * **Burned:** When exposed to fire, becomes [[en:rpd:chargrilled_meat_item|Chargrilled Meat]]
  * **Frozen:** When exposed to cold, becomes [[en:rpd:freeze_carpaccio|Frozen Carpaccio]]
  * **Poisoned:** When affected by poison, becomes [[en:rpd:rotten_food|Rotten Food]]

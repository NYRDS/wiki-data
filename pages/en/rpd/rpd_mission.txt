====== Project Mission ======

The project mission includes a number of objectives:

  *  Testing the idea of commercial viability of a free (in the GPL sense) game.
  *  Creating an open and extensible platform for developing roguelikes and pixel-art RPGs.
  *  Fun for development participants and players.
  *  Spreading ideas about the importance and usefulness of free software.
  *  Spreading knowledge about software development.
  *  Creating the coolest game in the world
  *  World domination

====== Development Principles ======

Development follows a path of continuous evolution taking into account player feedback and statistical data.

We are making 'chess', not 'dragon poker' - variety of situations should be generated by the interaction of a small number of simple rules, not a tangle of complex ones.

Each release should bring (or remove) a logically connected set of features to the game.

Development is preceded by a discussion and planning stage where the intended features should be presented as a list of small, clearly defined tasks.

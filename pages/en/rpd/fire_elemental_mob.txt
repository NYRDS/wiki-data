====== Fire Elemental ======

{{ rpd:images:fire_elemental_mob.png|Fire Elemental }}

==== Description ====
Fire Elementals are blazing creatures of living flame found in the [[en:rpd:caves|Caves]] and [[en:rpd:city|City]] levels of Remixed Dungeon. They attack by striking with their fiery bodies and can set their targets ablaze. These enemies are particularly dangerous due to their burning attack and fire immunities.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP/HT:** 65
  * **Defense Skill:** 20
  * **Attack Skill:** 25
  * **Damage:** 16-20
  * **Armor (DR):** 5
  * **EXP for Kill:** 10
  * **Maximum Level:** 20
  * **Speed:** Normal
  * **Type:** Elemental, Flying

==== Immunities ====
  * [[en:rpd:fire|Fire]] - Takes no damage from fire sources
  * [[en:rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] - Cannot be affected by burning status
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_firebolt_item|Wand of Firebolt]] - Immune to this wand
  * [[en:rpd:scroll_of_psionic_blast_item|Scroll of Psionic Blast]] - Immune to this scroll
  * [[en:rpd:bleeding_buff|Bleeding]] - Immune to bleeding status
  * Does not drop carcasses when killed (carcassChance = 0)

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Burning Attack:** On successful hit, has a 50% chance to inflict [[en:rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] on the target
  * **Burning Healing:** When affected by Burning, will heal a random amount up to 4x their max HP (1-260 HP)
  * **Frost Vulnerability:** When affected by Frost, will take damage up to 2/3 of their max HP (1-43 damage)
  * **Flying:** Can fly over terrain obstacles

==== Location ====
Fire Elementals can typically be found in:
  * [[en:rpd:caves|Caves]] (levels 13-14) - 0.02 chance on each level
  * [[en:rpd:city|City]] (levels 16-19) - Common enemy type on all city levels
  * [[en:rpd:elementals|Elemental]]-themed areas
  * Environments with [[en:rpd:fire|Fire]]-based mechanics

==== Spawn Rates ====
  * **Caves Level 13:** 0.02 (2%)
  * **Caves Level 14:** 0.02 (2%)
  * **City Level 16:** 1.0 (100%) - Most common enemy type on this level
  * **City Level 17:** 1.0 (100%) - Most common enemy type on this level
  * **City Level 18:** 1.0 (100%) - Most common enemy type on this level
  * **City Level 19:** 1.0 (100%) - Most common enemy type on this level

==== Drops ====
  * [[en:rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]] (10% chance)

==== Strategy ====
  * Use [[en:rpd:wand_of_frost_item|Wand of Frost]] or frost-based attacks for effective damage
  * Keep your distance and use ranged attacks when possible
  * Be prepared for their 50% chance to inflict burning on hit
  * Don't allow these enemies to surround you as multiple burning effects can be lethal
  * Frost-based attacks are especially effective against them due to vulnerability mechanic
  * Fire Elementals are dangerous due to their high damage and burning effect
  * Avoid using fire-based attacks as they are completely immune
  * Their flying ability allows them to ignore terrain
  * Be prepared for their burning attack effect on 50% of hits
  * They're immune to many status effects, making them resilient
  * Defeating them can provide [[en:rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]]

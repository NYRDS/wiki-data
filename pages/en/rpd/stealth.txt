====== Stealth Mechanic ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_invisibility_item.png|Invisibility Potion }}

**Stealth** refers to mechanics in Remixed Dungeon that allow the player to remain undetected by enemies. This is particularly important for classes like the [[en:rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] and its subclasses like the [[en:rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]] and [[en:rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]].

==== Mechanics ====
  * Stealth mechanics allow players to avoid detection by enemies
  * Successful stealth typically requires staying out of enemy vision and avoiding sounds
  * Some items, equipment, or character classes provide stealth bonuses
  * Moving through grass, shadows, or other terrain may affect stealth
  * Attacking from stealth may provide bonuses or trigger special effects

==== Invisibility ====
  * **[[en:rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]]** - Renders the player invisible
  * **[[en:rpd:invisibility_buff|Invisibility Buff]]** - Temporarily prevents enemy detection
  * **[[en:rpd:ring_of_shadows_item|Ring of Shadows]]** - Provides stealth bonuses
  * **[[en:rpd:magic_torch_spell|Magic Torch Spell]]** - Creates dark areas to hide in

==== Detection Factors ====
  * **Line of Sight** - Most enemies detect players in direct sight
  * **Stealth Level** - Affects ability to remain undetected
  * **Equipment Weight** - Heavier equipment may increase noise
  * **Movement** - Moving through certain terrain makes noise

==== Stealth-Enhancing Items ====
  * **[[en:rpd:ring_of_evasion_item|Ring of Evasion]]** - Improves stealth and detection avoidance
  * **[[en:rpd:ammo|Ammunition]]** - Some ammo types provide stealth benefits
  * **[[en:rpd:rogue_armor_item|Rogue Armor]]** - Armor that doesn't impede stealth
  * **[[en:rpd:shadow_mob|Shadow]]** - Entity associated with stealth mechanics

==== Related Classes ====
  * [[en:rpd:rogue_class|Rogue Class]] - Primary stealth-based class
  * [[en:rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin Subclass]] - Specializes in stealth and high-damage attacks
  * [[en:rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner Subclass]] - Focused on mobility and evasion

==== Strategy ====
  * Stay out of enemy line of sight to maintain stealth
  * Use terrain and environmental features to your advantage
  * Time your attacks carefully to maximize stealth benefits
  * Consider using invisibility potions or other stealth-enhancing items
  * **Situational Awareness** - Know what breaks stealth in different situations
  * **Timing** - Use stealth when most advantageous
  * **Equipment Planning** - Choose stealth-appropriate gear
  * **Movement** - Careful pathfinding to avoid detection

==== Countering Detection ====
  * **Distance** - Stay out of enemy detection range
  * **Blocking** - Use walls and obstacles to block line of sight
  * **Timing** - Wait for enemies to move away before proceeding
  * **Diversion** - Use items to distract enemies

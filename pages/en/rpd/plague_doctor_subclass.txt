====== Plague Doctor Subclass ======

{{ rpd:images:doctor_hero.png|Doctor Class }}

==== Status ====
**Coming Soon**: The "Plague Doctor" subclass does not currently exist in Remixed Dungeon as a playable hero subclass. The Doctor class does not have any associated subclasses implemented yet.

==== Current Game State ====
The **Doctor** hero class exists in Remixed Dungeon, but has no associated subclasses currently implemented. The Doctor class has related content involving the [[en:rpd:plague_doctor_mob|Plague Doctor NPC]] who offers quests and interactions related to disease mechanics.

==== Planned Content ====
Future updates may include subclasses for the Doctor class, potentially related to disease mechanics, plague, and toxic effects as suggested in related content.

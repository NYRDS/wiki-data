====== Electricity ======

**Electricity** is a damage type and debuff effect caused by lightning traps, lightning spells, or other sources of lightning damage.

==== Effects ====
  * **Damage**: Scales with the target's current HP
  * **Damage Calculation**: Damage value is randomly generated between 1/3 to 2/3 of the target's max HP
  * **Special Effect**: Charges (or discharges) the hero's equipment

==== Sources ====
  * [[en:rpd:lightning_trap|Lightning Trap]] - Trap found in the dungeon
  * [[en:rpd:shaman_mob|Shaman]] - Monster that uses lightning attacks
  * Other lightning-based spells or abilities

==== Resistances ====
Some creatures have resistance to electricity damage, such as:
  * [[en:rpd:shaman_mob|Shaman]] - Has lightning resistance
  * [[en:rpd:yogs_brain_mob|Yogs Brain]] - Has lightning resistance

==== Additional Information ====
  * In lightning traps, damage is calculated as max(1, random value(target HP/3, 2*target HP/3))
  * For the hero, lightning damage also causes charging (or discharging) of all equipment

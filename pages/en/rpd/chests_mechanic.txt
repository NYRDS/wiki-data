====== Chests ======

{{ rpd:images:chest_item.png|Chest }}

Chests are containers that hold treasure in Remixed Dungeon. They are found throughout the dungeon levels and contain valuable items.

==== Types of Chests ====
  * **Regular Chests**: Standard containers with typical treasure
  * **Crystal Chests**: [[en:rpd:crystal_merchant|Crystal Merchant]] exclusive containers with high-value items
  * **Locked Chests**: Require a key to open, contain better treasure
  * **Coffins**: Special containers that are more common in Necropolis
  * **Skeletons**: Bone remains that may contain items (found in Necropolis)

==== Treasure Types ====
Chests may contain:
  * [[en:rpd:gold_item|Gold]]
  * [[en:rpd:potion_item|Potions]]
  * [[en:rpd:scroll_item|Scrolls]]
  * [[en:rpd:weapon_item|Weapons]]
  * [[en:rpd:armor_item|Armor]]
  * [[en:rpd:ring_item|Rings]]
  * [[en:rpd:artifacts|Artifacts]]
  * [[en:rpd:food|Food]]
  * [[en:rpd:keys|Keys]]

==== Special Chest Mechanics ====
  * Chests can be trapped with various [[en:rpd:traps|traps]]
  * [[en:rpd:ring_of_wealth|Ring of Wealth]] increases the quality of chest contents
  * Locked chests require corresponding [[en:rpd:keys|keys]] to open
  * Crystal chests are guaranteed to be trap-free
  * Some rare items are only found in chests

==== Strategy ====
  * Always be cautious when opening chests - they may be trapped
  * Use [[en:rpd:ring_of_awareness|Ring of Awareness]] to detect traps before opening
  * Crystal chests are guaranteed to be trap-free and contain better items
  * Locked chests provide better rewards but require keys to open
  * Some items like [[en:rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] can only be found in specific chest types

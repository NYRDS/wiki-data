====== Toxic Gas Buff ======

{{ rpd:images:gases_immunity_buff.png|Toxic Gas Buff }}

**Toxic Gas** is a harmful status effect in Remixed Dungeon that deals damage over time to characters exposed to it.

==== Description ====
Toxic Gas is a dangerous environmental effect that appears in various forms throughout the dungeon. It inflicts damage over time and can be quite hazardous to unprepared adventurers.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Damage Type**: Damage over time (DoT) effect
  * **Application**: Afflicts characters standing in gas clouds
  * **Duration**: Persists until dissipated or characters leave the area
  * **Damage**: Deals damage per turn while in the gas cloud

==== Sources ====
  * **Traps**: Released by certain traps when triggered
  * **Enemies**: Generated by some enemies like Goo or Piranhas
  * **Environmental**: Naturally occurring in some dungeon areas
  * **Spells/Items**: Created by specific spells or items

==== Effects ====
  * **Continuous Damage**: Deals damage each turn spent in gas
  * **Accumulation**: Damage accumulates over time if not treated
  * **Spread**: Gas clouds may spread to adjacent tiles
  * **Persistence**: Remains for multiple turns after creation

==== Resistance and Immunity ====
  * **Certain classes** have reduced susceptibility to toxic gas
  * **Specific equipment** can provide resistance or immunity
  * **Some characters** are naturally immune to gas effects

==== Strategy ====
  * **Avoidance**: Move quickly through gas clouds when possible
  * **Preparation**: Carry items that can clear gas or provide immunity
  * **Timing**: Be aware of when toxic gas effects end
  * **Equipment**: Use gas-resistant gear when entering hazardous areas

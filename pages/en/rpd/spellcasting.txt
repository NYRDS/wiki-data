====== Spellcasting ======

{{ rpd:images:spell_book_item.png|Spell Book }}

==== Description ====
**Spellcasting** is a mechanic in Remixed Dungeon related to using [[en:rpd:spell|spells]] and [[en:rpd:wands|wands]] to affect enemies or the environment.

==== Mechanics ====
  * Spellcasting requires [[en:rpd:mana|mana]] or [[en:rpd:wands|charges]] from [[en:rpd:wands|wands]]
  * Some [[en:rpd:hero_subclass|subclasses]] receive bonuses from spellcasting
  * Spells can be used for attack, crowd control, or player enhancement
  * Spellcasting effectiveness may depend on [[en:rpd:hero_class|hero class]]

==== Types of Spells ====
  * Offensive spells
  * Support spells
  * Utility spells
  * Control spells

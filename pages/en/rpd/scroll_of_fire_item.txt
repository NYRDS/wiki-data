====== Scroll of Fire ======

{{ rpd:images:scroll_of_fire_item.png|Scroll of Fire }}

==== Description ====
The **Scroll of Fire** is a magic scroll in Remixed Dungeon that creates a burst of magical fire around the reader, damaging all nearby enemies.

==== Mechanics ====
When read, the Scroll of Fire:
  * Creates a blast of fire that damages all enemies in adjacent cells
  * Deals damage based on the hero's level and magic affinity
  * Does not damage the hero directly but may damage allies
  * Costs 1 turn to read
  * Can ignite flammable terrain or objects

==== Effects ====
  * Damage: Scales with hero level
  * Area of effect: 8 adjacent cells around the reader
  * May ignite explosive objects or terrain
  * Effective for clearing groups of weak enemies

==== Strategy ====
  * Use in crowded areas to clear multiple enemies
  * Be careful around allies or explosive items
  * Particularly effective against cold-vulnerable enemies
  * Can be used for environmental damage (igniting oil, etc.)

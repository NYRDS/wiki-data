====== Elementals ======

{{ rpd:images:fire_elemental_mob.png|Fire Elemental }}

Elementals are magical beings made of one of the fundamental elements: air, earth, fire, or water. Each type of elemental has unique abilities and characteristics based on the element they embody.

==== Types of Elementals ====
  * [[en:rpd:air_elemental_mob|Air Elemental]] - Flying creatures that can push enemies with wind attacks
  * [[en:rpd:earth_elemental_mob|Earth Elemental]] - Heavily armored creatures with high health and damage
  * [[en:rpd:fire_elemental_mob|Fire Elemental]] - Creatures that deal fire damage and ignite enemies
  * [[en:rpd:water_elemental_mob|Water Elemental]] - Creatures with abilities related to water and liquid

==== Common Properties ====
  * All elementals are typically found in their respective environmental levels or through certain feelings
  * Most elementals have some form of immunity or resistance related to their element
  * Elementals generally have stats that scale with dungeon depth
  * They are resistant to certain status effects due to their non-corporeal nature

==== Behavior ====
  * Elementals often exhibit AI behaviors that reflect their elemental nature
  * They may use special abilities that are thematically consistent with their element
  * Some elementals can move through terrain in ways that normal creatures cannot

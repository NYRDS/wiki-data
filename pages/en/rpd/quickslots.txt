====== Quickslots ======

**Quickslots** are special inventory slots in Remixed Dungeon that allow the hero to quickly access frequently used items during combat or exploration.

==== Description ====
Quickslots provide rapid access to important items such as potions, scrolls, bombs, food, or other consumables without having to open the full inventory screen. The number of quickslots can be adjusted in settings and depends on screen width (default is usually 4, but can be increased or decreased).

==== Usage ====
  * **Item Assignment:** Items can be assigned to quickslots by dragging them to the slot
  * **Quick Access:** Items can be used with a single tap during gameplay
  * **Reusability:** Empty quickslot can be refilled with new items of the same type
  * **Hotkeys:** On desktop versions, quickslots can be accessed with number keys (1-9,0 for first 10 slots)
  * **Adjustable Quantity:** The number of quickslots can be changed in the UI settings menu using plus/minus buttons

==== Types of Items for Quickslots ====
  * [[en:rpd_potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Emergency healing
  * [[en:rpd_scroll_of_teleportation_item|Scroll of Teleportation]] - Escape mechanism
  * [[en:rpd_ration_item|Iron Ration]] - Food for sustenance
  * [[en:rpd_artifacts|Artifacts]] - Frequently used artifact charges
  * [[en:rpd_bombs|Bombs]] - Explosive damage or utility
  * [[en:rpd_dart_item|Darts]] - Ranged ammunition for emergency attacks
  * [[en:rpd_throwing_weapons|Throwing Weapons]] - Category page for close-range offensive tools
  * [[en:rpd_ankh_item|Ankh]] - Emergency revival tool

==== Strategy ====
  * Reserve quickslots for life-saving or frequently used items
  * Consider the recharge time of reusable items when planning quickslot assignments
  * Plan quickslot layout based on the dungeon level and expected challenges
  * Update quickslots as the dungeon progresses and needs change

==== Mechanics ====
  * Items in quickslots are not consumed immediately upon assignment
  * Quickslots can be cleared by dragging items off the slot
  * Quickslot items can be used even when the main inventory is full
  * Some items may have special behavior when used from quickslots

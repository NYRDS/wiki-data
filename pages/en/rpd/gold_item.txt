====== Gold ======

**Gold** is the primary currency in Remixed Dungeon, used for purchasing items and services from shopkeepers and other NPCs throughout the dungeon.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in treasure chests
  * Dropped by various enemies
  * Sometimes scattered throughout dungeon levels
  * Rewards for completing certain quests
  * Sold by [[en:rpd:shopkeeper_npc|Shopkeeper]] and other vendors

==== Usage ====
  * Purchasing items from [[en:rpd:shopkeeper_npc|Shopkeeper]] and other vendors
  * [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|Purifying curses]] and [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|healing]] services from Bishop
  * [[en:rpd:wndfortuneteller|Identifying items]] by Fortune Teller

==== Enemies That Drop Gold ====
  * Many enemies have a chance to drop gold when defeated
  * [[en:rpd:gnoll_mob|Gnolls]], for example, have a 50% chance to drop gold
  * Higher level enemies may drop more gold

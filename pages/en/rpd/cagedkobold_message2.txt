====== Caged Kobold Message 2 ======

==== Text ====
You gotta help me out, buddy

==== Context ====
This is one of the messages that the [[en:rpd:caged_kobold_npc|Caged Kobold]] NPC may say when interacted with.

==== Description ====
The Caged Kobold asks for assistance from the hero with this message. This is part of the dialogue sequence when interacting with the captured kobold in the [[en:rpd:ice_caves_level|Ice Caves]].

==== Location ====
Found in the [[en:rpd:ice_caves_level|Ice Caves]] when encountering the [[en:rpd:caged_kobold_npc|Caged Kobold]].

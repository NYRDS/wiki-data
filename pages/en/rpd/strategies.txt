====== Strategies ======

{{ rpd:images:strategy_guide.png|Strategy}}

Strategies in Remixed Dungeon encompass tactical approaches to various aspects of the game, from item usage to combat to dungeon navigation.

==== General Strategies ====
  * Always keep healing items handy
  * Identify unknown items safely (use scrolls of identification strategically)
  * Manage your food carefully - starvation damage is real
  * Map out dangerous areas and plan safe routes
  * Understand your class strengths and play to them

==== Combat Strategies ====
  * Don't fight multiple enemies at once if possible
  * Use terrain to your advantage (doorways, corners)
  * Kite enemies when you have ranged advantages
  * Know when to retreat and heal
  * Prioritize dangerous enemies over weak ones

==== Resource Management ====
  * Don't waste powerful items on weak enemies
  * Balance upgrading weapons vs armor
  * Consider the cost of enchanting/imbuing items
  * Save certain items for boss encounters

==== Class-Specific Strategies ====
  * **[[en:rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]]** - Focus on melee equipment and HP management
  * **[[en:rpd:mage_class|Mage]]** - Utilize wands and magical equipment
  * **[[en:rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]]** - Make use of stealth and detection
  * **[[en:rpd:elf_class|Elf]]** - Balance between ranged and magical capabilities
  * **[[en:rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]]** - Summoning and undead management

==== Dungeon Navigation ====
  * Explore thoroughly but cautiously
  * Mark safe areas and escape routes
  * Understand level layouts before pushing deeper
  * Recognize dangerous areas and prepare accordingly

==== Equipment Strategies ====
  * Prioritize weapons or armor based on current situation
  * Keep backup equipment for emergencies
  * Understand enchantment synergies
  * Identify and use equipment with beneficial properties

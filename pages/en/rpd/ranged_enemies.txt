====== Ranged Enemies ======

==== Description ====
**Ranged Enemies** are mobs that prefer to attack from a distance rather than engaging in close combat. They typically have special abilities or attacks that are effective from afar.

==== List of Ranged Enemies ====
  * [[en:rpd:scorpio_mob|Scorpio]] - Uses ranged attacks and can inflict Cripple
  * [[en:rpd:acidic_scorpio_mob|Acidic Scorpio]] - Variant of Scorpio with damage reflection abilities
  * [[en:rpd:gnoll_shaman_mob|Gnoll Shaman]] - Uses magical spells from a distance
  * [[en:rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeleton]] - Shoots arrows from afar
  * [[en:rpd:golem_mob|Golem]] - Throws stones at range
  * [[en:rpd:eye_mob|Evil Eye]] - Uses magical gaze attacks
  * [[en:rpd:wraith_mob|Wraith]] - Can attack across multiple tiles

==== Common Tactics ====
  * Keep distance from the player
  * Use ranged attacks that may have special effects (Cripple, Paralysis, etc.)
  * Some may retreat when approached
  * May have reduced effectiveness in melee combat

==== Counter Strategies ====
  * Close distance quickly to force melee combat
  * Use teleportation or blinking abilities to bypass their range
  * Use armor or resistance to minimize damage from ranged attacks
  * Attack when they are moving or otherwise vulnerable

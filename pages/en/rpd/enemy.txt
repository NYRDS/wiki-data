====== Enemies ======

**Enemies** (also known as **mobs**) in Remixed Dungeon are hostile creatures that the player encounters throughout their journey.

==== Types of Enemies ====
  * **Basic Mobs** - Standard enemies with simple AI
  * **Elite Mobs** - Stronger versions of basic enemies
  * **Bosses** - Powerful enemies blocking progression to deeper levels
  * **Minions** - Weaker enemies that support stronger foes

==== Enemy Attributes ====
  * **HP** - Hit Points determine how much damage an enemy can take before dying
  * **Attack** - Determines the enemy's ability to hit the player
  * **Defense** - Reduces incoming damage from player attacks
  * **Damage** - The amount of damage the enemy can inflict
  * **EXP** - Experience granted when the enemy is defeated

==== Enemy Behaviors ====
  * **Aggressive** - Actively seek out and pursue the player character
  * **Passive** - Only attack when provoked or when the player enters their range
  * **Neutral** - Do not attack unless attacked first
  * **Intelligent** - May use special abilities or tactics against the player

==== Enemy Mechanics ====
  * **AI System** - Enemies have different behavioral patterns based on their type
  * **Spawn Rates** - The frequency of enemies varies by dungeon level
  * **Special Abilities** - Some enemies have unique powers or resistances
  * **Loot** - Defeated enemies may drop items or gold

==== Combat Against Enemies ====
  * **Melee Combat** - Close-range fighting with weapons
  * **Ranged Combat** - Attacking from a distance with bows or spells
  * **Tactical Considerations** - Positioning, timing, and resource management
  * **Defensive Tactics** - Using armor, shields, or defensive items

====== Pumpkin Pie ======

{{ rpd:images:pumpkin_pie_food.png|Pumpkin Pie }}

==== Description ====
A delicious pumpkin pie, perfect for special occasions. This [[en:rpd:food_item|food item]] is a seasonal treat that restores your hunger completely.

==== Stats ====
  * **Nutrition Value**: Restores complete hunger (Hunger.STARVING amount)
  * **Energy**: Hunger.STARVING (completely satiates hunger)
  * **Price**: 30 gold per pie
  * **Image**: Index 9 in food.png sprite sheet

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Satiates Hunger**: Completely restores the hunger bar when consumed
  * **Spoilage**: Can spoil and turn into Rotten Pumpkin Pie over time
  * **Transformation**: When poisoned, transforms into Rotten Pumpkin Pie

==== Strategy ====
  * Useful for completely restoring hunger when at low levels
  * More expensive than regular food but provides full satiation
  * Better to consume before it spoils

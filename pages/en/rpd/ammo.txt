====== Ammunition ======

{{ rpd:images:common_arrow_item.png|Ammunition }}

Ammunition refers to the projectiles used with ranged weapons in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Arrow Types ====
  * **[[en:rpd:common_arrow_item|Common Arrow]]** - Basic arrow with standard damage
  * **[[en:rpd:arrow_item|Arrow]]** - Standard arrow type
  * **[[en:rpd:arrows_item|Arrows]]** - General arrow category
  * **[[en:rpd:fire_arrow_item|Fire Arrow]]** - Arrows that cause burning
  * **[[en:rpd:frost_arrow_item|Frost Arrow]]** - Arrows that cause freezing
  * **[[en:rpd:health_arrow_item|Health Arrow]]** - Arrows that restore health
  * **[[en:rpd:poison_arrow_item|Poison Arrow]]** - Arrows that cause poisoning

==== Dart Types ====
Darts are thrown projectiles with various effects:
  * **[[en:rpd:curare_dart_item|Curare Dart]]** - Causes paralysis on hit
  * **[[en:rpd:incendiary_dart_item|Incendiary Dart]]** - Causes burning effect
  * **[[en:rpd:amok_dart_item|Amok Dart]]** - Causes amok effect
  * **[[en:rpd:health_dart_item|Health Dart]]** - Restores health on hit
  * **[[en:rpd:dart_item|Dart]]** - Basic dart type
  * **[[en:rpd:darts_item|Darts]]** - General dart category

==== Thrown Weapons ====
Some weapons can be thrown without requiring ammunition:
  * **[[en:rpd:shuriken_item|Shuriken]]** - Throwing stars with moderate damage
  * **[[en:rpd:boomerang_item|Boomerang]]** - Returns after being thrown
  * **[[en:rpd:javelin_item|Javelin]]** - Throwing spears with good range
  * **[[en:rpd:throwing_weapons|Throwing Weapons]]** - Fast, light throwing weapons (category page)

==== Ammunition Mechanics ====
  * Ammunition is consumed when firing ranged weapons
  * Different bows require specific ammunition types
  * Some special effects only work with certain ammunition types
  * Ammunition can be enchanted with special properties

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in chests throughout the dungeon
  * Available in shops at higher floors
  * Crafted with appropriate materials in alchemy
  * Dropped by certain enemies

==== Strategy ====
  * Keep a good supply of common ammunition for regular combat
  * Use special ammunition for specific tactical advantages
  * Consider the cost-effectiveness of special ammunition
  * Different ammunition works better against different types of enemies

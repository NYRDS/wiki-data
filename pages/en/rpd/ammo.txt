====== Ammunition ======

{{ rpd:images:common_arrow_item.png|Ammunition }}

Ammunition refers to the projectiles used with ranged weapons in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Arrow Types ====
  * **[[en:rpd_common_arrow_item|Common Arrow]]** - Basic arrow with standard damage
  * **[[en:rpd_arrow_item|Arrow]]** - Standard arrow type
  * **[[en:rpd_arrows_item|Arrows]]** - General arrow category
  * **[[en:rpd_fire_arrow_item|Fire Arrow]]** - Arrows that cause burning
  * **[[en:rpd_frost_arrow_item|Frost Arrow]]** - Arrows that cause freezing
  * **[[en:rpd_health_arrow_item|Health Arrow]]** - Arrows that restore health
  * **[[en:rpd_poison_arrow_item|Poison Arrow]]** - Arrows that cause poisoning

==== Dart Types ====
Darts are thrown projectiles with various effects:
  * **[[en:rpd_curare_dart_item|Curare Dart]]** - Causes paralysis on hit
  * **[[en:rpd_incendiary_dart_item|Incendiary Dart]]** - Causes burning effect
  * **[[en:rpd_amok_dart_item|Amok Dart]]** - Causes amok effect
  * **[[en:rpd_health_dart_item|Health Dart]]** - Restores health on hit
  * **[[en:rpd_dart_item|Dart]]** - Basic dart type
  * **[[en:rpd_darts_item|Darts]]** - General dart category

==== Thrown Weapons ====
Some weapons can be thrown without requiring ammunition:
  * **[[en:rpd_shuriken_item|Shuriken]]** - Throwing stars with moderate damage
  * **[[en:rpd_boomerang_item|Boomerang]]** - Returns after being thrown
  * **[[en:rpd_javelin_item|Javelin]]** - Throwing spears with good range
  * **[[en:rpd_throwing_weapons|Throwing Weapons]]** - Fast, light throwing weapons (category page)

==== Ammunition Mechanics ====
  * Ammunition is consumed when firing ranged weapons
  * Different bows require specific ammunition types
  * Some special effects only work with certain ammunition types
  * Ammunition can be enchanted with special properties

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in chests throughout the dungeon
  * Available in shops at higher floors
  * Crafted with appropriate materials in alchemy
  * Dropped by certain enemies

==== Strategy ====
  * Keep a good supply of common ammunition for regular combat
  * Use special ammunition for specific tactical advantages
  * Consider the cost-effectiveness of special ammunition
  * Different ammunition works better against different types of enemies

====== Dark Sacrifice ======

==== Description ====
**Dark Sacrifice** is a spell in Remixed Dungeon that allows the caster to sacrifice a loyal minion to create an explosion. This spell is part of the necromancy magic affinity.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Type**: Sacrifice-based spell
  * **Cost**: 3 mana points
  * **Level Requirement**: 3
  * **Cast Time**: 0.5 seconds
  * **Affinity**: [[en:rpd:necromancy_affinity_mechanic|Necromancy]]
  * **Targeting**: Character targeting (must be your own minion)
  * **Effect**: Creates Liquid Flame explosion at sacrifice location
  * **Damage**: Scales with minion's remaining HP and caster's skill level

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Resource conversion**: Converts minion into area-of-effect damage
  * **Risk/reward**: Higher minion HP yields larger explosion
  * **Necromancy connection**: Part of necromancy spell line
  * **Ownership check**: Minion must belong to caster (resists if not loyal)

==== Usage ====
  * Use strategically when you have a high-HP minion
  * Effective for clearing groups of enemies
  * Synergizes with summoning abilities and necromancy builds
  * Consider minion value vs. explosion benefit

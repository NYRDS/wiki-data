====== Bows and Arrows ======

Information about bows and arrows in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Bows ====
Bows are ranged weapons that require ammunition (arrows) to function. They are generally more accurate and deal more damage than throwing weapons, especially at higher levels.

  * [[en:rpd:wooden_bow_item|Wooden Bow]] - Basic bow available early in the game
  * [[en:rpd:elven_bow_item|Elven Bow]] - Superior bow with higher accuracy and damage

==== Arrows ====
Arrows are ammunition for bows. They come in various types with different effects:

  * [[en:rpd:arrow_item|Basic Arrow]]
  * [[en:rpd:common_arrow_item|Common Arrow]] - Standard ammunition
  * [[en:rpd:fire_arrow_item|Fire Arrow]] - Deals fire damage and may ignite targets
  * [[en:rpd:frost_arrow_item|Frost Arrow]] - Deals frost damage and may freeze targets
  * [[en:rpd:health_arrow_item|Health Arrow]] - Heals target
  * [[en:rpd:poison_arrow_item|Poison Arrow]] - Poisons target
  * [[en:rpd:paralysis_arrow_item|Paralysis Arrow]] - Paralyzes target
  * [[en:rpd:amok_arrow_item|Amok Arrow]] - Makes target attack other enemies
  * [[en:rpd:toxic_arrow_item|Toxic Arrow]] - Applies toxic gas to target

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Moistening**: Arrows can be combined with potions to create special arrows (e.g., fire arrows from arrows + liquid flame potion)
  * **Durability**: Arrows are consumed on use
  * **Accuracy**: Bows typically have higher accuracy than throwing weapons

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential for Elf class players who start with a bow and arrows
  * Arrows can be moistened with potions for additional effects
  * Arrows can be found in treasure, shops, or crafted in the alchemy system

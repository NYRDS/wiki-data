====== Beehive ======

{{ rpd:images:beehive_mob.png|Beehive Sprite }}

==== Description ====
Beehives are stationary level objects found in the Sewers and Prison levels. When damaged or destroyed, they spawn bees to defend themselves. Beehives themselves do not attack but serve as spawn points for hostile bees.

==== Stats ====
  * **Type:** Level Object
  * **HP:** 20
  * **Defense:** 0
  * **Attack:** None (spawns bees when damaged)
  * **Spawn Locations:** Sewers, Prison
  * **Spawns:** 3-8 Bees when damaged/destroyed

==== Behavior ====
  * **Passive:** Beehives do not attack or move
  * **Bee Spawning:** When a beehive takes damage, it releases 1 bee at a time until 3-8 total bees are spawned
  * **Final Swarm:** When the beehive is destroyed, any remaining bees are released all at once
  * **Bee Types:** Spawns regular [[en:rpd:bee_mob|Bees]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Beehives cannot fight back, so they can be safely destroyed from range
  * Be careful when destroying beehives as they will release multiple bees
  * Ranged attacks or thrown items are ideal for dealing with beehives
  * The bees spawned have high speed and poison attacks, so be prepared
  * Area of effect attacks can handle multiple bees at once

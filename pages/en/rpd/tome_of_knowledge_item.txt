====== Tome of Knowledge ======

{{ rpd:images:tome_of_knowledge_item.png|Tome of Knowledge }}

The Tome of Knowledge is a book that permanently increases the reader's skill level by 1.

==== Description ====
This ancient tome is filled with arcane riddles and mysteries! It may be hard to read, but if you can get through it, you'll definitely increase your magical powers. Reading this tome will permanently increase your skill level by 1.

==== Effects ====
  * **Skill Level Increase:** Permanently increases the hero's skill level by 1
  * **Magic Enhancement:** Indirectly enhances all magical abilities by increasing skill level

==== Mechanics ====
  * When read, the tome disappears from inventory
  * Shows a mastery spell sprite effect when used
  * Emits magic particles upon reading
  * Displays "+ 1" status message when used
  * Takes 1 action to read (same as reading other books)

==== Obtaining Tomes of Knowledge ====
Tomes of Knowledge can be found:
  * In shops (town shops and dungeon shops)
  * As rare drops from various sources

==== Related Items ====
  * [[en:rpd:spell_book_item|Spell Book]] - Another item for learning magical knowledge
  * [[en:rpd:book_of_bursts_item|Book of Bursts]] - Another skill-increasing book

==== Strategy ====
  * Best used when you have room to increase your skill level
  * Provides permanent benefits to all hero classes
  * Reading is safe as it has no negative effects

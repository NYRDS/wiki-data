====== Belongings ======

Belongings refers to the player character's inventory system in Remixed Dungeon, which includes all equipment and carried items.

==== Inventory Slots ====
  * [[en:rpd_weapons_mechanic|Weapon]] - Main hand weapon slot
  * [[en:rpd_armor_item|Armor]] - Body armor slot
  * [[en:rpd_ring_item|Ring]] - Ring slot (can carry up to 2 rings)
  * [[en:rpd_artifacts|Artifact]] - Special artifact slot
  * [[en:rpd_quickslots|Quickslots]] - Up to 4 items for quick access
  * [[en:rpd_backpack|Backpack]] - General inventory space

==== Inventory Management ====
  * [[en:rpd_gold_item|Gold]] - Currency stored in inventory
  * [[en:rpd_keys|Keys]] - Dungeon keys for locked doors/chests
  * [[en:rpd_potions|Potions]] - Consumable items
  * [[en:rpd_scrolls|Scrolls]] - Consumable items with magical effects
  * [[en:rpd_food_item|Food]] - Items for sustenance
  * [[en:rpd_wands|Wands]] - Magical items with charges

==== Mechanics ====
  * [[en:rpd_weight_mechanic|Weight]] - Items have weight affecting movement
  * [[en:rpd_identification|Identification]] - Process of discovering item properties
  * Dropping - Getting rid of unwanted items
  * Storing - Managing limited inventory space

==== Strategy ====
  * Keep important consumables in quickslots for easy access
  * Manage weight to avoid movement penalties
  * Prioritize valuable items over common ones
  * Consider leaving non-essential items in hero's tomb after death

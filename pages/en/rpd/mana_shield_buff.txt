====== Mana Shield ======

==== Description ====
**Mana Shield** is a [[en:rpd:buffs|buff]] in Remixed Dungeon that completely protects the character from incoming damage. The buff actively uses [[en:rpd:mana|mana]] to absorb damage.

==== Mechanics ====
  * Completely absorbs incoming damage at the cost of mana
  * When mana is depleted, the buff disappears
  * Some [[en:rpd:hero_subclasses|subclasses]] can create a mana shield under certain conditions

==== Acquisition ====
  * [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_subclass|Witch Doctor]] - creates a mana shield when casting spells
  * May be obtainable via [[en:rpd:spells|spells]] or [[en:rpd:items|items]]

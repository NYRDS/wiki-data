====== Healing ======

**Healing** refers to the restoration of health points (HP) for the hero or other characters in the dungeon.

==== Methods of Healing ====
  * **Potions**: [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] and other healing potions
  * **Spells**: [[en:rpd:heal_spell|Heal Spell]] and others
  * **Items**: Certain rings and artifacts provide healing over time
  * **NPCs**: Some NPCs may offer healing services
  * **Passive**: Some classes and subclasses have passive healing abilities

==== Healing Sources ====
  * [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Basic healing potion
  * [[en:rpd:potion_of_overhealing|Potion of Overhealing]] - Provides temporary health buffer
  * [[en:rpd:ring_of_mending|Ring of Mending]] - Provides gradual healing over time
  * [[en:rpd:regeneration_buff|Regeneration]] - Temporary buff that heals over time
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_regrowth_item|Wand of Regrowth]] - Can be used for healing in some cases

==== Healing Mechanics ====
  * Healing cannot restore HP above the character's maximum HP
  * Some healing methods are more efficient than others
  * Healing can be interrupted by taking damage

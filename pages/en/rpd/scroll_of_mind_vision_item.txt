====== Scroll Of Mind Vision ======

{{ rpd:images:item_ScrollHolder.png|Scroll Of Mind Vision }}

==== Description ====
The **Scroll of Mind Vision** grants the ability to see through walls for a brief period, revealing hidden doors, traps, and the layout of the current floor. It also reveals the presence of all creatures on the level.

==== Effects ====
  * Reveals all terrain, items, and creatures on the current level
  * Shows hidden doors and secret areas
  * Displays the position of all mobs until they leave the hero's FOV
  * Duration is limited and decreases over time

==== Mechanics ====
  * Consumes the scroll upon use
  * Provides complete level map visibility temporarily
  * Particularly useful for exploration and trap avoidance
  * Expires after a set period or when moving to a new level

==== Strategy ====
  * Use before exploring unknown areas to map them completely
  * Helpful to identify safe routes through dangerous areas
  * Great for locating stairs before your current vision expires
  * Ideal for finding hidden rooms with valuable items

==== Technical Details ====
  * **Java Class:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/scrolls/ScrollOfMindVision.java|ScrollOfMindVision.java]]
  * **Base Class:** Extends Scroll class
  * **Implementation:** Reveals all tiles on the current level for a duration

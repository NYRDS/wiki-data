====== Arrow ======

{{ rpd:images:common_arrow_item.png|Generic Arrow }}

**Arrow** refers to ammunition used with bows and other ranged weapons in Remixed Dungeon. Arrows are a type of missile weapon that can be specially treated with potions to create different effects.

==== Arrow Types ====
  * [[en:rpd:common_arrow_item|Common Arrow]] - Basic wooden arrows with iron heads
  * [[en:rpd:fire_arrow_item|Fire Arrow]] - Arrows treated with Potion of Liquid Flame
  * [[en:rpd:frost_arrow_item|Frost Arrow]] - Arrows treated with Potion of Frost
  * [[en:rpd:poison_arrow_item|Poison Arrow]] - Arrows treated with Potion of Toxic Gas
  * [[en:rpd:paralysis_arrow_item|Paralysis Arrow]] - Arrows treated with Potion of Paralytic Gas
  * [[en:rpd:health_arrow_item|Health Arrow]] - Arrows treated with Potion of Healing
  * [[en:rpd:amok_arrow_item|Amok Arrow]] - Arrows treated with Potion of Strength

==== Properties ====
  * **Type:** Missile Weapon
  * **Usage:** For use with bows and ranged weapons
  * **Consumable:** Each arrow is consumed after being shot
  * **Stackable:** Can stack up to very high quantities

==== Special Ability: Potion Moisten Effect ====
When combined with various potions (Potion of Liquid Flame, Potion of Frost, Potion of Toxic Gas, etc.), regular arrows can be "moistened" into special arrows with enhanced effects when used.

==== Acquisition ====
  * **Crafting:** Can be created via the "Moisten" effect by combining potions with regular arrows
  * **Shops:** Special arrow types may be available in shops
  * **Loot:** Can be found as dungeon loot

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential for characters using ranged weapons
  * Consider the "moistening" mechanic with potions for enhanced effects
  * Different arrow types provide strategic advantages in different situations

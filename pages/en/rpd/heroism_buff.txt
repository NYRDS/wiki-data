====== Heroism Buff ======

{{ rpd:images:buff_Heroism.png|Heroism Buff }}

==== Description ====
The Heroism buff significantly enhances all combat abilities for a temporary period. This is typically a powerful buff that makes the character much more effective in combat.

==== Effects ====
  * Enhances all combat abilities and effectiveness
  * Likely increases damage output significantly
  * May provide other combat-related bonuses
  * Overall improvement to the character's fighting capabilities

==== Duration ====
  * The duration of the Heroism buff varies depending on the source that applied it
  * When the buff expires, the character returns to normal combat effectiveness

==== Sources ====
  * Certain powerful items or abilities in the game can apply the Heroism buff
  * May be granted by special circumstances or rare items
  * The specific source depends on game context and available items

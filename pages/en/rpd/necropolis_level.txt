====== Necropolis Level ======


The **Necropolis Level** is a dungeon area in Remixed Dungeon, typically located after the caves and before the city or halls. It is characterized by undead-themed enemies and dark, tomb-like environments.

==== Description ====
The Necropolis Level is a dark and foreboding area filled with undead creatures and ancient horrors. This level features:
  * Undead enemies such as [[en:rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeletons]], [[en:rpd:zombie_mob|Zombies]]
  * Difficult terrain with barriers and obstacles
  * Rare items and powerful enemies
  * Themed around necromancy and death magic

Mobs found in the Necropolis Level include:
  * [[en:rpd:zombie_mob|Zombie]]
  * [[en:rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeleton]]
  * [[en:rpd:ghost_npc|Ghost]]
  * [[en:rpd:warlock_mob|Warlock]]
  * [[en:rpd:lich_mob|Lich]] (Boss)
  * [[en:rpd:death_knight_mob|Death Knight]]
  * [[en:rpd:zombie_gnoll_mob|Zombie Gnoll]]
  * [[en:rpd:enslaved_soul_mob|Enslaved Soul]]
  * [[en:rpd:cold_spirit_mob|Cold Spirit]]

==== Items ====
Items commonly found in the Necropolis Level include:
  * [[en:rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]]
  * Dark-themed weapons and armor

==== Strategy ====
When navigating the Necropolis Level:
  * Prepare for undead-specific challenges
  * Bring anti-undead items and spells
  * Be ready for different tactics against undead enemies
  * Watch for unique mechanics and traps

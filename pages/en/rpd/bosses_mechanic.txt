====== Bosses Overview ======

{{ rpd:images:goo_mob.png|Goo Boss }}

**Bosses** are powerful enemies that act as major challenges and often guard important areas or items in the dungeon.

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Power**: Much stronger than regular enemies
  * **Abilities**: Often have unique special abilities
  * **Health**: Significant HP pools compared to regular enemies
  * **Rewards**: Defeating bosses often provides important items or access to new areas
  * **Location**: Typically found at the end of major dungeon sections
  * **Immunities**: Bosses are typically immune to psionic blast, death enchantment, and other one-hit-kill effects

==== Known Bosses ====
  * [[en:rpd:goo_mob|Goo]] - Found in the Sewers level (level 5)
  * [[en:rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]] - Found in the Prison level (levels 6-10)
  * [[en:rpd:dm300_mob|DM-300]] - Found in the Caves level (level 15)
  * [[en:rpd:king_mob|Dwarf King]] - King of the Dwarves in the City level (level 20)
  * [[en:rpd:yog_mob|Yog-Dzewa]] - Main body of Yog-Dzewa in the Halls (level 25, final boss)
  * [[en:rpd:spider_queen_mob|Spider Queen]] - Found in the Spider Caves level (levels 11s-15s)
  * [[en:rpd:lich_mob|Lich]] - Undead boss in Necropolis level (levels necro1-necro4)
  * [[en:rpd:shadow_lord_mob|Shadow Lord]] - Boss in Shadow Caves level (levels shadow1-shadow4)
  * [[en:rpd:ice_guardian_core_mob|Ice Guardian Core]] - Boss in Ice Caves level (levels ice1-ice4)

==== Battle Strategy ====
  * Ensure proper preparation before engaging
  * Stock up on healing items
  * Consider equipment and enchantments that would be beneficial
  * Understand the boss's abilities and patterns
  * Have an escape plan if the battle turns against you

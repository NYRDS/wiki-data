====== Boss ======

**Bosses** are powerful enemies that act as major challenges and often guard important areas or items in the dungeon.

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Power**: Much stronger than regular enemies
  * **Abilities**: Often have unique special abilities
  * **Health**: Significant HP pools compared to regular enemies
  * **Rewards**: Defeating bosses often provides important items or access to new areas
  * **Location**: Typically found at the end of major dungeon sections
  * **Immunities**: Bosses are typically immune to psionic blast, death enchantment, and other one-hit-kill effects

==== Battle Strategy ====
  * Ensure proper preparation before engaging
  * Stock up on healing items
  * Consider equipment and enchantments that would be beneficial
  * Understand the boss's abilities and patterns
  * Have an escape plan if the battle turns against you

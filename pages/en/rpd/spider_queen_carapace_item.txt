====== Spider Queen Carapace ======

{{ rpd:images:spider_queen_carapace_materials.png|Spider Queen Carapace }}

The **Spider Queen Carapace** is a rare crafting material obtained from the Spider Queen. It is a shard of the Spider Queen's carapace with a piece of a spider gland inside that appears to be alive.

==== Description ====
The Spider Queen Carapace is a crafting material that appears to be made from the tough shell of the Spider Queen. Inside, you can see a piece of a spider gland that appears to be alive and moving. This material is used in advanced crafting recipes.

==== Properties ====
  * **Item Type**: Crafting Material
  * **Stackable**: Yes
  * **Rarity**: Very Rare (Boss Drop)
  * **Source**: [[en:rpd:spider_queen_mob|Spider Queen]] (drops after being defeated)

==== Uses ====
  * Currently unknown crafting recipes (future content)
  * Potential material for spider-themed equipment
  * Might be used to craft armor with web-related properties

==== Notes ====
This material is highly valued for its unique properties, containing a live spider gland that seems to be still active. It's sought after by players who wish to craft advanced spider-themed equipment.

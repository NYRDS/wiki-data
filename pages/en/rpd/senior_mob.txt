====== Senior Mob ======

{{ rpd:images:senior_mob.png|Senior }}

The **Senior** is an elite variant of the Monk mob found in the game. These monks are fanatics, who devoted themselves to protecting their city's secrets from all aliens. They don't use any armor or weapons, relying solely on the art of hand-to-hand combat. This particular monk has mastered the art of disabling his opponents.

==== Mob Overview ====
  * **Type**: Elite variant of Monk
  * **Level**: City level (typically around level 16-19)
  * **Special Ability**: 10% chance to apply Stun debuff when attacking
  * **Behavior**: Strong melee fighter with occasional stunning ability
  * **Sprite**: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/spritesDesc/Senior.json|Senior.json]]

==== Stats ====
  * **HP/HT**: Varies with dungeon level
  * **Attack Skill**: Higher than regular Monks
  * **Defense Skill**: Higher than regular Monks
  * **Damage**: 12-20 (base range)
  * **Speed**: Normal monk speed
  * **EXP for Kill**: Higher than regular Monks

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * When attacking, has a 10% chance to apply the Stun debuff to the target for 1.1 turns
  * Follows the same general behavior as regular Monks with special stunning capability
  * Uses kick animation for some attacks (30% chance)

==== Strategy ====
  * Senior monks can be dangerous due to their stunning ability
  * Be prepared for occasional stuns that can interrupt spellcasting or other actions
  * Consider using ranged attacks to avoid being stunned in melee combat

==== Obtaining ====
Seniors can be found in the City level (typically levels 16-19):
  * They spawn as part of the regular mob spawn pattern in the City area

====== Yogs Heart ======

{{ rpd:images:yogs_heart_mob.png|Yogs Heart }}

==== Description ====
**Yogs Heart** is one of Yog's guardians found in the Goo Depths. It is one of the secondary bosses protecting Yog along with Rotting Fist, Burning Fist, Yogs' Brain, and Yogs' Teeth.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP**: 450
  * **HT**: 450
  * **Defense Skill**: 40
  * **Attack Skill**: 26
  * **Damage**: 35-45
  * **DR (Damage Resistance)**: 22
  * **EXP for Kill**: 12

==== Abilities ====
  * **Larva Summoning**: When attacked, summons Larva mobs to fight alongside it
  * **Healing**: During its turn, attempts to heal a random living mob in the level by 20%
  * **Summoning**: When damaged, calls for help from nearby mobs
  * **Immunities**: Immune to Toxic Gas, Paralysis, Amok, Sleep, Terror, and Burning
  * **No Pet**: Cannot be turned into a pet

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Passively heals other monsters during its turn, making it dangerous in groups
  * Summons Larva when taking damage to aid in combat
  * Employs defensive tactics by summoning allies rather than directly engaging

==== Drops ====
  * Standard drops according to treasure category system

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Goo Depths (last level) as one of Yog's guardians

====== Mastery ======

Mastery in Remixed Dungeon refers to the advanced subclass system where heroes can specialize further after reaching certain milestones.

==== Unlocking Subclasses ====
  * Subclasses are unlocked by using a [[en:rpd:tome_of_mastery_item|Tome of Mastery]]
  * The tome can be used after defeating the second boss (Tengu)
  * Each class has two possible mastery paths

==== Subclass Paths ====
  * **Warrior**: [[en:rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]] or [[en:rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]]
  * **Mage**: [[en:rpd:battlemage_subclass|Battle Mage]] or [[en:rpd:warlock_subclass|Warlock]]
  * **Rogue**: [[en:rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]] or [[en:rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]]
  * **Huntress**: [[en:rpd:sniper_subclass|Sniper]] or [[en:rpd:warden_subclass|Warden]]
  * **Elf**: [[en:rpd:scout_subclass|Scout]] or [[en:rpd:shaman_subclass|Shaman]]
  * **Necromancer**: [[en:rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]] or [[en:rpd:witch_doctor_subclass|Witch Doctor]]
  * **Gnoll**: [[en:rpd:guardian_subclass|Guardian]] or [[en:rpd:witch_doctor_subclass|Witch Doctor]]

==== Benefits ====
  * Unique abilities and mechanics specific to each subclass
  * Enhanced combat effectiveness in specialized areas
  * Additional strategic options during gameplay

==== Strategy ====
  * Choose your subclass based on your preferred playstyle
  * Consider synergies with your class's existing strengths
  * Subclasses significantly change gameplay experience

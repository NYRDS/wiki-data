====== Speed ======

==== Description ====
Speed is a game mechanic that affects movement speed, allowing the character to move more quickly through the dungeon. This can be useful for escaping dangerous situations or reaching destinations faster.

This is a general topic page about the speed mechanic. For the specific buff, see [[en:rpd:speed_buff|Speed Buff]].

==== Effects ====
  * Increases movement speed of the character
  * Allows faster traversal of dungeon levels
  * May improve ability to avoid certain attacks or hazards
  * Reduces the time needed to move between locations

====== Potion of Might Item ======

The **Potion of Might** is a powerful potion that permanently increases the drinker's Strength and Health. It is most commonly obtained as a reward for completing the AzuterronNPC quest.

==== Overview ====
  * **Type**: Potion
  * **Effect**: Permanently increases Strength and Health
  * **Common Source**: Reward for completing the AzuterronNPC (Stranger) quest
  * **Effect Duration**: Permanent increase to stats

==== Effects ====
  * **Permanent Strength Increase**: Increases the drinker's Strength by 1 point
  * **Permanent Health Increase**: Increases the drinker's maximum HP by 5 points
  * **Immediate Healing**: Heals 5 HP immediately when consumed
  * **Instant Effect**: The effect is applied immediately upon drinking
  * **Cannot be Duplicated**: The effect only works once per character (subsequent Potions of Might have no effect)

==== Usage Notes ====
  * This is one of the most valuable potions in the game as it permanently increases the character's stats
  * Most commonly obtained by completing the quest from AzuterronNPC (Stranger) in special shop levels
  * Can also be found in rare circumstances in the dungeon or as reward for other achievements
  * Should be consumed at a safe location as it may attract attention from nearby enemies
  * Best consumed early in the game to maximize the benefit of increased stats throughout the run

====== Stealth Mechanics ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_invisibility_item.png|Invisibility Potion }}

==== Description ====
Stealth mechanics allow the player to avoid detection by enemies in various ways.

==== Invisibility ====
  * **[[en:rpd_potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]]** - Renders the player invisible
  * **[[en:rpd_invisibility_buff|Invisibility Buff]]** - Temporarily prevents enemy detection
  * **[[en:rpd_ring_of_shadows_item|Ring of Shadows]]** - Provides stealth bonuses
  * **[[en:rpd_magic_torch_spell|Magic Torch Spell]]** - Creates dark areas to hide in

==== Detection Factors ====
  * **Line of Sight** - Most enemies detect players in direct sight
  * **Stealth Level** - Affects ability to remain undetected
  * **Equipment Weight** - Heavier equipment may increase noise
  * **Movement** - Moving through certain terrain makes noise

==== Stealth-Enhancing Items ====
  * **[[en:rpd_ring_of_evasion_item|Ring of Evasion]]** - Improves stealth and detection avoidance
  * **[[en:rpd_ammo|Ammunition]]** - Some ammo types provide stealth benefits
  * **[[en:rpd_rogue_armor_item|Rogue Armor]]** - Armor that doesn't impede stealth
  * **[[en:rpd_shadow_mob|Shadow]]** - Entity associated with stealth mechanics

==== Applications ====
  * **Avoiding Combat** - Bypassing enemies without fighting
  * **Surprise Attacks** - Getting first attack advantage
  * **Theft** - Stealing items from enemies or chests
  * **Escape** - Getting away from dangerous situations

==== Strategy ====
  * **Situational Awareness** - Know what breaks stealth in different situations
  * **Timing** - Use stealth when most advantageous
  * **Equipment Planning** - Choose stealth-appropriate gear
  * **Movement** - Careful pathfinding to avoid detection

==== Countering Detection ====
  * **Distance** - Stay out of enemy detection range
  * **Blocking** - Use walls and obstacles to block line of sight
  * **Timing** - Wait for enemies to move away before proceeding
  * **Diversion** - Use items to distract enemies

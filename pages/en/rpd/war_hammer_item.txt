====== War Hammer ======

{{ rpd:images:war_hammer_item.png|War Hammer }}

The War Hammer is a type of [[en:rpd:melee_weapon|melee weapon]] in Remixed Dungeon that focuses on dealing heavy crushing damage.

==== Description ====
The War Hammer is a heavy, blunt weapon designed for dealing massive damage to enemies. It sacrifices speed for devastating impact power, making it particularly effective against heavily armored opponents.

==== Weapon Properties ====
  * **Damage Type:** Crushing damage that often ignores armor
  * **Speed:** Slow attack speed
  * **Accuracy:** Average accuracy
  * **Upgrade Potential:** Can be upgraded with [[en:rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * **Enchantments:** Compatible with [[en:rpd:weapon_enchantments|weapon enchantments]]
  * **High Damage:** Significant damage output per hit

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** 5 damage (base)
  * **Speed:** 1.2f speed factor (relatively slow)
  * **Accuracy:** 1f accuracy factor
  * **Strength Requirement:** High strength requirement to use effectively
  * **Durability:** Generally durable weapon

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Armor Piercing:** Damage often bypasses or reduces enemy armor
  * **Crushing Impact:** Particularly effective against armored enemies
  * **Slow but Powerful:** Trades speed for high damage potential (1.2f speed factor, 5 base damage)
  * **Strength Dependent:** Damage scales with hero's strength
  * **Heavy Attack:** Uses HEAVY_ATTACK animation class for special combat animations

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective against heavily armored enemies due to armor piercing
  * Best used with sufficient strength to meet requirements
  * Slow speed means careful positioning and timing are essential
  * Good for finishing off weakened opponents quickly
  * Excellent against enemies with high defense ratings

==== Obtaining ====
  * War hammers can be found throughout the dungeon
  * May be available in shops, depending on the level
  * Relatively rare due to high power level

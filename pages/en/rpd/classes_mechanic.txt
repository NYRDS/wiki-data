====== Classes ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_warrior.png|Warrior Class }}

Classes represent the different playable character archetypes in Remixed Dungeon, each with unique starting equipment, abilities, and specializations.

==== Main Classes ====
  * [[en:rpd_warrior_class|Warrior]] - The strong warrior with high health and melee focus
  * [[en:rpd_mage_class|Mage]] - The spell-casting master dealing magical damage
  * [[en:rpd_rogue_class|Rogue]] - The stealthy assassin with poisons and traps
  * [[en:rpd_huntress_class|Huntress]] - The ranged attacker with throwing weapons
  * [[en:rpd_elf_class|Elf]] - The versatile archer with magic capabilities
  * [[en:rpd_necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - The dark magic wielder who commands the undead
  * [[en:rpd_gnoll_class|Gnoll]] - The witchcraft-focused class with high strength and low health, forbidden actions, and friendly mobs
  * [[en:rpd_priest_class|Priest]] - The healing and sanctity focused class
  * [[en:rpd_doctor_class|Doctor]] - The plague and disease-focused class with accuracy penalty when not using Bone Saw

==== Characteristics ====
  * Each class has unique starting equipment
  * Classes determine the available specializations (subclasses)
  * Classes have distinct playstyles and strategies
  * Each class can be upgraded to Mastery through Tome of Mastery

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Warrior**: Short sword, armor, health potion, [[en:rpd_potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] (known), Body Armor spell (in quickslot)
  * **Mage**: Wand of Magic Missile, armor, health potion, [[en:rpd_scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]] (known), Wind Gust spell (in quickslot), Wand of Magic Missile (in quickslot)
  * **Rogue**: Ring of Shadows +1, leather armor, health potion, [[en:rpd_scroll_of_magic_mapping_item|Scroll of Magic Mapping]] (known), Cloak spell (in quickslot)
  * **Huntress**: Boomerang, studded armor, health potion, 15 HP, Shoot In Eye spell (in quickslot), Boomerang (in quickslot)
  * **Elf**: Elven bow +1 with arrows (20 CommonArrows), Elven dagger, elf armor, health potion, 9 STR, 15 HP, Magic Arrow spell (in quickslot), Common Arrow (in quickslot), immunity to Roots
  * **Necromancer**: Necromancer robe, health potion, Skeleton Key for level 7, Summon Deathling spell (in quickslot), [[en:rpd_potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] (known)
  * **Gnoll**: Gnoll Tamahawk, NoItem armor (starts with no armor), 8 Darts, Roar spell (in quickslot), high STR (15) low HP (5), 3 forbidden actions (Scroll_ACRead, TomeOfMastery_ACRead, Stylus_ACInscribe), friendly to Gnoll, Shaman, Brute, Shielded, ShamanElder mobs
  * **Priest**: Robe, health potion, [[en:rpd_scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]] (known)
  * **Doctor**: Robe, health potion, [[en:rpd_potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]], [[en:rpd_potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]], [[en:rpd_potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] (known)

==== Class Mechanics ====
  * Each class has a unique class armor that becomes available upon gaining enough strength to upgrade their starting weapon to level 3
  * Class armors provide enhanced abilities specific to each class
  * Classes have different strengths and weaknesses in various dungeon areas

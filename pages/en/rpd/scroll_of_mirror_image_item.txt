====== Scroll Of Mirror Image ======

{{ rpd:images:scroll_of_mirror_image_item.png|Scroll Of Mirror Image }}

==== Description ====
The **Scroll of Mirror Image** is a magical scroll that creates illusory duplicates of the reader. These mirror images can confuse enemies, helping the hero escape dangerous situations.

==== Effects ====
  * Creates 2-3 mirror images of the hero
  * Mirror images have minimal health but look identical to the hero
  * Lasts for a limited duration or until destroyed
  * Mirror images dissipate when hit by enemies

==== Mechanics ====
  * Consumes the scroll upon use
  * Creates temporary allies that look like the hero
  * Can be used defensively to confuse enemies
  * Particularly useful against strong monsters

==== Strategy ====
  * Use before engaging tough enemies to create confusion
  * Effective for escaping difficult situations
  * Can provide tactical advantages in groups of enemies
  * Helps preserve health during dangerous encounters

==== Technical Details ====
  * **Java Class:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/scrolls/ScrollOfMirrorImage.java|ScrollOfMirrorImage.java]]
  * **Base Class:** Extends Scroll class
  * **Implementation:** Creates temporary ally mobs that mimic the player

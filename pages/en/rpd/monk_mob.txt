====== Monk ======

{{ rpd:images:monk_mob.png|Monk }}

**Monk** is a human [[en:rpd:mob|mob]] found in the [[en:rpd:city_level|City]] (levels 14-19) of Remixed Dungeon. It's a fast [[en:rpd:melee_mechanic|melee]] combatant that can knock your weapon out of your hands during combat.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: 21 (max)
  * **HP**: 70
  * **EXP for killing**: 11
  * **Defense Skill**: 30
  * **Attack Skill**: 30
  * **Damage**: 12-16
  * **Damage Reduction**: 2
  * **Speed**: 2× (0.5s attack delay)

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Disarm**: When attacking the Monk, there's a chance it will knock your [[en:rpd:weapon|weapon]] out of your hands, forcing you to use fists
  * **High Evasion**: Very hard to hit due to high evasion stats
  * **Fast Movement**: Moves much faster than most other [[en:rpd:mobs|enemies]]
  * **Normal Damage**: Deals normal physical damage (not magical or elemental)

==== Strategy ====
  * Use [[en:rpd:ranged_weapon|ranged weapons]] or [[en:rpd:spell|spells]] to avoid its disarm ability
  * Wear [[en:rpd:armor_item|armor]] with high defense to reduce damage from its attacks
  * Be cautious when fighting in narrow corridors where you can't avoid its attacks
  * If disarmed, try to retrieve your weapon quickly or switch to a different [[en:rpd:weapon|weapon]]
  * Use [[en:rpd:weapon|weapons]] with [[en:rpd:enchantments|enchantments]] that trigger on hit to deal damage despite its high evasion

==== Drops ====
[[en:rpd:monk_mob|Monks]] can drop:
  * **[[en:rpd:dwarf_token_item|Dwarf Tokens]]** - During the [[en:rpd:blacksmith|Blacksmith's]] quest
  * [[en:rpd:gold_item|Gold coins]]
  * [[en:rpd:ration_item|Rations]] or other food items
  * Occasionally a piece of [[en:rpd:equipment|equipment]] or useful item

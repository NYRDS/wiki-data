====== Church ======

{{ rpd:images:church_level.png|Church }}

The **Church** is a special level in Remixed Dungeon where the [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|Bishop]] resides, along with other NPCs and unique features.

==== Description ====
The Church is a special level that serves as a safe zone in the dungeon. It contains the [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|Bishop]] NPC who offers various services in exchange for gold. The level has a religious theme with cathedral-style architecture.

==== Features ====
  * **NPCs**: Contains the [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|Bishop]] NPC
  * **Safety**: Generally safe from hostile creatures
  * **Services**: Access to [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|Bishop]]'s services (blessings, curse removal)
  * **Architecture**: Religious/cathedral-themed design with pews, altar, etc.

==== Services ====
The [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|Bishop]] offers the following services:
  * **Lesser Bless**: Provides [[en:rpd:blessed_buff|Blessed]] buff for ~100g
  * **Greater Bless**: Provides extended [[en:rpd:blessed_buff|Blessed]] buff for ~500g  
  * **Curse Removal**: Removes curses from all equipment for ~200g

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Punishment**: Killing the [[en:rpd:bishop_npc|Bishop]] results in damage to the hero (half max HP minimum 1)
  * **Location**: Typically accessible from the town area
  * **Return**: Players can leave the church level to return to other dungeon areas

==== Architecture ====
The church level features:
  * Wooden pews arranged in rows
  * Central altar area
  * Religious iconography
  * Stained glass windows (in some versions)

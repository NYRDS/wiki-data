====== Boss Badges ======

**Boss Badges** are special achievements awarded for defeating various bosses throughout the dungeon.

==== Known Boss Badges ====
  * [[en:rpd:badge_spider_queen_slain|Badge Spider Queen Slain]] - For defeating the Spider Queen
  * Badge for defeating the King of Dwarves
  * Badge for defeating the Yog-Dzewa
  * Badge for defeating the Goo
  * Badge for defeating the Tengu

==== Significance ====
  * Document the hero's victories against major challenges
  * Part of the overall achievement system
  * Mark significant milestones in a dungeon run
  * May have special recognition value

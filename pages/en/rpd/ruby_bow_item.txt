====== Ruby Bow ======

{{ rpd:images:ruby_bow_item.png|Ruby Bow }}

**Ruby Bow** is a unique [[en:rpd:ranged_weapon|ranged weapon]] in Remixed Dungeon, a powerful bow for expert archers.

==== General Information ====
  * **Type:** [[en:rpd:ranged_weapon|Ranged Weapon]]
  * **Classification:** [[en:rpd:bow_item|Bow]]
  * **Rarity:** Very Rare
  * **Slot:** Main Hand (Ranged Weapon)
  * **Range:** 5 tiles
  * **Damage:** 5 (base)
  * **Speed:** 0.8 (slow)
  * **Accuracy:** 1.5 (very high)

==== Special Properties ====
  * Almost every arrow fired from it pierces through the enemy, leaving a huge hole in their body
  * Requires significant strength for effective use
  * Very difficult to pull the bowstring

==== Strategy ====
  * Excellent for experienced players with high stats
  * Ideal for attacking multiple enemies in a line due to piercing shots
  * Requires appropriate strength level to use without penalties
  * Superior weapon for ranged carnage at medium range

==== Obtaining ====
  * Can be found as rare loot from strong enemies
  * Sometimes appears in treasure chests
  * May result from [[en:rpd:alchemy_mechanic|alchemical]] recipes

==== Comparison ====
  * Significantly outperforms basic bows in terms of damage
  * Slower than other weapon types
  * Ideal for ranged-focused characters with high stats

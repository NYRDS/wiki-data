====== Necromancy ======

**Necromancy** is a magic affinity in Remixed Dungeon that focuses on raising the dead and commanding undead creatures.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Summoning Spells:** Ability to summon undead creatures to fight alongside the caster
  * **Undead Mastery:** Improved control and strength of undead allies
  * **Life Drain:** Some spells drain health from enemies to heal the caster or allies
  * **Corpse Manipulation:** Ability to use corpses for various magical purposes

====== Game Mechanics ======

This page provides an overview of important game mechanics in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Combat ====
Combat in Remixed Dungeon is turn-based, with each action consuming time units. Key aspects include:
  * **Accuracy vs Evasion**: Attacks succeed based on the attacker's accuracy vs defender's evasion
  * **Damage Calculation**: Damage is reduced by the defender's armor (DR - Damage Reduction)
  * **Critical Hits**: Some weapons and effects can cause critical hits for extra damage
  * **Ranged Combat**: Ranged weapons have different mechanics and may require ammunition

==== Level Progression ====
  * **Hero Leveling**: Gain experience by defeating enemies and completing objectives
  * **Strength**: Affects your ability to wield weapons and armor
  * **Health**: Your hit points that decrease when taking damage
  * **Skill Points**: Used to power special abilities in certain classes

==== Item System ====
  * **Identification**: Magical items must be identified to know their properties
  * **Upgrading**: Items can be upgraded to improve their effectiveness
  * **Cursing**: Some items may be cursed and cause negative effects
  * **Durability**: Some items have limited use before breaking

==== Status Effects ====
  * **Buffs**: Positive effects that benefit the character
  * **Debuffs**: Negative effects that harm the character
  * **Duration**: Most effects last for a limited number of turns
  * **Resistance**: Some characters can resist certain effects based on class or equipment

==== Exploration ====
  * **Secrets**: Hidden doors and traps can be detected with certain items or abilities
  * **Dew Collection**: Morning dew provides health restoration
  * **Light Sources**: Torches and other light sources reveal nearby area
  * **Mapping**: Different methods of revealing the dungeon layout

==== Magic System ====
  * **Spells**: Different classes have access to various spells
  * **Wands**: Magical items that require charges to cast effects
  * **Scrolls**: Single-use magical items with powerful effects
  * **Potions**: Consumable items with beneficial effects

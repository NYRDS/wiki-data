====== Spider Queen ======

{{ rpd:images:spider_queen_mob.png|Spider Queen }}

==== Description ====
**Spider Queen** is a boss-level mob in Remixed Dungeon. She is the matriarch of the spider creatures found in the Spider Level.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP**: 120
  * **HT**: 120 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L25|SpiderQueen.java:L25]])
  * **Defense Skill**: 18 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L26|SpiderQueen.java:L26]])
  * **Attack Skill**: 21 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L27|SpiderQueen.java:L27]])
  * **Damage**: 12-20 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L29-L30|SpiderQueen.java:L29-L30]])
  * **DR (Damage Resistance)**: 10 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L28|SpiderQueen.java:L28]])
  * **EXP for Kill**: 11 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L27|SpiderQueen.java:L27]])

==== Abilities ====
  * **Spider summoning**: Randomly spawns Spider Eggs during combat (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L40-L44|SpiderQueen.java:L40-L44]])
  * **Poison attack**: Has a 50% chance to apply poison to enemies on hit (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L48-L51|SpiderQueen.java:L48-L51]])
  * **Fire immunity**: Immune to fire-based attacks and burning effects (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L55-L56|SpiderQueen.java:L55-L56]])
  * **Retreat behavior**: When below 50% HP, attempts to stay at range during combat (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L58-L63|SpiderQueen.java:L58-L63]])

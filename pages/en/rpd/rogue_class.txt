====== Rogue ======

{{ rpd:images:rogue_hero.png|Rogue }}

==== Description ====
The Rogue is one of the starting classes in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. This class specializes in stealth, mobility, and cunning tactics. Rogues excel in using traps and hidden passages to their advantage, making them excellent for players who prefer a more evasive and tactical approach to combat.

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * Worn Shortsword
  * Cloth armor
  * 2 Throwing Daggers
  * Amulet of Yendor

==== Stats ====
  * **Starting HP**: 12 (same as other classes)
  * **Starting Str**: 10 (same as other classes)
  * **HP Increase per Level**: 2 (same as other classes)
  * **Str Increase per Level**: +0.5 (same as other classes)

==== Affinities ====
  * **Magic Affinity**: [[en:rpd:rogue_affinity|Rogue Affinity]] - This class has a special connection to rogue-themed spells such as [[en:rpd:backstab_spell|Backstab]]

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Traps**: Rogues are sensitive to traps and will be warned of nearby ones
  * **Stealth**: Rogues have advantages in stealth and surprise attacks
  * **Dagger Mastery**: Rogues may have special bonuses when using daggers and other light weapons

==== Strategy ====
  * Take advantage of your trap sensitivity to navigate dungeon safely
  * Use stealth and surprise attacks to your advantage
  * Make use of hidden passages when available
  * Focus on mobility-based combat rather than direct confrontation

==== Subclasses ====
  * [[en:rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]] - A subclass focusing on powerful single-target strikes and elimination tactics

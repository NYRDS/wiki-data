====== Polearms ======

**Polearms** are a category of melee weapons characterized by their long reach, typically featuring a blade or head mounted on a long shaft.

==== List of Polearms ====
  * [[en:rpd:glaive_item|Glaive]]
  * [[en:rpd:spear_item|Spear]]
  * Glaive, Pike, Halberd, and War Scythe are not documented separately yet

==== Properties ====
  * **Extended Reach:** Can attack enemies from a distance (range 2)
  * **Two-Handed:** Requires two hands to wield effectively
  * **Slower Speed:** Generally slower attack speed than shorter weapons
  * **Higher Damage:** Often deal more damage than shorter weapons
  * **Tactical Advantage:** Excellent for controlling corridors and choke points

==== Strategy ====
  * Use in narrow spaces to prevent enemies from surrounding you
  * Effective for kiting enemies while staying out of their attack range
  * Good for classes with high strength to benefit from higher damage
  * Positioning is critical to maximize the reach advantage

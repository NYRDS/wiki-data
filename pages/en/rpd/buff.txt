====== Buffs ======

Buffs in Remixed Dungeon are temporary status effects that provide beneficial effects to characters. They contrast with debuffs which provide negative effects.

==== Types of Buffs ====
  * **Combat Buffs**: Improve combat effectiveness
  * **Utility Buffs**: Provide various gameplay advantages
  * **Resistance Buffs**: Provide protection against damage or debuffs
  * **Movement Buffs**: Affect speed or mobility

==== Common Buffs ====
  * [[en:rpd:haste_buff|Haste]]: Increases movement and attack speed
  * [[en:rpd:invisibility_buff|Invisibility]]: Makes you invisible to enemies
  * [[en:rpd:levitation_buff|Levitation]]: Allows movement over traps and water
  * [[en:rpd:light_buff|Light]]: Illuminates dark areas
  * [[en:rpd:mana_shield_buff|Mana Shield]]: Absorbs damage using mana
  * [[en:rpd:regeneration_buff|Regeneration]]: Restores health over time
  * [[en:rpd:shield_buff|Shield]]: Provides temporary damage absorption
  * [[en:rpd:time_stasis_buff|Time Stasis]]: Makes you invulnerable for a turn
  * [[en:rpd:blink_buff|Blink]]: Allows teleportation
  * [[en:rpd:counter_attack_buff|Counter Attack]]: Provides counter attack ability

==== Buff Mechanics ====
  * Buffs typically have duration limits measured in turns
  * Some buffs stack with themselves for enhanced effects
  * Buffs can be dispelled by certain attacks or effects
  * Some spells and items have increased effectiveness when applied to characters with specific buffs

====== Buffs ======

Buffs in Remixed Dungeon are temporary status effects that provide beneficial effects to characters. They contrast with debuffs which provide negative effects.

==== Types of Buffs ====
  * **Combat Buffs**: Improve combat effectiveness
  * **Utility Buffs**: Provide various gameplay advantages
  * **Resistance Buffs**: Provide protection against damage or debuffs
  * **Movement Buffs**: Affect speed or mobility

==== Common Buffs ====
  * [[en:rpd_invisibility_buff|Invisibility]]: Makes you invisible to enemies
  * [[en:rpd_levitation_buff|Levitation]]: Allows movement over traps and water
  * [[en:rpd_light_buff|Light]]: Illuminates dark areas
  * [[en:rpd_mana_shield_buff|Mana Shield]]: Absorbs damage using mana
  * [[en:rpd_regeneration_buff|Regeneration]]: Restores health over time
  * [[en:rpd_shield_buff|Shield]]: Provides temporary damage absorption

==== Buff Mechanics ====
  * Buffs typically have duration limits measured in turns
  * Some buffs stack with themselves for enhanced effects
  * Buffs can be dispelled by certain attacks or effects
  * Some spells and items have increased effectiveness when applied to characters with specific buffs

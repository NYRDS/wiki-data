====== Treasury ======

==== Description ====
The Treasury is a special level in Remixed Dungeon that contains valuable items and resources. It is one of the special rooms within the dungeon that adventurers can encounter during their journey.

==== Features ====
  * Contains valuable treasures and rare items
  * Often has special encounter mechanics
  * Can contain unique items like [[en:rpd_compound_bow_item|Compound Bow]] and [[en:rpd_spider_armor_item|Spider Armor]] (though Spider Armor has a 0 drop rate currently)

==== Layout ====
The Treasury features a design focused on protecting valuable items:
  * Guarded areas with monsters
  * Puzzle elements to reach treasures
  * Multiple chambers containing different types of loot

====== One Way Cursed Love Buff ======

{{ rpd:images/buff_OneWayCursedLoveBuff.png|One Way Cursed Love Buff }}

The **One Way Cursed Love Buff** is a status effect that occurs when using a cursed [[en:rpd:dried_rose_item|Dried Rose]].

==== Effects ====
  * ''Amorous'' effect - The rose looks beautiful but not very charming
  * Causes the user to become charmed in one direction

==== Notes ====
  * This buff is applied when the [[en:rpd:dried_rose_item|Dried Rose]] is cursed
  * The buff is part of the ghost's questline
  * Affects how NPCs interact with the player

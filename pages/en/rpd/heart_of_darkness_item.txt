====== Heart of Darkness ======

The **Heart of Darkness** is a quest item that is obtained after defeating the Treacherous Spirit. It is required to complete the quest given by the AzuterronNPC (Stranger).

==== Overview ====
  * **Type**: Quest Item
  * **Function**: Required to complete the AzuterronNPC quest
  * **Source**: Dropped by the Treacherous Spirit when defeated
  * **Quest**: Needed to trade with the Stranger (AzuterronNPC) for a Potion of Might

==== Mechanics ====
  * The Heart of Darkness is obtained as a drop from the Treacherous Spirit boss
  * When the player interacts with AzuterronNPC (Stranger) while possessing the Heart of Darkness, they can complete the quest
  * The item is consumed when trading it to AzuterronNPC in exchange for a Potion of Might
  * This item is part of the special quest system implemented in the Guts area of the dungeon

==== Quest Context ====
  * The Heart of Darkness is central to the AzuterronNPC questline
  * Players must defeat the Treacherous Spirit to obtain this item
  * Upon returning with the Heart of Darkness, players receive a Potion of Might as their reward
  * This represents a one-time interaction with the AzuterronNPC character

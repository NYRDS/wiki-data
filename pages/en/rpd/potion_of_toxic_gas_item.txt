====== Potion of Toxic Gas ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_toxic_gas_item.png|Potion of Toxic Gas }}

**Potion of Toxic Gas** is a utility potion in Remixed Dungeon that creates a cloud of toxic gas when thrown.

==== Description ====
When this potion is thrown at a location, it creates a cloud of toxic gas that damages any character that remains in the area. Unlike other potions, this is typically used as a tactical tool rather than for personal benefit.

==== Effects ====
  * **Gas Creation**: Creates a cloud of toxic gas at the target location
  * **Damage Over Time**: Characters in the gas cloud take damage each turn
  * **Area Effect**: Affects a small area around the impact point
  * **Duration**: Gas cloud persists for several turns

==== Stats ====
  * **Type**: Utility Potion
  * **Rarity**: Uncommon
  * **Weight**: 10 units
  * **Identified**: Requires identification to reveal effect
  * **Target**: Thrown at a tile (not self-use)

==== Usage ====
  * **Combat**: Used to damage groups of enemies
  * **Tactical**: Creating barriers of toxic gas to control enemy movement
  * **Emergency**: Used when surrounded to create an escape route
  * **Area Denial**: Blocking passages or chokepoints with gas

==== Strategy ====
  * **Positioning**: Consider where to throw it to affect multiple enemies
  * **Wind Direction**: If applicable, consider how gas clouds might spread
  * **Enemy Types**: Particularly effective against low-health enemies
  * **Self-Preservation**: Be careful not to trap yourself in gas clouds

==== Acquisition ====
  * **Found in**: Regular dungeon chests and shops
  * **Dropped by**: Various enemies
  * **Alchemy**: Potentially craftable if recipes exist

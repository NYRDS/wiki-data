====== Arachnid Mobs ======

This page lists all arachnid (spider-like) mobs in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Spider Mobs ====
  * [[en:rpd:scorpio_mob|Scorpio]] - Large arachnid-like demonic creatures that avoid close combat
  * [[en:rpd:acidic_scorpio_mob|Acidic Scorpio]] - Variant of Scorpio with special defensive abilities
  * [[en:rpd:spinner_mob|Spinner]] - Fast spider-like creature that shoots webs
  * [[en:rpd:spider_guard_mob|Spider Guard]] - Defensive spider mob
  * [[en:rpd:spider_mind_mob|Spider Mind]] - Spider with mental abilities
  * [[en:rpd:spider_mind_amber_mob|Spider Mind Amber]] - Variant of Spider Mind
  * [[en:rpd:spider_exploding_mob|Spider Exploding]] - Self-destructing spider
  * [[en:rpd:spider_servant_mob|Spider Servant]] - Spider that can be charmed
  * [[en:rpd:spider_egg_mob|Spider Egg]] - Mob that hatches into other spiders
  * [[en:rpd:spider_nest_mob|Spider Nest]] - Stationary mob that spawns other spiders
  * [[en:rpd:spider_queen_mob|Spider Queen]] - Powerful boss spider

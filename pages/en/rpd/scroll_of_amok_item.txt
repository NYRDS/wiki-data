====== Scroll Of Amok ======

{{ rpd:images:item_ScrollHolder.png|Scroll Of Amok }}

==== Description ====
The **Scroll of Amok** is a chaotic scroll that enrages nearby creatures, causing them to attack anything in sight regardless of friend or foe. This can turn fights to your advantage but can also be extremely dangerous.

==== Effects ====
  * Induces a state of frenzy in creatures within range
  * Affected creatures attack randomly
  * Lasts for a short duration
  * Affects both enemies and allies in the area

==== Mechanics ====
  * Consumes the scroll upon use
  * Creates chaos in the immediate area
  * Can be beneficial when surrounded by enemies
  * Risky when there are friendly creatures present

==== Strategy ====
  * Useful when surrounded by multiple enemies
  * Good for creating opportunities to escape
  * Can be used to make enemies fight each other
  * Avoid using when allied creatures are present

==== Technical Details ====
  * **Java Class:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/scrolls/ScrollOfAmok.java|ScrollOfAmok.java]]
  * **Base Class:** Extends Scroll class
  * **Implementation:** Applies Amok effect to nearby creatures

====== Well of Awareness ======

{{ rpd:images:well_of_awareness.png|Well of Awareness }}

**Well of Awareness** is a special dungeon feature in Remixed Dungeon that identifies all items in the hero's inventory.

==== Description ====
The Well of Awareness is a magical fountain that glows with psychic energy. When interacted with, it identifies all items in the hero's inventory, revealing their true properties and effects.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Identification**: Identifies all items in the inventory
  * **Single Use**: Becomes an empty well after use
  * **Interaction**: Requires direct interaction to activate
  * **Safety**: No risk associated with using this well

==== Effects ====
  * **Full Identification**: All items in inventory become fully identified
  * **Cursed Items**: Reveals which items are cursed
  * **Upgraded Items**: Reveals upgrade levels of identified items
  * **Equipment Status**: Shows true stats of weapons and armor

==== Strategy ====
  * **Timing**: Best used when inventory has many unidentified items
  * **Preparation**: Use before critical situations where item properties matter
  * **Efficiency**: More efficient than using scrolls of identification on individual items
  * **Cursed Items**: Allows you to know which items are cursed before equipping them

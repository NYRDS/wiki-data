====== Quest Items ======

Quest items are special items in Remixed Dungeon that are primarily used for completing various quests throughout the dungeon. These items are typically not for direct use by the player but are part of quest mechanics.

==== List of Quest Items ====
  * [[en:rpd_candy_item|Candy]] - Used for the Scarecrow NPC quest
  * [[en:rpd_candy_of_death_item|Candy of Death]] - Debug item that causes instant death
  * [[en:rpd_dark_gold_item|Dark Gold]] - Used for the Blacksmith NPC quest
  * [[en:rpd_pickaxe_item|Pickaxe]] - Required for the Blacksmith quest
  * [[en:rpd_rotberry_seed_item|Rotberry Seed]] - Used for the Wandmaker quest
  * [[en:rpd_giant_rat_skull_item|Giant Rat Skull]] - Alternative reward for the Ghost quest

==== Characteristics ====
  * Usually not consumable in normal gameplay
  * Primarily used for specific quest interactions
  * Often obtained through specific quest events
  * May have special mechanics tied to the quest system

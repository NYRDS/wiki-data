====== Barkskin Buff ======

{{ rpd:images:barkskin_buff.png|Barkskin }}

==== Description ====
**Barkskin** is a beneficial status effect in Remixed Dungeon that temporarily increases the character's armor, making them more resistant to damage.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Effect**: Increases armor value, reducing incoming physical damage
  * **Duration**: Varies based on the source

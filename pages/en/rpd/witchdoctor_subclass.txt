====== Witchdoctor Subclass ======

{{ rpd:images:witchdoctor_subclass.png|Witchdoctor }}

==== Description ====
The Witchdoctor is a subclass of the [[en:rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll hero class]] in Remixed Dungeon. Witchdoctors specialize in [[en:rpd:spellcasting|spellcasting]] and gain a [[en:rpd:mana_shield_buff|magic shield]] when casting spells, protecting them from incoming [[en:rpd:damage|damage]].

==== Abilities ====
  * **Mana Shield**: When casting a [[en:rpd:spells_overview|spell]], the Witchdoctor creates a [[en:rpd:mana_shield_buff|magic shield]] that completely protects from incoming [[en:rpd:damage|damage]]
  * **Spell Focus**: Enhanced effectiveness with magic-based [[en:rpd:items|items]] and [[en:rpd:spells_overview|spells]]

==== Specialization ====
  * Focuses on defensive [[en:rpd:spellcasting|spellcasting]] mechanics
  * Utilizes [[en:rpd:magic|magic]] [[en:rpd:items|items]] and [[en:rpd:wands|wands]] effectively
  * Tactical approach with [[en:rpd:protection|protection]] during [[en:rpd:spellcasting|spellcasting]]

==== Lore ====
The Witchdoctor subclass represents the mystical shamans of gnoll society, wielding [[en:rpd:magic|magical arts]] to protect their pack. They have a deep connection with magical energies and can create protective [[en:rpd:shields|shields]] during combat.

==== Acquisition ====
The Witchdoctor subclass can be unlocked by consuming a [[en:rpd:tengu_liver_item|Tengu Liver]] after defeating the second [[en:rpd:bosses|boss]], [[en:rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]]. It's only available to the [[en:rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll hero class]].

==== Equipment ====
  * Special armor: [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_armor|Witchdoctor Armor]] - Special armor for Witchdoctor subclass
  * Preferred weapons: [[en:rpd:magic|Magic]]-based [[en:rpd:items|items]], [[en:rpd:wands|wands]], and staves
  * Spell-enhancing [[en:rpd:items|items]]: [[en:rpd:equipment|Equipment]] that supports magical casting

==== Strategy ====
  * Cast [[en:rpd:spells_overview|spells]] strategically to activate the protective [[en:rpd:mana_shield_buff|mana shield]]
  * Use [[en:rpd:magic|magic items]] and [[en:rpd:wands|wands]] for both offense and defense
  * Leverage the protective [[en:rpd:shields|shield]] during dangerous [[en:rpd:spellcasting|spellcasting]] moments
  * Build around [[en:rpd:spellcasting|spellcasting]] synergies for optimal [[en:rpd:protection|protection]]

====== Enchantments ======

{{ rpd:images:item_sword.png|Enchanted Weapon }}

**Enchantments** are special magical properties that can appear on [[en:rpd_weapons|weapons]] in Remixed Dungeon, providing additional effects beyond their base damage and stats.

==== List of Enchantments ====
  * **[[en:rpd_fire_enchantment|Fire]]** - Chance to set enemies on fire on hit
  * **[[en:rpd_horror_enchantment|Horror]]** - Chance to cause enemies to flee in terror
  * **[[en:rpd_instability_enchantment|Instability]]** - Random enchantment effect on hit
  * **[[en:rpd_leech_enchantment|Leech]]** - Heals you for a portion of damage dealt
  * **[[en:rpd_luck_enchantment|Luck]]** - Increases damage with a chance for extra damage
  * **[[en:rpd_paralysis_enchantment|Paralysis]]** - Chance to stun enemies on hit
  * **[[en:rpd_piercing_enchantment|Piercing]]** - Chance to hit additional enemies behind the target
  * **[[en:rpd_poison_enchantment|Poison]]** - Chance to poison enemies on hit
  * **[[en:rpd_slow_enchantment|Slow]]** - Chance to slow enemies on hit
  * **[[en:rpd_swing_enchantment|Swing]]** - Hits all adjacent enemies when wielded with two hands
  * **[[en:rpd_death_enchantment|Death]]** - Chance to instantly kill non-boss enemies on hit

==== Identification ====
  * Enchantments are hidden until a weapon is [[en:rpd_identification|identified]]
  * Identified weapons will show their enchantment name
  * Cursed weapons have negative enchantments (curses)

==== Acquisition ====
  * Applied automatically when upgrading weapons with [[en:rpd_scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * Process is random - you don't choose which enchantment is applied
  * Higher-level weapons have a higher chance to receive an enchantment when upgraded
  * Only applies to weapons that can receive enchantments

==== Strategy ====
  * Identify weapons to reveal enchantments before use
  * Higher-level enchantments have stronger effects
  * Consider your playstyle when choosing weapons with specific enchantments
  * Some enchantments synergize well with specific hero classes

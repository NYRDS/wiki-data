====== Larva ======

{{ rpd:images:larva_mob.png|Larva }}

==== Description ====
**Larva** (also called **God Larva**) is a minion of the Old God Yog-Dzewa found in the Goo Depths. It is summoned by [[en:rpd_yogs_heart_mob|Yogs Heart]] and [[en:rpd_yogs_brain_mob|Yogs' Brain]] during combat.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP**: 120
  * **Base Defense Skill**: 20
  * **Base Attack Skill**: 30
  * **Damage**: 25-30
  * **Defense Rating (DR)**: 8
  * **Level**: 1
  * **EXP for Kill**: 0 (boss minion)

==== Behavior ====
  * **AI**: Hunting - actively pursues the player
  * **Special Ability**: At level 2+, upon death, spawns an **Imago** mob (Scorpio, Worm, or Eye) and dies
  * **Role**: Acts as a distraction and secondary threat during the Yog boss fight

==== Transformation ====
When a Larva reaches level 2 or higher and is killed, it transforms into one of three **Imago** forms:
  * **Scorpio** - Ranged attacker with poison
  * **Worm** - Burrowing melee attacker
  * **Eye** - Ranged magical attacker

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Goo Depths (last level) during the Yog boss fight
  * Summoned by [[en:rpd_yogs_heart_mob|Yogs Heart]] and [[en:rpd_yogs_brain_mob|Yogs' Brain]]

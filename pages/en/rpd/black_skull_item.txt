====== Black Skull ======

{{ rpd:images:black_skull_item.png|Black Skull Item }}

The **Black Skull** is a special [[en:rpd:artifact|artifact]] in Remixed Dungeon used for resurrection, particularly important for the [[en:rpd:lich_subclass|Lich subclass]].

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** [[en:rpd:artifact|Artifact]]
  * **Rarity:** Very Rare (Quest Item)
  * **Special Effect:** Pet resurrection and charge accumulation
  * **Usage:** Passive effect, activates on pet death when awakened
  * **Value:** Priceless (Quest item)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Charge System:** Accumulates charges (up to 10) when pets die while in inventory
  * **Awakened State:** Becomes "awakened black skull" when at 10 charges
  * **Pet Resurrection:** When awakened, revives dying pets for 10 charges per resurrection
  * **Deactivation:** Becomes dormant when charges drop to 0
  * **Passive Effect:** Works automatically when in inventory without user intervention
  * **Resurrection Message:** "The Black Skull has raised the dead monster to serve you!" (from string resources)
  * **Activation Message:** "The Black Skull has been awakened!" when reaching 10 charges
  * **Deactivation Message:** "The Black Skull is dormant, once more..." when charges drop to 0

==== Strategy ====
  * Keep one as a backup resurrection item for pets if playing as Necromancer
  * Essential for effective pet management in Necromancer builds
  * Best used by players who rely heavily on pet companions
  * Charge accumulation happens passively when pets die
  * Consider keeping pets in formation to maximize utility of resurrection
  * Useful for farming difficult areas with pet-based strategies
  * More reliable than other resurrection methods for pet companions
  * Can help preserve valuable pets during dangerous encounters
  * Essential for long-term pet-based gameplay

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found in [[en:rpd:necropolis_level|Necropolis]] as drop from various enemies
  * Occasionally obtained in special events in the dungeon
  * Can be found in containers in the [[en:rpd:necropolis_level|Necropolis]]
  * Sometimes available in shops in the Necropolis area

==== Effect ====
The Black Skull operates as a charge-based resurrection artifact. When pets die while the skull is in inventory, it accumulates charges (1 per pet death). When it reaches 10 charges, it becomes awakened and gains the ability to resurrect pets at the cost of 10 charges per resurrection. When charges drop to 0, it returns to dormant state and continues accumulating charges from pet deaths.

According to the game's string resources: "A small obsidian skull. It was the source of the Necropolis powers, but after the Lich's defet it seems to have lost all it's powers. However, when you hold it in your hands a strange thought crosses your mind: \" It hungers \"."

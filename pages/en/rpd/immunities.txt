====== Immunities ======

Immunities in Remixed Dungeon refer to resistances or complete prevention of certain status effects or damage types that characters or mobs can possess.

==== Types of Immunities ====

==== Complete Immunities ====
  * **Sleep**: Complete resistance to sleep status effect
  * **Paralysis**: Complete resistance to paralysis status effect
  * **Frost**: Complete resistance to freezing effects
  * **Burn**: Complete resistance to burning effects
  * **Toxic Gas**: Complete resistance to toxic gas effects
  * **Poison**: Complete resistance to poison damage over time
  * **Death**: Complete resistance to instant death effects
  * **Amok**: Complete resistance to amok status (turning hostile)
  * **Blindness**: Complete resistance to blindness status
  * **Terror**: Complete resistance to terror status (fleeing in fear)
  * **Vertigo**: Complete resistance to vertigo status (confused movement)
  * **Bleeding**: Complete resistance to bleeding damage over time

==== Partial Immunities ====
  * Resistance that reduces the effect of status conditions
  * May reduce duration or severity of effects

==== Acquiring Immunities ====
  * **Hero Classes**: Some hero classes have innate immunities (e.g., Lich has many immunities)
  * **Buffs**: Temporary immunities granted by buff effects
  * **Items**: Equipment that provides immunity to specific effects
  * **Mobs**: Certain mobs have immunities to specific status effects

==== Notable Immunities ====
  * **Lich**: Immune to Paralysis, Toxic Gas, Terror, Death, Amok, Blindness, Sleep, Poison, Vertigo, Bleeding
  * **Skeletons**: Usually burn to dust when killed
  * **Other undead**: May have immunities to specific effects

==== Strategy ====
  * Identify and target immunities when selecting equipment and tactics
  * Use different strategies against enemies with specific immunities
  * Consider immunity-granting equipment when facing certain enemies

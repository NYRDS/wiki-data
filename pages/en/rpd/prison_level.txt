====== Prison ======

**Prison** is the second dungeon level in Remixed Dungeon, representing an abandoned prison with dangerous prisoners and traps.

==== Level Features ====
  * **Traps:** Many different traps, including poison, fire, and ice traps
  * **Enemies:** Escaped prisoners, guards, and other dangerous entities
  * **Mechanics:** Special atmosphere and level logic, different from other levels

==== Special Elements ====
  * **[[en:rpd_tengu_mob|Tengu]]:** Level boss who uses teleportation and traps
  * **Locked Rooms:** Need [[en:rpd_skeleton_key_item|Skeleton Keys]] to open
  * **Hidden Passages:** Secret rooms with treasures and useful items
  * **Broken Traps:** Some traps have already been activated and are safe

==== Strategy ====
  * Move carefully, triggering traps from a safe distance
  * Save [[en:rpd_scroll_of_magic_mapping_item|Scroll of Magic Mapping]] for this level
  * Look for [[en:rpd_skeleton_key_item|Skeleton Keys]] from treasure rooms
  * Prepare for battle with [[en:rpd_tengu_mob|Tengu]] using wands or ranged strategy

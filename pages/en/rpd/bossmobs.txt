====== Boss Mobs ======

{{ rpd:images:tengu_mob.png|Boss Mob}}

Boss mobs are special, powerful enemies that appear at specific locations throughout the dungeon. They are much stronger than regular mobs and often have special abilities or mechanics.

==== Boss List ====
  * **[[en:rpd:gnoll_king_mob|Gnoll King]]** - Appears in the sewers
  * **[[en:rpd:dm300_mob|DM-300]]** - Appears in the prison
  * **[[en:rpd:king_mob|Dwarf King]]** - Appears in the caves
  * **[[en:rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]]** - Appears in the caves (special quest boss)
  * **[[en:rpd:yog_mob|Yog-Dzewa]]** - Appears in the center of the city of the dead

==== Common Boss Characteristics ====
  * Much higher HP, damage, and defense than regular mobs
  * Special abilities or unique mechanics
  * Often immune or resistant to certain effects
  * Guaranteed special drops upon defeat
  * More complex AI behavior

==== Boss-Specific Mechanics ====
Each boss has unique mechanics:
  * **[[en:rpd:dm300_mob|DM-300]]** - Self-repairs and has thick armor
  * **[[en:rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]]** - Uses smoke cloud mechanic and has multiple phases
  * **[[en:rpd:king_mob|Dwarf King]]** - Raises undead during combat
  * **[[en:rpd:yog_mob|Yog-Dzewa]]** - Complex multi-phase battle with helpers

==== Strategy ====
  * Prepare thoroughly before engaging bosses
  * Ensure you have healing and utilities
  * Understand the boss-specific mechanics
  * Bring equipment that counters boss resistances
  * Be patient - don't rush into attacks

==== Boss Drops ====
Bosses provide unique rewards:
  * Powerful artifacts or equipment
  * Rare crafting materials
  * Keys for special areas
  * Unique items with special properties

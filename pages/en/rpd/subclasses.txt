====== Subclasses ======

Subclasses are advanced specialization paths available to heroes in Remixed Dungeon after achieving certain milestones. Each playable class has its own unique subclass system that provides specialized abilities and gameplay changes.

==== Available Subclasses ====
  * [[en:rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]] - For the [[en:rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]], specializing in stealth and critical strikes
  * [[en:rpd:battlemage_subclass|Battlemage]] - For the [[en:rpd:mage_class|Mage]], combining magic with weapon enchantments
  * [[en:rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]] - For the [[en:rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]], specializing in rage and damage
  * [[en:rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]] - For the [[en:rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]], focusing on weapon mastery and combo attacks
  * [[en:rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]] - For the [[en:rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]], emphasizing mobility and evasion
  * [[en:rpd:sniper_subclass|Sniper]] - For the [[en:rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]], specializing in ranged combat
  * [[en:rpd:warden_subclass|Warden]] - For the [[en:rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]], focusing on nature and wooden equipment
  * [[en:rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]] - For the [[en:rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]], the pinnacle of necromantic power
  * [[en:rpd:warlock_subclass|Warlock]] - For the [[en:rpd:mage_class|Mage]], specializing in summoning and life drain
  * [[en:rpd:cleric_subclass|Cleric]] - For the [[en:rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]], focusing on divine magic and healing
  * [[en:rpd:healer_subclass|Healer]] - For the [[en:rpd:mage_class|Mage]], specializing in healing and support
  * [[en:rpd:scout_subclass|Scout]] - For the [[en:rpd:elf_class|Elf]], emphasizing exploration and stealth
  * [[en:rpd:shaman_subclass|Shaman]] - For the [[en:rpd:elf_class|Elf]], focusing on totems and elemental magic
  * [[en:rpd:witch_doctor_subclass|Witch Doctor]] - For the [[en:rpd:elf_class|Elf]], specializing in poison and disease
  * [[en:rpd:plague_doctor_subclass|Plague Doctor]] - For the [[en:rpd:doctor_class|Doctor]], focusing on disease and toxic effects

==== Acquisition ====
Subclasses are typically obtained through:
  * Special items found in deep dungeon levels (e.g., [[en:rpd:the_soulbringer_item|The Soulbringer]] for the Necromancer Lich subclass)
  * Class-specific challenges or quests
  * Special interactions with NPCs or level features
  * Unique mechanics for each class

==== Mechanics ====
  * Subclasses often provide unique abilities, stat changes, or gameplay mechanics
  * Some subclasses come with trade-offs (e.g., the [[en:rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]] gains necromantic power but loses physical strength)
  * Subclasses are permanent choices that significantly alter gameplay
  * Each subclass is designed to offer a different playstyle experience

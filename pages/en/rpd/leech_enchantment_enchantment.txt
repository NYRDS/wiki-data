====== Leech Enchantment ======

{{ rpd:images:enchant_leech.png|Leech Enchantment }}

==== Description ====
The Leech enchantment has a chance to heal the wielder for a portion of the damage dealt when hitting an enemy. This creates a sustain mechanic for melee combat.

==== Mechanics ====
  - Heals the wielder for a percentage of damage dealt
  - The healing amount is based on the weapon's level
  - Higher-level weapons provide more healing
  - Works with both physical and magical damage

==== Source Code ====
  - Java: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/weapon/enchantments/Leech.java|Leech.java]]
  - The effect is applied in the proc() method with healing amount scaling with weapon level

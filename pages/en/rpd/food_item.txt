====== Food ======

{{ rpd:images:ration_item.png|Food }}

**Food** is a category of items that satiate the hero's hunger and restore health.

==== Description ====
Food is necessary for survival in the dungeon. Without food, the hero will starve and eventually die of hunger.

==== Types of Food ====
  * [[en:rpd:ration_item|Ration]] - standard food that fully satisfies hunger
  * [[en:rpd:overpriced_ration_item|Overpriced Ration]] - more expensive but equally nutritious food
  * [[en:rpd:mystery_meat_item|Mystery Meat]] - can cause negative effects
  * [[en:rpd:chargrilled_meat_item|Chargrilled Meat]] - safe cooked food
  * [[en:rpd:rotten_ration_item|Rotten Ration]] - spoiled food that's harmful to health
  * [[en:rpd:christmas_turkey_item|Christmas Turkey]] - special food that doesn't spoil
  * [[en:rpd:pumpkin_pie_item|Pumpkin Pie]] - seasonal food

==== Effects ====
  * Satiates the hero's hunger
  * Some types of food may provide temporary buffs
  * Some types of food may cause negative effects
  * Some types of food do not spoil when exposed to toxic gas

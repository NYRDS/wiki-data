====== Witch Doctor Armor ======

Witch Doctor Armor is the class-specific armor obtained by using an Armor Kit on any regular armor for the Witch Doctor subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Use an Armor Kit on any regular armor while playing as a Necromancer who has chosen the Witch Doctor subclass
  * Transforms the base armor into Witch Doctor Armor with special properties

==== Special Properties ====
  * Inherits all properties of Gnoll Armor (Special ability varies)
  * Requires sufficient skill points to use special ability
  * Exclusive to the Witch Doctor subclass - cannot be equipped if not the Witch Doctor subclass

==== Usage ====
  * Essential armor for maximizing the effectiveness of the Witch Doctor subclass
  * Should be crafted as soon as possible after choosing the Witch Doctor subclass
  * Allows full utilization of subclass abilities

==== Notes ====
  * Part of the subclass identity system in Remixed Dungeon
  * Class armors represent the unique nature of each subclass
  * Cannot be worn by characters who haven't unlocked the appropriate subclass
  * Inherits the base Gnoll armor properties and special ability

====== Witchdoctor Armor ======

{{ rpd:images:witchdoctor_armor_item.png|Witchdoctor Armor }}

Witchdoctor Armor is the class-specific armor obtained by using an [[en:rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor for the [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_subclass|Witchdoctor]] subclass of the [[en:rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]] class in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Use an [[en:rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor while playing as a [[en:rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]] who has chosen the [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_subclass|Witchdoctor]] subclass
  * Transforms the base armor into Witchdoctor Armor with special properties

==== Special Properties ====
  * Inherits all properties of [[en:rpd:gnoll_armor_item|Gnoll Armor]]
  * Exclusive to the [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_subclass|Witchdoctor]] subclass - cannot be equipped unless you are the Witchdoctor subclass

==== Usage ====
  * Essential armor for maximizing the effectiveness of the [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_subclass|Witchdoctor]] subclass
  * Should be crafted as soon as possible after choosing the [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_subclass|Witchdoctor]] subclass
  * Allows full utilization of subclass abilities

==== Notes ====
  * Part of the subclass identity system in Remixed Dungeon
  * Class armors represent the unique nature of each subclass
  * Cannot be worn by characters who haven't unlocked the appropriate subclass
  * Inherits the base [[en:rpd:gnoll_armor_item|Gnoll armor]] properties and special ability

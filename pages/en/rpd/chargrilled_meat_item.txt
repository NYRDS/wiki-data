====== Chargrilled Meat ======

{{ rpd:images:chargrilled_meat_item.png|Chargrilled Meat }}

**Chargrilled Meat** is a food item in Remixed Dungeon. It can be obtained by cooking [[en:rpd:mystery_meat_item|Mystery Meat]] on a cauldron. It is a safe alternative to raw meat and provides moderate satiety.

==== Description ====
According to the game description: "A decent food which could pass for leather at first glance."

  * **Java class:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/food/ChargrilledMeat.java|ChargrilledMeat.java]]
  * **Inherits from:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/food/Food.java|Food.java]]

==== Properties ====
  * **Hunger satisfaction:** 120 units (Hunger.STARVING - Hunger.HUNGRY, where STARVING=400f and HUNGRY=280f)
  * **Price:** 7 gold per unit
  * **Type:** Food item
  * **Stackable:** Yes (up to 10 in a stack)
  * **Nutrition:** Moderate satiety, but less than a full ration

==== Usage ====
  * **Hunger restoration:** Consuming this item provides moderate satiety
  * **Safe food:** Unlike [[en:rpd:mystery_meat_item|Mystery Meat]], this item has no random effects
  * **Emergency food:** Reliable food option when other rations are not available

==== Obtaining ====
  * **Cooking:** Created by cooking [[en:rpd:mystery_meat_item|Mystery Meat]] in a cauldron in the dungeon
  * **Sometimes found:** Occasionally appears as dungeon loot, though rarely
  * **Mystery meat alternative:** Safer option than consuming raw mystery meat

==== Strategy ====
  * **Cook first:** Always cook [[en:rpd:mystery_meat_item|Mystery Meat]] before consuming to get chargrilled meat instead of risking negative effects
  * **Resource conservation:** Cooking mystery meat saves inventory space by converting a potentially harmful item into safe food
  * **Economical meal:** More economical option than purchasing overpriced rations from shops

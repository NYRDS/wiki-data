====== Sungrass Seed ======

{{ rpd:images:sungrass_seed_item.png|Sungrass Seed }}

The **Sungrass Seed** is a [[en:rpd:seed_item|seed]] found in Remixed Dungeon that can be planted to grow [[en:rpd:sungrass_level_object|Sungrass]] plants. It is a crucial ingredient for crafting [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]].

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** [[en:rpd:seed_item|Seed]]
  * **Rarity:** Common (appears regularly throughout dungeon)
  * **Usage:** Can be planted in [[en:rpd:high_grass|High Grass]] or crafted into [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] via [[en:rpd:alchemy_mechanic|alchemy]]

==== Obtaining ====
  * Dropped by various [[en:rpd:mobs|enemies]] throughout the dungeon
  * Found in [[en:rpd:chests_mechanic|chests]] and [[en:rpd:shops_mechanic|shops]]
  * Grows naturally from [[en:rpd:high_grass|High Grass]] when trampled
  * Can be obtained from [[en:rpd:alchemy_mechanic|alchemy]] processes

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Planting:** Can be planted in [[en:rpd:high_grass|High Grass]] to grow into [[en:rpd:sungrass_level_object|Sungrass]]
  * **Alchemy:** Used as an ingredient to craft [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] at an [[en:rpd:alchemy_pot_level_object|Alchemy Pot]]
  * **Seed Preservation:** If left uneaten on the floor through a level transition, seeds will be preserved
  * **Dungeon Generation:** Commonly found in earlier dungeon levels

==== Alchemy Recipes ====
  * **Potion of Healing:** 1 [[en:rpd:sungrass_seed_item|Sungrass Seed]] + Water = 1 [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]]

==== Strategy ====
  * **Conservation:** Since seeds are consumed by planting and trampling, it's important to conserve them early in the game
  * **Potion Efficiency:** Essential for creating [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]], one of the most important consumables
  * **Planting Strategy:** Plant in areas where you plan to rest to ensure [[en:rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] availability
  * **Seed Identification:** Identified by name when picked up or when seen in inventory after being identified

==== Effect ====
When planted in [[en:rpd:high_grass|High Grass]], the Sungrass Seed will grow into a [[en:rpd:sungrass_level_object|Sungrass]] plant that can be harvested to obtain another seed and [[en:rpd:potion_of_mending_item|Potion of Mending]].

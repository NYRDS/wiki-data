====== Knuckles ======

{{ rpd:images:knuckles_item.png|Knuckles }}

**Knuckles** (also known as Knuckleduster) is a melee weapon in Remixed Dungeon. It is a piece of iron shaped to fit around the knuckles.

==== Description ====
According to the game: "A piece of iron shaped to fit around the knuckles." This is a tier 1 weapon that requires 10 strength to use effectively. The average damage of this weapon is 3. This is a fairly fast weapon, and it can be used without serious loss of efficiency even with insufficient strength. A distinctive feature of Knuckles is that it cannot be disarmed.

==== Strategy ====
  * **Starting weapon:** A good choice for beginners since it doesn't reduce attack speed with insufficient strength
  * **Safety:** Cannot be disarmed, making it safe to use in close combat
  * **Affordability:** Low price makes it accessible for purchasing and upgrading

  * **Disadvantages:** Low base damage compared to other weapons of the same tier

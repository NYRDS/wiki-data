====== Chill ======

**Chill** is a negative status effect in Remixed Dungeon that reduces the target's movement and attack speed.

//Note: This page documents a legacy or modded effect. In the current version of Remixed Dungeon, the cold-based status effect is implemented as [[en:rpd:frost_buff|Frost]] buff.//

==== Effects ====
  * **Reduced Speed**: Affected characters move at a slower pace
  * **Slower Attacks**: Attack speed is reduced while chilled
  * **Duration**: The effect lasts for a limited time based on the source

==== How to Get ====
  * Being hit by certain enemies that cause chill effects
  * Being affected by certain spells or items that apply chill
  * Environmental effects in special dungeon areas

==== Countering Chill ====
  * The effect will wear off over time
  * Some items or abilities may remove the chill status
  * Potions of healing may remove status effects in some cases

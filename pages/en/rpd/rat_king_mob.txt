====== Rat King Mob ======

{{ rpd:images:rat_king_mob.png|Rat King }}

The Rat King is a unique boss mob in Remixed Dungeon. Despite its simple appearance, it commands great power and is often found in the sewers level.

==== General Information ====
  * **Type:** [[en:rpd_bosses_mechanic|Boss]] / [[en:rpd_mob|Mob]]
  * **Classification:** [[en:rpd_gnoll_mob|Gnoll]]-like creature
  * **Habitat:** Sewers level
  * **Behavior:** Stationary, summons rats, uses special attacks

==== Description ====
The Rat King is a special creature that is treated like a Gnoll for gameplay purposes but appears as a distinctive large rat. It sits on a throne and commands a small army of rats. While not as powerful as other dungeon bosses, it presents a significant challenge to early-game players.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** High for early game
  * **Accuracy:** Moderate
  * **Evasion:** Low
  * **Damage:** Moderate to high
  * **Defense:** Moderate
  * **Speed:** Slow

==== Abilities ====
  * **Rat Summoning:** Periodically summons additional rats to fight alongside it
  * **Throne Aura:** May have special effects due to sitting on a special throne
  * **Gnoll Mechanics:** Benefits from any Gnoll-specific mechanics or interactions

==== Combat Strategy ====
  * Prioritize eliminating summoned rats to avoid being overwhelmed
  * Keep distance to avoid multiple enemies at once
  * Use area-effect abilities carefully to avoid clearing too many summoned rats at once
  * Focus damage on the Rat King to prevent continued summoning

==== Drops ====
  * **[[en:rpd_giant_rat_skull_item|Giant Rat Skull]]** - Used for the Imp quest
  * **[[ru:rpd_rat_king_crown|Rat King's Crown]]** ([[ru:rpd_rat_king_crown|RU]]) - Special artifact

  * **[[en:rpd_gold_item|Gold]]** - Substantial gold reward
  * **[[en:rpd_food_ration|Food rations]]** or other useful items

==== Quest Connections ====
  * **Imp Quest:** Requires giant rat skulls, which the Rat King drops
  * Part of the early-game progression chain

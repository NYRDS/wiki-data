====== Hero Subclasses Overview ======

==== Description ====
A **Subclass** is a specialized path that a hero can take after reaching a certain level in the game. Each hero class has two possible subclasses that provide unique gameplay mechanics and abilities, allowing players to customize their playstyle.

==== Available Subclasses ====
Each hero class has two possible subclasses:

===== Warrior =====
  * [[en:rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]] - Focuses on weapon mastery and throwing mechanics
  * [[en:rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]] - Converts damage taken to rage, increasing damage output

===== Mage =====
  * [[en:rpd:battlemage_subclass|Battlemage]] - Enhances staff usage with special abilities
  * [[en:rpd:warlock_subclass|Warlock]] - Gains life-draining abilities

===== Rogue =====
  * [[en:rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]] - Masters stealth and critical strikes
  * [[en:rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]] - Excels at mobility and dodging

===== Huntress =====
  * [[en:rpd:sniper_subclass|Sniper]] - Enhances ranged combat capabilities
  * [[en:rpd:warden_subclass|Warden]] - Controls terrain and allies

===== Elf =====
  * [[en:rpd:shaman_subclass|Shaman]] - Specializes in staff combat
  * [[en:rpd:scout_subclass|Scout]] - Focuses on exploration and stealth

===== Necromancer =====
  * [[en:rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]] - Master of undead and dark magic
  * [[en:rpd:witchdoctor_subclass|Witchdoctor]] - Uses nature and healing magic

===== Doctor =====
  * [[en:rpd:plague_doctor_subclass|Plague Doctor]] - Masters disease and healing mechanics
  * [[en:rpd:healer_subclass|Healer]] - Focuses purely on healing capabilities

===== Priest =====
  * [[en:rpd:warden_subclass|Warden]] - Controls environment and allies
  * [[en:rpd:cleric_subclass|Cleric]] - Focuses on support and protection

===== Gnoll =====
  * [[en:rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]] - Focuses on raw damage and fury
  * [[en:rpd:guardian_subclass|Guardian]] - Specializes in defense and protection

==== Unlocking Subclasses ====
  * Subclasses are unlocked by reading a [[en:rpd:tome_of_mastery_item|Tome of Mastery]]
  * Tomes of Mastery can be found during the game, often in special rooms
  * The tome will unlock the subclass for the hero's current class
  * Once unlocked, the subclass is permanently available for that hero class

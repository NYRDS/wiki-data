====== Town Shop Level ======

{{ rpd:images:town_shopkeeper_mob.png|Town Shop }}

**Town Shop** is a special level in Remixed Dungeon where players can purchase various items using gold.

==== Description ====
The tavern is located on the surface and serves as the starting location for the player's adventure. Here, players can buy initial supplies such as food, potions, scrolls, and basic equipment.

==== Items for Sale ====
The Tavern Shop offers a variety of goods, including:
  * [[en:rpd:food_item|Food]]
  * [[en:rpd:potion_item|Potions]]
  * [[en:rpd:scroll_item|Scrolls]]
  * [[en:rpd:weapon_item|Weapons]]
  * [[en:rpd:armor_item|Armor]]
  * Occasionally special items such as [[en:rpd:christmas_turkey_item|Christmas Turkey]]

==== Shopkeeper ====
The shop is run by the [[en:rpd:shopkeeper_npc|Shopkeeper NPC]], who manages transactions and restocks items.

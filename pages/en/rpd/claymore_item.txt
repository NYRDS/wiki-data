====== Claymore ======

{{ rpd:images:claymore_item.png|Claymore }}

The Claymore is a heavy two-handed sword with balanced stats. This massive sword should be able to cut through anything.

==== Stats ====
  * **Tier**: 6 (Heaviest tier)
  * **Damage**: 16-25 (1d10+6 to 1d10+16)
  * **Durability**: 60
  * **Speed**: 1.0x (Normal speed)
  * **Accuracy**: 1.0x (Normal accuracy)
  * **Required Strength**: 20
  * **Weight**: 15

==== Description ====
The Claymore is a large two-handed sword that provides high damage output at the cost of being very heavy. It requires significant strength to wield effectively and its weight can limit other equipment choices.

==== Usage ====
  * As a tier 6 weapon, it's one of the heaviest and most damaging weapons available
  * Requires 20 strength to use without penalty
  * Due to its high weight, it can limit other equipment choices
  * Its high damage output makes it suitable for dealing with tough enemies

==== Availability ====
  * Can be found in treasure rooms
  * Sometimes sold in shops
  * May appear as a reward for certain quests

==== Strategy ====
  * The Claymore is most effective in the hands of characters with high strength
  * Due to its weight and two-handed nature, players must carefully balance other equipment
  * Best used by characters who can reach higher strength thresholds
  * The high damage output is excellent for quickly defeating tough enemies

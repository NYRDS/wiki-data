====== Scroll of Recharging ======

{{ rpd:images:scroll_of_recharging_item.png|Scroll of Recharging }}

The Scroll of Recharging is a scroll item in Remixed Dungeon that restores charges to all wands and other charge-based items in the player's inventory.

==== Effects ====
When read, the Scroll of Recharging completely recharges all wands in the player's inventory to full power. This is particularly useful when running low on charges for important wands.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Type**: Scroll
  * **Rarity**: Uncommon
  * **Effect**: Fully recharges all wands in inventory
  * **Message**: "a surge of energy courses through your pack, recharging your wand/wands" or "a surge of energy courses through your pack, but nothing happens" if no rechargeable items are present

==== Acquisition ====
The Scroll of Recharging can be found in various locations throughout the dungeon:
  * Regular chests
  * [[en:rpd:shops|Shops]]
  * [[en:rpd:treasury|Treasury]] levels
  * As drops from certain enemies

==== Strategy ====
The Scroll of Recharging is valuable in situations where you need to quickly restore charges to multiple wands. It's particularly useful during boss fights or when entering deep levels with low wand charges.

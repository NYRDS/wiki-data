====== Healer Subclass ======

{{ rpd:images:healer_subclass.png|Healer }}

==== Description ====
The Healer is a subclass for the Mage hero class in Remixed Dungeon. Healers focus purely on healing capabilities and support magic, specializing in restorative spells and protective abilities.

==== Abilities ====
  * **Enhanced Healing:** Improved effectiveness of healing spells and items
  * **Regeneration:** May have passive health regeneration or enhanced recovery
  * **Protective Magic:** Enhanced abilities to shield allies from harm
  * **Restorative Items:** Better effects when using healing-related items

==== Specialization ====
  * Focuses on healing and support magic
  * Utilizes restorative spells and protective enchantments
  * Often has abilities to heal both themselves and others

==== Lore ====
The Healer subclass represents the mystical healers of fantasy lore, dedicated to preserving life and protecting others through divine or natural magic. They have mastered the arts of restoration and protection.

==== Acquisition ====
The Healer subclass can be unlocked by reading a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss, Tengu. It's only available to the Mage hero class.

==== Equipment ====
  * Special armor: [[en:rpd:mage_armor_item|Mage Armor]] can be obtained using an [[en:rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor
  * Preferred items: Healing potions, protective rings, restoration-focused wands

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on healing and supporting both yourself and allies
  * Utilize restorative items more effectively
  * Build toward a support role in gameplay
  * Leverage protective magic for survival

====== Mechanical Mechanics ======

**Mechanical** refers to various mechanical devices and mechanisms found throughout the dungeon that serve various functions.

==== Types of Mechanics ====
  * **[[en:rpd:trap_trap|Traps]]**: Hidden dangers that trigger when stepped on or activated
  * **Doors**: Locked, bolted, or otherwise sealed entryways
  * **[[en:rpd:switch_npc|Switches]]**: Interactive elements that control other mechanical systems
  * **Machines**: More complex devices like the [[en:rpd:dm300_mob|DM-300]]

==== Related Entities ====
  * [[en:rpd:trap_trap|Traps]] - Various mechanical hazards
  * [[en:rpd:dm300_mob|DM-300]] - A major mechanical enemy
  * [[en:rpd:locks_and_keys_mechanic|Locks and Keys]] - Mechanical locking systems
  * [[en:rpd:statue_level_object|Statues]] - Often mechanical in nature
  * [[en:rpd:level_objects_mechanic|Level Objects]] - Various mechanical elements in levels

==== Interaction ====
  * Some mechanics can be disarmed or bypassed with the right skills
  * Others might require specific items or spells to activate or deactivate
  * Mechanical systems can sometimes be beneficial or harmful to the player

====== Forbidden Actions Mechanic ======

Game mechanics allow certain hero classes to have forbidden actions. Some classes cannot perform specific actions in the game.

==== List of Possible Actions to Forbid ====
  * ''Scroll_ACRead'' - Reading scrolls
  * ''Bag_ACOpen'' - Opening bags
  * ''DewVial_ACDRINK'' - Drinking from dew vial
  * ''Drink_ACDrink'' - Drinking potions
  * ''EquipableItem_ACEquip'' - Equipping items
  * ''EquipableItem_ACUnequip'' - Unequipping items
  * ''Item_ACDrop'' - Dropping items
  * ''Item_ACThrow'' - Throwing items
  * ''TomeOfMastery_ACRead'' - Reading tome of mastery
  * ''Torch_ACLight'' - Lighting torches
  * ''Weightstone_ACApply'' - Applying weightstones
  * ''Amulet_ACEnd'' - Ending game with Amulet of Yendor
  * ''ArmorKit_ACAplly'' - Applying armor kit
  * ''Pickaxe_ACMine'' - Mining with pickaxe
  * ''Plant_ACPlant'' - Planting seeds
  * ''Potion_ACMoisten'' - Moisten items with potions
  * ''ShortSword_ACReforge'' - Reforging with shortsword
  * ''Wand_ACZap'' - Zapping wands
  * ''WandOfMagicMissile_ACDisenchant'' - Disenchanting with wand of magic missile

==== Usage ====
These actions can be restricted in the ''InitHeroes.json'' configuration file for specific hero classes.

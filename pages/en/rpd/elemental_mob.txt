====== Elementals ======

**Elementals** are magical creatures representing the fundamental elements of nature: fire, water, air, and earth. Each type has unique properties and abilities related to their element. For more detailed information on each elemental type, see the comprehensive [[en:rpd:elementals_mob|Elementals]] page.

==== Types of Elementals ====
  * [[en:rpd:fire_elemental_mob|Fire Elemental]] - Creatures of living flame
  * [[en:rpd:ice_elemental_mob|Ice Elemental]] - Cold-based creatures
  * [[en:rpd:water_elemental_mob|Water Elemental]] - Fluid-based creatures
  * [[en:rpd:air_elemental_mob|Air Elemental]] - Flying creatures associated with wind
  * [[en:rpd:earth_elemental_mob|Earth Elemental]] - Sturdy, stone-based creatures

==== Common Characteristics ====
  * **Elemental Resistance**: Elementals are generally resistant to their own element
  * **Elemental Weakness**: Elementals are often weak to opposing elements
  * **Drops**: May drop special items based on their element (e.g. potions of liquid flame for Fire Elementals)
  * **Magical Nature**: All elementals are magical beings immune to certain status effects

==== Strategy ====
  * Use appropriate elements to exploit weaknesses (e.g., frost vs fire elementals)
  * Be prepared for high damage output and special abilities
  * Consider using resistances when engaging
  * Note that some elementals are immune to certain status effects

For complete stats, drops, and technical details for each elemental type, see the comprehensive [[en:rpd:elementals_mob|Elementals]] page.

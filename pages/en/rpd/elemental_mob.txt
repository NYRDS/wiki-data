====== Elemental Mob ======

**Elemental Mobs** are a category of enemies in Remixed Dungeon based on the classical elements.

==== Types of Elementals ====
  * [[en:rpd:fire_elemental_mob|Fire Elemental]]
  * [[en:rpd:ice_elemental_mob|Ice Elemental]]
  * [[en:rpd:air_elemental_mob|Air Elemental]]
  * [[en:rpd:water_elemental_mob|Water Elemental]]

==== Common Characteristics ====
  * **Resistance**: Elementals are generally resistant to their own element
  * **Weakness**: Elementals are often weak to opposing elements
  * **Drops**: May drop special items based on their element (e.g. fire seeds for Fire Elementals)

==== Strategy ====
  * Use appropriate elements to exploit weaknesses
  * Be prepared for high damage output
  * Consider using resistances when engaging

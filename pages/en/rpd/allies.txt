====== Allies ======

**Allies** in Remixed Dungeon refer to friendly characters or entities that assist the player character during their journey through the dungeon.

==== Types of Allies ====

==== Summoned Allies ====
  * **Summoned creatures** - Temporary allies created by spells or items
  * **Pet companions** - Creatures summoned using specific items or abilities

==== NPC Allies ====
  * **Faction NPCs** - Non-player characters that may become allies under certain conditions

==== Special Allies ====
  * **Player-summoned entities** - Creatures created by specific game mechanics

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Combat support** - Allies can engage enemies to assist the player
  * **Protection** - Some allies can protect the player from harm
  * **Tactical advantages** - Allies can create tactical opportunities

==== Spells and Items Related to Allies ====
  * **Heal spell** - Can heal allies in addition to the player (as mentioned in [[en:rpd:healing_spell|Healing Spell]])
  * **Summon spells** - Various spells that create temporary allies
  * **Pet summoning items** - Items that allow players to summon companions
  * [[en:rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King's Crown]] - An item that creates allies from rats when attacking them

==== Strategy ====
  * Use allies to draw enemy aggro away from the player
  * Protect valuable summoned allies during combat
  * Coordinate attacks with allies for maximum effectiveness
  * Manage ally positioning to prevent blocking movement
  * Consider the limited duration of summoned allies in planning

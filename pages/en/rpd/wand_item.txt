====== Wand ======

{{ rpd:images:item_WandOfMagicMissile.png|Wand }}

**Wands** are magic items in Remixed Dungeon that store charges to perform various magical effects.

==== Description ====
Wands are magical items that store a limited number of charges and use them to perform powerful magical effects. Each wand type has a unique effect and different recharge rate.

==== Properties ====
  * **Charges:** Number of magical charges available for use
  * **Charges per Rank:** How many charges are restored per turn at each level
  * **Max Charges:** Maximum number of charges the wand can hold
  * **Cursed:** If cursed, the wand may activate randomly or have negative effects

==== Wand Types ====
  * **[[en:rpd:wand_of_magic_missile_item|Wand of Magic Missile]]** - Basic damage wand
  * **[[en:rpd:wand_of_lightning_item|Wand of Lightning]]** - Electric damage in an arc
  * **[[en:rpd:wand_of_firebolt_item|Wand of Firebolt]]** - Fire damage to an area
  * **[[en:rpd:wand_of_frost_item|Wand of Cold Ray]]** - Cold damage with slowing effect
  * **[[en:rpd:wand_of_poison_item|Wand of Poison]]** - Creates poison gas clouds
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_disintegration_item|Wand of Disintegration]] - High damage through walls
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_bleeding_item|Wand of Bleeding]] - Causes bleeding damage over time
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_prismatic_light_item|Wand of Prismatic Light]] - Light damage to undead and demons
  * **[[en:rpd:wand_of_regrowth_item|Wand of Regrowth]]** - Creates grass and heals plants
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_transfusion_item|Wand of Transfusion]] - Heals allies and harms enemies
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_corrosion_item|Wand of Corrosion]] - Creates damaging gas clouds
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_amok_item|Wand of Amok]] - Causes enemies to fight each other
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_slowness_item|Wand of Slowness]] - Slows enemies down
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_flock_item|Wand of Flock]] - Summons decoy sheep
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_shadowbolt_item|Wand of Shadowbolt]] - Dark damage
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_teleportation_item|Wand of Teleportation]] - Teleports enemies
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_blink_item|Wand of Blink]] - Teleports the hero
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_avalanche_item|Wand of Avalanche]] - Creates rockslide damage
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_telekinesis_item|Wand of Telekinesis]] - Moves objects and enemies
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_holster_item|Wand of Holster]] - Stores multiple wands

==== Usage ====
  * **Charging:** Wands recharge over time while carried
  * **Targeting:** Most wands require targeting a location or enemy
  * **Recharge Speed:** Varies by wand type and upgrade level
  * **Cursed Wands:** May backfire or activate randomly when cursed

==== Upgrades ====
  * Wands can be upgraded using [[en:rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * Each upgrade increases maximum charges and recharge rate
  * Higher-level wands have more charges available

==== Strategy ====
  * **Charge Management:** Use charges wisely; they recharge slowly
  * **Situational Use:** Different wands excel in different situations
  * **Upgrade Priority:** Consider upgrading powerful wands over weak ones
  * **Cursed Wands:** Identify and remove curses quickly, or embrace the chaos
  * **Multiple Wands:** Consider carrying multiple wands for varied effects

==== Identification ====
  * Uncursed wands are [[en:rpd:identification|unidentified]] initially
  * [[en:rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scrolls of Identify]] reveal wand type permanently
  * [[en:rpd:potion_of_mind_vision_item|Potions of Mind Vision]] can temporarily identify wands

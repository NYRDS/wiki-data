====== Spiders Treasury ======

{{ rpd:images:chest_1_level_object.png|Treasure Chest}}

The **Spiders Treasury** is a special level in Remixed Dungeon themed around spiders and their treasures. It contains valuable spider-related items and resources that can be found throughout the dungeon.

==== Description ====
The Spiders Treasury is a spider-themed treasure level that offers unique loot and challenges. This level features web-covered environments and spider-related mechanics.

==== Features ====
  * Spider-themed environment and decorations
  * Contains valuable treasures, especially [[en:rpd:compound_bow_item|Compound Bow]] with a drop rate of 3 units
  * Often has web-related mechanics
  * May contain unique spider-related items
  * Web-covered areas that may affect movement
  * Spider nest-like structures

==== Layout ====
The Spiders Treasury features a design incorporating spider elements:
  * Web-covered areas
  * Spider nest-like structures
  * Multiple chambers with different types of spider-related loot
  * Possible encounters with spider mobs

==== Obtaining ====
  * Special treasure level accessed through dungeon progression
  * Contains spider-themed loot tables

====== Summoning Spells ======

{{ rpd:images:summon_deathling_spell.png|Summoning Spells }}

**Summoning Spells** are a category of spells that allow the player to summon creatures to aid in battle. These spells are primarily associated with Necromancy affinity.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Summon Limit:** Each spell has a maximum number of creatures that can be summoned at once
  * **Duration:** Summoned creatures last for a limited time before disappearing
  * **Control:** Summoned creatures fight for the player and follow basic AI patterns
  * **Statistics:** The health and damage of summoned creatures scale with the hero's level

==== Known Summoning Spells ====
  * [[en:rpd:summon_deathling_spell|Summon Deathling]] - Summons a deathling ally for combat assistance

====== Paralysis Arrow ======

{{ rpd:images:paralysis_arrow_item.png|Paralysis Arrow }}

**Paralysis Arrow** is a throwable missile weapon in Remixed Dungeon that stuns the target.

==== Stats ====
  * **[[en:rpd:weight_system|Weight]]**: 1
  * **[[en:rpd:items|Type]]**: [[en:rpd:arrow_item|Arrow]]
  * **[[en:rpd:damage|Damage]]**: 0-4
  * **[[en:rpd:durability|Durability]]**: 1 use

==== Effects ====
  * **[[en:rpd:stun_buff|Stun]]**: Causes [[en:rpd:stun_buff|stun status effect]] for 4 turns, preventing target actions
  * **[[en:rpd:throwing_weapons|Throwable]]**: Can be thrown at distant enemies
  * **[[en:rpd:one_time_use|Single Use]]**: Consumed after use
  * **[[en:rpd:missile_weapons|Missile]]**: Ranged weapon with standard missile mechanics

==== Description ====
Paralysis Arrows are special ammunition that, when used, will cause the target to become stunned temporarily. This makes them effective for controlling enemies and gaining tactical advantages in combat situations.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Created by using [[en:rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] on regular [[en:rpd:arrow_item|Arrows]] via alchemy
  * May be found occasionally in [[en:rpd:shops|shops]] or [[en:rpd:level_objects|level objects]] such as [[en:rpd:containers|crates]] or [[en:rpd:heap|heaps]]
  * Part of alchemical crafting system

==== Usage ====
  * Throw at enemies to apply [[en:rpd:stun_buff|stun effect]]
  * Effective for [[en:rpd:combat|combat control]] and creating breathing room
  * Particularly valuable against dangerous enemies or groups
  * Can be used as a tactical escape mechanism

==== Mechanics ====
  * Created through the [[en:rpd:moistening|moistening system]] by combining [[en:rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] with regular [[en:rpd:arrow_item|Arrows]]
  * Applies [[en:rpd:stun_buff|stun]] status effect for 4 turns upon hit (DURATION = 4f)
  * Follows standard [[en:rpd:throwing_weapons|throwing weapon]] mechanics
  * Quantity determined by the amount of potion used and arrow quality

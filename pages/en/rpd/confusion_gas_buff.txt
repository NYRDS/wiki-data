====== Confusion Gas ======

**Confusion Gas** is a [[en:rpd:gas|gas]] that can be created by [[en:rpd:rotten_food_item|rotten food]] when consumed or thrown.

==== Effects ====
  * Inflicts the [[en:rpd:confusion_buff|Confusion]] effect on all creatures in the area of effect
  * Causes confusion for 150+10*dungeon_depth turns
  * Duration depends on the current dungeon depth

==== Acquisition ====
  * With a 25% probability, it can form when consuming or throwing [[en:rpd:rotten_food_item|rotten food]]

==== Mechanics ====
  * Gas forms when rotten food spoils
  * Inflicts confusion effect on all creatures in the radius of effect
  * Duration depends on the current dungeon depth

==== Strategy ====
  * Avoid consuming rotten food to prevent creating gas
  * When throwing rotten food, consider the possibility of gas formation
  * Use gas consciously to affect enemies

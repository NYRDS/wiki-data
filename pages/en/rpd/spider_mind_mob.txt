====== Spider Mind (Mob) ======

{{ rpd:images:spider_mind_mob.png|Spider Mind Sprite }}

==== Description ====
Spider Minds are special spiders that can assist other friendly creatures by buffing them with beneficial status effects. They do not deal physical damage and instead use ranged psychic attacks.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 5
  * **Attack Skill:** 10
  * **Defense Skill:** 1
  * **Damage:** 0 (no physical damage)
  * **Speed:** 1Ã— normal speed
  * **EXP:** 6
  * **Max Level:** 9
  * **Defense:** 0

==== Abilities & Mechanics ====
  * Cannot deal physical damage (dmgMin and dmgMax are 0)
  * Can only perform ranged attacks
  * When attacking (zapping), has a chance to buff nearby friendly creatures with one of: Speed, Barkskin, Blessed, Sungrass Health, Earthroot Armor, Mana Shield, or Fury
  * Moves away from enemies when in hunting state (runs away rather than approaching)

==== Drops ====
  * Mystery Meat (6.7% chance)

==== Strategy ====
  * Since these spiders don't deal damage, they're more of a support threat
  * They'll try to maintain distance from you and buff other enemies
  * Prioritize eliminating them to prevent them from enhancing other foes

====== Ice Caves ======

{{ rpd:images:ice_caves_level.png|Ice Caves Level }}

**Ice Caves** is a special dungeon level in Remixed Dungeon that features cold-themed enemies and mechanics.

==== Description ====
The Ice Caves level is an underground area filled with ice-themed challenges and enemies. This level features unique mechanics related to freezing effects and cold-based creatures.

==== Features ====
  * **Environment**: An icy underground cave system with freezing effects
  * **Enemies**: Cold-themed creatures like ice elementals and frozen spirits
  * **Hazards**: Ice traps, freezing areas, and slippery terrain
  * **Special Mechanics**: Temperature-based effects and ice-related mechanics

==== Items ====
  * [[en:rpd:ice_key_item|Ice Keys]] for unlocking frozen areas
  * Cold-resistant equipment and gear

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Freezing Effects**: Areas that may freeze characters
  * **Slippery Terrain**: Movement may be affected by ice
  * **Cold Resistance**: Some equipment provides protection against cold

====== wandmaker ======

{{ rpd:images:wandmaker_sprite.png|Wandmaker Sprite }}

The **Wandmaker** is an NPC that appears in the [[en:rpd:prison|Prison Level]]. He is a mysterious old man seeking rare ingredients and offers high-quality wands in return for his help.

==== Encounter ====
The Wandmaker appears randomly on floors deeper than level 6 of the dungeon. He has a 1 in (10 - dungeon depth) chance to spawn in the prison level and offers one of two random quests:
  * **Rotberry quest**: He asks for a [[en:rpd:rotberry/seed|Rotberry Seed]]
  * **Corpse Dust quest**: He asks for [[en:rpd:corpse_dust_item|Corpse Dust]]

==== Mechanics ====
The Wandmaker is protected by a magic shield which makes him completely invulnerable to damage. He cannot be moved or killed until his quest is complete.

==== Rewards ====
Upon completion of his quest, the Wandmaker offers two types of wands to choose from:

**Battle Wands**:
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_avalanche|Wand of Avalanche]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_disintegration|Wand of Disintegration]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_firebolt_item|Wand of Firebolt]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_lightning|Wand of Lightning]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_poison_item|Wand of Poison]]

**Utility Wands**:
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_amok|Wand of Amok]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_blink|Wand of Blink]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_regrowth_item|Wand of Regrowth]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_slowness_item|Wand of Slowness]]
  * [[en:rpd:wand_of_telekinesis|Wand of Telekinesis]]

Each reward wand is randomly upgraded before being given to the player.

==== Location ====
[[en:rpd:prison|Prison Level]] (level 6-9)

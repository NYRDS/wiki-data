====== Tactics ======

{{ rpd:images:short_sword_item.png|Generic Tactics }}

**Tactics** refers to the strategic approaches and methods used in combat and exploration in Remixed Dungeon. Players employ various tactics to maximize their efficiency and survivability throughout the dungeon.

==== Combat Tactics ====
  * **Kiting:** Moving away after attacks to prevent retaliation
  * **Corner fighting:** Using level geometry to prevent multiple enemies
  * **Disengagement:** Moving away when health is low
  * **Corridor fighting:** Using narrow corridors to fight one enemy at a time
  * **Pre-identification:** Identifying items before critical use
  * **Resource management:** Efficient use of consumables like potions, scrolls, and food

==== Exploration Tactics ====
  * **Safe pattern:** Opening doors carefully and listening for sounds
  * **Light management:** Using torches and light sources effectively
  * **Potion management:** Preserving important potions for boss fights
  * **Trap awareness:** Learning to identify and avoid or trigger traps safely

==== Hero-Specific Tactics ====
Different hero classes require different tactical approaches:
  * **Warrior:** Focus on durability and direct combat
  * **Mage:** Ranged combat and magic usage
  * **Rogue:** Stealth and tactical positioning
  * **Huntress:** Ranged weapons and distance combat
  * **Elf:** Balanced use of ranged and melee
  * **Necromancer:** Managing minions and undead tactics
  * **Gnoll:** Unique mechanics and mob-friendly tactics
  * **Priest:** Support and utility-based tactics
  * **Doctor:** Medical-themed survival tactics

==== Weapon-Specific Tactics ====
  * **Close Combat:** Tactics for melee weapons
  * **Ranged Combat:** Tactics for bows, wands, and other distance weapons
  * **Throwing Weapons:** Efficient use of ammunition and throwable items

==== Level-Specific Tactics ====
Each dungeon level requires different tactical approaches:
  * **Sewers:** Managing weak enemies and conserving resources
  * **Prison:** Dealing with more dangerous enemies and special mechanics
  * **Caves:** Preparing for high HP enemies and ranged attacks
  * **City:** Strategic preparation for challenging encounters
  * **Halls:** Extreme caution tactics for difficult content

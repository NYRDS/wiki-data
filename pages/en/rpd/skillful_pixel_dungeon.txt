====== Skillful Pixel Dungeon ======

A fork of the original Pixel Dungeon, with the most significant change being the addition of active and passive skills.

Features:
  * Active and passive skills, different for each class
  * Zombie rats - now rats can also cause trouble
  * Death phrases - now not only bosses can speak
  * New items
  * Champion enemies - any monster can have a special aura, increased stats, and a special ability
  * Ability to choose game difficulty, significantly changing the power of monsters and the player
  * A vault located near the stairs of each floor, allowing you to store 5 unnecessary items

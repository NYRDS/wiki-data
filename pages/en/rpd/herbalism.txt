====== Herbalism ======

{{ rpd:images:ring_of_herbalism_item.png|Ring of Herbalism }}

**Herbalism** in Remixed Dungeon refers to the game mechanic associated with the [[en:rpd:ring_of_herbalism_item|Ring of Herbalism]], which increases the chance to collect dew drops and seeds from trampled grass.

==== Ring of Herbalism ====
The Ring of Herbalism is a special ring that enhances the player's ability to gather resources from the dungeon environment.

  * **Type:** Ring
  * **Rarity:** Uncommon
  * **Effect:** Increases chance to collect dew and seeds from trampled grass

==== Mechanics ====
  * When wearing the Ring of Herbalism, stepping on grass has an increased chance to drop:
    * [[en:rpd:dewdrop_item|Dewdrops]] - Used for healing and upgrading the Dew Vial
    * Various [[en:rpd:seeds|Seeds]] - Used for alchemy and crafting
  * The ring's effect scales with its upgrade level
  * Higher upgrade levels provide better resource gathering chances

==== Strategy ====
  * The Ring of Herbalism is particularly useful for long runs where resource gathering becomes important
  * Combining this ring with careful grass-trampling paths can yield significant resources over time
  * Most valuable when trying to collect specific seeds for alchemy recipes

==== Obtaining ====
  * Can be found in dungeon containers and chests
  * May be purchased from shops
  * Can be dropped by certain enemies

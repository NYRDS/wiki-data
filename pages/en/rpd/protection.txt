====== Protection ======

==== Description ====
**Protection** in Remixed Dungeon is a general mechanic related to reducing or preventing damage taken. It includes both permanent and temporary methods of defense.

==== Types of Protection ====
  * [[en:rpd_armor_mechanic|Armor]] - reduces physical damage
  * [[en:rpd_shields|Shields]] - reduce damage and can provide additional effects
  * [[en:rpd_buffs|Buffs]] - temporary enhancements that reduce damage
  * [[en:rpd_potions|Potions]] - one-time protection
  * [[en:rpd_spell|Spells]] - provide protective effects

==== Mechanics ====
  * Different types of protection can stack with each other
  * Some damage types may be immune to certain kinds of protection
  * Protection can be enhanced through [[en:rpd_enchantments|enchantments]] and [[en:rpd_rings|rings]]

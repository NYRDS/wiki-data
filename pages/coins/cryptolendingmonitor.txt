Simple lending rates monitor and approximate annual interest calculator for [[https://poloniex.com/signup?c=NKHAR2ZE|Poloniex]] cryptocurrency exchange.

App monitor lending rates:
  * on Poloniex for BTC, BTS, CLAM, DOGE, DASH, LTC, MAID, STR, XMR, XRP,  ETH, FCT tickers

Check all minimum and median loan rates for all coins at one glance.

Easily monitor your balances and active loans (API key required)

You can download it from [[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.nyrds.cryptoassistant&utm_source=wiki.nyrds.net|Google Play]].

Bug-reports & feature-request are welcome.

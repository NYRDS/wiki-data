===== Armor Properties (from Armor.java) =====
  * **HP/HT**: Base health/health total
  * **Defense Rating (DR)**: Damage reduction value
  * **STR Requirement**: Minimum strength to equip without penalty
  * **Degradation**: Armor degrades on hit, can be repaired
  * **Upgradeable**: Can be upgraded with Scrolls of Upgrade

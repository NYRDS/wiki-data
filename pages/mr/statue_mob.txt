====== mr:statue_mob ======

=== Entity Information ===
  * **Class Name**: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/actors/mobs/Statue.java|Statue.java]]
  * **Entity Kind**: Statue
  * **Type**: Mob
  * **Category**: Common

=== Statistics ===
  * **HP**: 15 + (5 × dungeon depth)
  * **HT**: 15 + (5 × dungeon depth)
  * **Defense Skill**: 4 + dungeon depth
  * **Attack Skill**: 4 + dungeon depth
  * **Damage Min**: dungeon depth / 4 + 1
  * **Damage Max**: dungeon depth
  * **EXP for Kill**: 0
  * **Carcass Chance**: 0

=== Special Abilities ===
  * **Passive Nature**: The statue is normally passive and will not attack first
  * **Aggro Trigger**: Becomes hostile when attacked, when a Scroll of Rage is used nearby, or when other hostile actions are taken
  * **Immunities**: Immune to Toxic Gas, Poison, Bleeding, Leech and Death status effects
  * **Resistances**: Resistant to Death enchantment and Scroll of Psionic Blast
  * **Equipment**: Carries a random weapon from the current dungeon level's treasure pool
  * **Weapon Drop**: Guarantees dropping its weapon upon death (100% drop rate)
  * **Dynamic Sprite**: Generated dynamically based on the weapon it carries
  * **No Beckoning**: Does not follow the player around the dungeon (overrides beckon method)

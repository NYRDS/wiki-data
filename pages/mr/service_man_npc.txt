===== Key Methods =====
  * **interact(Char hero)**: Handles player interaction, checks internet connection and viewing limits
  * **getReward()**: Returns gold reward item (base 150 gold + bonus based on films seen)
  * **getLimit()**: Calculates viewing limit (4 + hero level)
  * **resetLimit()**: Resets the filmsSeen counter

===== Fields =====
  * **BASIC_GOLD_REWARD**: Constant = 150 (base gold reward)
  * **filmsSeen**: Static tracked variable for total films watched by player

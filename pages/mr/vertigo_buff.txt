=== Entity Information ===
  * **Class Name**: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/actors/buffs/Vertigo.java|Vertigo.java]]
  * **Entity Kind**: Vertigo
  * **Type**: Buff

=== Statistics ===
  * **Default Duration**: 10 turns
  * **Icon ID**: BuffIndicator.VERTIGO
  * **Modified Duration**: Affected by RingOfElements.Resistance (r.durationFactor() * DURATION)

=== Special Abilities ===
  * **Movement Disorientation**: Affected characters cannot control their movement, moving in random directions instead of intended path
  * **Action Retention**: Characters can still act (attack, use items) while affected
  * **Special Movement**: Characters ignore vertigo for movement when using blink or teleport effects (adjacent(getPos(), step) check)
  * **Status Message**: Shows "У тебя кружится голова!" when attached to hero
  * **Visual Status**: Shows CharSprite.NEGATIVE status when attached

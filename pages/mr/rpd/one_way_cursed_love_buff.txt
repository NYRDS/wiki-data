==== Core Properties ====
  * **Buff Type**: Negative/Debuff (cursed variant)
  * **Icon**: `BuffIndicator.CURSED_ROSE`
  * **Source**: Cursed Dried Rose artifact
  * **Duration**: Permanent while cursed Dried Rose is equipped
  * **Removable**: Only by removing curse from Dried Rose or unequipping

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Effect**: One-way charm - causes NPCs to interact differently with player
  * **Charm Resistance**: In `Charm.java` resistance calculation:
    * If target has `OneWayLoveBuff`: charm power = 0 (immune to charm)
    * If target has `OneWayCursedLoveBuff`: charm power = 2 (enhanced charm effect)
  * **Quest Related**: Part of Ghost NPC questline
  * **Artifact Buff**: Extends `ArtifactBuff` base class

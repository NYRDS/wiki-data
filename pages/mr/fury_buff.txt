  * **Type**: Buff

=== Statistics ===
  * **Level Threshold**: 0.4f (40% of max HP)
  * **Icon ID**: BuffIndicator.FURY

=== Special Abilities ===
  * **Double Damage**: Doubles all damage dealt by the affected character (attackProc returns damage * 2)
  * **Auto-Removal**: Buff automatically detaches when character's HP rises above 40% of max HP
  * **Visual Status**: Shows "Ты впал в ярость!" status message when attached to player
  * **Visual Effect**: Shows CharSprite.NEGATIVE status when attached
  * **Warning Message**: Shows "%s впал в неистовство!" message when buff is attached if character is visible

===== Entity Kind =====
DM300

===== Stats =====
  * **Health**: 200 (HT: 200)
  * **EXP for Kill**: 30
  * **Defense Skill**: 18
  * **Attack Skill**: 28
  * **Damage**: 18-24
  * **Armor**: 10 DR (Defense Rating)
  * **Level**: Boss

===== Drops =====
  * [[rpd:chaos_crystal_item|Chaos Crystal]] (33.3% chance) or [[rpd:ring_of_thorns_item|Ring of Thorns]] (randomly enchanted, 33.3% chance)
  * [[rpd:skeleton_key_item|Skeleton Key]]

===== Immunities =====
  * [[rpd:toxic_gas_buff|Toxic Gas]]
  * [[rpd:bleeding_buff|Bleeding]]
  * [[rpd:terror_buff|Terror]]

===== Special Abilities =====
  * Releases toxic gas periodically (30 units of gas every turn)
  * Repairs itself when stepping on traps if HP < HT (heals 1-50% of max HP)
  * Stuns adjacent characters when moving (2 turns)
  * Creates rock debris when moving, with camera shake and sound effects

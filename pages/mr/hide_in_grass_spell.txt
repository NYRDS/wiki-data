===== Usage in Code =====
  * Spell is associated with Elf class and Elf affinity
  * Requires GRASS or HIGH_GRASS terrain to cast
  * Applies Cloak buff for duration based on caster's skill level
  * Uses "hide_in_grass" visual effect
  * Failure message when not on grass terrain: "Even I can't hide in plain sight."

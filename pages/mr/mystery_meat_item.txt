===== Implementation Details =====
  * **Class**: com.watabou.pixeldungeon.items.food.MysteryMeat
  * **Extends**: Food
  * **Sprite**: ItemSpriteSheet.MEAT
  * **Energy**: Hunger.STARVING - Hunger.HUNGRY (restores full hunger)
  * **Price**: 5 gold per piece
  * **Random Effects** (when eaten, 1 in 5 chance each):
    * Case 0: Applies Burning buff (fire damage over time)
    * Case 1: Applies Roots buff (immobilization)
    * Case 2: Applies Poison buff (poison damage over time)
    * Case 3: Applies Slow buff (reduced movement speed)
    * Case 4: No negative effect (stuffed)
  * **Transformations**:

    * freeze() -> morphTo(FrozenCarpaccio.class)
    * poison() -> morphTo(RottenMeat.class)

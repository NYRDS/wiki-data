===== Entity Properties =====
  * **Type**: Missile weapon (dart)
  * **Base Damage**: MIN=1, MAX=3
  * **Special Effect**: Applies Paralysis buff for 3 seconds on hit
  * **STR Requirement**: 14
  * **Random Quantity**: 2-4 when generated randomly (Random.Int(2, 5))
  * **Price**: 12 gold per dart
  * **Image ID**: 4 (from missiles atlas)
  * **Parent Class**: Dart
  * **Duration**: DURATION = 3f (3 seconds paralysis)

====== Yogs Heart Mob - Code References ======

{{ rpd:images:yogs_heart_mob.png|Yog's Heart }}

**Yog's Heart** is a boss mob in Remixed Dungeon, part of the Yog-Dzewa boss encounter. It is one of the organs of the Old God that must be defeated.

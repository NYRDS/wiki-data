===== Implementation Details =====
  * **Inheritance**: Extends [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/actors/mobs/Monk.java|Monk]] class
  * **Damage Range**: 12-20 damage (dmgMin = 12, dmgMax = 20)
  * **Special Ability**: 10% chance to apply [[en:rpd:stun_buff|Stun]] buff for 1.1 seconds on attack
  * **Animation**: 30% chance to play kick animation instead of normal attack
  * **Sprite Class**: Uses "spritesDesc/Senior.json" for sprite configuration

===== Usage in Code =====
  * Used in Badges.java to recognize Senior mob kills for rare mob badge
  * Registered in MobFactory.java for mob instantiation
  * Found in temple areas alongside other monk-type enemies

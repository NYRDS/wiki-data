===== Item Mechanics =====
  * Special mastery item for Necromancer class that unlocks Lich subclass
  * Provides a choice between becoming Lich or breaking the spell to get regular Black Skull item
  * Time to use: 10 turns (TIME_TO_READ constant)
  * Image index: 19 in items/artifacts.png sprite sheet

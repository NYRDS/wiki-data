====== Potion of Healing (Item) ======

==== Basic Properties ====
  * **Item Type**: Potion
  * **Class**: PotionOfHealing
  * **Rarity**: Common
  * **Price**: 30 gold (when identified)

==== Effect ====
  * **Healing**: Restores 100% of max HP to the drinker (full health restoration)
  * **Status Curing**: Removes Poison, Cripple, Weakness, and Bleeding status effects
  * **Shattering**: When shattered on the ground, heals 50% of max HP to any character on that tile
  * **Arrow Moistening**: Can be used to create Health Arrows

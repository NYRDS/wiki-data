====== Chaos Shield ======

==== Description ====
The Chaos Shield is the result of fusing a Chaos Crystal with ordinary shield, causing it to manifest random chaotic effects upon a successful block.

==== Stats ====
  * **Defense:** Varies based on upgrades
  * **Durability:** Normal
  * **Strength Required:** Moderate

==== Special Properties ====
  * Manifests random chaotic effects when successfully blocking attacks
  * Can be created by fusing a Chaos Crystal with a regular shield

==== Notes ====
Chaos Shield provides protection while adding unpredictable chaotic effects when blocking enemy attacks. The randomness of these effects can be both helpful and dangerous.

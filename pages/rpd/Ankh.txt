====== Ankh ======

==== Description ====
This ancient symbol of immortality grants an ability to return to life after death. Upon resurrection all non-equipped items are lost.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Provides one-time resurrection after death
  * Upon resurrection, all non-equipped items are lost
  * Equipped items are preserved during resurrection
  * Single use item

==== Notes ====
The Ankh is a powerful but risky item - it can save you from death, but at the cost of losing all your inventory items that weren't equipped. It's generally best to use it only in desperate situations.

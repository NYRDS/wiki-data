====== Encounters ======

**Encounters** refer to interactions between the player character and various elements in the dungeon, including monsters, traps, items, and other game features.

==== Types of Encounters ====

==== Hostile Encounters ====
  * **Mobs** - Enemies that actively engage the player in combat
  * **Traps** - Hidden mechanisms that activate when stepped on or triggered
  * **Hostile NPCs** - Non-player characters that attack the player

==== Neutral Encounters ====
  * **Passive Mobs** - Enemies that don't actively seek combat
  * **Utility Mobs** - Mobs that can be interacted with in special ways

==== Friendly Encounters ====
  * **NPCs** - Non-player characters that offer services or information
  * **Ally Mobs** - Creatures that assist the player

==== Environmental Encounters ====
  * **Level Objects** - Interactive features like fountains and altars
  * **Terrain Features** - Elements like water, grass, or lava that affect gameplay
  * **Stairs** - Access points between dungeon levels

==== Strategy ====
  * Approach unknown encounters with caution
  * Prepare appropriate items before entering new areas
  * Understand different encounter types to make tactical decisions
  * Be ready for surprises in the dungeon

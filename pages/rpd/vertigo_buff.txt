====== Vertigo ======

==== Description ====
**Vertigo** is a status effect in Remixed Dungeon that affects a character's ability to navigate the dungeon. It causes disorientation and difficulty in movement.

==== Stats ====
* **Duration**: 10 turns by default (can be modified by Resistance buffs)
* **Stacking**: Does not stack with similar effects
* **Removal**: Can be cured through various means (Ring of Elements resistance, etc.)

==== Effects ====
* **Visual Indicator**: Shows "Dizzy" status message when applied
* **Movement disruption**: Characters affected by vertigo may have trouble navigating properly
* **Direction confusion**: May cause character to move in unintended directions
* **Combat impairment**: May affect accuracy and defensive capabilities

==== Causes ====
* Certain enemy abilities
* Specific traps or environmental effects
* Magical items or spells
* Special level mechanics

==== Immunities ====
* Resistance from [[rpd:ring_of_elements_item|Ring of Elements]] may reduce duration

==== Strategy ====
* **For Players:** Avoid situations where vertigo could be dangerous
* **Defensively:** Be aware of vertigo-inducing enemies and mechanics
* **Offensively:** Use vertigo on enemies when tactical advantage is gained
* **Countermeasures:** Have means to cure vertigo if it becomes problematic

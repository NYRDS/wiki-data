====== Vertigo ======

Vertigo is a status effect in Remixed Dungeon that disorients the affected character, causing them to move randomly instead of following their intended path.

==== Stats ====
  * **Default Duration**: 10 turns (DURATION constant)
  * **Icon ID**: BuffIndicator.VERTIGO
  * **Modified Duration**: Affected by RingOfElements.Resistance (r.durationFactor() * DURATION)

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Effect:** Affected characters cannot control their movement
  * **Movement:** Characters move in random directions instead of intended path
  * **Action:** Characters can still act (attack, use items) while affected
  * **Special:** Characters ignore vertigo for movement when using blink or teleport effects (adjacent(getPos(), step) check)
  * **Message:** "У тебя кружится голова!" when hero is affected (Hero_StaVertigo)

==== Sources of Vertigo ====
  * [[rpd:kusarigama_item|Kusarigama]] - Inflicts 3 turns of vertigo on hit (Buff.prolong(tgt, Vertigo.class, 3))
  * [[rpd:wand_of_amok_item|Wand of Amok]] - Applies vertigo to victim (Buff.affect(victim, Vertigo.class, Vertigo.duration(victim)))
  * [[rpd:dreamweed_seed_item|Dreamweed]] - Plant effect causes vertigo for 20 turns (2× default) (Buff.affect(chr, Vertigo.class, Vertigo.DURATION * 2))
  * [[rpd:fadeleaf_seed_item|Fadeleaf]] - Plant effect causes vertigo for 20 turns (2× default) (Buff.affect(chr, Vertigo.class, Vertigo.DURATION * 2))
  * [[rpd:horror_enchantment_item|Horror Enchantment]] - Can inflict vertigo on hit (Buff.affect(defender, Vertigo.class, Vertigo.duration(defender)))
  * [[rpd:confusion_gas_trap_level_object|Confusion Gas]] - Can inflict vertigo (Buff.prolong(ch, Vertigo.class, Vertigo.duration(ch)))
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_curse_item|Scroll of Curse]] - May curse items with vertigo effect (Vertigo.class in curse list)
  * [[rpd:hunger_buff|Hunger]] - Critical hunger level can cause vertigo (Buff.prolong(target, Vertigo.class, Vertigo.duration(target)))

==== Strategy ====
  * **For Players:** Use vertigo strategically against enemies to limit their movement
  * **For Defense:** Be aware that vertigo can make escaping difficult
  * **Combat:** Vertigo can separate enemies from groups or dangerous positions
  * **Movement:** When affected, try to position yourself safely before the effect ends
  * **Note:** Characters with vertigo ignore it for movement when using blink or teleport effects

==== Countering Vertigo ====
  * Wait for the effect to naturally expire
  * Use Ring of Elements for resistance to reduce duration
  * Use terrain to your advantage (trapping enemies in corners)
  * Take advantage of enemy helplessness during the effect

==== Immunities ====
  * Some immobile creatures may be immune (e.g., statues)
  * [[rpd:enslaved_soul_mob|Enslaved Soul]] - Can be affected by vertigo (mentioned in their immunity list)

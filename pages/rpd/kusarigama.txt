====== Kusarigama ======

{{ rpd:images:kusarigama_kusarigama.png|Kusarigama Kusarigama  }}Kusarigama is unique weapon added in remix.19.2 release.

A traditional Japanese weapon combining a sickle (kama) with a chain (kusari) and a weight (fundo). This weapon offers unique combat mechanics and reach.

It has two unique abilities:
  * It can hit enemies at a distance ( range 2 ).
  * You can pull distant enemies (up to 4 cells away ) closer.

Also successeful hit can disorient the opponent.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** Moderate to high
  * **Reach:** Extended range compared to standard melee weapons
  * **Special Properties:** Unique attack pattern with chain mechanics

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Chain Reach:** Can attack enemies at distance
  * **Dual Function:** Combines cutting and striking capabilities
  * **Threat Range:** Can target enemies in multiple adjacent cells

Kusarigama idea belongs to Yuriy Frolov winner of [[spiders|spider nest]] story contest.

Final Kusarigama design implemented by Pavel Provorotov.

==== Notes ====
The Kusarigama is an exotic weapon that offers both reach and unique combat mechanics, making it useful for maintaining distance from enemies while still having a cutting edge.

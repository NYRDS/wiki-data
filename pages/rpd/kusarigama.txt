====== Kusarigama ======

Kusarigama is unique weapon added in remix.19.2 release.

It has two unique abilities:
  * It can hit enemies at a distance ( range 2 ).
  * You can pull distant enemies (up to 4 cells away ) closer.

Also successeful hit can disorient the opponent.

Kusarigama idea belongs to Yuriy Frolov winner of [[spiders|spider nest]] story contest.

Final Kusarigama design implemented by Pavel Provorotov.

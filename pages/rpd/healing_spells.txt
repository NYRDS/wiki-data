====== Healing Spells ======

**Healing Spells** are a category of magic spells in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that restore health to the caster or target characters.

==== List of Healing Spells ====
  * [[rpd:heal_spell|Heal]] - Restores health based on target's maximum HP
  * [[rpd:healing_potion_spell|Healing Potion Spell]] - (if exists, placeholder)
  * [[rpd:nature_armor_spell|Nature Armor]] - (may have healing properties depending on implementation)

==== Mechanics ====
Healing spells typically:
  * Restore a percentage of maximum HP
  * May have cooldown periods between uses
  * Often require mana to cast
  * Can target the caster or other characters

==== Affinities ====
Healing spells are typically associated with these magic affinities:
  * [[rpd:witchcraft_affinity|Witchcraft]]
  * [[rpd:priest_affinity|Priest]]
  * [[rpd:plague_doctor_affinity|PlagueDoctor]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for emergency health restoration during combat
  * Useful for maintaining pet/ally health
  * Consider mana costs when planning usage

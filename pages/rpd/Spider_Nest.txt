====== Spider Nest ======

==== Description ====
Spider Nests are locations in the dungeon where spiders congregate and reproduce. They are typically found in dark, secluded areas.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Spider Generation:** Continuously spawns spiders
  * **Threat Location:** Represents a dangerous area with multiple enemies
  * **Egg Production:** May produce spider eggs over time
  * **Defensive Webbing:** Often surrounded by webs that affect movement

==== Behavior ====
  * Continuously produces different types of spiders
  * May be controlled by a more powerful spider entity
  * Can become more dangerous if left unaddressed

==== Notes ====
Spider Nests are challenging locations that require careful approach. They represent persistent threats that can overwhelm unprepared adventurers.

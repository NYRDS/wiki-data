====== Rat King ======

{{ rpd:images:ratking_sprite.png|Rat King Sprite }}

The **Rat King** is a special NPC found in the [[rpd:sewers|Sewers]] level of Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
The Rat King is a non-hostile NPC that appears in a special room in the [[rpd:sewers|Sewers]]. He starts in a sleeping state and upon being awakened, he gives the player a [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]] and wanders around. If the player attacks him or becomes too aggressive, he becomes hostile.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 30
  * **HT:** 30
  * **Attack Skill:** 15
  * **Defense Skill:** 20
  * **Min Damage:** 4
  * **Max Damage:** 10
  * **Damage Resistance:** 5
  * **Experience for Kill:** 1

==== Behavior ====
  * Initially in a sleeping state
  * Awakens when player interacts with him
  * Gives player a [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]] on first interaction
  * Becomes hostile if attacked (anger level increases with each interaction)
  * Wanders around when awakened
  * Moves at twice the normal speed
  * Only remains friendly if anger level is less than 2

==== Location ====
  * Found exclusively in the [[rpd:sewers|Sewers]] level (depths 1-5)
  * Appears in a special "Rat King" room type
  * Part of the sewers' unique content

==== Interactions ====
  * **First interaction:** Wakes up, delivers dialogue, and gives [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]]
  * **Subsequent interactions:** Each interaction increases anger level
  * **When hostile:** Switches to hunting AI and attacks the player

==== Strategy ====
The Rat King is an important part of the early game content in the sewers. Players should be careful not to anger him until they have received the [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]], after which he can either be avoided or defeated for the item. He's an important source of the [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]], which provides a helpful buff.

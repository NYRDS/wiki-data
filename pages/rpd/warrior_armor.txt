====== Warrior Armor ======

**Warrior Armor** is the class-specific armor for the [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] hero class. It is the starting armor for Warriors and can be upgraded to the mastery armor [[rpd:gladiator_armor|Gladiator Armor]] or [[rpd:berserker_armor|Berserker Armor]].

==== Basic Information ====
  * **Class**: Warrior
  * **Armor Type**: Medium armor
  * **Armor Class**: [[rpd:armor|Armor]]
  * **Starting Equipment**: Given to Warrior at start of game
  * **Mastery Upgrades**: [[rpd:gladiator_armor|Gladiator Armor]] or [[rpd:berserker_armor|Berserker Armor]]

==== Properties ====
  * **Durability**: Starts at 15, degrades with use
  * **Armor Rating**: Provides moderate protection
  * **Strength Requirement**: Moderate (scales with upgrades)
  * **Speed**: Normal movement speed

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Basic Protection**: Standard armor abilities
  * **Upgrade Path**: Can be upgraded with [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * **Mastery Potential**: Can be transformed into class mastery armor with [[rpd:armor_kit|Armor Kit]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Warriors should prioritize keeping this armor upgraded
  * Early game protection for the warrior's melee-focused playstyle
  * Consider upgrading to mastery armor when possible
  * Works well with warrior's high strength stat

==== Upgrades ====
  * Using an [[rpd:armor_kit|Armor Kit]] on Warrior Armor creates either Gladiator Armor or Berserker Armor based on the hero's subclass choice

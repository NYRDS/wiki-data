====== Spiders ======

==== Description ====
Spiders are a category of enemies found in the dungeon. They come in various forms and present different challenges to adventurers.

==== Types of Spiders ====
  * [[rpd:spider_egg|Spider Eggs]] - Spawn additional spider minions
  * [[rpd:spider_nest|Spider Nests]] - Locations where spiders spawn
  * [[rpd:spider_servant|Spider Servants]] - Various types of spider minions
  * [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]] - A powerful spider boss (if exists in game)

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Web Creation:** Many spiders can create webs that affect movement
  * **Poison Attacks:** Some spiders have poisonous bites
  * **Web Traps:** Can create environmental hazards with their webs
  * **Group Tactics:** Often fight in groups or colonies

==== Notes ====
Spiders present unique challenges with their web-spinning abilities and can create difficult tactical situations for unprepared adventurers.

====== Treacherous Spirit ======

{{ rpd:images:treacherous_spirit_mob.png|Treacherous Spirit }}

==== Description ====
The **Treacherous Spirit** is a powerful ghost-like boss enemy found in the Guts level of the dungeon. It's an ethereal undead entity with the ability to teleport and possesses significant magical abilities.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** High (scales with dungeon level)
  * **Attack/Defense:** Very high
  * **Damage:** High
  * **Speed:** Fast
  * **Type:** Undead, Ghost
  * **EXP:** High

==== Abilities ====
  * **Teleportation:** Can teleport around the battlefield
  * **Magic Attacks:** Uses ranged magical attacks
  * **Ethereal:** May have special resistances or immunities
  * **Undead Nature:** Immune to certain status effects

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Guts (Yog) level
  * Acts as a boss or major challenge in that area
  * Appears in the deeper reaches of the dungeon

==== Strategy ====
  * Be prepared for unpredictable positioning due to teleportation
  * Use area-of-effect abilities carefully due to mobility
  * Prepare for magical attacks rather than physical combat
  * Bring protection against magical damage

==== Drops ====
  * [[rpd:heart_of_darkness_item|Heart of Darkness]] - A powerful artifact that summons spirits when taking damage

====== Treacherous Spirit ======

{{ :rpd:treacherous_spirit.png?nolink&50|}}

Treacherous Spirits are powerful dark creatures that appear as clouds of living darkness with a giant eye. They're associated with the mysterious Stranger NPC quest.

==== Description ====
This monstrous creature looks like a cloud of living darkness. It's gigantic eye stares at you while pulsating with each movement you make. It's one of the more dangerous spirits in the dungeon.

==== Combat ====
  * **Appearance:** Cloud of living darkness with a prominent eye
  * **Health:** High, for a spirit creature
  * **Attack:** Dark-based magical attacks
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Potentially ranged attacks
    * May have spirit-specific abilities
    * Dark magic resistance/immunities

==== Quest Significance ====
  * **Stranger's Request:** The [[rpd:azuterron_npc|Stranger]] asks players to defeat the Treacherous Spirit
  * **Reward:** Defeating it and returning the [[rpd:heart_of_darkness|Heart of Darkness]] to the Stranger provides a reward
  * **Quest Item:** Drops the [[rpd:heart_of_darkness|Heart of Darkness]]

==== Location ====
Treacherous Spirits can be found:
  * On specific dungeon levels (often related to the Stranger quest)
  * In darker areas of the dungeon
  * Possibly in special spirit-themed areas

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Spirit Nature:** May have standard spirit resistances/immunities
  * **Dark Magic:** Connected to dark magical properties
  * **Intelligence:** May show signs of tactical intelligence
  * **Aggression:** Likely aggressive when encountered

==== Tactics ====
  * Be prepared for magical attacks
  * Holy or light-based attacks may be particularly effective
  * Watch for area-of-effect abilities
  * Consider that the quest might be completable through Necromancy (see [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class options)

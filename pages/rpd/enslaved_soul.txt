====== Enslaved Soul ======

The **Enslaved Soul** is a ghostly undead enemy found in the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]].

==== Description ====
Enslaved Souls are tormented spirits bound to the Necropolis. They are ethereal undead beings that can pass through walls, making them difficult to avoid.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Moderate
* Defense: Moderate
* Attack: Moderate, with necromantic damage
* Speed: High
* Special: Wall passing ability, undead traits

==== Tactics ====
Enslaved Souls are difficult to avoid due to their wall-passing ability and undead nature. They should be defeated quickly before they can surround the hero. Holy or blessed weapons may be more effective against these undead spirits.

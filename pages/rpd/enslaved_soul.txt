====== Enslaved Soul ======

{{ rpd:images:enslaved_soul_sprite.png|Enslaved Soul }}

Enslaved Souls are spectral undead enemies found in the Necropolis area of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in Necropolis area
  * **HP**: Moderate
  * **Attack**: Moderate
  * **Defense**: Low
  * **EXP**: Moderate

==== Description ====
Enslaved Souls are the tortured spirits of adventurers who died in the Necropolis. They are bound to the will of the area's dark magic and attack intruders.

==== Behavior ====
  * Passes through walls and obstacles
  * Haunts the Necropolis area
  * Uses spectral attacks
  * Part of the undead category

==== Drops ====
Enslaved Souls typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Occasionally special items related to the Necropolis

==== Special Notes ====
  * Intangible - can pass through walls
  * Found specifically in the Necropolis area
  * Represents the tragic fate of previous adventurers
  * Part of the undead category which has special characteristics

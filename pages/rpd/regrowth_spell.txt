====== Regrowth ======

{{ rpd:images:regrowth_spell.png|Regrowth }}

**Regrowth** is a spell that creates plant growth in the dungeon, usually associated with Earth Elementals' special ability to generate regrowth when attacking.

==== Description ====
Regrowth is a magical effect that causes plant life to grow rapidly in the targeted area. It typically appears as grass or other vegetation covering the floor tiles.

==== Effects ====
  * **Terrain Change:** Converts targeted tiles to grass or similar vegetation
  * **Potential Healing:** May provide healing effects to characters standing on regrowth tiles
  * **Environmental Effect:** Creates a more natural environment in the dungeon

==== Occurrence ====
  * **Earth Elemental Ability:** Earth Elementals have a 50% chance to create regrowth when attacking
  * **Spell Casting:** Can be cast by various magical means
  * **Environmental Effects:** May appear from other magical sources in the dungeon

==== Strategy ====
  * **Advantages:** Can provide beneficial environmental effects or healing
  * **Disadvantages:** May block line of sight or movement in some contexts
  * **Usefulness:** Often beneficial for players who can take advantage of environmental bonuses

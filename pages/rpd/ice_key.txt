The Ice Key is a special key item that is likely used to access areas in the Ice Caves or other frozen-themed areas of the dungeon.

The Ice Key is a specialized key that opens specific doors or chests in frozen or ice-themed areas of the dungeon. It's part of a special set of keys for themed areas.

==== Properties ====
  * **Type:** Specialized key item
  * **Function:** Opens ice-themed locks or doors
  * **Location:** Primarily used in [[rpd:ice_caves|Ice Caves]]
  * **Rarity:** Specialized item for specific dungeon areas

==== Usage ====
  * **Access:** Opens special doors or chests in ice-themed areas
  * **Thematic:** Part of the Ice Caves area mechanics
  * **Exclusivity:** Likely only works in specific ice-themed locations
  * **Single Use:** Possibly consumed upon use (like other keys)

==== Location ====

  * May be dropped by ice-themed enemies

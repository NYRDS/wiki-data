The Ice Key is a special key used to access frozen areas and special rooms in Remixed Dungeon.
==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Special
  * **Weight**: 1
==== Description ====
The Ice Key is a crystalline key that can open special frozen locks and barriers in the dungeon. It's typically obtained by defeating the Ice Guardian Core boss.
==== Usage ====
  * Opens special frozen doors and barriers
  * Required to access certain areas of the dungeon
  * Single-use item for unlocking special areas
==== Acquisition ====
  * Dropped by the Ice Guardian Core boss
  * Found in the Ice Caves area
  * Allows access to special content in that area
==== Special Notes ====
  * Exclusive drop from Ice Guardian Core
  * Required for completing the Ice Caves area
  * Single-use item
  * Tied to the Ice Caves expansion content

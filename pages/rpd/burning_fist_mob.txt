====== Burning Fist ======

{{ rpd:images:burning_fist_mob.png|Burning Fist }}

==== Description ====
**Burning Fist** is one of Yog's guardians found in the Goo Depths. It is one of the secondary bosses protecting Yog along with Rotting Fist, Yogs' Brain, Yogs' Heart, and Yogs' Teeth.

==== Stats ====
* **HP**: 400
* **HT**: 400
* **Defense Skill**: 25
* **Attack Skill**: 26
* **Damage**: 40-62
* **DR (Damage Resistance)**: 15
* **EXP for Kill**: 0 (Does not grant experience)

==== Abilities ====
* **Ranged Attack**: Can attack enemies at range using zapping mechanics
* **Fire Aura**: Creates fire in adjacent cells each turn, dealing periodic damage
* **Summoning**: When damaged, summons all nearby mobs to its location
* **Immunities**: Immune to Amok, Sleep, Terror, Burning, and Fire-based attacks
* **Resistance**: Resistant to Toxic Gas
* **No Pet**: Cannot be turned into a pet

==== Special Mechanics ====
* Creates a fire field around itself each turn, affecting nearby cells
* Uses IZapper interface for ranged attacks
* Does not provide experience when defeated

==== Drops ====
* No standard drops (as it's a Yog guardian)

==== Location ====
* Found in the Goo Depths (last level) as one of Yog's guardians

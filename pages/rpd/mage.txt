====== Mage ======

{{ rpd:images:mage_sprite.png|Mage }}

The Mage is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He focuses on magical attacks and has higher spell capacity than other classes.

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 10
  * **Starting SP**: 15 (vs 10 for other classes)
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * Wand of Magic Missile (identified)
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Wand of Magic Missile in quickslot
    * Wind Gust spell (in quickslot)
    * Scroll of Identify (known item)
  * **Magic Affinity**: Elemental
  * **Spell User**: Yes

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Mage has more Spell Points (SP) than other classes (15 vs 10), allowing him to cast more spells per level.
  * Starts with an identified Wand of Magic Missile for immediate magical damage.
  * Wind Gust spell is available in the quickslot for crowd control.

==== Advantages ====
  * Higher spell capacity allows more spell usage per level
  * Wand of Magic Missile for ranged damage
  * Wind Gust spell for pushing enemies away
  * Scroll of Identify already known

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Wand charges can run out and require recharging
  * Lower durability than Warrior class
  * Limited to wand and spell resources

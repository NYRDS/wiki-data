====== Mage ======

The **Mage** is one of the playable classes in Remixed Dungeon. Mages excel at spellcasting and magical combat, with access to various magical abilities and spells.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Stat:** Magic Power
  * **Strength:** 8 (lower than Warrior and Scout, average for casters)
  * **Health:** Average
  * **Special Ability:** Magic Missile spell and ability to upgrade wands

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **Magic Missile:** The signature spell of the Mage - deals moderate damage and can be used to enchant wands
  * **Wand Mastery:** Mages can use wands as melee weapons and benefit from them more effectively
  * **Mages Staff:** Can craft a magical staff for improved spellcasting
  * **Spell Casting:** Access to various magical spells and abilities

==== Class Advantages ====
  * High magical damage potential
  * Can use wands more effectively
  * Access to utility spells
  * Good at ranged combat

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Lower health than Warrior class
  * Requires magical resources (mana)
  * Weaker in close combat without proper equipment

==== Class Armor ====
Mages can use their class armor ability to cast special spells like Magic Missile or other utility abilities depending on their mastery.

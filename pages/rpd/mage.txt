====== Mage ======

{{ rpd:images:mage_sprite.png|Mage }}
The Mage is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He focuses on magical attacks and has higher spell capacity than other classes.
==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 10
  * **Starting SP**: 15 (vs 10 for other classes)
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * Wand of Magic Missile (identified)
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Wand of Magic Missile in quickslot
    * Wind Gust spell (in quickslot)
    * Scroll of Identify (known item)
  * **Magic Affinity**: Elemental
  * **Spell User**: Yes
==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Mage has more Spell Points (SP) than other classes (15 vs 10), allowing him to cast more spells per level.
  * Starts with an identified Wand of Magic Missile for immediate magical damage.
  * Wind Gust spell is available in the quickslot for crowd control.
==== Advantages ====
  * Higher spell capacity allows more spell usage per level
  * Wand of Magic Missile for ranged damage
  * Wind Gust spell for pushing enemies away
  * Scroll of Identify already known
==== Disadvantages ====
  * Wand charges can run out and require recharging
  * Lower durability than Warrior class
  * Limited to wand and spell resources
==== Subclasses ====

  * [[rpd:battle_mage|Battle Mage]]

The **Mage** is one of the starting playable classes in Remixed Dungeon. Mages excel at spellcasting and magical combat, with access to various magical abilities and spells.
==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Stat:** Magic Power
  * **Starting Strength:** 10
  * **Health:** Average
  * **Magic Affinity:** Elemental
  * **Special Ability:** Magic Missile spell and ability to upgrade wands
==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Item:** Wand of Magic Missile (identified) - A unique wand that can be "disenchanted" to upgrade another wand
  * **Quickslots:** Wand of Magic Missile and Wind Gust spell
==== Class Perks ====
  * **Wand of Magic Missile:** Starts with a unique Wand of Magic Missile that can be "disenchanted" to upgrade another wand
  * **Faster Wand Recharge:** Recharges wands faster than other classes
  * **Wand Food Bonus:** When eaten, any piece of food restores 1 charge for all wands in the inventory
  * **Wand Melee:** Can use wands as a melee weapon
  * **Mana Spells:** Uses mana to cast Elemental spells (starts with 15 max SP)
  * **Identified Scrolls:** Scrolls of Identify are identified from the beginning
==== Class Abilities ====
  * **Magic Missile:** The signature spell of the Mage - deals moderate damage and can be used to enchant wands
  * **Wand Mastery:** Mages can use wands as melee weapons and benefit from them more effectively
  * **Mages Staff:** Can craft a magical staff for improved spellcasting
  * **Spell Casting:** Access to various magical spells and abilities
The Mage has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Battlemage:** When fighting with a wand in hand, inflicts additional damage depending on the current number of charges; every successful hit restores 1 charge to the wand
==== Class Advantages ====
  * High magical damage potential
  * Can use wands more effectively
  * Access to utility spells
  * Good at ranged combat
  * Food restores wand charges
  * Wands can be used as melee weapons
==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Lower health than Warrior class
  * Requires magical resources (mana)
  * Weaker in close combat without proper equipment
==== Class Armor ====
Mages can use their class armor ability to cast special spells like Magic Missile or other utility abilities depending on their mastery.
==== Spells ====
Mages have access to various spells including:
  * [[rpd:healing_spell|Healing]]
  * [[rpd:ignite|Ignite]]
  * [[rpd:root_spell|Root]]
  * [[rpd:wind_gust|Wind Gust]]
  * [[rpd:freeze_globe|Freeze]]
  * [[rpd:magic_torch|Magic Torch]]
==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on ranged combat using wands and spells
  * Make use of the wand food bonus to keep wands charged
  * Consider the Battlemage mastery for combining melee and magic
  * Manage mana effectively for spell casting

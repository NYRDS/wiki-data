====== Weapon Item ======

{{ rpd:images:short_sword_item.png|Weapon }}

==== Description ====
Weapons are items used by the hero to attack enemies. Each weapon has specific damage range, speed, and accuracy characteristics.

==== Weapon Categories ====
  * [[rpd:melee_weapon_item|Melee Weapons]] - Close combat weapons
  * [[rpd:ranged_weapon_item|Ranged Weapons]] - Weapons for distant combat
  * [[rpd:sword_item|Swords]] - Balanced melee weapons
  * [[rpd:axe_item|Axes]] - Heavy damage weapons
  * [[rpd:dagger_item|Daggers]] - Fast light weapons
  * [[rpd:bow_item|Bows and Crossbows]] - Ranged projectile weapons
  * [[rpd:spear_item|Spears]] - Mid-range thrusting weapons

==== Common Weapons ====
  * [[rpd:short_sword_item|Short Sword]] - Basic melee weapon
  * [[rpd:wooden_bow_item|Wooden Bow]] - Basic ranged weapon
  * [[rpd:dart_item|Dart]] - Basic throwing weapon
  * [[rpd:boomerang_item|Boomerang]] - Returning throwing weapon
  * [[rpd:wand_item|Wands]] - Magic-based weapons
  * [[rpd:staff_item|Staffs]] - Magic-enhanced weapons

==== Weapon Mechanics ====
  * **Damage:** Affects the amount of damage dealt to enemies
  * **Speed:** Determines how quickly the weapon attacks
  * **Accuracy:** Affects chance to hit enemies
  * **Strength Requirement:** Minimum strength needed to wield effectively
  * **Upgrades:** Weapons can be upgraded to improve stats
  * **Enchantments:** Can be added via [[rpd:scroll_of_enchantment_item|Scroll of Enchantment]]

==== Enchantments ====
  * [[rpd:fire_enchantment|Fire]] - Chance to ignite enemies
  * [[rpd:shocking_enchantment|Shocking]] - Chance to deal lightning damage
  * [[rpd:chilling_enchantment|Chilling]] - Chance to slow enemies
  * [[rpd:bleeding_enchantment|Bleeding]] - Chance to cause bleeding
  * [[rpd:luck_enchantment|Luck]] - Improves item drops
  * [[rpd:poison_enchantment|Poison]] - Chance to poison enemies
  * [[rpd:regrowth_enchantment|Regrowth]] - Grows plants around impact

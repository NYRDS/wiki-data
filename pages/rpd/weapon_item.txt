====== Weapon ======

{{ rpd:images:sword_item.png|Weapon }}

==== Description ====
**Weapon** is the base class for all melee and ranged weapons in the game. Weapons are used for combat and can be upgraded and enchanted to improve their effectiveness.

==== Properties ====
  * **Damage**: The minimum and maximum damage values the weapon can deal
  * **Delay**: The time needed to perform an attack with this weapon
  * **Accuracy**: How likely the weapon is to hit its target
  * **Strength**: The minimum strength required to use the weapon effectively
  * **Upgrades**: Most weapons can be upgraded using Scrolls of Upgrade
  * **Enchantments**: Many weapons can be enchanted using Scrolls of Enchantment

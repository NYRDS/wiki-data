====== Weapon ======

{{ rpd:images:weapon_item.png|Weapon }}

**Weapons** are items in Remixed Dungeon that allow the hero to attack enemies. Each weapon has different stats that affect combat effectiveness.

==== Description ====
Weapons are equipment items held in the hero's main hand. They determine attack skill, damage range, and may have special properties or enchantments.

==== Stats ====
  * **Attack Skill:** Bonus to hit chance
  * **Damage Range:** Minimum and maximum damage dealt
  * **Durability:** How many uses before breaking (if applicable)
  * **Enchantment:** Special magical effect (for upgraded weapons)
  * **Upgrade Level:** Level of upgrade applied to the weapon

==== Weapon Types ====
  * **[[rpd:melee_weapon_item|Melee Weapons]]** - Close combat weapons
  * **[[rpd:ranged_weapon_item|Ranged Weapons]]** - Weapons for distance combat (projectiles, wands)
  * **[[rpd:sword_item|Swords]]** - Versatile bladed weapons
  * **[[rpd:dagger_item|Daggers]]** - Fast, light piercing weapons
  * **[[rpd:axe_item|Axes]]** - Heavy-hitting weapons
  * **[[rpd:mace_item|Maces]]** - Blunt weapons with crushing damage
  * **[[rpd:spear_item|Spears]]** - Thrusting weapons with reach
  * **[[rpd:bow_item|Bows and Crossbows]]** - Projectile weapons
  * **[[rpd:wand_item|Wands]]** - Magic missile weapons

==== Weapon Classes ====
  * **[[rpd:swords|Swords]]** - Standard slashing weapons
  * **[[rpd:daggers|Daggers]]** - Light, quick piercing weapons
  * **[[rpd:axes|Axes]]** - Heavy impact weapons
  * **[[rpd:maces|Maces]]** - Blunt crushing weapons
  * **[[rpd:spears|Spears]]** - Reach weapons
  * **[[rpd:bows|Bows]]** - Projectile weapons

==== Upgrades ====
  * Weapons can be [[rpd:upgrade|upgraded]] using [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * [[rpd:enchantments|Enchantments]] can be applied to provide special effects
  * Higher levels provide better stats

==== Strategy ====
  * Choose weapons based on your [[rpd:hero_class|hero class]] and fighting style
  * Early game: Focus on weapons with higher damage output
  * Mid game: Consider durability and special properties
  * Late game: Look for weapons with useful [[rpd:enchantments|enchantments]]

====== Gnoll ======

{:rpd:images:gnoll_sprite.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:gnoll_sprite.png|Gnoll }}

The **Gnoll** is a playable hero class in Remixed Dungeon representing a playable version of the dungeon's gnoll enemies. This unique class has special abilities and starting equipment that reflect its gnoll nature.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Focus:** Versatile combat with special gnoll abilities
  * **Special Ability:** Unique gnoll-based mechanics and resistances
  * **Playstyle:** Balanced fighter with unique abilities

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 5 (significantly lower than other classes)
  * **Starting STR**: 15 (highest of all classes)
  * **Starting Equipment**:
    * [[rpd:gnoll_tomahawk|Gnoll Tamahawk]] (identified)
    * No armor (starts with NoItem instead of Cloth Armor)
    * 8 Darts (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Darts in quickslot
    * Roar spell (in quickslot)
  * **Magic Affinity**: Witchcraft

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **Gnoll Heritage:** Special resistances or abilities from being a gnoll
  * **Tracking:** Possibly enhanced detection of certain enemies
  * **Special Combat:** Unique fighting style

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Gnoll has very high starting strength (15) but very low HP (5)
  * Starts without armor, making her vulnerable to damage
  * Cannot read scrolls or use the Tome of Mastery or Arcane Stylus
  * Certain Gnoll-type mobs are friendly and won't attack (Gnoll, Shaman, Brute, Shielded, ShamanElder)
  * The Roar spell is available in the quickslot for intimidating enemies

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Unique playstyle different from standard classes
  * Highest starting strength of all classes, providing massive damage
  * Special starting weapon with unique properties
  * Roar spell can frighten enemies
  * Some enemies will not attack her
  * Darts provide ranged options
  * Possible resistances to effects that affect humanoids differently

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Less understood mechanics (as a more specialized class)
  * Very low starting HP (only 5) makes her fragile
  * Starts without armor, increasing vulnerability
  * Cannot use beneficial scrolls
  * Cannot use Tome of Mastery until unlocked through other means
  * Cannot inscribe items with Arcane Stylus
  * May have specific weaknesses due to non-humanoid nature

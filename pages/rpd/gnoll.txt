====== Gnoll ======

The **Gnoll** is a playable class in Remixed Dungeon representing a playable version of the dungeon's gnoll enemies. This unique class has special abilities and starting equipment that reflect its gnoll nature.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Focus:** Versatile combat with special gnoll abilities
  * **Special Ability:** Unique gnoll-based mechanics and resistances
  * **Playstyle:** Balanced fighter with unique abilities

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **Gnoll Heritage:** Special resistances or abilities from being a gnoll
  * **Unique Equipment:** May have access to special gnoll-themed items
  * **Tracking:** Possibly enhanced detection of certain enemies
  * **Special Combat:** Unique fighting style

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Weapon:** [[rpd:gnoll_tomahawk|Gnoll Tomahawk]] (special starting weapon)
  * **Armor:** Armor with specific absorption rating
  * **Food:** Additional food ration

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Unique playstyle different from standard classes
  * Special starting weapon with unique properties
  * Possible resistances to effects that affect humanoids differently
  * Different progression path from standard classes

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Less understood mechanics (as a more specialized class)
  * May have specific weaknesses due to non-humanoid nature
  * Potentially different resource management

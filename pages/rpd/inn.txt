====== inn ======

{{ rpd:images:inn_bed.png|Inn Bed }}

The **Inn** is a safe location in the town area where the hero can rest and recover between dungeon expeditions.

==== Description ====
The Inn is part of the town area in Remixed Dungeon, typically featuring inn beds and often populated by the [[rpd:innkeeper_npc|Innkeeper]] or other NPCs. It serves as a sanctuary where players can rest and restore their health before continuing their dungeon adventures.

==== Functions ====
  * **Healing**: Allows the hero to rest and restore full health
  * **Status Restoration**: May cure negative status effects
  * **Safety**: A secure location away from dungeon dangers
  * **Preparation**: A place to prepare for the next dungeon expedition
  * **NPC Interaction**: Often contains NPCs like the Innkeeper

==== Location ====
  * Found in the town area before entering the dungeon
  * Part of the starting town levels (Cinema_2021_03.json and other town levels)
  * Serves as a safe haven between dungeon runs
  * Usually contains inn beds and NPCs

==== NPCs ====
  * [[rpd:innkeeper_npc|Innkeeper NPC]] - Typically manages the inn and provides services
  * Other town NPCs may also be found nearby

==== Decorations ====
  * **Inn Beds**: Special bed decorations found in the inn areas
  * **Signs**: INN signs that identify the location
  * **Furniture**: Tables, chairs, and other common inn furnishings
  * **Other Decorations**: Fireplaces, shelves, and other inn-specific decor

==== Mechanics ====
  * Provides a safe place to rest and restore health
  * May offer services through NPCs like the Innkeeper
  * Allows the hero to begin a new dungeon run in full health
  * Safe from combat and hostile entities

====== Badge Spider Queen Slain ======

The **Badge Spider Queen Slain** is an achievement obtained by defeating the [[rpd:spider_queen_mob|Spider Queen]] boss.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Defeat the [[rpd:spider_queen_mob|Spider Queen]] in combat
  * The badge is automatically awarded upon victory

==== Significance ====
  * Marks completion of a significant challenge in the dungeon
  * Part of the collection of badges that document the hero's accomplishments
  * May be required for certain achievements or recognition

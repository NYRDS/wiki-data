====== Spider Egg ======

{{ rpd:images:spideregg_sprite.png|Spider Egg }}

The **Spider Egg** is an object/mob in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that hatches into a random spider.

==== Description ====
This fragile egg is pulsating briefly, inside it you can see something of many legs. It's laid by spider queens and has a limited lifespan before hatching.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 2
  * **Defense Skill:** 1
  * **Attack Skill:** 1
  * **Speed:** 0 (immobile)
  * **EXP:** 0
  * **Max Level:** 9

==== Properties ====
  * **Type:** Mob/Object
  * **Immobile:** Cannot move from its position
  * **Fragile:** Very low HP, easily destroyed
  * **Hatching:** Automatically hatches after 20 turns
  * **Pet Immunity:** Cannot be tamed or made into a pet

==== Behavior ====
  * **Immobile:** Remains in place until destroyed or hatched
  * **Hatching Timer:** Automatically hatches after 20 turns
  * **Spawn:** Upon hatching, creates a random spider mob
  * **Destruction:** If killed before hatching, it is destroyed without producing a spider

==== Spawn ====
  * **Spider Type:** Upon hatching, spawns a random spider mob
  * **Level Range:** Typically spawns spiders appropriate to the current dungeon level
  * **Probability:** Spawned by Spider Queen and other spider-related mechanics

==== Strategy ====
  * **Immediate Threat:** Though fragile, poses a threat as it will soon hatch into a spider
  * **Timing:** You can choose to destroy it or let it hatch depending on the situation
  * **Avoid Crowding:** Be careful not to let multiple eggs hatch near each other
  * **Advantage:** If you have ways to tame or control spiders, letting it hatch may be beneficial

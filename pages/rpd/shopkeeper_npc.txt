====== Shopkeeper NPC ======

**Shopkeeper** is an NPC in Remixed Dungeon who operates shops on various dungeon levels.

==== Role ====
  * Operates shops found throughout the dungeon
  * Buys items from the player at fair prices
  * Sells various useful items, equipment, and consumables

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Trading**: Buys and sells items at the shop
  * **Bag Disenchanting**: Can disenchant bag items for gold based on their type
  * **Item Identification**: Automatically identifies items in the shop for the player

==== Interactions ====
  * Found in shop areas on various dungeon levels
  * Does not attack or provide combat assistance
  * Provides an essential economic system for the game

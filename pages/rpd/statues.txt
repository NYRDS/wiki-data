====== Statue Types ======

**Statues** are various static objects found throughout the dungeon that may have different properties and functions.

==== Types of Statues ====
  * **Decorative Statues**: Serve only as environmental elements with no special properties (terrain features)
  * **Animated Statues**: Come to life as enemies when disturbed or approached (mob statues)
  * **Special Statues**: Have unique properties, such as the [[rpd:gold_statue|Gold Statue]]

==== Notable Statues ====
  * [[rpd:gold_statue|Gold Statue]] - Special statue with unique properties
  * [[rpd:statue|Statue]] - General information about statues

==== Interaction ====
  * Some statues can be smashed to reveal contents
  * Others may activate when stepped on or approached
  * Some may be part of puzzles or mechanical systems
  * Breaking certain statues may trigger traps or spawn enemies

==== Strategy ====
  * Examine statues carefully before interacting with them
  * Consider the potential risks versus rewards
  * Some statues may be beneficial to leave undisturbed
  * Others may be necessary to break for progression

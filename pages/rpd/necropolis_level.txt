====== Necropolis Level ======

{{ rpd:images:necropolis_level.png|Necropolis Level }}

The **Necropolis** is an area in Remixed Dungeon, typically found in the later parts of the game.

==== Description ====
The Necropolis is a graveyard-themed area filled with undead creatures. It serves as a dark and dangerous area full of undead enemies and necromantic magic.

==== Layout ====
The necropolis typically features tombstones, crypts, and burial grounds with a variety of undead creatures. The layout may vary depending on the mod version.

==== Enemies ====
The Necropolis contains various undead enemies:
  * [[rpd:zombie_mob|Zombies]] - Basic undead creatures
  * [[rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeletons]] - Reanimated bones (1 in 10 chance to appear)
  * [[rpd:wraith_mob|Wraiths]] - Ethereal undead that can pass through walls
  * [[rpd:lich_mob|Liches]] - Powerful undead spellcasters
  * [[rpd:enslaved_soul_mob|Enslaved Souls]] - Trapped spirits

==== Special Features ====
  * Undead-themed enemies and environment
  * Necromantic magic effects
  * Special undead-related mechanisms
  * Potentially contains unique items or artifacts related to necromancy

==== Strategy ====
  * Be prepared for numerous undead enemies with various abilities
  * Holy/light-based attacks are often effective against undead
  * Healing magic may harm undead enemies
  * Watch for environmental hazards related to necromancy

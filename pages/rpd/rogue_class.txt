====== Rogue Class ======

{{ rpd:images:rogue_hero.png|Rogue Hero }}

The **Rogue** is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. Known for stealth, agility, and unique throwing spells like Kunai Throw.

==== Class Characteristics ====
  * **Primary Stat**: Dexterity and stealth
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * [[rpd:dagger_item|Dagger]]
    * [[rpd:leather_armor_item|Leather Armor]]
    * [[rpd:ration_item|Food ration]]
    * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]]
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]]
  * **Special Abilities**: Enhanced detection of secrets and traps
  * **Mana System**: Uses mana for throwing spells like [[rpd:kunai_throw_spell|Kunai Throw]]

==== Advantages ====
  * **Trap Detection**: Better at avoiding traps due to high awareness
  * **Stealth**: Better at avoiding random encounters
  * **Stealing**: Can steal from shops if caught
  * **Exploration**: Starts with an item that can identify equipment

==== Disadvantages ====
  * **Weaker Armor**: Starts with leather armor which has lower protection
  * **Average Weapon**: Starts with a dagger which has modest damage
  * **Mana Resource**: Requires managing an additional resource (mana)

==== Abilities ====
  * **Sneak Detection**: Reveals nearby hidden doors and traps
  * **Enhanced Awareness**: Better chance to detect secret areas
  * **Mana System**: Access to magical throwing spells that use mana

==== Specializations ====
The Rogue class can specialize in the following subclasses:
  * **Freerunner** - Enhanced movement capabilities
  * **Assassin** - Enhanced damage capabilities

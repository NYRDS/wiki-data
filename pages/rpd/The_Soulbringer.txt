====== The Soulbringer ======

==== Description ====
A powerful necromantic item that connects to the realm of death, allowing the wielder to control souls and undead.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Soul Manipulation:** Allows control over souls and undead creatures
  * **Necromantic Focus:** Enhances necromantic abilities
  * **Rare Artifact:** A powerful and rare item in the dungeon

==== Uses ====
  * Used by necromancers for advanced abilities
  * May be required for specific necromantic rituals
  * Provides bonuses to undead control

==== Notes ====
The Soulbringer is an advanced necromantic artifact that significantly enhances the powers of those who practice death magic.

====== Magic Arrow Spell ======

{{ rpd:images:magic_arrow_spell_icon.png|Magic Arrow Spell Icon }}

**Magic Arrow** is an [[rpd:elf|Elf]] affinity spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that fires a magical arrow at a target, dealing damage based on the caster's max HP.

==== Description ====
Magic Arrow fires a magical arrow at the target, dealing damage based on the caster's max HP. It's a reliable ranged attack spell that's particularly useful when you run out of ammunition.

==== Stats ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** [[rpd:spell_affinities#elf|Elf]]
  * **Targeting:** Character (not self)
  * **Level:** 1
  * **Mana Cost:** 5
  * **Cast Time:** 0.1 turns
  * **Cooldown:** 1 turn
  * **Damage:** Random value between 1 and caster's max HP / 2

==== Effects ====
  * Deals damage to the targeted enemy
  * Damage scales with caster's max HP (1 to max HP / 2)
  * Creates a visual arrow effect between caster and target
  * Has a 1-turn cooldown after casting

==== Usage ====
The Magic Arrow spell can be used for:
  * Reliable ranged damage against single targets
  * Consistent damage output when ammunition is low
  * Triggering mechanisms or switches from a safe distance
  * Dealing with enemies at range when other options aren't available

==== Classes ====
Classes that can use this spell include:
  * [[rpd:elf|Elf]] (naturally has [[rpd:spell_affinities#elf|Elf]] affinity)
  * Other classes that gain access through special means

==== Strategy ====
How to effectively use the Magic Arrow spell:
  * Best used when health is at maximum for optimal damage output
  * Effective for conserving physical ammunition
  * Useful against enemies that require ranged attacks to defeat safely
  * Consider the cooldown period when planning subsequent actions

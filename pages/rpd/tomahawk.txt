====== Tomahawk ======

{{ rpd:images:item_Tomahawk2.png|Tomahawk }}

Tomahawk is a throwing weapon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Description ====
The Tomahawk is a throwing axe that deals moderate damage and causes bleeding on hit. It's useful for ranged attacks and can be particularly effective against groups of enemies due to the bleeding effect.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** 4-20
  * **Strength Required:** 17
  * **Durability:** Variable (quantity-based)
  * **Speed:** Fast
  * **Accuracy:** Standard

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Bleeding:** Causes bleeding damage on hit equal to the damage dealt
  * **Throwing:** Can be thrown at enemies from a distance

==== Availability ====
  * Found in various dungeon levels
  * May appear in shops
  * Often found in containers and chests

==== Strategy ====
  * Useful for dealing with enemies while maintaining distance
  * The bleeding effect makes it particularly effective against groups of enemies
  * Be mindful of strength requirements when using

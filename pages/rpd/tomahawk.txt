====== Tomahawk ======

{{ rpd:images:tomahawk_sprite.png|Tomahawk }}

The **Tomahawk** is a throwing weapon in Remixed Dungeon. The [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] class specifically starts with a special variant called the [[rpd:gnoll_tomahawk|Gnoll Tomahawk]].

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** 4-20
  * **Strength Required:** 17
  * **Durability:** Standard
  * **Weight:** Varies by quantity

==== Description ====
A traditional throwing axe. The standard Tomahawk has moderate damage and requires significant strength to use effectively.

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Causes bleeding damage equal to the attack damage dealt
  * Can be thrown at enemies from a distance

==== Acquisition ====
  * Can be found throughout the dungeon
  * [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] class starts with the special [[rpd:gnoll_tomahawk|Gnoll Tomahawk]] variant
  * May be available in shops

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for dealing with single enemies
  * Causes bleeding damage which can be useful against tough enemies
  * Good for ranged attacks when you need to maintain distance
  * Can be re-used if it doesn't break on impact

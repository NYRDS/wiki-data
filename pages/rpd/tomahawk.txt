====== Tomahawk ======

{{ rpd:images:tomahawk_sprite.png|Tomahawk }}

The **Tomahawk** is a throwing weapon associated with the [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] class in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Throwing Weapon
  * **Damage:** Moderate to high
  * **Durability:** Standard
  * **Throw Range:** Medium
  * **Special Effect:** Deals damage to enemies and can hit multiple targets in a line

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Thrown weapons that can potentially hit multiple enemies in a straight line
  * Part of the Gnoll's thematic arsenal
  * Can be used both offensively and for tactical positioning

==== Acquisition ====
  * Starting weapon for the [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] class
  * Can also be found as a rare drop in the dungeon
  * May be available in certain shops

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for dealing with clustered enemies
  * Good for ranged attacks when you need to maintain distance
  * Useful for softening up enemies before engaging in melee
  * Can be re-used if it doesn't break on impact

==== Class Restrictions ====
  * While any class can use the Tomahawk, it's closely associated with the [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] class

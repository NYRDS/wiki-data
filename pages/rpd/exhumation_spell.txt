====== Exhumation ======

{{ rpd:images:exhumation_spell_icon.png|Exhumation Spell Icon }}

**Exhumation** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Opening the tomb is a sinister act on it own, but not enough for someone serving Death itself. Performer of this Dark Ritual has a great chance (and it will grow with the performer\'s mastery) to subdue a poor soul of one lying here.

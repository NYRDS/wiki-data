====== Exhumation Spell ======

{{ rpd:images:exhumation_spell_icon.png|Exhumation Spell Icon }}

**Exhumation** is a Necromancy spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that allows the caster to raise creatures from graves.

==== Description ====
Opening the tomb is a sinister act on its own, but not enough for someone serving Death itself. Performer of this Dark Ritual has a great chance (and it will grow with the performer's mastery) to subdue a poor soul of one lying here.

==== Stats ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** [[rpd:necromancy|Necromancy]]
  * **Targeting:** cell
  * **Level:** 2
  * **Mana Cost:** 10
  * **Cast Time:** 2 turns

==== Mechanics ====
When cast on a tomb or skeleton heap, the spell:
  * Opens the grave and raises any contents
  * Has a chance to convert nearby [[rpd:wraith|Wraiths]] into the caster's pet based on the caster's magic level
  * Formula: chance = 1 - (1 / (caster:magicLvl() + 1))

==== Usage ====
The Exhumation spell can be used for:
  * Raising wraiths from tombs as pets
  * Strategic applications in combat by gaining allies
  * Utility purposes for resource generation

==== Classes ====
Classes that can use this spell include:
  * Classes with [[rpd:necromancy|Necromancy]] affinity
  * [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class and related subclasses

==== Strategy ====
How to effectively use the Exhumation spell:
  * Best used when wraiths spawn near you after opening graves
  * Higher magic level increases the chance to tame wraiths
  * Synergizes with necromancy-related talents and equipment

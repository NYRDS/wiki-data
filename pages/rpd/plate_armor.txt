====== plate armor ======

{{ rpd:images:platearmor_sprite.png|Plate Armor Sprite }}

==== Description ====
**Plate Armor** is a high-tier armor in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. Enormous plates of metal are joined together into a suit that provides unmatched protection to any adventurer strong enough to bear its staggering weight.

==== Stats ====
  * **Armor Tier**: High
  * **Durability**: Very High
  * **Speed Penalty**: High
  * **Protection**: Highest among basic armor types

==== Mechanics ====
  * Provides the highest level of protection among basic armor types
  * Significantly slows the hero's movement
  * Requires high strength to use effectively without movement penalty
  * Constructed from large metal plates joined together

==== Availability ====
  * Found in high-level treasure rooms
  * Appears in advanced shops
  * Generally found later in the dungeon

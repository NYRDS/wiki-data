====== Quests ======

Quests in Remixed Dungeon are special missions or objectives that players can complete for rewards.

==== Main Quests ====
  * **Ghost's Quest**: Help the ghost in the sewers by obtaining a [[rpd:rat_skull_item|Giant Rat Skull]] from the [[rpd:fetid_rat_mob|Fetid Rat]]
  * **Imp's Quest**: Help the Imp in the sewers by crafting a special ring
  * **Blacksmith's Quest**: Help the Blacksmith in the prison by finding two [[rpd:dwarf_token_item|Dwarf Tokens]]
  * **Wandmaker's Quest**: Help the Wandmaker by finding the three elemental emblems to upgrade wands

==== Side Quests ====
  * Various optional quests that provide unique rewards
  * Often involve finding specific items or defeating special enemies

==== Quest Rewards ====
  * Unique items or equipment
  * Special abilities or knowledge
  * Access to new areas or mechanics
  * Currency or valuable resources

==== Quest Mechanics ====
  * Quests are typically initiated by NPCs in specific dungeon areas
  * Most quests require defeating a special enemy or finding specific items
  * Quest completion is tracked through the game's quest system

==== Strategy ====
  * Complete quests for valuable rewards that can significantly impact gameplay
  * Prioritize quest objectives when exploring areas where quest NPCs are located
  * Quests often require venturing off the main path but provide significant benefits

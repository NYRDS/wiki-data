====== Spider Mind ======

{{ rpd:images:spider_mind_mob.png|Spider Mind }}

The **Spider Mind** is a special type of spider in Remixed Dungeon that has psychic abilities and can control other spiders.

==== Mob Overview ====
  * **Type:** Enemy Mob
  * **Category:** Spiders
  * **Level:** Spider level (typically found in Spider Nest areas)
  * **Special Ability:** Mind control over other spiders

==== Statistics ====
  * **HP:** High HP for spider type
  * **Attack:** Moderate attack power
  * **Defense:** Moderate defense rating
  * **EXP for Kill:** Moderate experience reward

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Mind Control:** Can command nearby spiders to attack your target
  * **Group Tactics:** Works with other spiders to overwhelm the player
  * **Web Casting:** May slow player movement with web attacks

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus fire on the Spider Mind to break the control over other spiders
  * Use area-of-effect attacks to hit multiple spiders at once
  * Be aware of its ability to command other spiders during combat

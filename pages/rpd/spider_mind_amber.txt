====== SpiderMindAmber ======

{{ rpd:images:spidermindamber_sprite.png|Spider Mind Amber }}

==== Overview ====
The **Spider Mind Amber** is a special variant of spider found in the Spider levels. It has unique psychic-based abilities and behaves differently from other spiders.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP**: 30
  * **Defense Skill**: 5
  * **Attack Skill**: 15
  * **Damage**: 1-1 (physical damage)
  * **Damage Reduction**: 10
  * **EXP for Kill**: 9
  * **Max Level**: 10
  * **Base Speed**: 1.0

==== Abilities ====
  * **Ranged Attack**: Can only attack at range using mental abilities
  * **Mental Buffs**: When hitting enemies, has a chance to apply one of several mental debuffs:
    * **Blindness**: Reduces enemy accuracy
    * **Slow**: Reduces enemy movement and attack speed
    * **Weakness**: Reduces enemy damage output
  * **Behavior**: When hunting, tries to get further from target instead of closer

==== Drops ====
  * **Mystery Meat**: Has a 6.7% chance to drop Mystery Meat when killed

==== Special Properties ====
  * Immune to various status effects due to being a special mob
  * Uses ranged mental attacks instead of physical attacks
  * Moves away from enemies when in hunting mode
  * Part of the Spider level mob family

====== Levitation Buff ======

{{ rpd:images:buff_Levitation.png|Levitation Buff }}

==== Description ====
The Levitation buff allows the character to fly temporarily, enabling movement over traps and special terrain that would normally impede progress. While levitating, the character is immune to most ground-based effects.

==== Effects ====
  * Allows movement over traps without triggering them
  * Allows movement over special terrain that normally blocks passage
  * Character floats above ground-level effects
  * Immune to most ground-based interactions while active

==== Duration ====
  * The duration of the Levitation buff varies depending on the source that applied it
  * When the buff expires, the character returns to ground level

==== Sources ====
  * Potions of Levitation can grant this effect
  * Some spells and equipment may provide this buff
  * Various other items in the game may grant temporary levitation

==== Counters ====
  * The effect naturally expires after a certain duration
  * Some game mechanics may end the effect prematurely

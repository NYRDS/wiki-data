====== Mob ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_Rat.png|Mob }}

**Mobs** (Mobiles) are mobile entities in the dungeon, including both enemies and neutral creatures. Most mobs are hostile and will attack the hero when approached or seen.

==== Common Mobs ====
  * [[rpd:rat_mob|Rat]] - Weak but numerous enemies in the sewers
  * [[rpd:gnoll_mob|Gnoll]] - Various types of warrior and shaman enemies
  * [[rpd:crab_mob|Crab]] - High defense enemies in the sewers
  * [[rpd:golem_mob|Golem]] - Strong magical constructs
  * [[rpd:elemental_mob|Elemental]] - Fire, ice, shock, and chaos elementals
  * [[rpd:spider_mob|Spider]] - Various types of web-spinning creatures

==== Mob Stats ====
  * **Level** - Determines relative power compared to hero
  * **HP** - Health points before defeated
  * **Damage** - Attack power against hero
  * **Defense** - Reduces damage from hero attacks
  * **Speed** - Determines how often the mob acts

==== AI Behavior ====
  * Most mobs will pursue and attack the hero when aware
  * Some mobs have special behaviors (fleeing, summoning allies, etc.)
  * Mobs may have special abilities or resistances

==== Strategy ====
  * Learn enemy resistances and weaknesses
  * Use terrain to fight enemies in advantageous positions
  * Identify strong enemies before engaging
  * Some enemies are better avoided than fought

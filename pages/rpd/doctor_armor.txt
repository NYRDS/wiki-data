====== Doctor Coat ======

The **Doctor Coat** (internal name: Doctor Armor) is the class-specific armor for the [[rpd:doctor|Doctor]] class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. It's designed to support the Doctor's expertise in harmful potions and toxic abilities.

==== Description ====
Like other class armors, the Doctor Coat has unique special abilities accessible through the Special action. It specifically supports the Doctor's toxic-themed gameplay style.

==== Special Ability ====
The Doctor Coat likely features disease or poison-based special effects, possibly including:
* Disease spreading or toxic area effects
* Enhanced control over harmful gases or toxins
* Abilities related to the Doctor's plague-themed powers

==== Strategy ====
The Doctor Coat complements the Doctor's core abilities of using harmful substances strategically. It's designed to enhance the character's toxic combat approach and environmental control through harmful effects.

==== Class Restriction ====
This armor can only be effectively used by the Doctor class. If worn by other classes, it may not function properly or could have negative effects.

====== High Grass ======

**High Grass** is a terrain feature found throughout the dungeon that provides special properties and interactions.

==== Properties ====
  * **Movement**: Characters can move through high grass normally
  * **Concealment**: High grass can hide items, traps, and sometimes enemies
  * **Exploration**: Stepping into high grass reveals what it conceals
  * **Visibility**: Does not typically block line of sight

==== Contents ====
  * **Hidden Items**: Potions, scrolls, or other items may be concealed in high grass
  * **Traps**: Some traps may be hidden in high grass waiting for a character to step on them
  * **Enemies**: Certain enemies may be concealed until discovered
  * **Seeds**: High grass areas may contain seeds that can be harvested

==== Interaction ====
  * Moving over high grass reveals any items or hazards it was concealing
  * Some class abilities may interact specially with high grass
  * High grass may be a source of seeds for alchemy

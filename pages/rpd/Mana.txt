====== Mana ======

==== Description ====
Mana is a magical energy resource used by certain classes and items in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Mechanics ====
  * Certain classes use mana to cast spells
  * Mana can be restored using [[rpd:mana_potion|Mana Potions]]
  * Some classes regenerate mana over time
  * Different spells consume different amounts of mana

==== Classes Using Mana ====
  * [[rpd:mage|Mages]] use mana for elemental spells
  * [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]] use mana for death spells
  * Other magical classes may use mana for special abilities

==== Items Using Mana ====
  * Various magical items and equipment may consume mana
  * Some items provide mana regeneration bonuses

==== Notes ====
Managing mana effectively is crucial for spellcasters. Running out of mana prevents the use of magical abilities until it's restored.

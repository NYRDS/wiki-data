====== Spirit of Pain ======

{{ rpd:images:spirit_of_pain_mob.png|Spirit of Pain }}

==== Description ====
The **Spirit of Pain** is an undead spirit summoned by the [[rpd:heart_of_darkness_item|Heart of Darkness]] artifact when the wearer takes damage. These spirits fight on behalf of their summoner but have a limited lifespan.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 80
  * **Attack/Defense:** 30
  * **Damage:** 5-10
  * **Damage Reduction:** 20
  * **Speed:** Normal
  * **EXP:** 0 (no experience when killed)
  * **Lifespan:** Takes 6 self-damage each turn, dies after a few turns

==== Abilities ====
  * **Flying:** Can pass over terrain that blocks other creatures
  * **Temporary Nature:** Has limited lifespan due to continuous self-damage
  * **Loyalty:** Fights for the owner of the Heart of Darkness

==== Summoning ====
  * Automatically summoned by [[rpd:heart_of_darkness_item|Heart of Darkness]] when wearer takes damage
  * Appears in an adjacent empty cell to the wearer
  * Temporary ally that will fight enemies

==== Strategy ====
  * Provides additional combat strength when taking damage
  * Spirits are temporary but can serve as distractions
  * More effective in situations where taking damage is likely
  * Plan tactics knowing spirits will expire after a few turns

====== Spirit of Pain ======

{{ rpd:images:spiritofpain_sprite.png|Spirit of Pain }}

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 80
  * **Defense:** 30
  * **Attack:** 30
  * **Damage:** 5-10
  * **DR:** 20
  * **EXP:** 0
  * **Carcass:** Never drops carcass
  * **Movement:** Flies (can pass through walls)
  * **Special:** Loses 6 HP each turn (suicide behavior)

==== Description ====
A shard of living darkness. Spirit of Pain is a unique mob that appears as a small, flying dark entity. It possesses the ability to fly through walls, making it difficult to avoid in tight spaces.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Suicide Tactic:** Loses 6 HP each turn, causing rapid self-destruction
  * **Flight:** Can move through walls, making it difficult to trap or avoid
  * **No Experience:** Provides no experience when defeated
  * **No Carcass:** Never drops a carcass when defeated
  * **High Damage Resistance:** Has significant damage reduction (DR: 20)

==== Location ====
Spirits of Pain are typically encountered:
  * Summoned by [[rpd:treacherous_spirit|Treacherous Spirits]] during combat
  * In the [[rpd:guts|Guts]] level as regular enemies
  * Occasionally as a result of certain cursed items or effects

==== Tactics ====
  * Due to their suicide behavior, Spirits of Pain will quickly come to melee range
  * Their high damage resistance makes them hard to kill quickly
  * Position carefully when fighting Treacherous Spirits to avoid multiple Spirits of Pain at once
  * Ranged attacks are effective as they can't block at range
  * Be cautious in narrow corridors where their flying ability is most problematic

====== Spirit of Pain ======

{{ :rpd:spirit_of_pain.png?nolink&50|}}

Spirit of Pain is a creature made of living darkness, described as a shard of living darkness. It's part of the dark spirit family of enemies in the dungeon.

==== Description ====
A shard of living darkness. Spirits of Pain are smaller and potentially more numerous than [[rpd:treacherous_spirit|Treacherous Spirits]], but still possess dangerous dark magical properties.

==== Combat ====
  * **Appearance:** A dark, shard-like creature
  * **Health:** Moderate for a spirit creature
  * **Attack:** Pain-based dark attacks
  * **Abilities:**
    * May inflict status effects related to pain or darkness
    * Potentially able to move through dark spaces easily
    * Dark resistance/immunity properties

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Spirit Nature:** Has standard spirit properties
  * **Dark Properties:** Connected to dark magic and possibly pain effects
  * **Size:** Described as a "shard", suggesting smaller size
  * **Aggression:** Likely aggressive to adventurers

==== Location ====
Spirits of Pain can be found in:
  * Darker areas of the dungeon
  * Areas with other dark creatures
  * Possibly near locations connected to the [[rpd:treacherous_spirit|Treacherous Spirit]]
  * Undead or cursed areas

==== Tactics ====
  * Holy or light-based attacks are potentially very effective
  * Be prepared for pain-related status effects
  * Multiple spirits may appear together
  * May be weaker individually than [[rpd:treacherous_spirit|Treacherous Spirits]]

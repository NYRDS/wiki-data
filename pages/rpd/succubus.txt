====== succubus ======

{{ rpd:images:succubus_sprite.png|Succubus Sprite }}

**Succubus** is a powerful magic-using mob found in the Halls level of Remixed Dungeon.

==== Description ====
The Succubus is a seductive demon that uses charm magic to control its enemies. With its ability to blink and charm targets, it poses a significant threat to heroes who are unprepared for magical combat.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level:** 25 (appears in Halls level)
  * **HP:** 80
  * **Defense:** 25
  * **Attack:** 40 (base attack skill)
  * **Damage:** 15-25
  * **Armor:** 10 (Damage Reduction)
  * **EXP:** 12 when killed
  * **Carcass:** No (doesn't leave a corpse for Cook)

==== Abilities ====
  * **Charm:** On successful hit, has a 33% chance to charm the target for 2-5 turns, causing them to be unable to attack the Succubus
  * **Blink:** If more than 2 tiles away and in line of sight, can blink to the target's location with a 5-turn cooldown
  * **Resistance:** Immune to Leech enchantments
  * **Immunity:** Immune to Sleep status effect
  * **Loot:** Has a 5% chance to drop Scroll of Lullaby

==== Strategy ====
  * **Approach:** Be careful when approaching from a distance, as the Succubus can blink to you
  * **Charm:** Be prepared to be charmed, which will prevent you from attacking the Succubus
  * **Ranged Combat:** If possible, try to fight from close range to minimize the blink ability's effectiveness
  * **Dispel:** Use items that remove charm effects to free yourself if charmed
  * **Equipment:** Be aware that your gear effectiveness might be limited if you're charmed

====== Chaos Crystal ======

==== Description ====
You feel streams of energy, running through your hands while you holding it. Chaotic powers flow through this crystal. Maybe it might be useful.

==== Use ====
Chaos Crystal can be fused with various items to create chaotic versions of them:

  * **With weapons:** Creates chaotic weapons with special properties
  * **With wands:** Creates [[rpd:chaos_staff|Chaos Staff]]
  * **With scrolls:** Creates chaotic versions of scrolls
  * **With armor:** Creates [[rpd:chaos_armor|Chaos Armor]]
  * **With shields:** Creates [[rpd:chaos_shield|Chaos Shield]]

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be used with the "FUSE" action
  * When fused with items, creates powerful chaotic variants
  * Different item types produce different chaotic effects

==== Notes ====
Chaos Crystals are valuable components for creating some of the most powerful equipment in the game, though the chaotic nature may introduce unpredictable effects.

====== Ignite ======

**Ignite** is a spell available to Mages in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Ignite sets a target or area on fire, dealing damage over time and potentially spreading to nearby flammable objects. This spell is particularly effective against enemies vulnerable to fire.

==== Stats ====
* Mana Cost: Moderate
* Range: Medium
* Effect: Deals fire damage over time

==== Usage ====
This spell is particularly useful for:
  * Fighting enemies weak to fire
  * Lighting dark areas
  * Combusting flammable objects
  * Creating environmental advantages
  * Weakening groups of enemies with burning damage

==== Classes ====
  * **Mage**: Natural affinity, part of elemental magic skill tree
  * **Other classes**: May learn through class-specific abilities

==== Strategy ====
Ignite works well in combination with other fire-based items and abilities. Consider using it in areas with flammable objects to create additional environmental hazards.

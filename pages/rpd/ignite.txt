====== Ignite ======

**Ignite** is an Elemental Affinity spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Ignite sets a targeted cell on fire, creating a fire blob that spreads and damages any character that enters it.

==== Stats ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Elemental
  * **Targeting:** Cell
  * **Level:** 2
  * **Mana Cost:** 2
  * **Special Effects:** creates fire

==== Usage ====
The Ignite spell can be used for:
  * Creating fire in targeted locations
  * Damaging enemies that stand in the fire
  * Burning flammable terrain and objects
  * Strategic environmental control

==== Classes ====
Classes that can use this spell include:
  * Classes with Elemental affinity (e.g., [[rpd:spell_affinities|Elemental class]])
  * Other classes that gain access through special means

==== Strategy ====
How to effectively use the Ignite spell:
  * Use to block passages or create barriers against enemies
  * Effective in areas with flammable grass or objects that can spread the fire
  * Good for slowing down enemy movement by blocking their paths
  * Consider the mana cost versus other spells at level 2

==== Notes ====
  * The fire created will spread to adjacent cells if they contain flammable objects
  * The fire effect lasts for several turns before extinguishing
  * Fire damage is applied to any character that enters the burning cell

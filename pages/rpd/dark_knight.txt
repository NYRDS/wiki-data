====== Dark Knight ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_DeathKnight.png|Death Knight }}

This page refers to the **Death Knight** and **Dread Knight** mobs found in the Necropolis area of Remixed Dungeon.

==== Death Knight ====
The **Death Knight** is an undead mob found in the necropolis. It has a chance to deal double damage on attack.

===== Mechanics =====
  * **HP**: 35
  * **Defense**: 12
  * **Attack**: 15
  * **Damage**: 7-14
  * **Armor**: 7
  * **Experience**: 7
  * **Max Level**: 15
  * **Type**: Undead
  * **Special**: 1 in 7 chance to deal double damage on attack

==== Dread Knight ====
The **Dread Knight** is a rare variant of the Death Knight, also found in the necropolis. It has enhanced stats and additional abilities compared to the regular Death Knight.

===== Mechanics =====
  * **HP**: 40
  * **Defense**: 15
  * **Attack**: 17
  * **Damage**: 8-16
  * **Armor**: 12
  * **Experience**: 8
  * **Max Level**: 15
  * **Type**: Undead
  * **Special 1**: 1 in 4 chance to deal double damage on attack
  * **Special 2**: 1 in 10 chance to cause stun on attack

==== Strategy ====
  * Both variants can deal double damage occasionally, making them dangerous
  * Dread Knights are rarer but more powerful than regular Death Knights
  * They spawn in the Necropolis area
  * The stun effect of Dread Knights can be particularly problematic during combat

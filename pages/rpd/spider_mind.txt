====== Spider Mind ======

{{ rpd:images:spider_mind.png|Spider Mind }}

The **Spider Mind** (also known as **Psi-Spider**) is an enemy mob in Remixed Dungeon. This creature is a type of spider that possesses psionic abilities, allowing it to support other spiders in its vicinity with beneficial buffs.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP**: 5
  * **HT**: 5
  * **Defense Skill**: 1
  * **Attack Skill**: 10
  * **Damage**: 0-0 (non-damaging attacker)
  * **Armor**: 0 DR
  * **EXP for Kill**: 6
  * **Maximum Level**: 9
  * **Speed**: 1.0x

==== Abilities ====
  * **Ranged Attack**: Can only attack at range using a special zap attack
  * **Buff Allies**: When attacking, randomly applies beneficial buffs to nearby friendly mobs, including: Speed, Barkskin, Blessed, Health, Armor, Mana Shield, or Fury
  * **Cowardly Behavior**: Tends to move away from the player character when hunting
  * **Support Role**: Functions as a support unit by enhancing nearby spider allies

==== Occurrence ====
  * **Primary Location**: Found in the Spider Nest level where other spider variants appear
  * **Spawn Frequency**: Less common than regular spiders
  * **Group Behavior**: Often found with other spider types it supports

==== Strategy ====
  * The Spider Mind itself does no direct damage but can buff nearby enemies
  * Prioritize elimination to prevent it from enhancing other spiders
  * Be prepared for increased threat from nearby spiders due to its buffs
  * When it attacks, it will buff one random friendly mob in its vicinity

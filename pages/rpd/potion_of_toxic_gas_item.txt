====== Potion of Toxic Gas ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_toxic_gas_item.png|Potion of Toxic Gas }}

The **Potion of Toxic Gas** is an uncommon potion in Remixed Dungeon that releases a cloud of poisonous gas when shattered.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Potion
  * **Rarity:** Uncommon
  * **Special Effect:** Creates a cloud of toxic gas
  * **Usage:** Single use item (consumed upon drinking or shattering)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Gas Cloud:** When shattered on the ground, creates a cloud of toxic gas that affects all creatures in the area
  * **Poison Effect:** The gas inflicts poison damage over time to affected creatures
  * **Shattering:** Can be intentionally shattered by throwing at the floor or at enemies
  * **Drinking:** When drunk, creates a toxic gas cloud around the hero (harmful to the hero too)

==== Strategy ====
  * Useful against groups of enemies, especially in corridors or small rooms
  * Can be used to clear out weak enemies from rooms before entering
  * Be careful when using in enclosed spaces as it may affect the hero as well
  * Good for escaping from dangerous situations by creating a gas barrier
  * Effective when facing enemies that rely on surrounding the hero

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found throughout dungeon levels
  * Available in shops at moderate prices (when identified)
  * Dropped by various enemies
  * Found in containers and special rooms
  * Starting item for the [[rpd:doctor_class|Doctor]] class (known)

==== Effect ====
When shattered, the Potion of Toxic Gas creates a cloud of poison gas that damages creatures within it over time.

According to the game's string resources: "Uncorking or shattering this pressurized glass will cause its contents to explode into a deadly cloud of toxic green gas. You might choose to fling this potion at distant enemies instead of uncorking it by hand."

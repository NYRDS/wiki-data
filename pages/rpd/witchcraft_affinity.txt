====== Witchcraft Affinity ======

**Witchcraft Affinity** is a magic affinity in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that provides access to a variety of spells focused on utility, healing, and offensive abilities.

==== Classes ====
Classes that use Witchcraft Affinity:
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Primary affinity
  * [[rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]] - Primary affinity

==== Spells ====
Spells associated with Witchcraft Affinity:
  * [[rpd:roar_spell|Roar]] - Intimidates enemies
  * [[rpd:lightning_bolt_spell|Lightning Bolt]] - Deals damage in a line
  * [[rpd:heal_spell|Heal]] - Restores health to target
  * [[rpd:order_spell|Order]] - Improves accuracy and evasion

==== Mechanics ====
Witchcraft spells typically:
  * Have moderate mana costs
  * Provide a balance of offensive and utility effects
  * Are effective for both direct damage and support roles

==== Strategy ====
  * Utilize for balanced spell selection including healing
  * Good for versatile gameplay approaches
  * Effective for supporting pet characters

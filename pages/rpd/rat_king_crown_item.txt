====== Rat King Crown ======

{{ rpd:images:item_RatKingCrown.png|Rat King Crown }}

The **Rat King Crown** is a unique quest item in Remixed Dungeon that serves as both a reward and a key to access the Rat King's special lair.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Quest item
  * **Weight**: 1
  * **Price**: Cannot be sold
  * **Identified**: Yes

==== Description ====
The Rat King Crown is a distinctive golden crown that once belonged to the Rat King. It has magical properties that teleport the bearer to the Rat King's lair when used.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found randomly in dungeon floors 1-4, typically in chests or as mob drops
  * Once found, using the crown teleports the player to the Rat King's special level
  * This begins the Rat King quest

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Teleportation**: Using the crown instantly teleports the player to the Rat King's lair
  * **Quest Item**: Essential for completing the Rat King quest
  * **One-time Use**: After teleporting to the Rat King's lair, the crown is consumed
  * **Unique**: Only one crown can be found per dungeon run

==== Quest Mechanics ====
  * Finding and using the crown starts the Rat King encounter
  * The Rat King quest rewards the player with the [[rpd:rat_skull|Giant Rat Skull]]
  * This quest is part of the early game progression system

==== Strategy ====
  * Save the crown until you're ready to face the Rat King
  * Prepare for a unique encounter as the Rat King is not immediately hostile
  * Be ready for a difficult fight once the Rat King becomes hostile

====== Items ======

{{ rpd:images:items_illustration.png|Items }}

**Items** are objects that can be found, purchased, or crafted in Remixed Dungeon. They include equipment, consumables, and special objects that aid the hero in their journey through the dungeon.

==== Item Categories ====
  * **Weapons**: Tools for combat, increasing damage output
  * **Armor**: Protects against physical attacks
  * **Potions**: Liquid items with various beneficial effects
  * **Scrolls**: Parchments with magical effects
  * **Rings**: Accessories that provide permanent bonuses
  * **Wands**: Magical tools with limited charges
  * **Food**: Restores health and satisfies hunger
  * **Keys**: Unlock special doors and containers
  * **Artifacts**: Powerful special items with unique properties
  * **Seeds**: Plant materials for alchemy or special effects
  * **Bombs**: Explosive items for damage or utility
  * **Missile Weapons**: Thrown or fired ranged ammunition
  * **Spell Books**: Sources for learning new spells

==== Equipment Items ====
  * **Weapons**: Increase damage in combat
    * [[rpd:swords|Swords]] - Balanced melee weapons
    * [[rpd:axes|Axes]] - High-damage, slower weapons
    * [[rpd:maces|Maces]] - Moderate damage with special properties
    * [[rpd:daggers|Daggers]] - Fast, low-damage weapons
    * [[rpd:spears|Spears]] - Reach weapons
    * [[rpd:bows|Bows]] - Ranged weapons using arrows
  * **Armor**: Reduces incoming physical damage
    * [[rpd:cloth_armor_item|Cloth Armor]] - Tier 1 armor
    * [[rpd:leather_armor_item|Leather Armor]] - Tier 2 armor
    * [[rpd:mail_armor_item|Mail Armor]] - Tier 3 armor
    * [[rpd:scale_armor_item|Scale Armor]] - Tier 4 armor
    * [[rpd:plate_armor_item|Plate Armor]] - Tier 5 armor

==== Consumable Items ====
  * **Potions**: Liquid items with beneficial effects
    * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Restores health
    * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] - Increases strength
    * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]] - Temporarily turn invisible
    * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]] - Permanently increase strength and health
    * [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]] - Provides experience points
  * **Scrolls**: Magical parchments with various effects
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]] - Identifies items
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_teleportation_item|Scroll of Teleportation]] - Teleports to another location
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_magic_mapping_item|Scroll of Magic Mapping]] - Reveals the level layout
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scroll of Upgrade]] - Improves an item
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_recharging_item|Scroll of Recharging]] - Recharges wands and artifacts

==== Special Items ====
  * **Rings**: Accessories providing passive bonuses
    * [[rpd:ring_of_strength_item|Ring of Strength]] - Increases strength
    * [[rpd:ring_of_detection_item|Ring of Detection]] - Improves identification
    * [[rpd:ring_of_haste_item|Ring of Haste]] - Increases movement speed
    * [[rpd:ring_of_accuracy_item|Ring of Accuracy]] - Improves accuracy
    * [[rpd:ring_of_elements_item|Ring of Elements]] - Provides resistances
  * **Wands**: Magical items with charges
    * [[rpd:wand_of_lightning_item|Wand of Lightning]] - Deals electrical damage
    * [[rpd:wand_of_fireblast_item|Wand of Fireblast]] - Creates area fire damage
    * [[rpd:wand_of_frost_item|Wand of Frost]] - Deals cold damage and slows
    * [[rpd:wand_of_teleportation_item|Wand of Teleportation]] - Teleports targets
    * [[rpd:wand_of_disintegration_item|Wand of Disintegration]] - Penetrating damage
  * **Artifacts**: Powerful unique items
    * [[rpd:ankh_item|Ankh]] - Allows resurrection after death
    * [[rpd:dried_rose_item|Dried Rose]] - Summons a ghost to assist
    * [[rpd:timekeeper_hourglass_item|Timekeeper's Hourglass]] - Manipulate time
    * [[rpd:cauldron_of_brewing_item|Cauldron of Brewing]] - Alchemy crafting

==== Item Identification ====
  * **Identified Items**: Show full stats and effects
  * **Unidentified Items**: Show only basic information
  * **Identification Methods**:
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]] - Primary identification
    * [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]] - For equipment
    * **Wearing/Using** - Gradual identification through use
  * **Cursed Items**: Identified cursed items show as "cursed"

==== Item Upgrading ====
  * **Scroll of Upgrade**: Improves item quality
  * **Weapon Upgrades**: Increase damage and special effects
  * **Armor Upgrades**: Improve defense and special effects
  * **Limitations**: Equipment has upgrade limits
  * **Risks**: Cursed items become more dangerous when upgraded

==== Item Management ====
  * **Inventory Limits**: Limited carrying capacity
  * **Quickslots**: 4 slots for quick access to items
  * **Belongings**: Main inventory, weapon slot, armor slot, and misc slots
  * **Thieves**: Enemies that steal items from your inventory
  * **Item Destruction**: Some items can be destroyed by certain effects

==== Item Rarity ====
  * **Common Items**: Found frequently throughout the dungeon
  * **Uncommon Items**: Less common but still regularly encountered
  * **Rare Items**: Powerful items found infrequently
  * **Legendary Items**: Extremely powerful unique items
  * **Dungeon Level**: Item quality generally increases with dungeon depth

==== Item Mechanics ====
  * **Curses**: Some items are cursed and have negative effects
  * **Enchantments**: Weapons and armor can be enchanted for special effects
  * **Glypghs**: Armor can be enchanted with protective properties
  * **Charges**: Many special items have limited uses
  * **Durability**: Equipment degrades with use

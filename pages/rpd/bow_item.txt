====== Bow Item ======

Bows are ranged weapons in Remixed Dungeon that require arrows to function. They offer high damage at range but require ammunition.

==== Description ====
Bows are ranged weapons that fire arrows at enemies. Unlike other weapons, bows require ammunition in the form of arrows to be effective. They offer high damage at range, allowing for tactical combat approaches.

  * [[rpd:long_bow_item|Long Bow]] - Higher damage bow option
  * [[rpd:composite_bow_item|Composite Bow]] - Advanced composite construction

==== Mechanics ====
  * Bows require arrows to attack at range
  * Different bows have different damage ranges and accuracy
  * Bow damage calculations may include special properties
  * Bows can be enchanted like other weapons
  * Some classes may have special interactions with bows

==== Ammunition ====
  * Bows use various types of arrows as ammunition
  * [[rpd:arrow_item|Arrows]] - Basic ammunition
  * Special arrows like poison or fire arrows provide additional effects
  * Ammunition must be carried separately from the bow

==== Class Interactions ====
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] and [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] classes may have special bow interactions
  * Some classes may have affinity for bow-related [[rpd:spells|spells]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Maintain adequate arrow supplies when using bows
  * Bows allow safe engagement from a distance
  * Different bows are optimal for different situations
  * Consider enchantments that complement your playstyle

==== Enchantments ====
Like other weapons, bows can be enchanted with various [[rpd:weapon_enchantments|Weapon Enchantments]] that modify their properties and effects.

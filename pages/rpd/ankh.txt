The Ankh is a powerful artifact in Remixed Dungeon that allows the player to return from the dead once.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Very Rare
  * **Appearance**: Once per game
  * **Weight**: 1

==== Description ====
The Ankh is a symbol of life and rebirth. When the player dies while carrying an Ankh, they will be resurrected with full health and a single broken Ankh instead of dying permanently. The broken Ankh provides no further benefit.

==== Usage ====
  * When your health reaches 0, the Ankh will automatically activate
  * Restores full health and removes the Ankh from your inventory
  * Replaces the Ankh with a broken Ankh (no benefit)
  * Allows one chance to continue playing after death

==== Acquisition ====
  * Rarely found in the dungeon
  * Usually appears in more dangerous areas
  * May be dropped by powerful enemies or found in special rooms

==== Special Notes ====
  * Provides a one-time resurrection
  * Essential for high-risk strategies
  * Once broken, provides no benefit
  * Only one Ankh can be carried at a time

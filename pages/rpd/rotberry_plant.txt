====== Rotberry Plant ======

{{ rpd:images:levelObject_Rotberry.png|Rotberry Plant }}

==== Description ====
The Rotberry plant grows into a special plant that eventually produces a Rotberry. In the original Pixel Dungeon, Rotberry plants would grow into Rotberries that could be harvested. When touched, the Rotberry plant provides special effects related to its growth mechanic.

==== Effects ====
  * Begins the process of growing a Rotberry
  * Provides unique mechanics related to Rotberry growth
  * In some versions, provides an experience benefit
  * The plant disappears after being touched in most implementations

==== Mechanics ====
  * Rotberry plants can be found growing in the dungeon
  * They can also be grown by planting [[rpd:rotberry_seed_item|Rotberry Seeds]]
  * The plant transforms over time in some implementations
  * Provides access to unique items or mechanics when fully grown

==== Strategy ====
  * Rotberry plants are valuable for the unique items they provide
  * The Rotberry fruit provides useful items when fully grown
  * Worth preserving if you have the time and resources
  * The resulting items can be quite valuable

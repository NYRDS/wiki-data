====== Magic Torch ======

**Magic Torch** is an utility spell available to several classes in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Magic Torch creates a temporary light source that illuminates the surrounding area. This spell is particularly useful in dark dungeon levels where visibility is limited.

==== Stats ====
* Mana Cost: Low
* Range: Self
* Effect: Creates a light source for a duration

==== Usage ====
This spell is particularly useful for:
  * Navigating dark dungeon levels
  * Revealing hidden traps
  * Exploring unknown areas safely
  * Improving visibility during combat

==== Classes ====
  * **Mage**: Available through elemental magic affinity
  * **Other classes**: May learn through class-specific abilities

==== Strategy ====
Magic Torch is especially valuable on levels with reduced visibility. It can be used strategically to reveal dangerous areas before approaching them.

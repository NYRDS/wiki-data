====== Pets and Companions ======

Pets and companions are friendly creatures that follow and assist the player character during their dungeon adventure. These can be summoned by various items, abilities, or spells.

==== Summoned Pets ====
  * **Deathling**: Summons by the **[[rpd:summon_deathling_spell|Summon Deathling]]** spell
  * **Beast**: Summons by the **[[rpd:summon_beast_spell|Summon Beast]]** spell

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Loyalty**: Pets remain loyal to the player and will fight enemies
  * **Temporary**: Most pets have a limited duration before disappearing
  * **Combat Assistance**: Pets can tank damage and provide additional offensive capability
  * **Follow Behavior**: Pets follow the player character through the dungeon

==== Strategy ====
  * Use pets strategically during difficult encounters to provide additional combat support
  * Pets can help with tanking damage from multiple enemies
  * Different pets may have different stats and abilities depending on hero level
  * Consider using pets in combination with other tactical items for maximum effectiveness

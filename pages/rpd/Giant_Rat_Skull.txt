====== Giant Rat Skull ======

==== Description ====
It could be a nice hunting trophy, but it smells too bad to place it on a wall. On the other hand, it demonstrates that owner is capable of killing rats, even big and smelly ones.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Trophy Value:** Serves as proof of defeating a giant rat
  * **Possible Effects:** May have special properties when used
  * **Pungent Odor:** Has an unpleasant smell

==== Uses ====
  * May be required for specific quests or achievements
  * Could serve as a component for certain crafting recipes
  * Might provide benefits or effects when carried

==== Notes ====
The Giant Rat Skull is a trophy from defeating a significant enemy in the dungeon. Despite its odor, it represents a successful hunt.

====== larva ======

{{ rpd:images:larva_sprite.png|Larva Sprite }}

==== Description ====
The **Larva** is a mob found in the Guts level of Remixed Pixel Dungeon. It is a transforming creature that will evolve into another type of mob after gaining enough levels.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP**: 120
  * **Defense**: 20
  * **Attack**: 30
  * **Damage**: 25-30
  * **Defense Rating**: 8
  * **EXP**: 0 (no experience gained on kill)
  * **Level**: 1 (starts at level 1)

==== Behavior ====
  * The Larva will evolve into another mob after reaching level 2 during its turn
  * When evolving, it will transform into either a [[rpd:scorpio|Scorpio]], [[rpd:worm|Worm]], or [[rpd:eye|Eye]]
  * The transformation is accompanied by shadow particles and a cursed sound effect

==== Location ====
  * Found in the [[rpd:guts|Guts]] level

====== Spider Queen ======

{{ rpd:images:spider_queen_mob.png|Spider Queen }}

==== Description ====
**Spider Queen** is a boss-level mob in Remixed Dungeon. She is the matriarch of the spider creatures found in the Spider Level.

* **HP**: 120
* **HT**: 120 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L8|SpiderQueen.java:8]])
* **Defense Skill**: 18 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L9|SpiderQueen.java:9]])
* **Attack Skill**: 21 (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L10|SpiderQueen.java:10]])

==== Abilities ====
* **Spider summoning**: Randomly spawns Spider Eggs during combat (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L28-L32|SpiderQueen.java:28-32]])
* **Poison attack**: Has a 50% chance to apply poison to enemies on hit (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L36-L39|SpiderQueen.java:36-39]])
* **Fire immunity**: Immune to fire-based attacks and burning effects (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L43-L44|SpiderQueen.java:43-44]])
* **Retreat behavior**: When below 50% HP, attempts to stay at range during combat (source: [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/nyrds/pixeldungeon/mobs/spiders/SpiderQueen.java#L46-L51|SpiderQueen.java:46-51]])

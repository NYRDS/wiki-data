====== Spider Queen ======

{{ rpd:images:spider_queen_mob.png|Spider Queen }}

==== Description ====
**Spider Queen** is a boss-level mob in Remixed Dungeon. She is the matriarch of the spider creatures found in the Spider Level.

==== Stats ====
* **HP**: High
* **HT**: High
* **Defense Skill**: High
* **Attack Skill**: High
* **Damage**: High range
* **DR (Damage Resistance)**: Moderate to high
* **EXP for Kill**: High

==== Abilities ====
* **Spider summoning**: Can summon other spider creatures to aid in combat
* **Web attacks**: Uses web-based attacks to immobilize enemies
* **Poison resistance**: Highly resistant to poison-based attacks
* **High mobility**: Can move quickly across the battlefield

==== Special Mechanics ====
* Controls the spider nest and its inhabitants
* May have special mechanics related to the spider level environment
* Summoned creatures may provide additional challenges during the fight

==== Drops ====
* **[[rpd:spider_queen_carapace_item|Spider Queen Carapace]]** - A valuable armor piece
* Other valuable spider-related items
* Potential treasure

==== Location ====
* Found in the Spider Level as the boss of that area

The Rogue is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He focuses on stealth and mobility, with a focus on exploring and discovering secrets.

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 10
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * +1 Ring of Shadows (identified)
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Cloak spell (in quickslot)
    * Scroll of Magic Mapping (known item)
  * **Magic Affinity**: Rogue

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Rogue starts with an identified Ring of Shadows at +1, providing invisibility bonuses.
  * The Cloak spell is available in the quickslot for stealth.
  * Starts with a known Scroll of Magic Mapping for dungeon exploration.

==== Advantages ====
  * Enhanced stealth with Ring of Shadows
  * Good at finding secrets with Scroll of Magic Mapping
  * The Cloak spell provides escape and stealth options
  * Higher success rate with traps and locks

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Lower direct combat effectiveness than Warrior
  * Ring of Shadows requires energy to be effective

The **Rogue** is one of the starting playable classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is a master of stealth and cunning, specializing in stealth, detection, and agility. Known for high evasion and detection capabilities.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Focus:** Stealth and agility
  * **Special Ability:** Enhanced detection and high evasion
  * **Playstyle:** Evasive combat with detection advantages

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Weapon:** Dagger (fast but low damage weapon)
  * **Ring:** Ring of Shadows +1
  * **Armor:** Cloth Armor with 12 points of damage absorption
  * **Food:** Additional food ration
  * **Quickslot:** Cloak spell

==== Class Perks ====
  * **Ring of Shadows +1:** Starts with a Ring of Shadows +1
  * **Ring Identification:** Identifies the type of a ring on equipping it
  * **Light Armor Proficiency:** Proficient with light armor, dodging better while wearing it
  * **Detection Expertise:** Proficient in detecting hidden doors and traps
  * **Longer Fasting:** Can go without food longer than other classes
  * **Identified Maps:** Scrolls of Magic Mapping are identified from the beginning

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **High Evasion:** Higher chance to dodge enemy attacks
  * **Detection:** Better at finding hidden doors and traps
  * **Deception:** Special class armor ability for invisibility
  * **Agility:** Fast movement and quick reactions

The Rogue has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Assassin:** When performing a surprise attack, inflicts additional damage to the target
  * **Freerunner:** Can move almost twice as fast as most monsters; when running, is much harder to hit (requires being unencumbered and not starving)

==== Class Advantages ====
  * High evasion makes difficult to hit
  * Better at detecting traps and secret doors
  * Fast weapons for more frequent attacks
  * Special abilities for stealth and escape
  * Good for players who prefer strategic positioning
  * Ring of Shadows is pre-identified and enhanced

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Lower health than Warrior
  * Weaker starting weapon
  * More dependent on finding good equipment quickly
  * Less damage output in direct confrontation

==== Class Armor ====
Rogues have a special class armor that grants temporary invisibility, allowing for escape or strategic positioning.

==== Strategy ====
  * Use high evasion to avoid taking damage
  * Rely on detection to navigate safely
  * Use fast weapons frequently for potential combo attacks
  * Take advantage of stealth abilities when needed
  * Look for opportunities to backstab or gain advantage
  * Consider mastery path based on playstyle preferences

====== Hero ======

The Hero is the player character in Remixed Dungeon. The hero explores the dungeon, fights enemies, collects items, and attempts to retrieve the Amulet of Yendor.

==== Hero Classes ====
The hero can choose from several classes at the beginning of the game:
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]]: Starts with additional health and a broken seal
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]]: Starts with a magical staff and book of recipes
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]]: Starts with a cloack of shadows and throwing stones
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]]: Starts with a special bow and extra vision range
  * [[rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]]: Starts with claws that improve with levels and health
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]]: Starts with a bone spear and skeleton key

==== Hero Attributes ====
  * **Health**: Determined by class and level, can be permanently increased
  * **Strength**: Affects ability to wear armor and damage with weapons
  * **Level**: Increases with experience gained from defeating enemies
  * **Experience**: Earned by defeating enemies and completing objectives
  * **Hunger**: Affects performance if not maintained with food

==== Hero Mechanics ====
  * **Equipment**: The hero can wear armor, wield weapons, wear rings, and carry items
  * **Quickslots**: Allows for easy access to important items during combat
  * **Inventory**: Limited space for carrying items
  * **Resting**: Hero can wait faster to regenerate health
  * **Search**: Hero can search for secret doors and traps

==== Advancement ====
  * Heroes level up by gaining experience from defeating enemies
  * Higher levels provide increased health and damage
  * At certain levels, heroes gain access to mastery classes
  * Strength and health can be increased with Potions of Strength and Might

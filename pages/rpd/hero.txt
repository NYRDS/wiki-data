====== Hero ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_warrior.png|Hero }}

==== Description ====
The Hero is the player character in Remixed Dungeon. The hero begins their journey in the Sewers and must descend through increasingly dangerous levels to reach the Amulet of Yendor at the bottom of the dungeon.

==== Hero Classes ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - Balanced fighter with healing potion bonus
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Powerful spellcaster with staff bonus
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - Stealthy fighter with light armor and invisibility bonuses
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] - Ranger with bonus throwing weapon
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Magic-resistant archer with magical resistance
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - Undead summoner with soul mechanics
  * [[rpd:doctor_class|Doctor]] - Healing specialist with alchemy focus

==== Hero Subclasses ====
  * [[rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]] (Warrior) - Focuses on melee combat
  * [[rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]] (Warrior) - Powerful melee with bloodthirst
  * [[rpd:battlemage_subclass|Battlemage]] (Mage) - Combines staff abilities with spells
  * [[rpd:warlock_subclass|Warlock]] (Mage) - Focuses on enemy debuffs
  * [[rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]] (Rogue) - High mobility and evasion
  * [[rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]] (Rogue) - Powerful critical strikes
  * [[rpd:sniper_subclass|Sniper]] (Huntress) - Enhanced ranged combat
  * [[rpd:warden_subclass|Warden]] (Huntress) - Nature-based abilities
  * [[rpd:shaman_subclass|Shaman]] (Elf) - Elemental magic
  * [[rpd:scout_subclass|Scout]] (Elf) - Mobility and detection
  * [[rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]] (Necromancer) - Undead form with magic bonuses
  * [[rpd:plague_doctor_subclass|Plague Doctor]] (Doctor) - Disease and poison expertise

==== Core Mechanics ====
  * **Health:** Hit points that can be restored through various means
  * **Strength:** Affects ability to wear armor and upgrade items
  * **Level:** Gained through combat and completing quests
  * **Hunger:** Gradually decreases and affects health if not addressed
  * **Experience:** Earned by defeating enemies and completing objectives
  * **Inventory:** Limited slots for carrying equipment and consumables

====== Hero ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_default.png|Hero Character }}

The **Hero** is the main character controlled by the player in Remixed Dungeon. The hero explores the dungeon, fights enemies, collects items, and attempts to retrieve the Amulet of Yendor from the deepest level.

==== Main Attributes ====
  * **[[rpd:health_mechanic|Health]]**: Current and maximum hit points (HP)
  * **[[rpd:strength|Strength]]**: Affects ability to carry items and wear armor
  * **[[rpd:level|Level]]**: Experience level that increases with gained experience
  * **[[rpd:experience_system|Experience]]**: Points gained from defeating enemies
  * **[[rpd:gold_item|Gold]]**: Currency for purchasing items from shops

==== [[rpd:hero_classes|Hero Classes]] ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - Starts with a unique weapon and armor
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Starts with a magical weapon and a book of recipes
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - Starts with a unique armor and a set of throwing knives
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] - Starts with a unique missile weapon and a quiver of ammunition
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Special class, unlocked after defeating a boss with another class
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - Special class, unlocked after defeating a boss with another class
  * [[rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]] - Special class, unlocked after defeating a boss with another class
  * [[rpd:doctor_class|Doctor]] - Special class, unlocked after defeating a boss with another class

==== Core Statistics ====
  * **Health Points (HP)**: Determines how much damage a hero can take before dying
  * **Strength (STR)**: Affects ability to carry heavy items and armor, and melee damage
  * **Accuracy**: Affects chance to hit enemies
  * **Evasion**: Affects chance to avoid enemy attacks
  * **Speed**: Affects movement and attack speed
  * **Magic Power**: Affects spell damage and effectiveness

==== Inventory ====
The hero can carry various types of items:

  * **[[rpd:weapon_item|Weapons]]** - For attacking enemies
  * **[[rpd:armor_item|Armor]]** - For protection against damage
  * **[[rpd:potion_item|Potions]]** - Consumable items with various effects
  * **[[rpd:scroll_item|Scrolls]]** - Magical items that produce various effects
  * **[[rpd:ring|Jewelry]]** - Items providing passive bonuses
  * **[[rpd:food_item|Food]]** - To satisfy hunger
  * **[[rpd:keys_and_locks|Keys]]** - To open locked doors and chests
  * **[[rpd:quest_items|Quest Items]]** - Special items needed for quests

==== Equipment ====
The hero can equip several types of items:

  * [[rpd:weapon_item|Weapon]] - In main hand
  * [[rpd:armor_item|Armor]] - Body armor
  * [[rpd:ring|Ring 1]] - First finger
  * [[rpd:ring|Ring 2]] - Second finger
  * [[rpd:artifacts|Artifact]] - Special slot for artifacts (if available)

==== Inventory and Equipment Slots ====
The hero has an inventory system with multiple slots:
  * Weapon slot
  * Armor slot
  * Ring slots (2)
  * Quickslots (4)
  * Inventory (12 slots by default)

  * **[[rpd:hunger_mechanic|Hunger]]** - Requires food to prevent weakening

==== Starting Equipment ====
Each class starts with specific equipment:

  * Warrior: Short sword, armor, ration, potion of healing
  * Mage: Mages staff, armor, ration, potion of healing, seeds
  * Rogue: Runic blade, studded armor, cloak of shadows, three throwing knives
  * Huntress: Hunters bow, studded armor, twenty throwing knives
  * Other classes have their own unique starting equipment

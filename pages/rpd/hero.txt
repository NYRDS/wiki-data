====== Hero ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_WARRIOR.png|Hero}}

The **Hero** is the playable character in Remixed Dungeon. Players choose from different [[rpd:hero_class|hero classes]], each with unique starting stats, abilities, and playstyles.

==== Hero Classes ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - High strength and health, excels in direct combat
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Magic-user with varied spells and abilities
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - Stealth-focused with high evasion and detection abilities
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] - Ranged combat specialist with thrown weapons
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Nature-focused with immunity to roots and high speed
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - Undead summoner with death magic
  * [[rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]] - Feral warrior with forbidden actions and friendly mobs
  * [[rpd:priest_class|Priest]] - Support-focused (Coming Soon)
  * [[rpd:doctor_class|Doctor]] - Disease-focused (Coming Soon)

==== Core Statistics ====
  * **Health Points (HP)**: Determines how much damage a hero can take before dying
  * **Strength (STR)**: Affects ability to carry heavy items and armor, and melee damage
  * **Accuracy**: Affects chance to hit enemies
  * **Evasion**: Affects chance to avoid enemy attacks
  * **Speed**: Affects movement and attack speed
  * **Magic Power**: Affects spell damage and effectiveness

==== Inventory and Equipment ====
The hero has an inventory system with multiple slots:
  * Weapon slot
  * Armor slot
  * Ring slots (2)
  * Quickslots (4)
  * Inventory (12 slots by default)

==== Mechanics ====
  * [[rpd:hunger_buff|Hunger]] - Affects hero performance over time
  * [[rpd:experience_system|Experience]] - Gained through defeating enemies and completing challenges
  * [[rpd:levels|Levels]] - Progression through dungeon depths
  * [[rpd:talents|Talents]] - Special abilities gained at milestones
  * [[rpd:belongings|Belongings]] - Inventory and equipment management

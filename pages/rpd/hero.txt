====== Hero ======

The Hero is the player character in Remixed Dungeon. The hero begins their journey at the surface and attempts to reach the Amulet of Yendor at the bottom of the dungeon.

==== Classes ====
The hero can choose from several different classes, each with unique abilities and playstyles:
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - High health and melee damage, starts with a unique short sword
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Powerful magic abilities, starts with a unique staff
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - Stealth and evasion, starts with a unique cloak of shadows
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] - Ranged combat and tracking, starts with unique throwing weapons
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Balanced abilities with magical aptitude, starts with a unique bow
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - Necromancy abilities and pet summons, starts with a unique staff
  * [[rpd:doctor_class|Doctor]] - Alchemy mastery and toxic abilities, starts with special items

==== Attributes ====
The hero has several key attributes:
  * **Strength (STR)** - Affects ability to wear armor and wield weapons
  * **Health Points (HP)** - Damage tolerance before death
  * **Level** - Character progression and power
  * **Experience** - Points gained through combat and exploration
  * **Hunger** - Food requirement system

====== Hero ======

The Hero is the player character in Remixed Dungeon, representing the adventurer exploring the dungeon.

==== Description ====
The Hero is the controllable character in Remixed Dungeon representing the player. The Hero can be one of several [[rpd:hero_class|classes]], each with unique starting equipment and abilities.

==== Hero Classes ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - Melee-focused class with high health and starting equipment
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Magic-focused class with wand and scroll affinity
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - Stealth and trap-discovery focused class
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] - Ranger class with thrown weapon bonuses
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Natural magic and ranged combat class
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - Undead summoning and dark magic class
  * [[rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]] - High strength but low health class
  * [[rpd:priest_class|Priest]] - Healing and support focused class
  * [[rpd:doctor_class|Doctor]] - Chemistry-focused class with specialized equipment

==== Attributes ====
  * **Health (HP)** - Current and maximum hit points
  * **Strength (STR)** - Affects how much equipment the hero can carry and melee damage
  * **Level** - Affects stats and spell power
  * **Experience** - Earned by defeating enemies and completing objectives
  * **Gold** - Currency for purchasing items in shops

==== Core Mechanics ====
  * [[rpd:inventory|Inventory]] - Item carrying and management
  * [[rpd:equipment|Equipment]] - Weapon, armor, and accessories
  * [[rpd:stats|Stats]] - Detailed attribute information
  * [[rpd:level|Leveling]] - Experience and advancement system
  * [[rpd:hunger_buff|Hunger]] - Hunger system affecting performance
  * [[rpd:quickslots|Quick Slots]] - Fast access to items

==== Starting Equipment ====
Each hero class begins with different equipment:
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]]: Short sword, armor, potion of strength
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]]: Wand of magic missile, scroll of identify, cloth armor
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]]: Ring of shadows +1, cloth armor
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]]: Boomerang, studded armor
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]]: Elven bow and arrows, cloth armor
  * Other classes have their own starting equipment

==== Hero Subclasses ====
Each class can unlock a mastery subclass after certain conditions:
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]]: [[rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]], [[rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]]
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]]: [[rpd:warlock_subclass|Warlock]], [[rpd:battle_mage_subclass|Battle Mage]]
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]]: [[rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]], [[rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]]
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]]: [[rpd:sniper_subclass|Sniper]], [[rpd:warden_subclass|Warden]]
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]]: [[rpd:scout_subclass|Scout]], [[rpd:shaman_subclass|Shaman]]
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]]: [[rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]], [[rpd:witch_doctor_subclass|Witch Doctor]]
  * [[rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]]: [[rpd:guardian_subclass|Guardian]], [[rpd:witch_doctor_subclass|Witch Doctor]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Choose a hero class that matches your playstyle
  * Each class has unique advantages and challenges
  * Consider starting equipment when planning your approach
  * Learn class-specific mechanics and synergies

====== Hero ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_warrior.png|Hero }}

The **Hero** is the player character in Remixed Dungeon. The hero explores the dungeon, fights enemies, collects items, and strives to reach the Amulet of Yendor at the bottom.

==== Hero Stats ====
  * **Level** - Increases with experience, boosts health and damage
  * **Health** - When reduced to 0, the game ends
  * **Strength** - Affects ability to carry and use heavy items
  * **Experience** - Gained by defeating enemies and completing quests
  * **Gold** - Collected to purchase items from shops

==== Hero Classes ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - High starting health and melee bonus
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Starts with a magical staff and bonus wand damage
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - High evasion and detection ability
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Balanced stats and benefits with magical items
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - Dark magic specialist focused on summoning

==== Equipment ====
  * **Weapon** - For attacking enemies
  * **Armor** - For reducing damage taken
  * **Ring** - Accessories with various effects (slots: 2)
  * **Miscellaneous** - Amulet, artifact, or other utility item

==== Hero Mechanics ====
  * **Hunger** - Gradually decreases and affects health if not managed
  * **Resting** - Press the wait button to rest and heal faster
  * **Belongings** - Inventory management and equipment system
  * **Quickslots** - Fast access to useful items during combat

==== Strategy ====
  * Choose equipment carefully based on your class and situation
  * Conserve healing items for difficult encounters
  * Identify items before making important decisions
  * Manage food resources carefully
  * Learn enemy weaknesses and resistances

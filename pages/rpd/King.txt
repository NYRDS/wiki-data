====== King of Dwarves ======

==== Description ====
The King of Dwarves, also known as the Dwarf King, rules over the Dwarven Metropolis deep in the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Very high
  * **Attack Skill:** High
  * **Defense Skill:** High
  * **Damage:** High
  * **Exp for Kill:** High

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Royal Guard:** Often has minions or guards
  * **Dwarven Powers:** Uses abilities related to dwarven magic
  * **Leadership:** May boost nearby dwarven allies
  * **High Magic Resistance:** Resistant to magical attacks

==== Notes ====
The Dwarf King is a major boss in the Dwarven Metropolis area of the dungeon. He represents the fallen royalty of the dwarven civilization.

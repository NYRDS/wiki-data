====== DM-300 ======

==== Description ====
DM-300 is a massive mechanical guardian created by the dwarves to defend the Goo area of the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Very high
  * **Attack Skill:** High
  * **Defense Skill:** Very high
  * **Damage:** High
  * **Exp for Kill:** High

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Mechanical Nature:** Immune to many status effects that affect living creatures
  * **Ranged Attacks:** Can attack from distance
  * **Durability:** High resistance to damage
  * **Special Abilities:** Uses various mechanical attacks

==== Notes ====
DM-300 is one of the major bosses in the dungeon, representing the dwarven technological defense system. It requires special tactics to defeat due to its mechanical nature.

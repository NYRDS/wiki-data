====== sniper_armor ======

{{ rpd:images:armor_armor.png|Sniper Armor }}

===== Description =====
Sniper Armor is the class-specific armor for the Sniper subclass of the Huntress class in Remixed Dungeon. It is part of the Huntress armor family that features the Spectral Blades special ability.

===== Stats =====
  * **Tier:** 6 armor (Class Armor)
  * **Required Strength:** Based on the armor that was transformed into this armor
  * **Damage Resistance:** Based on the armor that was transformed into this armor
  * **Durability:** Not applicable (not upgradable)
  * **Class:** Class Armor
  * **Cursed:** Cannot be cursed

===== Acquisition =====
  * Use an [[rpd:armor_kit|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor while playing as a Huntress who has chosen the Sniper subclass
  * Transforms the base armor into Sniper Armor with special properties
  * The resulting armor inherits the strength requirement and damage resistance of the original armor

===== Special Properties =====
  * Inherits all properties of Huntress Armor (Spectral Blades ability)
  * Exclusive to the Sniper subclass - cannot be equipped if not the Sniper subclass
  * Not upgradable - level remains constant
  * Always identified
  * Cannot be cursed

===== Special Ability: Spectral Blades =====
  * **Name:** SPECTRAL BLADES
  * **Effect:** Creates a fan of spectral blades that target all enemies visible to the wearer
  * **Damage:** Damage is based on the wearer's currently equipped melee weapon
  * **Mana Cost:** Skill Points Max / 3 + 1 (minimum 2)
  * **Range:** ShadowCaster.MAX_DISTANCE (typically line of sight)
  * **Targeting:** All enemies in field of view within range
  * **Animation:** Each blade targets a single enemy with visual effect

===== Mechanics =====
  * Can only be equipped by a Sniper subclass hero
  * Special ability can only be used when the armor is equipped and the hero has sufficient skill points
  * The armor's stats (DR and strength requirement) are based on the original armor that was transformed
  * The special ability affects all enemies visible to the wearer in their field of view

===== Usage =====
  * Essential armor for maximizing the effectiveness of the Sniper subclass
  * Should be crafted as soon as possible after choosing the Sniper subclass
  * Allows full utilization of subclass abilities
  * Best used with high-damage melee weapons to maximize Spectral Blades damage

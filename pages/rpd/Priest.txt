====== Priest ======

==== Description ====
The Priest is an unlockable class in Remixed Dungeon focused on divine magic and healing.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Divine Magic:** Access to special divine spells and abilities
  * **Healing Focus:** Enhanced healing capabilities compared to other classes
  * **Blessing Powers:** Can receive special blessings that enhance various attributes

==== Unlock Method ====
The Priest class can be unlocked by slaying a specific boss with any other class (exact requirements may vary).

==== Special Notes ====
The Priest class specializes in support and healing magic, making it valuable for longer dungeon runs where health management is crucial.

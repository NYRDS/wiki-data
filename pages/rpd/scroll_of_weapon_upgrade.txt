====== Scroll of Weapon Upgrade ======

{{ rpd:images:scrollofweaponupgrade_sprite.png|Scroll of Weapon Upgrade }}

The **Scroll of Weapon Upgrade** is a magical scroll in Remixed Dungeon that permanently improves the enchantment of a weapon.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Scroll
  * **Rarity:** Rare
  * **Special Effect:** Applies a random enchantment to a weapon
  * **Usage:** Single use item

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Permanently applies a random [[rpd:enchantment|enchantment]] to the target weapon
  * The enchantment is determined randomly from all possible enchantments
  * Once applied, the enchantment cannot be removed or changed
  * Can only be used on weapons, not other equipment

==== Strategy ====
  * Best used on high-quality or high-level weapons to maximize benefit
  * Consider the risks vs. rewards as effects are random - you may get a highly desirable enchantment or a less useful one
  * Very valuable for improving weapons with offensive or utility enchantments
  * Most effective when applied to weapons you plan to use for the remainder of your run

==== Obtaining ====
Scrolls of Weapon Upgrade can be found:
  * As rare drops in dungeon levels
  * In special treasure areas
  * Potentially for purchase in advanced shops
  * As rewards for completing challenging tasks

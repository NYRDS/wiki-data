====== Tengu Liver ======

==== Description ====
A rare drop from the Tengu boss. This organ has special properties related to necromancy.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Necromancy Component:** Used in necromantic rituals and items
  * **Rare Drop:** Only obtained by defeating the Tengu boss
  * **Crafting Material:** May be used in crafting powerful necromantic items

==== Uses ====
  * Crafting component for advanced necromantic items
  * May be required for specific class abilities or upgrades
  * Valuable trading material

==== Notes ====
Tengu Liver is a valuable component for necromancers and those following necromantic paths, as it's associated with powerful undead magic.

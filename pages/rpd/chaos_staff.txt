====== Chaos Staff ======

{{ rpd:images:chaos_staff.png|Chaos Staff }}

Earned by fusing a [[rpd:chaos_crystal|Chaos Crystal]] with at least 50 charges with a wand.

==== Description ====
[[rpd:mage|Mage]]'s weapon. Staff imbued with Power Of Chaos! What else a mage can dream or fear of?

==== Stats ====
  * **Type:** Magic Weapon
  * **Speed:** Normal
  * **Damage:** 3-8 (scaling with level)
  * **Durability:** 15
  * **Strength Required:** 10 (reduces with [[rpd:mage|mage]]'s skill level)

==== Special Properties ====
  * A powerful magical staff infused with chaotic energy
  * Can be created by fusing a [[rpd:chaos_crystal|Chaos Crystal]] with a wand via the ''Fuse'' action
  * The staff charges as the wielder takes damage (the Chaos Crystal item charges when taking damage)
  * Upon zapping, creates a random chaos effect at the target location, consuming charge

==== Chaos Effects ====
When zapped, the staff performs one of these random effects:
  * [[rpd:confusion_gas|Confusion Gas]]
  * [[rpd:liquid_flame|Liquid Flame]]
  * [[rpd:paralytic_gas|Paralytic Gas]]
  * [[rpd:regrowth|Regrowth]]
  * [[rpd:toxic_gas|Toxic Gas]]

==== Upgrades ====
  * Gains levels as the wielder uses it (owner does damage)
  * Changes sprite appearance based on level (0-4 images)

==== Notes ====
Chaos Staff is a high-level magical weapon created by fusing a [[rpd:chaos_crystal|Chaos Crystal]] with a wand. It provides [[rpd:mage|Mages]] with powerful chaotic magic at the cost of some predictability.

====== Chaos Staff ======

Earned by fusing a [[Chaos Crystal]] with at least 50 charges with a wand.

The staff will be unidentified and always have 2/2 charges. Upon attacking enemies a few times, will add spell combos upon the staff followed by repeatedly upgrading the weapon every few tiers (+1,+2 etc.) as well as further adding effects.

It is recommended that if there is a specific enchantment desired, save the game and then monster hunt until the desired combo is reached.

==== Description ====
Staff imbued with Power Of Chaos! What else a mage can dream or fear of?

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** Varies based on enchantment
  * **Durability:** Normal
  * **Strength Required:** Based on wielder's magic level

==== Special Properties ====
  * A powerful magical staff infused with chaotic energy
  * Can be created by fusing a Chaos Crystal with a wand
  * Provides powerful chaotic magical effects

==== Notes ====
Chaos Staff is a high-level magical weapon created by fusing a Chaos Crystal with a wand. It provides mages with powerful chaotic magic at the cost of some predictability.

====== Treacherous Spirit Mob ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_TreacherousSpirit.png|Treacherous Spirit }}

The **Treacherous Spirit** is a special boss mob that appears as part of the AzuterronNPC quest. It is a dark creature that the player must defeat to complete the quest.

==== Overview ====
  * **Type**: Quest Boss
  * **Location**: Spawns on the current level when the AzuterronNPC quest is accepted
  * **Quest**: Defeat this monster and bring its heart back to the Stranger (AzuterronNPC)
  * **Reward**: Completing this quest rewards the player with a Potion of Might

==== Mechanics ====
  * The Treacherous Spirit is spawned in a valid random location on the current level when the AzuterronNPC quest is initiated
  * The spirit is part of the Guts level area content in the Remixed Dungeon game
  * As a boss creature, it likely has special abilities and higher stats than regular monsters
  * Must be defeated to obtain the Heart of Darkness quest item

==== Quest Context ====
  * The Treacherous Spirit appears as part of a special quest given by the AzuterronNPC (Stranger)
  * The Stranger requests that the player defeat this creature and bring back its heart
  * This is a one-time quest that helps introduce players to the Guts area of the dungeon

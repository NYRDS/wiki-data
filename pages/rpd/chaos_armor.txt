====== Chaos Armor ======

{{tag> rpd items}}

==== Description ====
This armor consists of blood-red plates that are sewn together with dark red chains. They irradiate chaotic energy as you touch them. It is part of the Chaos Set, which features chaotic properties and auto-upgrading mechanics.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage Resistance:** Up to 6 points of damage absorption (varies based on upgrades)
  * **Required Strength:** 13 points
  * **Tier:** 3 armor
  * **Durability:** Normal
  * **Class:** Heavy Armor

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Blood-red plates with dark red chains:** Distinctive appearance that irradiates chaotic energy
  * **Auto-Upgrading:** Upgrades itself as you progress through the dungeon and kill monsters
  * **Chaos Fusion:** Can be created by fusing a [[rpd:chaos_crystal|Chaos Crystal]] with regular armor
  * **Upgrade Decay:** May lose upgrades if not used for some time

==== Mechanics ====
Like other items in the Chaos Set, Chaos Armor has auto-upgrading properties:
  * It upgrades itself as you progress through the dungeon and kill monsters
  * The upgrade system works similarly to the Chaos Sword - it gains power through use
  * It may lose upgrades if not used for some time

==== Strategy ====
  * Chaos Armor provides solid protection with the added benefit of self-improvement over time
  * To maintain its effectiveness, ensure regular use to keep its upgrade level high
  * The armor's upgrade level affects its damage resistance, so maintaining active use is important
  * Consider using this armor through multiple floors to maximize its effectiveness

==== Obtaining ====
Chaos Armor can be obtained through:
  * Fusing a [[rpd:chaos_crystal|Chaos Crystal]] with regular armor
  * As a rare drop from chaos-themed enemies or events
  * Through special crafting systems in the game

====== Chaos Armor ======

**Chaos Armor** is a tier-3 armor in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that is part of the Chaos Set. It features chaotic properties and auto-upgrading mechanics.

==== Description ====
This armor consists of blood-red plates that are sewn together with dark red chains. They irradiate chaotic energy as you touch them.

==== Stats ====
* DR: Up to 6 points of damage absorption
* Required Strength: 13 points
* Tier: 3 armor

==== Mechanics ====
Like other items in the Chaos Set, Chaos Armor has auto-upgrading properties:
* It upgrades itself as you progress through the dungeon and kill monsters
* The upgrade system works similarly to the Chaos Sword - it gains power through use
* It may lose upgrades if not used for some time

==== Strategy ====
Chaos Armor provides solid protection with the added benefit of self-improvement over time. To maintain its effectiveness, ensure regular use to keep its upgrade level high.

==== Obtaining ====
Chaos Armor can potentially be obtained through:
* Chaos Crystal fusion mechanics
* Special crafting systems
* As a rare drop from chaos-themed enemies or events

====== Armor ======

{{ rpd:images:armor_tier4.png|Armor }}

**Armor** provides protection to the hero by reducing the damage taken from attacks. Different types of armor offer varying levels of protection and have different requirements and characteristics.

==== Armor Tiers ====
  * **Cloth Armor** (Tier 1): Lowest protection, no strength requirement
  * **Leather Armor** (Tier 2): Basic protection, moderate strength requirement
  * **Mail Armor** (Tier 3): Good protection, higher strength requirement
  * **Scale Armor** (Tier 4): Better protection, high strength requirement
  * **Plate Armor** (Tier 5): Highest protection, very high strength requirement

==== Armor Properties ====
  * **Defense Rating (DR)**: Reduces incoming physical damage
  * **Strength Requirement**: Minimum strength needed to wear without penalties
  * **Durability**: Armor degrades with use
  * **Weight**: Affects movement speed and hunger consumption

==== Armor Mechanics ====
  * **Damage Reduction**: Higher armor provides better protection against physical attacks
  * **Enchantments**: Armor can be enchanted with glyphs for special effects
  * **Identification**: Armor must be identified to see its exact defense value
  * **Curses**: Cursed armor cannot be removed until blessed

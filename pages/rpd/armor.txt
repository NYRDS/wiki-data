====== Armor ======

{{ rpd:images:armor_sprite.png|Armor }}

Armor in Remixed Dungeon is protective equipment that reduces damage from enemy attacks. Different types of armor offer different levels of protection and special properties.

==== Armor Categories ====
  * **Light Armor:** Higher mobility but less protection
    * [[rpd:cloth_armor|Cloth Armor]] - Basic armor
    * [[rpd:leather_armor|Leather Armor]] - Mid-tier light armor
  * **Medium Armor:** Balanced protection and mobility
    * [[rpd:mail_armor|Mail Armor]] - Balanced protection
    * [[rpd:scale_armor|Scale Armor]] - Higher protection option
  * **Heavy Armor:** Maximum protection but reduced mobility
    * [[rpd:plate_armor|Plate Armor]] - Maximum protection
  * **Specialty Armor:** Class-specific or unique armor
    * [[rpd:class_armor|Class Armor]] - Mastery armors

==== Armor Properties ====
  * **Defense Rating:** How much damage is reduced from attacks
  * **Durability:** How much damage the armor can take before breaking
  * **Strength Requirement:** Minimum strength needed to wear effectively
  * **Speed Penalty:** How much armor may slow you down (based on weight vs strength)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Glyphs:** Armor can have special magical properties added via [[rpd:arcane_stylus|Arcane Stylus]] (formally called Stylus in the code)
  * **Upgrading:** Armor can be upgraded with Scrolls of Upgrade to improve defense rating
  * **Class Specialization:** Some classes get special benefits from specific armor types
  * **Weight vs. Strength:** Heavier armor slows you down if insufficient strength

==== Armor Tiers ====
  * **Tier 1:** [[rpd:cloth_armor|Cloth Armor]]
  * **Tier 2:** [[rpd:leather_armor|Leather Armor]]
  * **Tier 3:** [[rpd:mail_armor|Mail Armor]]
  * **Tier 4:** [[rpd:scale_armor|Scale Armor]]
  * **Tier 5:** [[rpd:plate_armor|Plate Armor]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Balance protection with mobility based on your playstyle
  * Consider your strength when choosing armor to avoid speed penalties
  * Upgrade armor when possible to improve protection
  * Consider glyphs that complement your build

==== Class Interactions ====
  * [[rpd:warrior|Warrior]] typically focuses on heavy armor
  * [[rpd:rogue|Rogue]] may prefer lighter armor for stealth abilities
  * [[rpd:mage|Mage]] often uses medium armor to balance protection with spellcasting
  * [[rpd:huntress|Huntress]] can use medium armor effectively for ranged combat

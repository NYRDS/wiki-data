====== Heart of Darkness ======

==== Description ====
A mysterious artifact emanating dark energy. This item is associated with powerful necromantic forces.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Dark Energy:** Contains significant dark or necromantic power
  * **Rare Artifact:** A powerful and rare item in the dungeon
  * **Potential Uses:** May enable powerful necromantic abilities

==== Uses ====
  * Used in advanced necromantic rituals
  * May be required for creating powerful necromantic items
  * Could be a component for special abilities

==== Notes ====
The Heart of Darkness is a powerful artifact associated with deep necromantic forces. It's likely valuable to characters following necromantic paths.

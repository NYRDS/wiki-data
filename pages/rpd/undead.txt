====== Undead ======
{:rpd:images:undead_sprite.png?200|}
{{ rpd:images:undead_sprite.png|Undead }}
The Undead are powerful undead enemies that spawn during the King boss fight in Remixed Dungeon.
==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Appears during King boss fight (level 21-22)
  * **HP**: High
  * **Attack**: High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: Significant
==== Description ====
During the battle with the King, additional Undead are summoned to aid the King. These are former Dwarven heroes that have been corrupted by the King's dark magic.
==== Behavior ====
  * Aggressive toward the player
  * Assist the King during the boss fight
  * Appear multiple times during the battle
==== Drops ====
The Undead typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Random equipment or items
  * May drop unique items during the King fight
==== Special Notes ====
  * Spawned specifically during the King boss fight
  * Multiple Undead can appear at once
  * Part of the King's defensive forces
  * Defeating all Undead is necessary to reach the King directly

====== Undead Mobs ======
{{ rpd:images:undead_sprite.png|Undead Sprite  }}Undead mobs are creatures that have been reanimated after death, often through necromancy or other dark magic. They are typically immune to some status effects that affect living creatures and may have other special properties.

==== Properties of Undead ====
  * **Status Immunities:** Undead are typically immune to poison, bleeding, and other effects that target living tissue
  * **Necromancy Interactions:** In games with necromancer class, some undead may be influenced by necromancy spells
  * **Healing Effects:** Some healing abilities may harm undead instead of healing them
  * **Special Resistances:** Undead may have resistance to certain damage types
==== Tactics Against Undead ====
  * Use weapons and abilities that don't rely on affecting living tissue
  * Holy or blessed weapons/potions may be more effective
  * Fire-based attacks often prove effective against undead
  * Be aware that some healing effects may strengthen undead

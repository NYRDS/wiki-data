====== Undead Mobs ======

{{ rpd:images:undead_sprite.png|Undead Sprite  }}

Undead mobs are creatures that have been reanimated after death, often through necromancy or other dark magic in Remixed Dungeon. They are typically immune to some status effects that affect living creatures and may have other special properties.

==== Description ====
Undead Mobs are a category of monsters in Remixed Dungeon that have been reanimated after death through various magical means. In the case of the "Undead" mob specifically, they are former Dwarven heroes corrupted by the King's dark magic.

==== Specific Undead Entity: The Undead =====
  * **Stats**: HP 28, Defense Skill 15, Attack Skill 16, Damage 12-16, Defense (DR) 5
  * **Level**: Appears during King boss fight (levels in necropolis)
  * **EXP**: 0 (no experience gained)
  * **Special Ability**: 1/7 chance to apply Stun on hit
  * **Spawn**: Summoned by the King during his boss fight
  * **Drops**: No specific drops (as shown in code)

==== Properties of Undead ====
  * **Status Immunities:** Undead are typically immune to poison, bleeding, and other effects that target living tissue
  * **Necromancy Interactions:** Some undead may be influenced by necromancy spells
  * **Healing Effects:** Some healing abilities may harm undead instead of healing them
  * **Special Resistances:** Undead may have resistance to certain damage types
  * Generally immune to fear and terror effects
  * Often resistant or immune to certain status effects like paralysis or sleep
  * May be affected differently by healing/dealing damage mechanics
  * Some undead can be turned or repelled by certain items or abilities

==== Tactics Against Undead ====
  * Use weapons and abilities that don't rely on affecting living tissue
  * Holy or blessed weapons/potions may be more effective
  * Fire-based attacks often prove effective against undead
  * Be aware that some healing effects may strengthen undead

==== Notes ====
Different classes may have special abilities or bonuses when fighting undead creatures. The King specifically summons Undead minions during his boss fight.

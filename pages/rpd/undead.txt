====== Undead ======

The **Undead** is a mob category in Remixed Dungeon that includes various types of supernatural beings. These creatures typically have special resistances and vulnerabilities that differ from living creatures.

==== Description ====
Undead creatures are once-living beings that have been reanimated through dark magic. They are typically immune to certain status effects that affect living beings, but vulnerable to others that specifically target their undead nature.

==== Undead Mobs ====
Undead mobs in Remixed Dungeon include:
  * [[rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeleton]]
  * [[rpd:zombie_mob|Zombie]]
  * [[rpd:ghost_mob|Ghost]]
  * [[rpd:lich_mob|Lich]]
  * [[rpd:wraith_mob|Wraith]]
  * [[rpd:undead_mob|Undead]] (Boss)
  * [[rpd:death_knight_mob|Death Knight]]
  * [[rpd:zombie_gnoll|Zombie Gnoll]]
  * [[rpd:spectre|Spectre]]

==== Immunities ====
Undead creatures are typically immune to:
  * [[rpd:paralysis_buff|Paralysis]]
  * [[rpd:poison_buff|Poison]]
  * [[rpd:sleep_buff|Sleep]]
  * [[rpd:charm_buff|Charm]]

==== Vulnerabilities ====
Undead creatures are typically vulnerable to:
  * [[rpd:heal_spell|Heal]] spells (which damage them instead)
  * [[rpd:potion_of_holy_water|Potion of Holy Water]]
  * Certain weapon enchantments and armor glyphs

==== Tactics ====
When fighting undead enemies:
  * Use weapons with anti-undead enchantments
  * Avoid using healing effects on them
  * Take advantage of their immunities when affected by status effects
  * Consider using light-based items or spells

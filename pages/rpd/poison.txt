====== Poison ======

{{ rpd:images:poison_buff.png|Poison Buff }}

**Poison** is a harmful status effect in Remixed Dungeon that causes damage over time to affected characters.

==== Effects ====
  * **Damage Over Time**: Causes 1 damage per turn while active
  * **Duration**: Gradually decreases over time until it expires
  * **Stacking**: Multiple poison effects can stack to increase damage rate
  * **Weakening**: May impair character's performance in combat

==== Sources ====
  * **[[rpd:poison_trap|Poison Traps]]**: Activated when stepped on
  * **[[rpd:spells|Spells]]**: Some spells can inflict poison
  * **[[rpd:wands|Wands]]**: Wand of Poison can inflict poison
  * **[[rpd:mobs|Mobs]]**: Some enemies have poisonous attacks (e.g., [[rpd:scorpio_mob|Scorpio]])
  * **[[rpd:enchantments|Weapon Enchantments]]**: Poison enchantment can inflict poison on hit

==== Resistance and Immunity ====
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_elements|Ring of Elements]]**: Provides resistance to poison
  * **[[rpd:armor_glyphs|Armor Glyphs]]**: Some glyphs provide poison protection
  * **[[rpd:mobs|Mobs]]**: Some creatures are naturally immune to poison
  * **[[rpd:buffs|Buffs]]**: Special status effects may provide temporary immunity

==== Countering Poison ====
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]]**: Clears poison status immediately
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_mending|Ring of Mending]]**: May help resist poison effects
  * **Quick Action**: Using cures quickly before poison becomes severe

====== Berserker ======

{{ rpd:images:berserker_sprite.png|Berserker }}

The Berserker is the Warrior's subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Unlocking ====
The Berserker subclass can be unlocked by using a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss (Tengu).

==== Special Mechanics ====
When severely wounded, the _Berserker_ enters a state of wild fury significantly increasing his damage output.

==== Special Armor ====
Using an Armor Kit on any armor will transform it into the [[rpd:berserk_armor|Berserker Armor]], which is specifically designed for this subclass.

==== Advantages ====
  * Massive damage increase when health is low
  * Can turn near-death situations into offensive opportunities
  * Very effective at finishing fights when low on health

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Only activates when severely wounded
  * Requires taking damage to reach the berserker state
  * May encourage reckless play to trigger the ability

====== Skeleton ======

{{ rpd:images:skeleton_sprite.png|Skeleton Sprite  }}{{ :rpd:skeleton.png?nolink&50|}}

Skeletons are undead warriors that have reanimated after death. They are composed of bones and typically wear remnants of ancient armor and wield rusty weapons.

==== Description ====
Skeletons are basic undead enemies that can be found throughout the dungeon. They are the reanimated bones of former adventurers or dungeon dwellers.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Moderate, but with some physical resistance
  * **Attack:** Melee attacks with weapons (swords, clubs, etc.)
  * **Abilities:** Standard undead traits (immune to some status effects)
  * **Armor:** May wear rusty armor providing basic protection

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Undead Nature:** Immune to poison and bleeding
  * **Physical Resistance:** May have resistance to certain physical attacks
  * **Drops:** May carry old weapons, armor, or bones that can be useful

==== Location ====
Skeletons can be found in:
  * [[rpd:prison|Prison]] levels
  * [[rpd:caves|Caves]] levels  
  * [[rpd:city|City]] levels
  * [[rpd:halls|Halls]] levels
  * Anywhere the undead roam

==== Tactics ====
  * Fire-based attacks are often effective against undead
  * Holy/light-based attacks may be particularly effective
  * Be aware that healing magic may harm skeletons
  * Physical attacks work, but may be resisted

==== Related ====
  * Part of the [[rpd:undead|Undead]] category
  * Often found in areas with other undead creatures
  * May be encountered in groups

  * **Attack Skill:** Moderate

  * **Exp for Kill:** Low to moderate

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Undead Nature:** Immune to certain status effects like fear and terror
  * **Bone Drop:** May drop bones when defeated
  * **Resistant to certain damage types:** Some damage types may be less effective

==== Notes ====
Skeletons are common undead enemies found throughout the dungeon. They are relatively weak individually but can be dangerous in groups. They are immune to some status effects due to their undead nature.

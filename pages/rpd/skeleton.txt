Skeletons are undead enemies found throughout the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found throughout the dungeon, typically starting around level 3-4
  * **HP**: Moderate
  * **Attack**: Moderate
  * **Defense**: Moderate
  * **EXP**: Provides experience when defeated

==== Description ====
Skeletons are reanimated bones of former adventurers and dungeon dwellers. They are mindless undead that pose a moderate threat to unprepared adventurers.

==== Behavior ====
  * Aggressive toward the player
  * Moves and attacks directly toward the player
  * Does not have any special abilities

==== Drops ====
Skeletons typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Bones
  * Random items (potions, scrolls, equipment)

==== Special Notes ====
  * Skeletons are undead creatures
  * May be affected by certain spells or abilities that target undead specifically
  * Common enemies that provide moderate challenge and rewards

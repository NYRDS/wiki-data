====== Scroll of Remove Curse ======

{{ rpd:images:item_043_sprite.png|Scroll of Remove Curse }}

The **Scroll of Remove Curse** is a magical scroll in Remixed Dungeon that removes curse effects from items.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Scroll
  * **Rarity:** Uncommon
  * **Special Effect:** Removes curses from equipment
  * **Usage:** Single use item
  * **Sprite Index:** ItemSpriteSheet.SCROLL_YNGVI (43)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Removes cursed status from weapons, armor, rings, and other equipment
  * Allows cursed equipment to be removed normally after being equipped
  * Dispel Invisibility from the hero upon use
  * Removes the Weakness debuff from the hero
  * Affects all cursed items in the hero's inventory simultaneously

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential for dealing with cursed equipment that cannot be removed
  * Useful before upgrading equipment to ensure best possible results
  * Important to carry when exploring deeper dungeon levels where cursed items are more common
  * Can be used preventatively on mysterious items
  * Particularly valuable when you find a powerful but cursed item that you want to equip

==== Obtaining ====
Scrolls of Remove Curse can be found:
  * In regular dungeon levels
  * In special treasure areas
  * Potentially for purchase in shops
  * As rewards for completing tasks
  * Relatively common in shops compared to other specialized scrolls

==== Usage ====
  * Read the scroll to activate its effect
  * All cursed equipment in the hero's inventory will be uncursed
  * The hero's Invisibility buff will be removed (if active)
  * The hero's Weakness debuff will be removed (if active)

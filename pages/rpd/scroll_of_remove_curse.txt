====== Scroll of Remove Curse ======

{{ rpd:images:item_ScrollOfRemoveCurse.png|Scroll of Remove Curse }}

The **Scroll of Remove Curse** is a type of scroll in [[rpd:remixed_dungeon|Remixed Dungeon]] that removes curses from all equipped items and carried artifacts.

==== Effects ====
When read, the Scroll of Remove Curse:
  * Removes curses from all currently equipped items (weapon, armor, rings, etc.)
  * Removes curses from carried [[rpd:artifacts|artifacts]]
  * Does not affect items in your backpack that are not equipped
  * Does not remove beneficial curses (certain items may have useful but cursed properties)

==== Usage ====
The scroll can be used to:
  * Uncurse [[rpd:cursed_items|cursed items]] that are preventing you from removing them
  * Remove negative effects from cursed equipment
  * Clear cursed artifacts that are affecting your character negatively
  * Prepare for removing unwanted equipment that was previously cursed

==== Acquisition ====
The Scroll of Remove Curse can be found in:
  * [[rpd:chests|Chests]] in the dungeon
  * [[rpd:shops|Shops]] (occasionally available)
  * As a drop from certain enemies
  * In [[rpd:treasury|Treasury]] levels

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential if you become equipped with multiple cursed items
  * Best saved for when you have multiple cursed items equipped
  * Can be used proactively if you suspect a newly equipped item is cursed
  * Does not take up an inventory slot when applied (unlike potions that need consumption)
  * Often valuable to keep at least one in reserve

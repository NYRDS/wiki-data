====== WindGust ======

{{ rpd:images:wind_gust_spell_icon.png|WindGust Spell Icon }}

**WindGust** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon (Elemental Affinity).

==== Description ====
Wind Gust creates a gust of wind that pushes enemies back. If an enemy is pushed into an obstacle, they may take damage.

==== Stats ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Elemental
  * **Targeting:** Cell
  * **Level:** 1
  * **Mana Cost:** 1

====== Class Armor ======

{{ rpd:images:armor_class.png|Class Armor }}

**Class Armor** refers to the unique armor pieces that are associated with each [[rpd:hero_class|hero class]]. These armors are typically obtained through the [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] quest.

==== Classes and Their Armors ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class]] → [[rpd:warrior_armor_item|Warrior Armor]]
  * [[rpd:mage_class]] → [[rpd:mage_armor_item|Mage Armor]]
  * [[rpd:rogue_class]] → [[rpd:rogue_armor_item|Rogue Armor]]
  * [[rpd:huntress_class]] → [[rpd:huntress_armor_item|Huntress Armor]]
  * [[rpd:elf_class]] → [[rpd:elf_armor_item|Elf Armor]]
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class]] → [[rpd:necromancer_armor_item|Necromancer Armor]]

==== Obtaining Class Armor ====
Class armor is obtained by using an [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on a regular armor piece of the appropriate tier. The armor kit transforms the regular armor into a special class-specific armor with unique abilities.

==== Special Abilities ====
Each class armor has unique special abilities related to its class:
  * Warrior armor special abilities
  * Mage armor special abilities
  * Rogue armor special abilities
  * Huntress armor special abilities
  * Elf armor special abilities
  * Necromancer armor special abilities

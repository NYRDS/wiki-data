====== Class Armor and Special Abilities ======

Each playable class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon has a distinctive class armor with unique special abilities that can be used in combat.

==== Warrior Armor: Shield Barrage ====

The Warrior's class armor enables a special ability called **Shield Barrage** (previously "Body Armor" in some references):

  * **Ability**: Leap to a targeted location and create a shockwave that stuns all adjacent enemies
  * **Effect**: Allows the warrior to reposition in combat and disable multiple enemies briefly
  * **Strategy**: Useful for escaping dangerous situations or setting up advantageous positioning

==== Mage Armor: Mage's Intuition ====

The Mage's class armor ability provides enhanced spellcasting capabilities:

  * **Ability**: Various effects depending on the specific Mage subclass
  * **Effect**: May include teleportation or area effects to support spell usage
  * **Strategy**: Enhances the Mage's already powerful magical abilities

==== Rogue Armor: Deft Defense ====

The Rogue's class armor focuses on mobility and evasion:

  * **Ability**: Enhanced stealth and mobility options
  * **Effect**: Various rogue-specific abilities that complement stealth gameplay
  * **Strategy**: Supports the Rogue's hit-and-run combat style

==== Huntress Armor: Nature's Wrath ====

The Huntress's armor channels natural forces:

  * **Ability**: May include summoning or nature-based effects
  * **Effect**: Supports the Huntress's connection to nature and animals
  * **Strategy**: Can involve summoning creatures or nature-based attacks

==== Elf Armor: Arcane Defense ====

The Elf's armor combines magic and physical defense:

  * **Ability**: Various arcane-enhanced defensive measures
  * **Effect**: Reflects the Elf's magical nature and enhanced abilities
  * **Strategy**: Supports the Elf's ranged magical combat style

==== Necromancer Armor: Necrotic Field ====

The Necromancer's armor has unique necromantic properties:

  * **Ability**: Activate a necrotic field that spreads to enemies (Necrotism)
  * **Effect**: Creates a spreading death effect that can damage multiple enemies
  * **Strategy**: Supports the Necromancer's undead summoning and necromantic abilities
  * For more details, see [[rpd:necromancer_armor|Necromancer Armor]]

==== Gnoll Armor: Pack Mentality ====

The Gnoll's armor reflects their pack-based nature:

  * **Ability**: Various effects that may enhance summoned pets or pack tactics
  * **Effect**: Complements the Gnoll's animal companion abilities
  * **Strategy**: Supports the Gnoll's pet-based combat approach

==== Lich Robe ====

The Lich class has special undead-themed armor abilities:

  * **Ability**: Based on death magic and immunity to various status effects
  * **Effect**: Reflects the Lich's undead nature and magical power
  * **Strategy**: Supports the Lich's magical and necromantic strengths

==== Priest Vestments ====

The Priest's armor focuses on purification and healing:

  * **Ability**: Divine-themed abilities focused on protection and healing
  * **Effect**: Supports the Priest's healing and curse-removal capabilities
  * **Strategy**: Emphasizes protection and support in combat
  * For more details, see [[rpd:priest_armor|Priest Armor]]

==== Doctor Coat ====

The Doctor's armor reflects their expertise in harmful substances:

  * **Ability**: Disease or poison-based special effects
  * **Effect**: Supports the Doctor's toxic-based combat approach
  * **Strategy**: Enables the Doctor to control areas with toxic effects
  * For more details, see [[rpd:doctor_armor|Doctor Coat]]

==== Strategy Tips ====

  * Class armor abilities typically require mana to use
  * Each armor ability is unique to its class and complements the class's playstyle
  * Class armor is automatically equipped when the corresponding subclass is selected
  * Higher-level class abilities can significantly impact combat outcomes

====== Potion of Toxic Gas ======

{{ :rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas.png?nolink&50|}}

The Potion of Toxic Gas is a consumable item that releases a cloud of poisonous gas, damaging and potentially poisoning enemies in the area of effect.

==== Description ====
When thrown, this potion releases a greenish cloud of toxic gas that spreads in the area, affecting all creatures within its range. The gas is harmful to most living things in the dungeon.

==== Effects ====
  * **Area Effect:** Creates a cloud of toxic gas that affects multiple targets
  * **Damage:** Inflicts damage to creatures in the cloud
  * **Poison:** May cause poison status effect for ongoing damage
  * **Environmental:** The gas lingers briefly in the area

==== Usage ====
  * **Combat:** Effective against groups of enemies
  * **Ranged:** Can be thrown to affect distant targets
  * **Crowd Control:** Useful for managing multiple opponents
  * **Environmental:** Can be used to block passages temporarily

==== Classes That Benefit ====
  * [[rpd:plague_doctor|Plague Doctor]] - Especially effective for toxic specialists
  * Any class for dealing with groups of enemies
  * Players with limited direct combat options

==== Tactics ====
  * Most effective against groups of weaker enemies
  * Can be used defensively to create a barrier of toxic gas
  * Consider wind direction or gas spread patterns when relevant
  * Be careful of effects on pets or summoned creatures
  * Can be used to clear areas before entering

====== Potion of Toxic Gas ======

==== Description ====
This potion will release a deadly cloud of toxic gas when shattered. The gas will damage all creatures in the area of effect over time.

==== Effects ====
  * **Toxic Gas Cloud:** Creates a harmful gas cloud at the location
  * **Damage Over Time:** Causes periodic damage to creatures in the cloud
  * **Area of Effect:** Affects all creatures within the gas cloud

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be thrown at groups of enemies to damage them
  * Creates a lingering hazard that continues to deal damage
  * Can be used both offensively and defensively

==== Notes ====
Potion of Toxic Gas is useful for dealing with groups of enemies or creating temporary barriers. The toxic gas affects all creatures within its area, including the player if they remain within it.

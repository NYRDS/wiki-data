====== Potion of Toxic Gas ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_toxic_gas_item.png|Potion of Toxic Gas }}

The Potion of Toxic Gas is a throwable potion that creates a cloud of poisonous gas in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Uncommon
  * **Weight**: 4
  * **Type**: Throwable Potion

==== Description ====
When shattered, this potion releases a deadly cloud of toxic gas that damages and poisons any creature that breathes it. The gas cloud persists for a short time.

==== Usage ====
  * Throws the potion at enemies or into groups
  * Creates a cloud of toxic gas that damages all in the area
  * Causes poison damage over time
  * Effective against groups of enemies

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found throughout the dungeon
  * More common in areas with alchemical themes
  * May be available in shops

==== Special Notes ====
  * Area effect damage
  * Causes poison status effect
  * Dangerous to use near the player
  * Part of the Doctor class starting equipment

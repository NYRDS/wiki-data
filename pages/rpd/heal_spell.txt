====== Heal ======

{{ rpd:images:heal_spell_icon.png|Heal Spell Icon }}

**Heal** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon with Witchcraft Affinity (also available to Priest and PlagueDoctor affinities).

==== Description ====
The Heal spell restores a portion of the target's health instantly, making it valuable for emergency situations. The healing amount is calculated as 20% of the target's max HP per skill level of the caster.

==== Stats ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Witchcraft (also available in Priest and PlagueDoctor affinities, according to CustomSpellsList.lua)
  * **Targeting:** Char (can target any character on the map)
  * **Level:** 2
  * **Mana Cost:** 5
  * **Special Effects:** Heals the target character; heals a target for (target HT / 5) * caster skill level (minimum 1)

==== Mechanics ====
The spell heals the target character for (Max HP / 5 * Caster's skill level) hit points, with a minimum of 1 point healed.

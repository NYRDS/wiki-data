====== Shadows Buff ======

{{ rpd:images:buff_Shadows.png|Shadows Buff }}

==== Description ====
The Shadows buff hides the character in darkness, reducing detection by enemies. This buff provides stealth benefits, making it harder for foes to spot the character in dark areas.

==== Effects ====
  * Hides the character in darkness, reducing detection
  * Makes the character less likely to be seen by enemies
  * May provide stealth benefits in dark areas
  * Reduces the chance of being targeted by enemies

==== Duration ====
  * The duration of the Shadows buff varies depending on the source that applied it
  * When the buff expires, the character returns to normal visibility

==== Sources ====
  * Potions or items related to stealth and darkness
  * Equipment that provides stealth benefits
  * Abilities or mechanics that enhance hiding in shadows
  * Various other game elements that provide stealth benefits

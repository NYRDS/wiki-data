====== Yog ======

{{ rpd:images:yog_sprite.png|Yog }}

Yog is the fourth boss in Remixed Dungeon, found in the Halls area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Level 24 (end of Halls)
  * **HP**: Extremely High
  * **Attack**: Extremely High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: Very High

==== Description ====
Yog is an ancient god of destruction and one of the most feared entities in the dungeon. It is protected by multiple servants and has devastating magical abilities. Only the most experienced adventurers should face this entity.

==== Behavior ====
  * Protected by multiple servants (Rotting Fist, Burning Fist, etc.)
  * Uses devastating magical attacks
  * Has multiple phases with different abilities
  * Summons additional entities during the fight

==== Drops ====
Yog typically drops:
  * Powerful magical items
  * Unique artifacts
  * Large amount of gold
  * Items related to the deepest parts of the dungeon

==== Special Notes ====
  * Fourth boss, located at the end of the Halls level
  * Ancient god of destruction
  * Defeating Yog unlocks access to the deepest parts of the dungeon
  * The fight is one of the most challenging in the game
  * Requires defeating multiple servants before reaching Yog directly

====== Yog-Dzewa ======

{{ :rpd:yog.png?nolink&50|}}

Yog-Dzewa is the final boss of Remixed Dungeon, an ancient and immensely powerful entity with multiple bodily components that must all be defeated to win the game.

==== Description ====
Yog-Dzewa is an ancient god-like entity that dwells in the deepest reaches of the dungeon. It has multiple components: a central brain, multiple eyes that serve as servants, and a heart. To defeat Yog-Dzewa, all of these components must be destroyed. It represents the ultimate challenge for adventurers.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Extremely high across multiple components
  * **Attack:** Multiple devastating special attacks
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Summons spawns to aid in combat
    * Powerful magical and physical attacks
    * Regeneration if components are not destroyed simultaneously
    * Special arena mechanics
  * **Components:** Multiple body parts that must all be defeated

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Multiple Targets:** Several components that must be destroyed
  * **Spawn Summons:** Constantly creates additional enemies to fight
  * **Coordinated Attack:** Components may coordinate their actions
  * **Arena:** Takes place in a specially designed multi-level arena

==== Location ====
  * Found in the deepest area of the dungeon (level 31-32)
  * Appears in a specially designed arena for the final battle

==== Preparation ====
  * **Equipment:** The best weapons, armor, and accessories available
  * **Consumables:** Maximum healing items and utility scrolls
  * **Strategy:** Plan for an extremely long and complex fight
  * **Resources:** Must be fully prepared as no resupply is possible

==== Tactics ====
  * Focus on one component at a time while managing spawns
  * Expect a very long fight that requires stamina management
  * Use powerful area attacks to manage multiple spawns
  * Coordinate attacks efficiently between different components
  * Be prepared for the unexpected as Yog is extremely powerful

==== Rewards ====
  * The Amulet of Yendor - The ultimate artifact
  * Game completion and victory
  * Entry into the hall of fame (in some versions)

====== Yog-Dzewa ======

==== Description ====
Yog-Dzewa is an Old God, a powerful entity from the realms of chaos. A century ago, the ancient dwarves barely won the war against its army of demons, but were unable to kill the god itself. Instead, they then imprisoned it in the halls below their city, believing it to be too weak to rise ever again.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Very high
  * **Attack Skill:** Extremely high
  * **Defense Skill:** High
  * **Damage:** Devastating
  * **Exp for Kill:** Very high

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Demon Summoning:** Can summon various demon minions
  * **Chaos Powers:** Uses powerful chaotic abilities
  * **High Resistance:** Immune to many status effects
  * **Multiple Phases:** Battle progresses through multiple phases

==== Notes ====
Yog-Dzewa is the final boss of the dungeon, representing a powerful Old God from the chaos realms. Defeating it is the ultimate challenge in the game.

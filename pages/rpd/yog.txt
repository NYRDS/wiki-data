====== Yog ======

{{ rpd:images:yog_sprite.png|Yog }}

Yog is the fourth boss in Remixed Dungeon, found in the Halls area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Level 24 (end of Halls)
  * **HP**: Extremely High
  * **Attack**: Extremely High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: Very High

==== Description ====
Yog is an ancient god of destruction and one of the most feared entities in the dungeon. It is protected by multiple servants and has devastating magical abilities. Only the most experienced adventurers should face this entity.

==== Behavior ====
  * Protected by multiple servants (Rotting Fist, Burning Fist, etc.)
  * Uses devastating magical attacks
  * Has multiple phases with different abilities
  * Summons additional entities during the fight

==== Drops ====
Yog typically drops:
  * Powerful magical items
  * Unique artifacts
  * Large amount of gold
  * Items related to the deepest parts of the dungeon

==== Special Notes ====
  * Fourth boss, located at the end of the Halls level
  * Ancient god of destruction
  * Defeating Yog unlocks access to the deepest parts of the dungeon
  * The fight is one of the most challenging in the game
  * Requires defeating multiple servants before reaching Yog directly

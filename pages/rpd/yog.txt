====== Yog-Dzewa ======

{{ :rpd:yog.png?nolink&50|}}

Yog-Dzewa is the final boss of Remixed Dungeon, an ancient and immensely powerful entity with multiple bodily components that must all be defeated to win the game.

==== Description ====
Yog-Dzewa is an ancient god-like entity that dwells in the deepest reaches of the dungeon. It has multiple components: a central brain, multiple eyes that serve as servants, and a heart. To defeat Yog-Dzewa, all of these components must be destroyed. It represents the ultimate challenge for adventurers.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Extremely high across multiple components
  * **Attack:** Multiple devastating special attacks
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Summons spawns to aid in combat
    * Powerful magical and physical attacks
    * Regeneration if components are not destroyed simultaneously
    * Special arena mechanics
  * **Components:** Multiple body parts that must all be defeated

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Multiple Targets:** Several components that must be destroyed
  * **Spawn Summons:** Constantly creates additional enemies to fight
  * **Coordinated Attack:** Components may coordinate their actions
  * **Arena:** Takes place in a specially designed multi-level arena

==== Location ====
  * Found in the deepest area of the dungeon (level 31-32)
  * Appears in a specially designed arena for the final battle

==== Preparation ====
  * **Equipment:** The best weapons, armor, and accessories available
  * **Consumables:** Maximum healing items and utility scrolls
  * **Strategy:** Plan for an extremely long and complex fight
  * **Resources:** Must be fully prepared as no resupply is possible

==== Tactics ====
  * Focus on one component at a time while managing spawns
  * Expect a very long fight that requires stamina management
  * Use powerful area attacks to manage multiple spawns
  * Coordinate attacks efficiently between different components
  * Be prepared for the unexpected as Yog is extremely powerful

==== Rewards ====
  * The Amulet of Yendor - The ultimate artifact
  * Game completion and victory
  * Entry into the hall of fame (in some versions)

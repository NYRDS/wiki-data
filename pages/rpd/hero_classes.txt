====== Hero Classes ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_warrior.png|Warrior Class }}

The **Hero Classes** in Remixed Dungeon represent different character archetypes with unique starting equipment, abilities, and characteristics. Each class offers a different playstyle and experience.

==== Main Classes ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]]: Strong front-liner with bonus health and a special ability to reforge items
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]]: Powerful spellcaster with bonus wand damage and a magical staff
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]]: Stealthy character with bonus evasion and unique detection ability
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]]: Hybrid class with balanced stats and special benefits with magical items
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]]: Dark magic specialist focused on summoning and life drain

==== Class Characteristics ====
  * **Starting Stats**: Each class has different base health and strength values
  * **Unique Items**: Each class has a unique starting item that can't be found elsewhere in the dungeon
  * **Special Abilities**: Each class has passive or active special abilities
  * **Equipment Preference**: Classes are generally better suited to certain types of equipment

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Warrior**: [[rpd:short_sword_item|Short Sword]], [[rpd:armor|Armor]] and a [[rpd:ration_item|Potion of Strength]]
  * **Mage**: [[rpd:mages_staff_item|Wand of Magic Missile]] as a staff, [[rpd:cloth_armor_item|Cloth Armor]] and a [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]]
  * **Rogue**: [[rpd:dart_item|Darts]], [[rpd:leather_armor_item|Leather Armor]] and a [[rpd:scroll_of_magic_mapping_item|Potion of Mind Vision]]
  * **Elf**: [[rpd:short_sword_item|Short Sword]], [[rpd:cloth_armor_item|Cloth Armor]] and [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potions of Healing]]
  * **Necromancer**: [[rpd:bones_staff_item|Bones Staff]], [[rpd:cloth_armor_item|Cloth Armor]] and a [[rpd:ankh_item|Ankh]]

==== Class Advancement ====
  * As heroes gain levels, they unlock [[rpd:hero_subclasses|Subclasses]] that offer more specialized playstyles
  * Subclasses are unlocked by using weapons of specific classes (melee, missile, or spell)

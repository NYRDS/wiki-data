====== Ice Key ======

==== Description ====
A special key that appears to be made of enchanted ice. It can unlock specific ice-themed doors or containers.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Ice Enchantment:** Made with magical ice properties
  * **Specific Locks:** Only opens certain types of doors or containers
  * **Theme Consistency:** Related to ice-themed areas of the dungeon

==== Uses ====
  * Opens ice-themed locked doors
  * Unlocks special ice-themed containers
  * May be required for access to specific dungeon areas

==== Notes ====
Ice Keys are specialized keys that unlock ice-themed locked areas of the dungeon, often found in frozen or cold regions.

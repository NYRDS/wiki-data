====== Freeze Globe ======

**Freeze Globe** is an advanced spell available primarily to Mages in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Freeze Globe creates a globe of freezing energy that immobilizes enemies within its area of effect. It's more powerful than simple cold attacks and can affect multiple enemies simultaneously.

==== Stats ====
* Mana Cost: High
* Range: Medium
* Effect: Freezes multiple targets in place

==== Usage ====
This spell is particularly useful for:
  * Stopping groups of aggressive enemies
  * Creating breathing room during difficult encounters
  * Setting up opportunities for area attacks
  * Preventing dangerous enemies from acting

==== Classes ====
  * **Mage**: Natural affinity, advanced spell in elemental magic skill tree
  * **Other classes**: May learn through class-specific abilities

==== Strategy ====
Freeze Globe is most effective when used to control groups of enemies, allowing for more strategic combat. It can be combined with other spells or attacks for devastating effects.

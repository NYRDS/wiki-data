====== Rat King ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_RatKing.png|Rat King }}

The **Rat King** is a unique NPC in Remixed Dungeon found in a special Rat King's lair. It is associated with a quest that rewards the player with the [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]].

==== Stats ====
  * **Type:** NPC/Boss
  * **Level:** 5
  * **HP:** 30
  * **Attack:** 15
  * **Defense:** 20
  * **Damage:** 4-10
  * **EXP:** 1 (for defeating)
  * **Speed:** 2.0Ã— normal

==== Description ====
The Rat King is a powerful rat-like entity that appears in a unique area within the dungeon. Despite being aggressive if attacked, it initially behaves more like an NPC, sleeping in its lair until disturbed.

==== Behavior ====
  * Initially sleeps in its room until approached
  * When approached, it becomes agitated and starts wandering
  * If attacked multiple times, it becomes hostile and attacks the player
  * Has a unique interaction system - it doesn't immediately become hostile when approached
  * Drops the [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]] as a reward

==== Quest Mechanics ====
  * The Rat King quest is initiated by finding the [[rpd:rat_king_crown_item|Rat King Crown]] elsewhere in the dungeon
  * The crown teleports the player to the Rat King's lair
  * Players can interact with the Rat King multiple times before it becomes hostile
  * The quest is part of the early game progression system

==== Strategy ====
  * Approach carefully and understand the interaction system before attacking
  * The Rat King is not initially hostile, so players can observe its behavior
  * Be prepared for a difficult fight once it becomes hostile
  * The quest reward (Rat King Crown) is important for progression

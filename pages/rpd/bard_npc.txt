====== BardNPC ======

{{ rpd:images:bardnpc_sprite.png|BardNPC Sprite }}

BardNPC is a non-player character (NPC) found in the town area of Remixed Dungeon. The town bard is playing his lute and provides a peaceful presence in the town.

==== Description ====
The town bard is playing his lute. As an NPC, he doesn't engage in combat but adds to the atmosphere of the town.

==== Behavior ====
  * Spawns in town/inn levels
  * Performs an action each turn where musical notes appear above his head (using NOTE and SPECK_UP particle effects)
  * Interacts with the player by saying "BardSong_1" (the actual text is defined in the string resources)
  * Uses "NpcDefault" AI behavior when spawned

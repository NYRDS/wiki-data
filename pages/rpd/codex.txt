====== Codex ======

Codex - book unique for [[start|Remixed Pixel Dungeon]] found in libraries.

The codex describes some of the features of the game mechanics and some stories written by fans of the game.

In practice, codex are not valuable items, but they contain information that may be useful for beginners.

In modding, arbitrary text can be assigned to codex.

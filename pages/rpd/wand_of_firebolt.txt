====== Wand of Firebolt ======

{{ rpd:images:wand_of_firebolt_item.png|Wand of Firebolt }}

==== Description ====
The Wand of Firebolt is a magical wand that shoots bolts of fire at enemies, potentially setting them on fire.

==== Stats ====
  - **Max Charges:** Varies by level
  - **Delay:** 1 turn
  - **Damage:** Varies by level
  - **Element:** Fire

==== Abilities ====
  - Shoots firebolts that can ignite enemies
  - Fire damage may cause [[rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] status effect
  - Effective against enemies weak to fire

==== Upgrading ====
  - Upgrading increases damage and maximum charges
  - Does not affect the chance to cause burning

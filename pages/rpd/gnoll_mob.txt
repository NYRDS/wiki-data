====== Gnoll Scout (Mob) ======

{{ rpd:images:gnoll_sprite.png|Gnoll Scout Sprite }}

==== Description ====
**Gnoll Scouts** are hyena-like humanoids that dwell in sewers and dungeons, venturing up to raid the surface from time to time. They are regular members of their pack and are not as strong as brutes or as intelligent as shamans.

==== Stats ====
* **HP**: 12
* **HT**: 12
* **Defense Skill**: 4
* **Attack Skill**: 11
* **Damage**: 2-5
* **Armor**: 2 DR
* **EXP for Kill**: 2
* **Maximum Level**: 12
* **Loot**: Gold with 50% chance

==== Notes ====
Gnoll Scouts are common enemies found in the early dungeon areas, particularly sewers. They represent the basic gnoll warrior in the pack structure.

**Note**: This is the dungeon enemy. For the playable hero class, see [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]].

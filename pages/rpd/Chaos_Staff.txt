====== Chaos Staff ======

==== Description ====
Staff imbued with Power Of Chaos! What else a mage can dream or fear of?

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** Varies based on enchantment
  * **Durability:** Normal
  * **Strength Required:** Based on wielder's magic level

==== Special Properties ====
  * A powerful magical staff infused with chaotic energy
  * Can be created by fusing a Chaos Crystal with a wand
  * Provides powerful chaotic magical effects

==== Notes ====
Chaos Staff is a high-level magical weapon created by fusing a Chaos Crystal with a wand. It provides mages with powerful chaotic magic at the cost of some predictability.

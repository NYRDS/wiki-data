====== Heal ======

{{ rpd:images:healing_spell_icon.png|Heal Spell Icon }}

**Heal** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon with Witchcraft Affinity (also available to Priest and PlagueDoctor affinities).

==== Description ====
The Heal spell restores a portion of the target's health instantly, making it valuable for emergency situations. The healing amount is calculated as 20% of the target's max HP per skill level of the caster.

==== Stats ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Witchcraft (also available in Priest and PlagueDoctor affinities)
  * **Targeting:** Char (can target any character on the map)
  * **Level:** 2
  * **Mana Cost:** 5
  * **Special Effects:** Heals the target character

==== Mechanics ====
The spell heals the target character for (Max HP / 5 * Caster's skill level) hit points, with a minimum of 1 point healed.

==== Usage ====
The Heal spell can be used for:
  * Healing yourself in combat situations
  * Healing allies or pets
  * Emergency health restoration during dangerous encounters

==== Classes ====
Classes that can use this spell include:
  * [[rpd:spell_affinities|Witchcraft]] affinity users
  * [[rpd:spell_affinities|Priest]] affinity users
  * [[rpd:spell_affinities|PlagueDoctor]] affinity users
  * Other classes that gain access through special means

==== Strategy ====
How to effectively use the Heal spell:
  * Best used when health is critically low
  * Can heal allies and pets as well as yourself
  * Good for supporting pets during challenging fights
  * The healing scales with your skill level

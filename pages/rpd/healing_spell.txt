====== Healing ======

**Healing** is a restorative spell available to several classes in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Healing restores a portion of the hero's health instantly. This spell is essential for survival during dangerous dungeon expeditions, especially when healing items are scarce.

==== Stats ====
* Mana Cost: Moderate
* Effect: Restores a percentage of max HP

==== Usage ====
This spell is particularly useful for:
  * Recovering from damage in combat
  * Conserving healing potions for more critical moments
  * Sustaining health during long dungeon runs
  * Emergency healing situations

==== Classes ====
  * **Priest**: Natural affinity, enhanced healing effects
  * **Gnoll**: Some healing abilities
  * **Other classes**: May learn through class-specific abilities

==== Strategy ====
Healing should be used strategically to balance mana usage with health preservation. It's particularly valuable when healing potions are low or when facing multiple challenging enemies.

====== Rotting Fist ======

{{ rpd:images:rotting_fist_mob.png|Rotting Fist }}

==== Description ====
The **Rotting Fist** is one of the guardians of Yog, the God of Death. It is encountered in the Goo Depths and is one of the secondary bosses protecting Yog along with Burning Fist, Yogs' Brain, Yogs' Heart, and Yogs' Teeth.

==== Stats ====
* **HP**: 500
* **HT**: 500
* **Defense Skill**: 25
* **Attack Skill**: 36
* **Damage**: 34-46
* **DR (Damage Resistance)**: 15
* **EXP for Kill**: 0 (Does not grant experience)

==== Abilities ====
* **Ooze Attack**: Has a 33% chance (Random.Int(3) == 0) to inflict Ooze status effect on hit
* **Summoning**: When damaged, summons all nearby mobs to its location
* **Regeneration**: Heals 10 HP when standing in water (and HP is below max)
* **Immunities**: Immune to Amok, Sleep, Terror, Poison, and Burning
* **Resistance**: Resistant to Toxic Gas
* **No Pet**: Cannot be turned into a pet

==== Special Mechanics ====
* The Rotting Fist will call for help from nearby mobs when taking damage
* It regenerates health when standing in water, making it more dangerous in certain terrain

==== Drops ====
* No standard drops (as it's a Yog guardian)

==== Location ====
* Found in the Goo Depths (last level) as one of Yog's guardians

====== Statue ======

**Statues** are static objects found throughout the dungeon that may have various properties and functions.

==== Types of Statues ====
  * **Decorative**: Serve only as environmental elements
  * **Interactive**: May contain items or activate when touched
  * **Animated**: Can come to life as enemies when disturbed
  * **Locked**: Connected to mechanical systems and may be sealed

==== Known Statues ====
  * [[rpd:gold_statue|Gold Statue]] - Can grant special rewards
  * [[rpd:statue|Statue]] - Various types of statues with different properties

==== Interaction ====
  * Some statues can be smashed to reveal contents
  * Others may trigger harmful effects when disturbed
  * Some may be part of puzzles or mechanical systems

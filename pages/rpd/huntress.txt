====== Huntress ======

{{ :rpd:huntress.png?nolink&50|}}

The **Huntress** is a playable class in Remixed Dungeon specializing in ranged combat and marksmanship. She excels at ranged attacks and has special abilities related to throwing weapons.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Focus:** Ranged combat and throwing weapons
  * **Special Ability:** Enhanced throwing weapon effectiveness and accuracy
  * **Playstyle:** Ranged attacker with good survivability

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **Ranged Mastery:** Thrown weapons have increased accuracy and range
  * **Throwing Expertise:** Thrown weapons don't break as often
  * **Marksmanship:** Better chance to hit at range
  * **Special Armor:** Class armor ability provides special ranged bonuses

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Weapon:** Throwing stones (x20)
  * **Armor:** Armor with 12 points of damage absorption
  * **Food:** Additional food ration

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Effective ranged combat without need for bows or arrows
  * Thrown weapons don't break as frequently
  * Good accuracy with ranged attacks
  * Can engage enemies from safe distance
  * Good mobility while maintaining ranged advantage

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Lower health than Warrior
  * Weaker in close combat situations
  * Dependent on ranged weapons/ammo supply
  * May struggle against fast-moving enemies

==== Class Armor ====
Huntresses have special class armor that allows them to perform a powerful shot with their current ranged weapon, dealing significant damage and potentially stunning the target.

==== Strategy ====
  * Maintain distance to use ranged advantages effectively
  * Conserve and collect thrown weapons
  * Use terrain to maintain safe distance
  * Take advantage of the accuracy bonus
  * Position carefully to avoid being cornered

====== Huntress ======

==== Description ====
The Huntress is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. She is an expert with ranged weapons and tracking.

==== Starting Perks ====
  * **15 Health:** Starts with 15 points of Health
  * **Upgradeable Boomerang:** Starts with a unique upgradeable boomerang
  * **Missile Weapon Proficiency:** Proficient with missile weapons and gets a damage bonus for excessive strength when using them
  * **Enhanced Dewdrop Healing:** Gains more health from dewdrops than other classes
  * **Monster Detection:** Senses neighboring monsters even if they are hidden behind obstacles

==== Class Abilities ====
The Huntress has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Sniper:** Able to detect weak points in an enemy's armor, effectively ignoring it when using a missile weapon
  * **Warden:** Has a strong connection with forces of nature, able to gather dewdrops and seeds from plants; trampling high grass grants temporary armor buff

==== Special Notes ====
The Huntress specializes in ranged combat and has natural tracking abilities that make her excellent for exploring.

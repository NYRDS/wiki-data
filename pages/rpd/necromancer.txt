====== Necromancer ======

The **Necromancer** is a playable character class in Remixed Dungeon that specializes in dark magic and commanding undead creatures.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Focus:** Necromancy and undead control
  * **Special Ability:** Ability to consume souls and summon undead minions
  * **Playstyle:** Summoner that relies on undead followers to fight

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **Soul Consumption:** Gains souls from defeated enemies for casting necromancy spells
  * **Undead Summoning:** Ability to summon deathlings and other undead minions
  * **Necromancy Spells:** Uses soul-based magic with various effects
  * **Necromancer Equipment:** Special armor and robes that enhance necromantic abilities

==== Necromancer Equipment ====
  * [[rpd:necromancer_armor|Bone Armor]] - Allows summoning multiple deathlings
  * [[rpd:necromancer_robe|Necromancer Robe]] - Allows summoning single deathlings
  * Special items that enhance soul collection or necromancy effects

==== Summoned Minions ====
  * [[rpd:deathling|Deathling]] - Basic undead minion that grows stronger with hero level
  * Potentially other undead creatures

==== Necromancy Spells ====
  * [[rpd:summon_deathling|Summon Deathling]] - Creates an undead minion
  * [[rpd:dark_sacrifice|Dark Sacrifice]] - Sacrifice minion for an explosion
  * [[rpd:exhumation|Exhumation]] - Raise dead from graves
  * [[rpd:raise_dead|Raise Dead]] - Revive recently defeated creatures
  * [[rpd:necrotism|Necrotism]] - Spread a magical plague

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Powerful summoning abilities
  * Can build an army of undead minions
  * Unique playstyle focused on dark magic
  * Can turn defeated enemies into souls for power

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Dependent on having minions to fight effectively
  * Requires strategic management of souls and minions
  * Different from traditional class mechanics

==== Advanced Path ====
The Necromancer has an advanced path:
  * **Lich** - An undead form that increases necromantic powers but reduces physical capabilities

==== Class Unlocking ====
The Necromancer class may need to be unlocked by:
  * Defeating the Lich boss with another class
  * Completing specific necromancy-related challenges

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on building and managing your undead army
  * Protect your summoned minions when possible
  * Collect souls efficiently to cast powerful spells
  * Use minions as shields and damage dealers

====== Necromancer ======

==== Description ====
The Necromancer is an unlockable class in Remixed Dungeon. He practices the dark arts of death magic.

==== Starting Perks ====
  * **15 Health:** Starts with 15 points of Health
  * **Mana-based Death Spells:** Uses mana to cast Death spells
  * **Necromancer's Robe:** Starts with a unique Necromancer's robe that grants 1 mana point for each slain creature
  * **Level 7 Skeleton Key:** Starts with a level 7 skeleton key
  * **Identified Healing:** Potions of Healing are identified from the beginning

==== Class Abilities ====
The Necromancer has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Lich:** A necromancer who reached the pinnacle of his abilities, existing between life and death; excels in necromancy but has greatly reduced combat skills compared to ordinary people

==== Special Notes ====
The Necromancer specializes in raising undead followers and consuming souls. The class can be unlocked by slaying the Lich boss with any other class.

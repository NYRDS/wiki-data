====== Elemental Affinity ======

Elemental Affinity is one of the primary magical affinities in Remixed Pixel Dungeon, focusing on spells related to fire, ice, nature, and other elemental forces. The [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] class has Elemental as its primary affinity.

==== Associated Spells ====
Spells that fall under the Elemental affinity include:
  * [[rpd:wind_gust_spell|Wind Gust]] - Creates a blast of wind that can push enemies and activate switches
  * [[rpd:ignite_spell|Ignite]] - Sets enemies or area on fire
  * [[rpd:freeze_globe_spell|Freeze Globe]] - Creates an area of freezing effect
  * [[rpd:lightning_bolt_spell|Lightning Bolt]] - Casts a bolt of lightning at an enemy
  * [[rpd:root_spell|Root]] - Immobilizes enemies in place
  * [[rpd:nature_armor_spell|Nature Armor]] - Provides protection through natural means

==== Classes with Elemental Affinity ====
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Primary affinity for the Mage class
  * Other classes may gain access to elemental spells through special means

==== Mechanics ====
Elemental affinity spells often involve:
  * Direct damage effects
  * Area of effect abilities
  * Environmental manipulation
  * Element-based status effects like burning or freezing

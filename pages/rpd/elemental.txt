====== Elemental Affinity ======

Elemental Affinity is a spell category in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that includes fire, ice, nature, and other elemental magic spells. This is the primary affinity for the [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] class.

==== Spells with Elemental Affinity ====
  * [[rpd:fire|Fire Magic]] Spells:
    * [[rpd:ignite_spell|Ignite]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_firebolt|Wand of Firebolt]]
    * [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame|Potion of Liquid Flame]]
  * [[rpd:frost|Frost Magic]] Spells:
    * [[rpd:wand_of_frost|Wand of Frost]]
    * [[rpd:potion_of_frost|Potion of Frost]]
  * [[rpd:nature_armor_spell|Nature Armor]]
    * [[rpd:magic_torch_spell|Magic Torch]]
    * [[rpd:regeneration|Healing effects]]

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Mages** have the highest proficiency with elemental spells
  * Damage and effectiveness are maximized when cast by heroes with elemental affinity
  * Some elemental spells can interact with environmental features
  * Elemental spells often have secondary effects beyond direct damage

==== Benefits ====
  * Mages equipped with elemental spells have enhanced damage output
  * Elemental spells often have area effects, allowing for crowd control
  * Can be used to solve environmental puzzles
  * Some elemental effects can trigger environmental interactions (water + fire = steam, etc.)

==== Strategy ====
  * Mages should prioritize elemental spells for maximum combat effectiveness
  * Combine elemental spells with environmental features for maximum effect
  * Be aware of enemy resistances to specific elements
  * Elemental spells can be used for tactical positioning, forcing enemies into disadvantageous positions

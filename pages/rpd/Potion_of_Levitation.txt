====== Potion of Levitation ======

==== Description ====
This potion will cause the drinker to become weightless and float in the air for a short time.

==== Effects ====
  * **Levitation:** Allows the drinker to float over traps and certain terrain
  * **Duration:** Temporary effect
  * **Immunity:** Provides temporary immunity to harm from certain hazards

==== Special Properties ====
  * Allows floating over pits and other terrain hazards
  * Prevents triggering pressure plates and other weight-based traps
  * Does not protect against all types of hazards

==== Notes ====
Potion of Levitation is particularly useful for navigating dangerous areas with traps or pits. However, it won't protect against all hazards while levitating.

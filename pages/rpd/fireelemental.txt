====== Fire Elemental ======

{{ rpd:images:fireelemental_sprite.png|Fire Elemental Sprite }}

==== Description ====
**Fire Elementals** are wandering fire elementals that are a byproduct of summoning greater entities. They are too chaotic in their nature to be controlled by even the most powerful demonologist.

==== Stats ====
* **HP:** 65
* **HT:** 65
* **Defense Skill:** 20
* **Attack Skill:** 25
* **Damage:** 16-20
* **Armor:** 5 DR
* **EXP for Kill:** 10
* **Maximum Level:** 20
* **Loot:** [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame|Potion of Liquid Flame]] (10% chance)

==== Special Properties ====
* **Flying:** Fire Elementals can fly over terrain obstacles
* **Fire Immunity:** Immune to [[rpd:fire|Fire]] and [[rpd:burning|Burning]] effects
* **Bleeding Immunity:** Immune to [[rpd:bleeding|Bleeding]] effects
* **Wand Immunity:** Immune to [[rpd:wand_of_firebolt|Wand of Firebolt]] effects
* **Psionic Blast Immunity:** Immune to [[rpd:scroll_of_psionic_blast|Scroll of Psionic Blast]] effects
* **Carcass Chance:** 0% chance to drop food when killed (as an elemental creature)

==== Special Abilities ====
* **Burning Attack:** On successful hit, has a 50% chance to inflict [[rpd:burning|Burning]] on the target
* **Burning Healing:** When affected by Burning, will heal a random amount up to 4x their max HP
* **Frost Vulnerability:** When affected by Frost, will take damage up to 2/3 of their max HP

==== Location ====
Fire Elementals can typically be found in:
* [[rpd:caves|Caves]] (City level)
* [[rpd:elementals|Elemental]]-themed areas
* Environments with [[rpd:fire|Fire]]-based mechanics

==== Tactics ====
* Fire Elementals are dangerous due to their high damage and burning effect
* Avoid using fire-based attacks as they are completely immune
* Frost-based attacks are especially effective against them
* Their flying ability allows them to ignore terrain
* Be prepared for their burning attack effect
* They're immune to many status effects, making them resilient

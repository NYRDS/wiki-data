The Lich is the Necromancer's subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Unlocking ====
The Lich subclass can be unlocked by using a Black Skull of Mastery (obtained from Necropolis) after achieving maximum skill points.

==== Special Mechanics ====
The Lich has immunities to multiple status effects:
  * Paralysis
  * Toxic Gas
  * Terror
  * Death
  * Amok
  * Blindness
  * Sleep
  * Poison
  * Vertigo
  * Bleeding

==== Special Armor ====
Using an Armor Kit on any armor will transform it into [[rpd:necromancer_armor|Necromancer Armor]], which is specifically designed for this subclass.

==== Advantages ====
  * Extensive immunities to status effects
  * Very high survivability against various dangers
  * Resistance to many common enemy abilities
  * Can withstand conditions that would incapacitate other characters

==== Disadvantages ====
  * More difficult to unlock (requires maximum skill points)
  * Special unlock method (Black Skull of Mastery)
  * Fewer immunities might apply in early game

=======
{{ rpd:images:lich_sprite.png|Lich Sprite  }}

The **Lich** is an advanced form of the Necromancer class. It's a necromancer who has reached the pinnacle of his abilities, existing between life and death.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Focus:** Advanced necromancy and undead control
  * **Special Ability:** Extensive immunities and powerful necromantic abilities
  * **Playstyle:** Undead specialist with reduced physical combat but stronger magic

==== Starting Perks ====
  * **Necromancy Mastery:** Exists between life and death, excelling in necromancy
  * **Reduced Combat Skills:** Combat skills are greatly lower than those of an ordinary person
  * **Soul Capacity:** Soul capacity is doubled compared to regular necromancers
  * **Strength Penalty:** Loses strength points upon becoming a Lich

==== Class Immunities ====
  * **Extensive Immunities:** The Lich is immune to many status effects including Paralysis, Toxic Gas, Terror, Death, Amok, Blindness, Sleep, Poison, Vertigo, and Bleeding

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Necrotism:** Can use the "Necrotism" ability which spreads a magic plague that may infect any creature in the vicinity, dead or alive
  * **Death Rejection:** Powers reject death itself, but causes loss of collected souls and possessions
  * **Enhanced Undead Control:** Superior control over undead minions

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Extensive immunities to status effects
  * Doubled soul capacity compared to regular necromancers
  * Powerful necromantic abilities
  * Undead nature makes it resilient in dangerous environments

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Greatly reduced combat skills compared to ordinary people
  * Loses strength points upon becoming a Lich
  * Death rejection causes loss of collected souls and possessions

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on necromantic abilities rather than physical combat
  * Take advantage of extensive immunities to navigate dangerous areas
  * Use doubled soul capacity for more powerful spells
  * Manage the trade-offs of the undead nature carefully

==== Class Unlocking ====
The Lich class is achieved by using a [[rpd:black_skull_of_mastery|Black Skull of Mastery]] as a Necromancer. This transformation grants powerful necromantic abilities but reduces physical combat effectiveness. The [[rpd:lich_mob|Lich boss]] drops a Black Skull of Mastery when defeated by a Necromancer character.

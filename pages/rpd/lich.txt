====== Lich ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_Lich.png|Lich }}

The **Lich** is an advanced [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] [[rpd:subclass|subclass]] that can be unlocked through special means. This is the strongest form a Necromancer can achieve, gaining powerful undead abilities.

==== Unlocking ====
The Lich subclass can be unlocked by obtaining the [[rpd:black_skull_of_mastery|Black Skull of Mastery]] and using it on a level 10+ [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]]. The Black Skull of Mastery can be obtained by defeating the [[rpd:lich_mob|Lich]] boss in the Necropolis.

==== Abilities ====
  * Advanced [[rpd:necromancy|necromancy]] powers
  * Enhanced control over undead minions
  * Resistance to certain magical effects
  * Access to unique spells and items

====== Lich ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_Lich.png|Lich }}

The **Lich** is a powerful undead creature and boss in Remixed Dungeon that serves as a unlock mechanism for the Lich subclass.

==== Description ====
The Lich is a powerful and dangerous undead boss found in the Necropolis level. It's both a challenging enemy to defeat and a necessary step to unlock the Lich subclass for the Necromancer class. This mob has powerful magic abilities and considerable health, making it one of the more challenging bosses in the game.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** High (scales with dungeon depth)
  * **Attack:** High magical damage
  * **Defense:** Moderate to high
  * **Speed:** Moderate
  * **EXP:** Very high (one of the highest in the game)
  * **Level:** High (boss level)

==== Abilities ====
  * **Magic Attacks:** Uses powerful magical attacks against the player
  * **Summoning:** Can summon additional undead creatures
  * **Teleportation:** May teleport around the battlefield
  * **Area Effects:** Uses area-of-effect spells

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Subclass Unlock:** Defeating the Lich unlocks the Lich subclass for Necromancer
  * **Item Reward:** Has a chance to drop the [[rpd:black_skull_of_mastery|Black Skull of Mastery]]
  * **Undead Nature:** Is an undead creature with all associated immunities and weaknesses

==== Strategy ====
  * **Preparation:** Ensure good equipment, potions, and scrolls before engaging
  * **Area Awareness:** Be aware of summoned minions as well as the Lich itself
  * **Distance Management:** Control distance to avoid area attacks
  * **Disruption:** Disrupt summoning abilities when possible
  * **Healing Items:** Bring healing items as the fight may be prolonged

====== Debuffs ======

Debuffs are negative status effects that temporarily weaken the player character in Remixed Dungeon. They are the opposite of [[rpd:buffs|buffs]].

==== Common Debuffs ====
  * [[rpd:poison_buff|Poison]] - Causes continuous damage over time
  * [[rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] - Causes damage and can burn items
  * [[rpd:paralysis_buff|Paralysis]] - Prevents action until the effect ends
  * [[rpd:blindness_buff|Blindness]] - Reduces vision and prevents identification of items
  * [[rpd:weakness_buff|Weakness]] - Reduces strength and damage
  * [[rpd:slow_buff|Slow]] - Reduces movement speed
  * [[rpd:terror_buff|Terror]] - Forces the affected character to flee from enemy
  * [[rpd:charm_buff|Charm]] - Causes enemies to temporarily become friendly
  * [[rpd:amok_buff|Amok]] - Causes the affected character to attack randomly

==== Removal ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] can remove most debuffs from the player
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]] can remove some cursed debuffs
  * Some debuffs expire automatically after a certain number of turns
  * [[rpd:healing_spell|Heal spell]] and [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] remove some debuffs

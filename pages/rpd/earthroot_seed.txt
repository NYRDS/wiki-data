====== Seed of Earthroot ======

==== Description ====
A seed from the Earthroot plant that can be planted or consumed for various effects.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be planted to grow Earthroot plants
  * May have healing properties when consumed
  * Part of the game's farming/alchemy system
  * Dropped by [[rpd:earth_elemental|Earth Elementals]]

==== Usage ====
Seeds can be planted in soil or consumed directly for various effects depending on the specific seed type.

==== Notes ====
The Earthroot seed is one of many different seeds in the game that can be used for farming and alchemy purposes.

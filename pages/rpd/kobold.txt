====== Kobold ======

{{ rpd:images:kobold_sprite.png|Kobold Sprite  }}The **Kobold** is a small humanoid enemy found in the [[rpd:ice_caves|Ice Caves]].

==== Description ====
Kobolds are small, aggressive creatures that inhabit the Ice Caves. While individually not very powerful, they can pose a threat in groups.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Low to Moderate
* Defense: Low
* Attack: Moderate
* Speed: Moderate

==== Tactics ====
Kobolds are most dangerous in groups, where they can overwhelm the hero through numbers. They're generally easier to defeat individually, but caution is advised when facing several at once. They're most effectively dealt with through quick, direct combat.

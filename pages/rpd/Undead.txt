====== Undead Mobs ======

==== Description ====
Undead Mobs are a category of monsters in Remixed Dungeon that have been reanimated after death through various magical means.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Generally immune to fear and terror effects
  * Often resistant or immune to certain status effects like paralysis or sleep
  * May be affected differently by healing/dealing damage mechanics
  * Some undead can be turned or repelled by certain items or abilities

==== Notes ====
Different classes may have special abilities or bonuses when fighting undead creatures. Understanding their common weaknesses can be advantageous when navigating areas with many undead enemies.

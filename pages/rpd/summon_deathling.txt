====== Summon Deathling ======

**Summon Deathling** is a necromantic spell available primarily to Necromancers in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Summon Deathling creates a temporary undead minion that fights alongside the hero. The number of deathlings that can be summoned simultaneously is limited by the hero's level.

==== Stats ====
* Mana Cost: Moderate
* Range: Self
* Effect: Summons a deathling minion

==== Usage ====
This spell is particularly useful for:
  * Gaining additional combat strength
  * Distracting enemies during combat
  * Tanking damage for the hero
  * Exploring dangerous areas safely

==== Classes ====
  * **Necromancer**: Natural affinity, core class ability
  * **Other classes**: May learn through class-specific abilities

==== Strategy ====
Deathlings scale in power based on the hero's level, making them more valuable as the hero progresses deeper into the dungeon. Managing your summon limit effectively is crucial to maximize the benefit of this spell.

====== Spider Elite Guard ======

{{ rpd:images:spiderelite_sprite.png|Spider Elite Guard }}

The **Spider Elite Guard** is a powerful spider mob found in the Spider Nest area of the dungeon. These elite guards are sworn to protect their kingdom and are equipped with venom that can disorient their targets.

==== Mob Overview ====
  * **Type**: Elite Spider Guard
  * **Level**: Spider Nest level 10s
  * **Special Ability**: Venom that causes Vertigo (disorientation) effect
  * **Behavior**: Defensive, protecting the spider colony
  * **Spawn Rate**: 0.3 on level 10s of Spider Nest
  * **Gender**: Masculine

==== Stats ====
  * **HP/HT**: 40
  * **Attack Skill**: 20
  * **Defense Skill**: 18
  * **Damage**: 10-15
  * **Damage Reduction (DR)**: 8
  * **Speed**: 1.2x normal
  * **EXP for Kill**: 5
  * **Max Level**: 13
  * **Loot Chance**: 7% (drops Mystery Meat)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * When attacking, has a 20% chance to apply the Vertigo debuff to the target for (caster level) turns
  * Vertigo causes the target to have difficulty moving in a straight line
  * The elite guard's venom is specifically used to disorient their target
  * Serves as a stronger variant of regular Spider Guards

==== Strategy ====
  * Spider Elite Guards are tougher than regular spiders and have special crowd control abilities
  * Be prepared for the Vertigo effect which makes movement difficult
  * The Vertigo debuff can significantly impact your ability to navigate or escape
  * Consider bringing items that can remove debuffs if fighting multiple elite guards

==== Obtaining ====
Spider Elite Guards can be found in the Spider Nest area:
  * Level 10s: Spawn rate of 0.3 (relatively rare)

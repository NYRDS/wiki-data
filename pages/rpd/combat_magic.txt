====== Combat Magic ======

**Combat Magic** is one of the magic affinities available in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. It is the default affinity for the [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior class]] and provides access to various combat-oriented spells.

==== Spells ====
Spells associated with Combat Magic include:
  * [[rpd:body_armor_spell|Body Armor]]
  * [[rpd:smash_spell|Smash]]
  * [[rpd:die_hard_spell|Die Hard]]
  * [[rpd:dash_spell|Dash]]

==== Mechanics ====
Combat magic spells are designed to enhance the user's combat effectiveness through various means:
  * Defensive buffs like armor and damage mitigation
  * Offensive abilities that enhance damage or apply status effects
  * Utility spells that improve combat positioning or survivability

==== Classes ====
Classes that use Combat Magic:
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - Primary affinity

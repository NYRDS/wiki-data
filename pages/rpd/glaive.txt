====== Glaive ======

{{rpd:images:glaive_sprite.png?200|Glaive Sprite}}

==== Description ====
A glaive is a polearm weapon consisting of a single-edged blade on the end of a pole. It's a powerful melee weapon with excellent reach.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** High
  * **Reach:** Extended reach compared to standard melee weapons
  * **Durability:** Moderate to high
  * **Strength Required:** High

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Extended Reach:** Can attack enemies at range
  * **Sweeping Attacks:** May be able to attack multiple adjacent enemies
  * **Polearm Mechanics:** Unique combat mechanics due to its length

==== Notes ====
The glaive is an excellent weapon for maintaining distance from enemies while still dealing significant damage. Its extended reach makes it useful in narrow corridors and for kiting enemies.

====== Status Effects ======

Status effects in Remixed Dungeon are temporary conditions that modify character abilities, attributes, or game mechanics. They can be beneficial (buffs) or harmful (debuffs).

==== Beneficial Effects (Buffs) ====
  * [[rpd:regeneration|Regeneration]] - Gradually heals HP
  * [[rpd:haste_spell|Haste]] - Increases movement and attack speed
  * [[rpd:invisibility_buff|Invisibility]] - Makes you undetectable to enemies
  * [[rpd:levitation|Levitation]] - Allows movement over traps and special terrain
  * [[rpd:shield|Shield]] - Provides temporary damage absorption
  * [[rpd:strength|Strength Boost]] - Temporary increase in strength
  * [[rpd:heroism|Heroism]] - Enhances all combat abilities
  * [[rpd:barkskin|Barkskin]] - Reduces physical damage taken
  * [[rpd:blessed|Blessed]] - Increases accuracy and evasion
  * [[rpd:candle_of_mind_vision|Candle of Mind Vision]] - Reveals nearby dungeon layout
  * [[rpd:awareness|Awareness]] - Reveals all creatures on the level
  * [[rpd:light|Light]] - Provides light in dark areas
  * [[rpd:mind_vision|Mind Vision]] - See all creatures on the level
  * [[rpd:mana_regeneration|Mana Regeneration]] - Restores mana over time
  * [[rpd:shadows|Shadows]] - Hides you in darkness, reducing detection
  * [[rpd:speed|Speed]] - Increases movement speed
  * [[rpd:snipers_mark|Sniper's Mark]] - Increases accuracy against marked target
  * [[rpd:combo|Combo]] - Increases damage based on consecutive attacks
  * [[rpd:fury_buff|Fury]] - Increases damage but prevents healing
  * [[rpd:item_action|Item Action]] - Allows quick item usage

==== Harmful Effects (Debuffs) ====
  * [[rpd:poison|Poison]] - Gradually damages HP
  * [[rpd:burning|Burning]] - Damages while on fire
  * [[rpd:bleeding|Bleeding]] - Damages over time
  * [[rpd:paralysis|Paralysis]] - Prevents all actions
  * [[rpd:weakness|Weakness]] - Reduces strength and damage
  * [[rpd:slow_buff|Slow]] - Reduces movement and attack speed
  * [[rpd:blindness|Blindness]] - Reduces accuracy and prevents identification
  * [[rpd:charm_spell|Charm]] - Makes enemies fight for you temporarily
  * [[rpd:roots|Roots]] - Prevents movement
  * [[rpd:ooze|Ooze]] - Deals continuous damage until cleaned
  * [[rpd:stun|Stun]] - Temporarily disables actions
  * [[rpd:terror|Terror]] - Forces character to flee in fear
  * [[rpd:vertigo|Vertigo]] - Causes disorientation, random movement
  * [[rpd:frost_buff|Frost]] - Causes cold damage and prevents action
  * [[rpd:sleep|Sleep]] - Puts character to sleep
  * [[rpd:amok|Amok]] - Forces character to attack randomly
  * [[rpd:cripple|Cripple]] - Reduces movement speed by 50%

==== Duration and Mechanics ====
  * Most status effects have a timed duration that counts down each turn
  * Some effects stack, others overwrite previous instances of the same type
  * Powerful effects may override lesser effects of the same type
  * Duration can be affected by certain class abilities or equipment
  * Some effects have special removal methods (e.g., water removes Ooze)

==== Remedies and Prevention ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] can cure poison, bleeding, blindness, and paralysis
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]] can cure cursed status
  * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]] can remove debuffs
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] can extinguish burning
  * Stepping into water removes burning and ooze effects
  * Some class abilities provide resistance to specific effects
  * Certain equipment provides protection against common status effects

==== Strategic Use ====
  * Apply beneficial effects before challenging encounters
  * Remove harmful effects with appropriate items or abilities
  * Some enemies are particularly vulnerable to specific status effects
  * Certain equipment synergizes well with specific status effects
  * Timing is critical for maximum effect from buffs
  * Debuffs can turn the tide of difficult battles

==== Class Interactions ====
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mages]] can apply various elemental status effects
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogues]] have abilities to grant stealth or apply debuffs
  * [[rpd:priest_class|Priests]] can provide healing and protective buffs
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancers]] can apply and exploit various debuffs
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warriors]] can use certain buffs to enhance combat effectiveness
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntresses]] have abilities that interact with several status effects

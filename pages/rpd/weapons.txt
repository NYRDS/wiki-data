====== Weapons ======

{{ rpd:images:shortsword_sprite.png|Weapons }}

Weapons in Remixed Dungeon are equipment items used to attack enemies. They come in various types and tiers, each with different properties and special abilities.

==== Weapon Categories ====
  * **Melee Weapons:** Used in close combat
    * [[rpd:short_sword|Short Sword]] - Basic sword
    * [[rpd:boomerang|Boomerang]] - Returning thrown weapon
    * [[rpd:bone_saw|Bone Saw]] - Doctor's special weapon
  * **Ranged Weapons:**
    * [[rpd:wooden_bow|Wooden Bow]] - Basic ranged weapon
    * [[rpd:elven_bow|Elven Bow]] - Elf's specialty weapon
    * [[rpd:chaos_bow|Chaos Bow]] - Powerful chaos weapon
  * **Throwing Weapons:** [[rpd:throwing_weapons|Throwing Weapons]]
    * [[rpd:dart|Dart]] - Basic thrown weapon
    * [[rpd:throwing_stone|Throwing Stone]] - Basic thrown stone
    * [[rpd:tomahawk|Tomahawk]] - Throwing axe
    * [[rpd:gnoll_tamahawk|Gnoll Tamahawk]] - Gnoll hero class's special throwing axe (Gnoll's starting weapon)

==== Weapon Properties ====
  * **Damage:** Physical damage dealt per hit (MIN-MAX values in code)
  * **Durability:** How many hits the weapon can take before breaking
  * **Speed:** Attack speed modifier (DLY value in code - 1.0 is normal speed)
  * **Accuracy:** Hit chance modifier (ACU value in code - 1.0 is normal accuracy)
  * **STR:** Strength requirement to wield effectively (STR value in code)
  * **Tier:** Quality level (1-5 for regular weapons; special weapons like ChaosSword are tier 3 with special mechanics)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Enchantments:** Weapons can have special magical properties added via [[rpd:scroll_of_weapon_upgrade|Scroll of Weapon Upgrade]]
  * **Upgrading:** Weapons can be upgraded with Scrolls of Upgrade
  * **Class Bonuses:** Some classes get bonuses with specific weapon types

==== Weapon Tiers ====
  * **Tier 1:** [[rpd:dart|Dart]], [[rpd:throwing_stone|Throwing Stone]], [[rpd:shuriken|Shuriken]]
  * **Tier 2:** [[rpd:short_sword|Short Sword]], [[rpd:hand_axe|Hand Axe]], [[rpd:spear|Spear]], [[rpd:quarterstaff|Quarterstaff]], [[rpd:boomerang|Boomerang]]
  * **Tier 3:** [[rpd:sword|Sword]], [[rpd:mace|Mace]], [[rpd:war_hammer|War Hammer]], [[rpd:chaos_sword|Chaos Sword]]
  * **Tier 4:** [[rpd:longsword|Longsword]], [[rpd:battle_axe|Battle Axe]], [[rpd:flail|Flail]]
  * **Tier 5:** [[rpd:greatsword|Greatsword]], [[rpd:halberd|Halberd]], [[rpd:hand_crossbow|Hand Crossbow]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Choose weapons that complement your class and playstyle
  * Consider the trade-offs between damage, speed, and durability
  * Upgrade weapons when you have the resources and opportunity
  * Consider enchantments that complement your build

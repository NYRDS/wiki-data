====== Weapons ======

{{ rpd:images:item_ShortSword.png|Short Sword }}

**Weapons** are items in Remixed Dungeon that increase the hero's combat effectiveness by providing damage and special abilities.

==== Weapon Types ====
  * [[rpd:melee_weapons|Melee Weapons]] - Close combat weapons
  * [[rpd:ranged_weapons|Ranged Weapons]] - Weapons for distance combat
  * [[rpd:throwing_weapons|Throwing Weapons]] - One-time use ranged weapons
  * [[rpd:wands|Wands]] - Magical weapons using charges

==== Weapon Stats ====
  * **Damage**: The range of damage dealt on successful hits
  * **Speed**: Attack speed relative to normal (lower is faster)
  * **Accuracy**: Bonus to hit chance
  * **Durability**: How much damage the weapon can take before breaking
  * **Strength**: Minimum strength required to use the weapon effectively

==== Weapon Classes ====
  * **Swords**: Balanced weapons with good stats
  * **Axes**: High damage, slower attacks
  * **Maces**: Good balance of damage and speed
  * **Daggers**: Fast attacks, lower damage
  * **Bows**: Ranged weapons requiring [[rpd:arrows|arrows]]
  * **Staves**: Magical weapons with special abilities

==== Special Properties ====
  * [[rpd:enchantments|Enchantments]] - Special magical properties that enhance weapons
  * [[rpd:upgrading|Upgrading]] - Improves weapon stats using scrolls
  * [[rpd:curses|Curses]] - Negative properties that can be found on weapons
  * [[rpd:durability|Durability]] - Wear and tear over time

==== Strategy ====
  * Upgrade your main weapon as you find [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * Switch weapons when you find ones with better damage potential
  * Consider strength requirements when choosing weapons
  * Identify weapons to reveal hidden enchantments or curses

==== Availability ====
  * Found in [[rpd:shops|shops]] and [[rpd:chests|chests]]
  * Dropped by enemies
  * Sometimes [[rpd:alchemy|alchemized]] from other materials

====== Hero Subclasses ======

Hero Subclasses (Mastery) are advanced specializations available when the hero gains a level at depths 6, 10, 14, 18, or 22. Each hero class has access to two unique subclasses that specialize their playstyle.

==== Warrior Subclasses ====
  * [[rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]]: Focuses on melee combat and weapon mastery
  * [[rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]]: Gains strength when health is low

==== Mage Subclasses ====
  * [[rpd:battlemage_subclass|Battle Mage]]: Combines magical and physical combat
  * [[rpd:warlock_subclass|Warlock]]: Gains power from defeated enemies

==== Rogue Subclasses ====
  * [[rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]]: Specializes in surprise attacks and critical hits
  * [[rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]]: Moves faster with higher armor penalties

==== Huntress Subclasses ====
  * [[rpd:spearmaid_subclass|Spearmaid]]: Specializes in melee combat with spears
  * [[rpd:warden_subclass|Warden]]: Gains strength from nature and dew collection

==== Gnoll Subclasses ====
  * [[rpd:shaman_subclass|Shaman]]: Commands spirits and uses nature magic
  * [[rpd:gnoll_warrior_subclass|Gnoll Warrior]]: Focused on physical combat and damage

==== Necromancer Subclasses ====
  * [[rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]]: Transforms into undead with powerful magic
  * [[rpd:soul_eater_subclass|Soul Eater]]: Feeds on souls to power abilities

==== Subclass Mechanics ====
  * Subclasses are chosen after gaining experience at specific levels (6, 10, 14, 18, 22)
  * Each subclass provides unique abilities and bonuses
  * Subclass abilities can only be used by heroes of the corresponding main class
  * Subclasses significantly alter the gameplay experience for each class

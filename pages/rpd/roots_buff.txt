====== Roots Buff ======

{{ rpd:images:roots_buff.png|Roots Effect }}

==== Description ====
Roots is a status effect that immobilizes the target, preventing them from moving freely.

==== Effects ====
  * **Immobilization** - Prevents all movement until the effect ends
  * **Continues During Turn** - Effect persists until the root breaks or duration ends
  * **Break on Damage** - Sometimes breaks when the target takes damage

==== Duration ====
  * Standard duration is 10 turns when applied by [[rpd:scroll_of_curse_item|Scroll of Curse]]
  * Duration may vary based on the source of the effect
  * Some resistances can shorten the duration

==== Sources ====
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_curse_item|Scroll of Curse]]** - One of the possible curse effects
  * **[[rpd:root_trap_level_object|Root Trap]]** - Environmental trap
  * **[[rpd:earth_enchantment|Earth Enchantment]]** - On armor
  * **[[rpd:evil_eyes_mob|Evil Eyes]]** - Gaze attack can root targets

==== Countering ====
  * **Resistance** - Some items provide resistance to root effects
  * **Potions** - [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] can remove the effect
  * **Time** - Effect expires naturally after duration
  * **Breaking free** - Sometimes possible by spending extra turns

==== Strategy ====
  * While rooted, concentrate on ranged attacks or magical abilities
  * Be especially careful of enemies that attack through melee
  * Try to position yourself advantageously before becoming rooted
  * Use the immobility strategically, such as when surrounded by enemies

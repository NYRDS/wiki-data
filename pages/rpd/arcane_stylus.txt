====== Arcane Stylus ======

{{ rpd:images:arcane_stylus_item.png|Arcane Stylus }}
The Arcane Stylus is a magical tool used to inscribe special properties onto scrolls in Remixed Dungeon.
==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Rare
  * **Weight**: 1
  * **Type**: Crafting Tool
==== Description ====
The Arcane Stylus allows the user to inscribe additional magical properties onto blank scrolls, transforming them into special scrolls with enhanced powers.
==== Usage ====
  * Combine with blank scrolls to create special scroll variants
  * Used for advanced scroll crafting
  * Requires blank scrolls to function
  * Single-use tool per inscription
==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in magical or scholarly areas of the dungeon
  * May be available in magical shops
  * Relatively rare but valuable item
==== Special Notes ====
  * Essential for creating special scroll variants
  * Requires blank scrolls to use
  * Can create more powerful scroll effects
  * Valuable for spell-focused characters
  * Part of advanced magical crafting mechanics

This arcane stylus is made of some dark, very hard stone. Using it you can inscribe a glyph on armor or enchantment on a weapon, but you have no power over choosing what it will be, the stylus will decide it for you.
==== Properties ====
  * **Durability:** Single use item
  * **Class Restriction:** Can be used by all hero classes
  * **Curses:** Cannot be used on cursed items
==== Function ====
  * **Inscription:** Allows inscribing random glyphs on armor or enchantments on weapons
  * **Random Effects:** The specific glyph or enchantment is randomly determined when used
  * **Equipment Enhancement:** Improves items with magical properties
  * **Target Items:** Can be used on armor, weapons, and [[rpd:blank_scroll|Blank Scrolls]]
==== Special Interactions ====
  * **With Armor:** Adds a random [[rpd:glyph|glyph]] to armor
  * **With Weapons:** Adds a random [[rpd:enchantment|enchantment]] to weapons
  * **With Blank Scrolls:** Transforms the blank scroll into a random [[rpd:scroll|scroll]]
==== Strategy ====
  * Best used on high-quality or enchanted equipment to maximize benefit
  * Consider the risks vs. rewards as effects are random - you may get a highly desirable effect or a less useful one
  * Very valuable for improving defensive equipment with useful glyphs
  * Can be used to create scrolls when none are available
  * Most effective when applied to +0 or low-level equipment to see the benefit clearly
==== Obtaining ====
Arcane Styluses can be found:
  * As rare drops in dungeon levels
  * In special treasure areas
  * Potentially for purchase in advanced shops
  * As rewards for completing challenging tasks
==== Related Items ====

  * [[rpd:enchantment|Enchantments]] - Effects that can be added to weapons

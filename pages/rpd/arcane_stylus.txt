====== Arcane Stylus ======

{{ :rpd:arcane_stylus.png?nolink&50|}}

The Arcane Stylus is a special tool made of dark, very hard stone that allows users to inscribe glyphs or runes on items. It's an essential tool for enhancing equipment with magical properties.

==== Description ====
This arcane stylus is made of some dark, very hard stone. Using it you can inscribe a glyph or a rune, but you have no power over choosing what it will be, the stylus will decide it for you.

==== Function ====
  * **Inscription:** Allows inscribing glyphs or runes on equipment
  * **Random Effects:** The specific glyph or rune created is determined by the stylus
  * **Equipment Enhancement:** Improves items with magical properties

==== Usage ====
  * **Target Items:** Can be used on armor and other equipment
  * **Random Selection:** The stylus chooses what to inscribe
  * **Permanent Effect:** The inscription becomes part of the item

==== Obtaining Arcane Styluses ====
Arcane Styluses can be found:
  * As rare drops in dungeon levels
  * In special treasure areas
  * Potentially for purchase in advanced shops
  * As rewards for completing challenging tasks

==== Related Items ====
  * [[rpd:blank_scroll|Blank Scroll]] - Another item that might interact with magical inscription
  * Enhanced armor with glyphs/runes
  * Other magical crafting materials

==== Strategy ====
  * Best used on high-quality or enchanted equipment
  * Consider the risks vs. rewards as effects are random
  * Can significantly improve defensive equipment with useful glyphs

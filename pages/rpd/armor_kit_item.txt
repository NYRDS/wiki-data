====== Armor Kit ======

{{ rpd:images:armor_kit_item.png|Armor Kit }}

The **Armor Kit** is a consumable item in Remixed Dungeon that allows the transformation of any regular armor into its corresponding class-specific armor.

==== Description ====
An armor kit contains the tools and materials necessary to modify regular armor. When used on a piece of armor, it transforms it into the special armor associated with your character's class or subclass.

==== Function ====
  * **Transformation**: When used on any regular armor (e.g. Cloth Armor, Leather Armor, Mail Armor, Scale Armor, Plate Armor), transforms it into the corresponding class armor for your current hero class/subclass
  * **Class-Specific Result**: 
    * Warrior → [[rpd:gladiator_armor_item|Gladiator Armor]] or [[rpd:berserker_armor|Berserker Armor]]
    * Mage → [[rpd:mage_armor_item|Mage Armor]]
    * Rogue → [[rpd:rogue_armor|Rogue Armor]]
    * Huntress → [[rpd:huntress_armor_item|Huntress Armor]]
    * Elf → [[rpd:elf_armor|Elf Armor]]
    * Necromancer → [[rpd:necromancer_armor_item|Necromancer Armor]]
    * Gnoll → [[rpd:gnoll_armor|Gnoll Armor]]

==== Acquisition ====
  * Dropped by the King (DM-300) on floor 7 of the City level
  * Can be very valuable for heroes who wish to change their class armor without finding a new piece

==== Strategy ====
  * Best saved for high-level armor that has been upgraded significantly
  * Allows customization of armor appearance without losing enchantments or upgrades
  * Provides a way to get class armor even if your class wasn't able to use armor that dropped

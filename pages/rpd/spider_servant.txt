====== Spider Servant ======

Spider Servants are specialized spider creatures found in [[rpd:spider_nest|Spider Nest]] levels. They are more advanced and dangerous than basic spiders.

==== Description ====
Spider Servants are intelligent spider creatures that serve a greater purpose in spider colonies. They are typically stronger than regular spiders and may have special abilities.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Higher than basic spiders
  * **Attack:** May have special web-based attacks
  * **Abilities:** Potentially able to create webs or trap heroes
  * **Tactics:** Will likely work together with other spider creatures

==== Location ====
Spider Servants can be found in:
  * [[rpd:spider_nest|Spider Nest]] levels
  * Areas near [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]]
  * Spider Colony areas

==== Loot ====
Defeating Spider Servants may yield:
  * Spider-related items
  * Web-related materials
  * Standard mob drops

====== Spider Servant ======

==== Description ====
Spider Servants are specialized spiders that serve more powerful spider entities in the dungeon. They are typically stronger than regular spiders.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Moderate to high
  * **Attack Skill:** Moderate to high
  * **Defense Skill:** Moderate
  * **Damage:** Moderate
  * **Exp for Kill:** Moderate

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Web Creation:** Can create webs to hinder movement
  * **Poison Bite:** May have poisonous attacks
  * **Group Tactics:** Works with other spiders in coordinated attacks
  * **Loyalty:** Serves more powerful spider entities

==== Notes ====
Spider Servants are more dangerous than regular spiders and often appear in groups in spider-infested areas of the dungeon.

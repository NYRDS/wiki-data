====== Elementals ======

==== Description ====
Elementals are magical creatures representing the fundamental elements of nature. They are commonly found throughout the dungeon.

==== Types of Elementals ====
  * [[rpd:air_elemental|Air Elemental]] - Flying creatures made of living air
  * [[rpd:water_elemental|Water Elemental]] - Creatures associated with water and moisture
  * [[rpd:earth_elemental|Earth Elemental]] - Creatures made of earth and stone
  * [[rpd:fire_elemental|Fire Elemental]] - Creatures of flame and heat (if exists in game)

==== Common Properties ====
  * **Magical Nature:** All elementals are magical beings
  * **Elemental Resistance:** Each type has resistances to opposing elements
  * **Environmental Affinity:** May be stronger in certain environments
  * **Drops:** Often drop useful reagents or potions

==== Notes ====
Elementals serve as representations of the core elements in the dungeon ecosystem and often drop useful items when defeated.

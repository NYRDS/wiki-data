====== Necropolis ======

The **Necropolis** is one of the special branch dungeons in Remixed Pixel Dungeon, focused on undead-themed content.

==== Overview ====
The Necropolis is accessed from depth 7 of the main dungeon and consists of 5 interconnected levels (necro1-necro5). It features unique undead enemies and mechanics related to death and necromancy.

==== Levels ====
  * **necro1** (depth 8) - Entry level with basic undead creatures
  * **necro2** (depth 9) - More challenging undead with some elite enemies
  * **necro3** (depth 10) - Advanced undead enemies and mechanics
  * **necro4** (depth 11) - Most challenging regular area
  * **necro5** (depth 12) - Boss level with the Lich as the final encounter

==== Strategy ====
The Necropolis requires preparation for undead-specific challenges. Many undead enemies have resistances to common damage types and may require specialized equipment or tactics to defeat effectively.

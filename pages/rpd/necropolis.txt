====== Necropolis ======

The **Necropolis** is a dungeon area/level in Remixed Dungeon, typically located after the caves and before the city or halls. It is characterized by undead-themed enemies and dark, tomb-like environments.

==== Description ====
The Necropolis is a dark and foreboding area filled with undead creatures and ancient horrors. This level features:
  * Undead enemies such as [[rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeletons]], [[rpd:zombie_mob|Zombies]]
  * Difficult terrain with barriers and obstacles
  * Rare items and powerful enemies
  * Themed around necromancy and death magic

==== Enemies ====
Mobs found in the Necropolis include:
  * [[rpd:zombie_mob|Zombie]]
  * [[rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeleton]]
  * [[rpd:ghost_mob|Ghost]]
  * [[rpd:warlock_mob|Warlock]]
  * [[rpd:lich_mob|Lich]] (Boss)
  * [[rpd:death_knight_mob|Death Knight]]
  * [[rpd:zombie_gnoll|Zombie Gnoll]]
  * [[rpd:enslaved_soul_mob|Enslaved Soul]]
  * [[rpd:cold_spirit_mob|Cold Spirit]]

==== Items ====
Items commonly found in the Necropolis include:
  * [[rpd:potion_of_holy_water|Holy Water Potions]]
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Remove Curse Scrolls]]
  * Dark-themed weapons and armor

==== Strategy ====
When navigating the Necropolis:
  * Prepare for undead-specific challenges
  * Bring anti-undead items and spells
  * Be ready for different tactics against undead enemies
  * Watch for unique mechanics and traps

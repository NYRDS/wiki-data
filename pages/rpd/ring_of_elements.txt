====== Ring of Elements ======

{{ rpd:images:ringofelements_item.png|Ring of Elements }}

The Ring of Elements is a powerful ring in Remixed Dungeon that provides resistance to various magical and elemental effects.

==== Effects ====
  * Provides resistance to elemental and magical attacks
  * Reduces damage from wands and magical effects
  * Provides protection against status effects from magical sources
  * Reduces duration of negative status effects

==== Stats ====
  * **Primary Effect:** Resistance to elemental and magical damage
  * **Secondary Effect:** Reduced effect duration from magical status effects
  * **Upgrade Benefits:** Higher upgrades provide stronger resistance

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in treasure rooms
  * May be sold in shops in later levels
  * Sometimes obtained as quest rewards

==== Strategy ====
  * Extremely valuable against magic-using enemies and wands
  * Helps against various status effects and magical traps
  * Particularly valuable for classes that face many magical enemies
  * Good for players who frequently encounter magical hazards

==== Identification ====
  * Like all rings, starts unidentified and must be identified
  * Effects become clearer after identification
  * Quality affects the strength of the resistance

====== Dungeon Levels ======

Dungeon **Levels** in Remixed Dungeon are distinct floors of the dungeon that players explore. Each level has a specific theme, layout, enemies, and challenges.

==== Level Structure ====
The dungeon consists of multiple levels, each representing a different area with unique characteristics:
  * **Depth**: Levels increase in number as you progress deeper into the dungeon
  * **Themes**: Each area (Sewers, Prison, Caves, City, Halls) has multiple levels
  * **Layout**: Procedurally generated using various room types and connections
  * **Enemies**: Enemy difficulty and types scale with depth

==== Level Areas ====
  * [[rpd:sewers_level|Sewers]] (Levels 1-5): The starting area with basic enemies and simple layouts
  * [[rpd:prison_level|Prison]] (Levels 6-10): Abandoned prison with more challenging enemies
  * [[rpd:caves_level|Caves]] (Levels 11-15): Underground caves with tougher monsters
  * [[rpd:city_level|City]] (Levels 16-20): The Dungeon's center with elite enemies
  * [[rpd:halls_level|Halls]] (Levels 21-25): The deepest area with the most dangerous creatures

==== Level Mechanics ====
  * **Scaling**: Enemy stats, HP, and damage increase with level depth
  * **Item Drops**: Better items appear on deeper levels
  * **Stairs**: Downward stairs lead to deeper levels, upward stairs return to the surface
  * **Bosses**: Each area (except Sewers) has a boss on the last level
  * **Special Features**: Each level type may have unique terrain, traps, or interactive elements

==== Level Generation ====
Levels are procedurally generated using:
  * Room-based layout algorithms
  * Special room types (shops, gardens, armories, etc.)
  * Connection algorithms to ensure all areas are reachable
  * Weighted spawning of items, traps, and features

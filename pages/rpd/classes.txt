====== Classes ======

Classes represent the different playable character archetypes in Remixed Dungeon, each with unique starting equipment, abilities, and specializations.

==== Main Classes ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - The strong warrior with high health and melee focus
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - The spell-casting master dealing magical damage
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - The stealthy assassin with poisons and traps
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] - The ranged attacker with throwing weapons
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - The versatile archer with magic capabilities
  * [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer]] - The dark magic wielder who commands the undead

==== Characteristics ====
  * Each class has unique starting equipment
  * Classes determine the available specializations (subclasses)
  * Classes have distinct playstyles and strategies
  * Each class can be upgraded to Mastery through Tome of Mastery

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Warrior**: Short sword, armor, health potion
  * **Mage**: Mages staff, armor, health potion
  * **Rogue**: Dagger, leather armor, health potion
  * **Huntress**: Shortbow with arrows, studded armor, health potion
  * **Elf**: Elven bow with arrows, elf armor, health potion
  * **Necromancer**: Sacrificial sword, necromancer robe, health potion

==== Class Mechanics ====
  * Each class has a unique class armor that becomes available upon gaining enough strength to upgrade their starting weapon to level 3
  * Class armors provide enhanced abilities specific to each class
  * Classes have different strengths and weaknesses in various dungeon areas

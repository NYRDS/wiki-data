====== Corpse Dust ======

{{ rpd:images:corpse_dust_sprite.png|Corpse Dust }}

Corpse Dust is a cursed artifact obtained during the Wandmaker's quest. It is found on skeleton remains in the Demon Halls.

==== Properties ====
  * **Type**: Cursed Artifact (Quest Item)
  * **Cursed**: Always cursed and cannot be uncursed
  * **Identified**: Always identified
  * **Effect**: When equipped, provides Bloodlust buff with attack damage doubled but random self-damage

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Bloodlust Effect**: When equipped, provides the Bloodlust buff which:
    * Doubles attack damage (attackProc damage * 2)
    * Randomly causes 1-5 damage to the player (10% chance per turn when HP > 20% of max HP)
    * Provides visual indicator (Bloodlust icon)

==== Acquisition ====
  * Obtained from skeleton remains during the [[rpd:wandmaker|Wandmaker]]'s quest in Demon Halls (Goo, Tengu, DM-300, King levels)
  * Can be dropped by any of the 4 major bosses in the game
  * May also appear on skeleton heaps in Demon Halls levels

==== Strategy ====
  * Provides a high-risk, high-reward combat option
  * Doubled attack damage can be useful in critical situations
  * Self-damage can be dangerous, especially when HP is low
  * Consider using when high damage output is needed but be aware of the self-harm risk

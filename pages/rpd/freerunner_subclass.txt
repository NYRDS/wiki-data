====== Freerunner ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_ROGUE_FREERUNNER.png|Freerunner Hero Sprite }}

**Freerunner** is a [[rpd:subclass|subclass]] for the [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] class.

==== Description ====
The Freerunner is a subclass of the Rogue that specializes in mobility and quick movement. This subclass provides enhanced abilities for traversing the dungeon quickly and evading enemies.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Enhanced Movement Speed:** Improved movement speed that allows for quicker traversal of the dungeon
  * **Evasion Enhancement:** Better ability to avoid enemy attacks
  * **Trap Avoidance:** Increased chance to avoid trap effects when moving through them

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on exploration and quickly traversing the dungeon
  * Use speed advantage to avoid tough enemies when needed
  * Take advantage of quick movement to position for optimal attacks
  * Utilize the ability to quickly retreat when facing overwhelming odds

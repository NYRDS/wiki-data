====== Wraith ======

==== Description ====
A wraith is a dangerous undead creature, formed from the spirit of someone who died violently or with unfinished business.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Moderate
  * **Attack Skill:** High
  * **Defense Skill:** High
  * **Damage:** Moderate to high
  * **Speed:** Fast
  * **Exp for Kill:** Moderate

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Flight:** Can pass through walls and obstacles
  * **Phased Attacks:** Can attack from unexpected positions due to movement through walls
  * **Undead Nature:** Immune to certain status effects like fear and terror

==== Notes ====
Wraiths are particularly dangerous enemies because of their ability to pass through walls, making them difficult to avoid or escape from. They can appear suddenly from unexpected locations.

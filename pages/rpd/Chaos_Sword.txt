====== Chaos Sword ======

==== Description ====
The blade of this sword is made of pure chaos and it mildly vibrates sometimes. Great powers, stored inside this weapon can cut in half almost anything. Although, great skills are needed, to cope the mighty force of the weapon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** Varies based on enchantment
  * **Durability:** Normal
  * **Strength Required:** High

==== Special Properties ====
  * A chaotic weapon with unpredictable but powerful effects
  * Requires high skill to wield effectively due to its chaotic nature
  * Can be created by fusing a Chaos Crystal with a regular weapon

==== Notes ====
Chaos Sword is one of the most powerful weapons in the game, created by fusing a Chaos Crystal with another weapon. The chaotic energy within makes it extremely effective but difficult to control.

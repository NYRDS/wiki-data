====== Undead ======

**Undead** is a category of mobs in Remixed Dungeon that share special characteristics and immunities.

==== Description ====
Undead mobs are reanimated creatures or spirits that were once living beings. They have special properties that make them different from living creatures, including immunities to certain status effects and resistances to specific types of damage.

==== Common Properties ====
  * **Immunities:** Immune to [[rpd:poison_buff|Poison]] and [[rpd:bleeding_buff|Bleeding]] effects
  * **Vulnerabilities:** Often vulnerable to holy/light-based attacks and fire
  * **Special Interactions:** Healing magic typically damages undead creatures
  * **Resistances:** May have resistance to physical attacks

==== Interactions ====
  * **Healing:** Healing spells and effects typically damage undead
  * **Status Effects:** Immune to certain status effects like poison and bleeding
  * **Strategy:** Fire-based and holy/light-based attacks are often effective against undead

==== Code References ====
  * **Java Implementation:** Various undead mobs implement the undead trait differently in their respective classes
  * **Common Properties:** The undead trait is typically implemented through the setUndead() method in mob classes

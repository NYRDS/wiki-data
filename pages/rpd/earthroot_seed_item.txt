====== Seed of Earthroot ======

{{ rpd:images:item_Earthroot.Seed.png|Earthroot Seed }}

==== Description ====
A seed from the Earthroot plant that can be planted or consumed for various effects. When consumed, it provides defensive capabilities by applying the [[rpd:roots_buff|Roots]] debuff for 25 turns and the [[rpd:barkskin_buff|Barkskin]] buff equal to 1/4 the player's strength.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be planted to grow Earthroot plants
  * Can be consumed to apply [[rpd:roots_buff|Roots]] debuff (25 turns) and [[rpd:barkskin_buff|Barkskin]] buff (equal to 1/4 of player's strength)
  * Part of the game's farming/alchemy system
  * Dropped by [[rpd:earth_elemental_mob|Earth Elementals]] with 10% chance
  * Used in the [[rpd:sprout_spell|Sprout Spell]] which randomly plants one of various seeds

==== Usage ====
  * **Planting:** Can be planted on any tile to grow an Earthroot plant that provides [[rpd:barkskin_buff|Barkskin]] buff when touched
  * **Consuming:** When eaten, applies [[rpd:roots_buff|Roots]] for 25 turns (preventing movement) and [[rpd:barkskin_buff|Barkskin]] buff (armor equal to 1/4 of player's strength)
  * **Spell Component:** Used in [[rpd:sprout_spell|Sprout Spell]] as part of the random seed generation

==== Crafting & Alchemy ====
  * **Potion Creation:** Used to craft [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] in alchemy system
  * **Treasury Item:** Can be found in treasure rooms (Treasury levels)

==== Strategy ====
  * Consuming provides significant armor but locks you in place for 25 turns - only use when safe
  * Planting is useful when you need defensive benefits in a specific location
  * Consider carrying for emergency protection in safe areas

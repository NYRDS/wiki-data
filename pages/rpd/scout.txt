====== Scout ======

==== Description ====
Scout is a subclass of the [[rpd:elf|Elf]] class in Remixed Dungeon. Elven Scouts are masters of stealth.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Stealth Mastery:** Enhanced stealth capabilities
  * **Invisibility:** Trampling high grass grants temporary invisibility buff
  * **Speed:** Even faster than regular Elves
  * **Stealth Movement:** Can move more quietly and avoid detection

==== Notes ====
The Scout subclass is focused on stealth and speed, making it excellent for exploration and avoiding dangerous encounters.

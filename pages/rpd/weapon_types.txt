====== Weapon Types ======

This page provides information about different weapon types in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Melee Weapons ====
Melee weapons are used for close combat and include:

  * **Swords** - Standard melee weapons with balanced stats
  * **Axes** - Heavy damage weapons with slower attack speed
  * **Maces** - Mid-tier weapons with moderate damage
  * **Spears** - Long-range melee weapons with reach advantage
  * **Daggers** - Fast weapons with lower damage but high attack rate

==== Ranged Weapons ====
Ranged weapons allow attacking from a distance:

  * **Bows** - Use ammunition like arrows to attack from range
  * **Crossbows** - Higher damage than bows but slower
  * **Slingshots** - Basic projectile weapons
  * **Custom Ranged** - Special ranged weapons with unique properties

==== Missile Weapons ====
Missile weapons are thrown or fired at enemies:

  * **Throwing Knives** - Fast, light projectiles
  * **Javelins** - Medium-range throwing spears
  * **Shurikens** - Small throwing stars
  * **Darts** - Basic ranged ammunition

==== Ammunition ====
Various types of ammunition used with ranged weapons:

  * **Arrows** - Used with bows
  * **Bolts** - Used with crossbows
  * **Special Ammunition** - Enhanced projectiles with effects

==== Special Weapon Properties ====
  * **Enchantments** - Magical effects that enhance weapon properties
  * **Upgrades** - Enhancement levels that improve weapon stats
  * **Durability** - Weapons degrade over time and need repair/replacement

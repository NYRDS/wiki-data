====== Gnoll Tomahawk ======

{{ :rpd:gnoll_tomahawk.png?nolink&50|}}

The Gnoll Tomahawk is a unique throwing weapon specifically associated with the [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] playable class. It's the starting weapon for the Gnoll class and represents their distinctive combat style.

==== Description ====
The Gnoll Tomahawk is a specialized throwing axe that represents the gnoll's native weapon. It combines the functionality of both a melee and throwing weapon, though it excels in the latter role.

==== Properties ====
  * **Type:** Throwing/melee weapon
  * **Class:** [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] (specific starting weapon)
  * **Base Damage:** Moderate to high for early game
  * **Durability:** Good durability for throwing weapons
  * **Accuracy:** High accuracy when thrown

==== Special Features ====
  * **Dual Functionality:** Can be used for both throwing and melee combat
  * **Gnoll Bonus:** May have special properties when used by the [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] class
  * **Throwing Efficiency:** Better performance when thrown than most melee weapons
  * **Starting Equipment:** Exclusive to the [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] class

==== Combat Usage ====
  * **Ranged:** Effective at range when thrown
  * **Melee:** Can be used in close combat if needed
  * **Ammunition:** Doesn't break as easily as standard throwing weapons
  * **Damage:** Good damage output for its level

==== Related ====

==== Strategy ====
  * Use primarily as a throwing weapon for maximum effectiveness
  * Keep as backup melee weapon when needed
  * Collect additional throwing weapons to complement the Tomahawk
  * Take advantage of its durability to conserve other throwing items

====== Gnoll Tomahawk ======

==== Description ====
A distinctive weapon used by gnolls, particularly the Gnoll Hero class.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** 3-17 points
  * **Strength Required:** 15
  * **Durability:** Normal

==== Special Properties ====
  * Unique starting weapon for the [[rpd:gnoll_hero|Gnoll Hero]] class
  * Thrown weapon with good range
  * Can be upgraded like other weapons

==== Notes ====
The Gnoll Tomahawk is the signature weapon of the Gnoll Hero class, providing a powerful ranged option for these hyena-like warriors.

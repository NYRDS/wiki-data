====== Priest Vestments ======

The **Priest Vestments** (internal name: Priest Armor) is the class-specific armor for the [[rpd:priest|Priest]] class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. It's designed to support the Priest's healing and purification abilities.

==== Description ====
Like other class armors, the Priest Vestments have unique special abilities accessible through the Special action. They are specifically designed to complement the Priest's divine-themed abilities.

==== Special Ability ====
The Priest Vestments likely feature divine-themed abilities, possibly including:
* Healing effects for the wearer or nearby allies
* Curse removal or purification abilities
* Protective blessings or shields

==== Strategy ====
The Priest Vestments enhance the Priest's support and protection capabilities. They're designed to reinforce the character's role as a healer and protector in combat situations.

==== Class Restriction ====
This armor can only be effectively used by the Priest class. If worn by other classes, it may not function properly or could have negative effects.

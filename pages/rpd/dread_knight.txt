====== Dread Knight ======

The **Dread Knight** is an elite undead enemy found in the deeper levels of the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]].

==== Description ====
The Dread Knight is an even more powerful variant of the Death Knight, representing one of the most challenging regular enemies in the Necropolis. These knights possess enhanced combat abilities and strong necromantic defenses.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Very High
* Defense: Very High
* Attack: Very High
* Speed: Moderate
* Special: Resistance to various status effects

==== Tactics ====
Dread Knights are extremely challenging enemies that require significant preparation to defeat. They're among the most dangerous non-boss enemies in the Necropolis and require the hero to be well-equipped with high-level weapons, armor, and consumables to have a reasonable chance of victory.

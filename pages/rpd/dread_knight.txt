====== Dread Knight ======

{{ rpd:images:dreadknight_sprite.png|Dread Knight }}

==== Description ====
The Dread Knight is an undead warrior found in the later levels of the dungeon. He is a formidable opponent related to the Necromancer class and Lich subclass.

==== Stats ====
* **HP:** High (scales with dungeon level)
* **Attack Skill:** Very high
* **Defense Skill:** High
* **Damage:** High (scales with dungeon level)
* **Armor:** High DR
* **EXP:** High
* **Speed:** Normal
* **Max Level:** Limited by dungeon level

==== Abilities ====
* **Undead Nature:** Immune to certain status effects (poison, fear, etc.)
* **Heavy Attacks:** Inflicts significant damage
* **High Defense:** Difficult to defeat with physical attacks
* **Dark Magic:** May use dark magic abilities

==== Location ====
* Found in [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]] levels
* Part of the undead theme of the necropolis area

==== Strategy ====
* Use magic or special abilities to bypass physical defense
* Keep distance when possible to avoid heavy melee attacks
* Be prepared with healing potions for the tough encounter
* Consider using ranged attacks or spells

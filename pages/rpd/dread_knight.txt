====== Dread Knight ======

{{ rpd:images:dread_knight_sprite.png|Dread Knight }}

Dread Knights are elite undead warriors found in the later areas of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in later areas of the dungeon
  * **HP**: High
  * **Attack**: High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: High

==== Description ====
Dread Knights are powerful undead warriors, once noble knights who were corrupted by dark magic. They are heavily armored and wield formidable weapons.

==== Behavior ====
  * Aggressive melee combat
  * High defensive capabilities due to heavy armor
  * Deals significant damage in combat
  * Part of the undead category

==== Drops ====
Dread Knights typically drop:
  * High-quality weapons or armor
  * Gold
  * Potions or scrolls

==== Special Notes ====
  * Powerful undead enemy
  * Heavily armored and durable
  * Represents a significant challenge for most heroes
  * Part of the undead category which has special characteristics

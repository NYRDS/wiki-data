====== Bishop ======

{{ rpd:images:bishopnpc_sprite.png|Bishop }}

The **Bishop** is an NPC found in the [[rpd:church|Church]] level. He is the Bishop of the Holy Church of Santa the Depth Lord, and serves as a friendly character in the game.

==== Description ====
The Bishop is a friendly NPC encountered in the Church level. He is the religious leader of the Holy Church of Santa the Depth Lord. He remains in place and does not move, serving as a landmark character in the Church environment.

==== Properties ====
  * **Faction:** Neutral/Friendly
  * **Mob Type:** NPC
  * **Behavior:** Immovable, passive
  * **Location:** [[rpd:church|Church]] level
  * **Interaction:** Talkable (though has no special dialog)

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Immobile:** The Bishop does not move from his position
  * **Friendly:** Does not attack the player
  * **Immortal:** Cannot be killed
  * **Passive AI:** Remains in a passive state at all times

==== Appearance ====
The Bishop appears in the Church level, typically positioned in a prominent location within the church structure. He maintains a static position and serves as a distinctive landmark for players exploring the area.

==== Notes ====
  * This NPC cannot be interacted with in any special way
  * He does not provide quests or items
  * His presence serves primarily as world-building and environmental storytelling
  * Found in levels defined in Church.json and Church_2021_03.json

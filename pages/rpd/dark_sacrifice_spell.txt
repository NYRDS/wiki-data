====== DarkSacrifice ======

{{ rpd:images:dark_sacrifice_spell_icon.png|DarkSacrifice Spell Icon }}

**DarkSacrifice** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Sacrifice your loyal minion to cause an explosion. The area covered by the explosion will depend on the servant\'s remaining health and your skill level.

* Targeting: char
* Level: 3

====== DarkSacrifice ======

{{ rpd:images:dark_sacrifice_spell_icon.png|DarkSacrifice Spell Icon }}

**DarkSacrifice** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon (Necromancy Affinity).

==== Description ====
Sacrifice your loyal minion to cause an explosion. The area covered by the explosion will depend on the servant\'s remaining health and your skill level.

==== Stats ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Necromancy
  * **Targeting:** char
  * **Level:** 3
  * **Mana Cost:** 3
  * **Cast Time:** 0.5 turns
  * **Special Effects:** damages area around target

==== Mechanics ====
When cast on a minion that belongs to the caster:
  * The spell creates a Liquid Flame blob at the minion's position with strength equal to `minion's HP * caster's skill level`
  * The minion takes damage equal to its current HP (causing death)
  * A curse particle effect is shown on the minion
  * A cursed sound effect is played
  * The minion says "My life is yours!" when sacrificed

When cast on an invalid target (not owned by caster):
  * The target says "You have no power over me!" instead
  * The spell fails and the player is prompted to select a loyal servant to sacrifice

==== Usage ====
The DarkSacrifice spell can be used for:
  * **Offensive applications**: Area damage to enemies and surroundings
  * **Strategic applications**: Controlling minion populations and creating fire zones
  * **Resource management**: Sacrificing low-value minions to harm enemies

==== Classes ====
Classes that can use this spell include:
  * Classes with Necromancy affinity
  * Other classes that gain access through special means

==== Strategy ====
How to effectively use the DarkSacrifice spell:
  * **Best situations**: Use when minions are near enemies to maximize area damage
  * **Timing considerations**: Most effective when enemies are in close proximity to your minions
  * **Resource management**: Sacrifice lower-value minions to eliminate stronger enemies

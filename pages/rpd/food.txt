====== Food ======

{{ rpd:images:ration_item.png|Food }}

**Food** items in Remixed Dungeon satisfy hunger and keep the hero in good health. Without food, the hero will become hungry, weak, and eventually start losing health.

==== Food Types ====
  * [[rpd:ration_item|Iron Ration]] - Standard food item that provides 280 hunger points (satiety)
  * [[rpd:overpriced_ration_item|Overpriced Ration]] - Expensive food from shops, provides 120 hunger points (satiety)
  * [[rpd:rotten_food|Rotten Food]] - Dangerous food that poisons the hero and causes harmful effects
  * [[rpd:rotten_ration_item|Rotten Ration]] - Rotten version of Iron Ration
  * [[rpd:freeze_carpaccio_item|Frozen Carpaccio]] - Special food that provides 120 hunger points and gives random beneficial effects
  * [[rpd:mystery_meat_item|Mystery Meat]] - Food with random effects (good or bad), provides 120 hunger points
  * [[rpd:chargrilled_meat_item|Chargrilled Meat]] - Cooked meat from cooking Mystery Meat, provides 120 hunger points
  * [[rpd:pasty_item|Pasty]] - Special food that provides 400 hunger points (full satiety)
  * [[rpd:pseudo_pasty_item|Pseudo Pasty]] - Special food that provides 400 hunger points (full satiety)
  * [[rpd:pumpkin_pie_item|Pumpkin Pie]] - Seasonal food that provides 400 hunger points (full satiety)
  * [[rpd:christmas_turkey_item|Christmas Turkey]] - Seasonal food that provides 400 hunger points (full satiety)

==== Hunger Mechanics ====
  * Hunger levels: Normal (0-279), Hungry (280-399), Starving (400+)
  * **Iron Ration** provides 280 hunger points (brings from Starving to Normal)
  * **Overpriced Ration** provides 120 hunger points (reduces hunger level by 120)
  * **Frozen Carpaccio** provides 120 hunger points and potential beneficial effects
  * **Mystery Meat** and **Chargrilled Meat** provide 120 hunger points
  * **Pasty**, **Pseudo Pasty**, **Pumpkin Pie**, and **Christmas Turkey** provide 400 hunger points (full satiety)
  * Without food, hunger increases until the hero starts losing health when Starving
  * [[rpd:hunger|Hunger]] system details

==== Effects of Eating Frozen Carpaccio ====
When eating Frozen Carpaccio, one of these effects occurs randomly:
  * 20% chance: Gain Invisibility for a short duration
  * 20% chance: Gain Barkskin (armor increase)
  * 20% chance: Cure Poison, Cripple, Weakness, and Bleeding
  * 20% chance: Heal for 25% of max HP and gain some additional HP
  * 20% chance: Normal food effect only

==== Effects of Eating Mystery Meat ====
When eating Mystery Meat, one of these effects occurs randomly:
  * 20% chance: Ignite the hero (Burning)
  * 20% chance: Root the hero in place (Roots)
  * 20% chance: Poison the hero
  * 20% chance: Slow the hero
  * 20% chance: Normal food effect only

==== Strategy ====
  * Always carry at least 2-3 food items when entering the dungeon
  * Ration food carefully in longer levels
  * [[rpd:freeze_carpaccio_item|Frozen Carpaccio]] is valuable for its healing and beneficial effects
  * [[rpd:mystery_meat_item|Mystery Meat]] can be cooked at a cauldron to make safe food
  * [[rpd:rotten_food|Rotten food]] items produce harmful gas clouds when thrown on the ground
  * Higher-value food items like Pasties should be saved for when you're starving

==== Acquisition ====
  * **Iron Ration**: Found in stores and special rooms, sometimes dropped by enemies
  * **Overpriced Ration**: Sold in stores for higher prices
  * **Mystery Meat**: Dropped by many enemies, especially meat-based ones
  * **Chargrilled Meat**: Created by burning Mystery Meat at a cauldron
  * **Frozen Carpaccio**: Created by freezing Mystery Meat at an Ice Cauldron
  * **Rotten Food**: Created when food items are contaminated by Rotberry plants or other sources

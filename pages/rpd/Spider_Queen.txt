====== Spider Queen ======

==== Description ====
The Spider Queen is a powerful spider boss that rules over spider-infested areas of the dungeon. She is significantly more powerful than ordinary spiders.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Very high
  * **Attack Skill:** High
  * **Defense Skill:** High
  * **Damage:** High
  * **Exp for Kill:** Very high

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Web Mastery:** Advanced web creation and control abilities
  * **Poison Mastery:** Potent poisonous attacks
  * **Spider Command:** Can command other spiders in the area
  * **Egg Production:** Can create multiple spider eggs during battle

==== Drops ====
  * **Spider Queen Carapace:** A powerful armor or crafting material
  * Other valuable spider-related items

==== Notes ====
The Spider Queen is a significant boss encounter in spider-infested areas, requiring special tactics to defeat due to her ability to control other spiders and create new threats during combat.

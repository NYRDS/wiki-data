====== Town Features ======

The Town is the starting area of Remixed Dungeon where players can prepare for their dungeon expedition. It contains various buildings and services that aid the adventurer.

==== Overview ====

The Town serves as the safe starting area where players can:
  * Purchase equipment and supplies
  * Heal and restore health
  * Receive blessings and special services
  * Learn about the dungeon and its dangers

==== Buildings in Town ====

The Town contains several important buildings:

  * **Church** - Provides healing services and blessings
    * Allows players to heal wounds
    * May offer blessings that enhance abilities
    * Run by NPCs like the Bishop

  * **Cinema** - Special entertainment area
    * Unique location with special content
    * May contain unique items or quests

  * **Inn** - Rest and recovery
    * Provides a safe place to rest
    * Allows for full healing and restoration

  * **Library** - Source of knowledge
    * Contains information about the dungeon
    * May offer special items or services

  * **Fortune Teller** - Divination services
    * Provides special information or abilities
    * May offer unique services to adventurers

  * **Shop** - Equipment and supplies
    * Standard shop for purchasing items
    * Sells weapons, armor, potions, scrolls, and other equipment

==== Town NPCs ====

The Town is populated by various NPCs offering different services:
  * Shopkeepers - Sell equipment and supplies
  * Healers - Provide healing services
  * Merchants - Offer special items or services
  * Quest givers - Provide information or tasks

==== Advantages of Town ====

  * **Safety:** No dangerous monsters
  * **Preparation:** Opportunity to prepare for dungeon descent
  * **Services:** Access to healing, equipment, and special services
  * **Information:** Learn about dungeon challenges

==== Returning to Town ====

In some game variations, players may be able to return to town using special items like portals, though in most cases progression is forward-only through the dungeon until completion.

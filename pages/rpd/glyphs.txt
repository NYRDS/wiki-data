====== Armor Glyphs ======

Armor Glyphs are special enchantments that can be applied to armor in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. Each armor piece can only have one glyph at a time, and glyphs provide various beneficial effects in combat and exploration.

==== List of Available Glyphs ====

^ Glyph Name ^ Effect ^ Description ^
| [[rpd:glyph_of_bounce|Bounce]] | Physical Counterattack | Deals damage back to attackers when blocking |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_affection|Affection]] | Charm Effect | Can charm enemies when they attack you |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_anti_entropy|Anti-Entropy]] | Cold/Fire Protection | Provides cold and fire resistance, and can freeze attackers |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_multiplicity|Multiplicity]] | Cloning | May create duplicates when attacked |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_potential|Potential]] | Wand Charging | Gains energy when damaged for wand usage |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_metabolism|Metabolism]] | Healing Conversion | Converts food into health when damaged |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_stench|Stench]] | Gas Clouds | Creates toxic clouds that damage enemies |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_viscosity|Viscosity]] | Delayed Damage | Stores damage taken and releases it slowly over time |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_displacement|Displacement]] | Teleportation | Teleports you away when taking damage |
| [[rpd:glyph_of_entanglement|Entanglement]] | Plant Protection | Creates grass armor that protects and may heal |

==== Glyph Application ====
  * **Armor Kit**: Can be used to inscribe a random glyph on existing armor
  * **Scroll of Upgrade**: May add or upgrade a glyph when upgrading armor
  * **Special Items**: Some items in the dungeon may contain glyphs
  * **Random Generation**: New armor may spawn with random glyphs already inscribed

==== Glyph Selection Strategy ====
  * **Combat Style**: Choose glyphs that match your combat approach
  * **Survival Needs**: Prioritize glyphs that help with your primary challenges
  * **Equipment**: Consider how glyphs interact with other equipment
  * **Risk Tolerance**: Some glyphs are more defensive, others more offensive

==== Advanced Mechanics ====
  * **Incompatibility**: Some glyphs may not work well with certain armor or character builds
  * **Level Scaling**: Higher level armor can have more powerful glyph effects
  * **Curses**: Some cursed armor can have negative effects when glyphed

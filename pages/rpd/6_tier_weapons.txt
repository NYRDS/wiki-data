====== 6 Tier Weapons ======

Tier 6 weapons are the highest tier of melee weapons in Remixed Dungeon. They require considerable strength to wield effectively but deal massive damage.

==== Available Tier 6 Weapons ====
  * [[rpd:halberd|Halberd]] - A polearm weapon requiring 20 strength with moderate accuracy but high damage
  * [[rpd:claymore|Claymore]] - A heavy sword requiring 20 strength with balanced stats

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Tier:** 6 (highest available)
  * **Strength Requirement:** 20 STR
  * **Damage:** Very high for their speed
  * **Speed:** Varies by weapon type (Halberd is 1.1x speed, Claymore is 1.0x speed)
  * **Durability:** Standard durability for tier 6 weapons
  * **Enchantments:** Can receive standard weapon enchantments

==== Halberd ====
  * **Damage Range:** 40-50
  * **Accuracy:** 110% of base weapon accuracy
  * **Speed:** 1.1x (faster than normal for its damage)
  * **Range:** 2 tiles (can attack enemies 2 tiles away)
  * **Type:** Polearm (special range attack)

==== Claymore ====
  * **Damage Range:** 45-50
  * **Accuracy:** 100% of base weapon accuracy
  * **Speed:** 1.0x (normal speed)
  * **Range:** 1 tile (melee range)
  * **Type:** Heavy sword
  * **Special:** Requires left hand to be free for blocking

==== Crafting and Availability ====
  * Tier 6 weapons can be found as rare drops from powerful enemies
  * May appear in shops at higher dungeon levels
  * Can be transmuted from other weapons using the [[rpd:water_of_transmutation|Water of Transmutation]]

==== Usage Tips ====
  * These weapons require 20 strength to use without penalties
  * Pair with a high-strength hero class or use strength-boosting items/potions
  * The Halberd's range gives it tactical advantages over the Claymore
  * The Claymore has higher base damage than the Halberd
  * Consider your hero's strength when choosing between the two options
  * Both can be enchanted to provide additional benefits in combat

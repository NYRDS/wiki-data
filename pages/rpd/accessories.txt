====== Accessories ======

Accessories are special equipment items in Remixed Dungeon that provide passive bonuses to the hero. Unlike weapons and armor, accessories are equipped in special slots and provide continuous benefits.

==== Types of Accessories ====
  * [[rpd:rings|Rings]] - Worn on fingers, providing various magical effects
  * [[rpd:artifacts|Artifacts]] - Powerful unique items with special abilities

==== Ring Slots ====
The hero has two ring slots available for wearing different rings. Each ring provides a different effect, allowing for strategic combinations.

==== Notable Accessories ====
  * [[rpd:ring_of_haste_item|Ring of Haste]] - Increases movement and action speed
  * [[rpd:ring_of_accuracy|Ring of Accuracy]] - Improves hit chance in combat
  * [[rpd:ring_of_evasion|Ring of Evasion]] - Increases chance to dodge attacks
  * [[rpd:ring_of_elements|Ring of Elements]] - Provides resistances to elemental damage
  * [[rpd:ring_of_haggler_item|Ring of Haggler]] - Reduces shop prices
  * [[rpd:ring_of_mending|Ring of Mending]] - Provides health regeneration
  * [[rpd:ring_of_power|Ring of Power]] - Increases magical damage
  * [[rpd:ring_of_satiety_item|Ring of Satiety]] - Reduces hunger rate
  * [[rpd:ring_of_shadows_item|Ring of Shadows]] - Improves stealth and reduces enemy detection
  * [[rpd:ring_of_thorns_item|Ring of Thorns]] - Reflects some damage back to attackers

====== Accessories ======

Accessories are special equipment items that provide unique benefits in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Accessories are special equipment slots in the game that provide various beneficial effects to the player. These items typically provide passive benefits that remain active while equipped.

==== Common Accessories ====
  * [[rpd:rings|Rings]] - Items worn on fingers that provide various effects
  * [[rpd:artifacts|Artifacts]] - Unique items with special properties and often charge-based mechanics
  * [[rpd:amulet_item|Amulet of Yendor]] - The ultimate goal item
  * [[rpd:dried_rose_item|Dried Rose]] - Item for ghost hero revival
  * [[rpd:timekeeper_hourglass_item|Timekeeper's Hourglass]] - Item that allows time manipulation

==== Accessory Mechanics ====
  * **Slots**: Accessories occupy special equipment slots
  * **Passive Effects**: Most accessories provide continuous benefits
  * **Active Effects**: Some accessories can be used for special actions
  * **Charges**: Many accessories use charges that regenerate over time

==== Accessory Categories ====
  * **Enhancement**: Items that improve character stats
  * **Utility**: Items that provide special abilities or functions
  * **Protection**: Items that offer defensive capabilities
  * **Offense**: Items that improve combat effectiveness

====== Black Skull ======

{{ rpd:images:black_skull_sprite.png|Black Skull }}
{{ rpd:images:black_skull.png|Black Skull }}Ancient artifact created by one of the first Necromancers.
Black Skulls are powerful undead enemies that appear in the Necropolis area in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in Necropolis area
  * **HP**: High
  * **Attack**: High
  * **Defense**: Moderate
  * **EXP**: High

==== Description ====
Black Skulls are powerful undead constructs animated with dark necromantic magic. They are more formidable than regular Exploding Skulls and pose a significant threat to adventurers.

==== Behavior ====
  * Aggressive movement toward the player
  * More durable than regular Exploding Skulls
  * Self-destructs when destroyed, causing significant damage
  * Part of the undead category

==== Drops ====
Black Skulls typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Items related to necromancy or the Necropolis
  * Could potentially drop parts needed for mastery items

==== Special Notes ====
  * More powerful version of Exploding Skull
  * Self-destructs when killed, causing significant damage
  * Found specifically in the Necropolis area
  * Part of the undead category which has special characteristics
  * May be related to the Black Skull of Mastery unlock item

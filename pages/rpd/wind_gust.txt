====== Wind Gust ======

**Wind Gust** is a spell available to Mages in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Wind Gust creates a strong gust of wind that pushes enemies and objects away from the hero. It can be used strategically to reposition enemies or to activate distant switches.

==== Stats ====
* Mana Cost: Moderate
* Range: Medium
* Effect: Pushes targets away from hero

==== Usage ====
This spell is particularly useful for:
  * Breaking enemy formations
  * Pushing enemies off chasms
  * Activating distant pressure plates
  * Creating distance from dangerous enemies
  * Managing crowd control situations

==== Classes ====
  * **Mage**: Natural affinity, available early
  * **Other classes**: May learn through class-specific abilities

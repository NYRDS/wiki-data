====== Badges ======

Badges are achievements that are unlocked by completing certain challenges or milestones in the game. They provide permanent benefits and recognition for accomplishments.

==== Badge Categories ====
  * **Hero Badges:** Related to hero classes and subclasses
  * **Item Badges:** Related to finding special items
  * **Mastery Badges:** Related to mastering subclasses
  * **Challenge Badges:** Related to completing challenges
  * **Achievement Badges:** General achievements

==== Common Badges ====
  * [[rpd:badge_spider_queen_slain|Badge: Defeat Spider Queen]]
  * [[rpd:gnoll_unlocked|Badge: Heal Gnoll Brute]]

==== Unlock Requirements ====
Some badges have specific requirements for unlocking hero classes:
  * **Huntress:** Defeat 3rd boss (DM300) OR donate 1+
  * **Elf:** Defeat 4th boss (The King) OR donate 2+
  * **Gnoll:** Heal Gnoll Brute at town priest OR donate 3+

==== Mechanics ====
Badges are stored in the player's profile and persist across games. Some badges unlock additional content or provide minor gameplay benefits.

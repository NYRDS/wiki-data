====== Goo ======

{{ :rpd:goo.png?nolink&50|}}

Goo (The Black Blob) is a powerful boss enemy found in the Sewers area of the dungeon. It's a large, amorphous mass of living sludge with unique mechanics.

==== Description ====
Goo is a massive living sludge that dwells in the sewers beneath the city. It has grown to large proportions by consuming waste and detritus in the sewers, and has become highly dangerous. It's the boss of the Sewers area.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Moderate but with special mechanics
  * **Attack:** Powerful ranged attack that can damage multiple tiles
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Puddle creation that spreads around the arena
    * Splitting when damaged (in some variants)
    * Powerful ranged "pumping" attack
  * **Mechanics:** Interacts with water tiles in special ways

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Puddles:** Goo creates puddles that can heal the boss or damage the player
  * **Water Interaction:** Goo becomes more dangerous near water
  * **Pumping Attack:** A signature move that damages a wide area
  * **Arena:** Takes place in a specially designed arena with water features

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Sewers area (level 5)
  * Appears in a boss arena specially designed with water features

==== Preparation ====
  * **Equipment:** Ranged weapons preferred to avoid close combat
  * **Consumables:** Bring healing items and utility scrolls
  * **Strategy:** Understand puddle mechanics for positioning
  * **Footwear:** Avoid getting stuck in puddles

==== Tactics ====
  * Stay out of puddles that appear around the arena
  * Use ranged attacks to maintain distance
  * Dodge the pumping attack by moving along the edges
  * Attack during safe windows after the pumping attack
  * Don't let the fight go on too long as Goo becomes more aggressive

==== Rewards ====
  * Access to the next dungeon area (Prison)
  * Unique equipment or artifacts
  * Valuable experience and treasure

====== Spider Eggs ======

Spider Eggs are special objects or creatures related to spider-themed content in the dungeon. They are typically associated with spider infestation areas.

==== Description ====
Spider Eggs represent locations where spiders lay their eggs or where young spiders emerge. They may be found in:
  * [[rpd:spider_nest|Spider Nests]]
  * [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]] areas
  * Spider-infested dungeon levels

==== Interactions ====
  * May contain spiderlings or other spider creatures
  * Could potentially be destroyed to prevent more spiders from appearing
  * May be part of spider-themed level design

==== Locations ====
Spider Eggs are most commonly found in:
  * Spider Nest levels
  * Infested areas of the dungeon
  * Special areas designed for spider-themed content

==== Related Mobs ====
  * [[rpd:spider_servant|Spider Servant]]
  * [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]]
  * Various other spider creatures

====== Spider Egg ======

==== Description ====
Spider Eggs are objects that contain developing spiders. They are typically found in spider-infested areas of the dungeon.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Hatching:** Will eventually hatch into spider enemies
  * **Threat:** Represents a future danger if left unchecked
  * **Destruction:** Can be destroyed before hatching
  * **Spider Generation:** Part of spider nest mechanics

==== Behavior ====
  * Will hatch into various types of spiders depending on the area
  * May contain different spider types (normal, poisonous, etc.)
  * Destroying eggs prevents additional spider spawns

==== Notes ====
Spider Eggs represent an ongoing threat in spider areas and should be dealt with carefully to prevent being overwhelmed by newly hatched spiders.

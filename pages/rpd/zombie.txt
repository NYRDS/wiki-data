====== Zombie ======

The **Zombie** is an undead enemy found in the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]].

==== Description ====
Zombies are basic undead creatures that inhabit the Necropolis. They are reanimated corpses with limited intelligence but considerable durability.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Moderate
* Defense: Low to Moderate
* Attack: Moderate
* Speed: Slow

==== Tactics ====
Zombies are generally less dangerous than other undead enemies in the Necropolis but still pose a threat in groups. They're most effectively dealt with through direct combat.

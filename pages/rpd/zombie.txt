Zombies are undead enemies found throughout the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found throughout the dungeon, typically in later areas
  * **HP**: Moderate to High
  * **Attack**: Moderate
  * **Defense**: Moderate
  * **EXP**: Moderate

==== Description ====
Zombies are reanimated corpses that once were dungeon dwellers. They are slow but durable enemies with a tendency to follow the player persistently.

==== Behavior ====
  * Slow but persistent movement toward the player
  * Takes damage over time from certain effects
  * May have special interactions with Necromancer class

==== Drops ====
Zombies typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Random items (potions, scrolls)
  * Occasional equipment pieces

==== Special Notes ====
  * Zombies are undead creatures
  * May be affected by certain spells or abilities that target undead specifically
  * Generally found in later areas of the dungeon
  * Part of the undead category which has special characteristics

Zombies are undead creatures reanimated after death through dark magic or other means. They are slow but durable enemies that can be found throughout the dungeon.

==== Description ====
Zombies are shambling undead creatures that were once living beings. They have been reanimated by dark forces and now serve as mindless servants or simply roam the dungeon.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** High, making them durable opponents
  * **Attack:** Slow but powerful melee attacks
  * **Speed:** Slower than most creatures
  * **Abilities:** Standard undead resistances and immunities

==== Characteristics ====
  * **High HP:** Difficult to take down due to high health
  * **Slow Movement:** Moves slower than player and most other creatures
  * **Undead Nature:** Immune to poison, bleeding, and other effects that target living tissue
  * **Simple AI:** Usually not very tactically complex

==== Location ====
Zombies can be found in:
  * [[rpd:sewers|Sewers]] levels
  * Early dungeon levels
  * Areas with other undead creatures
  * [[rpd:prison|Prison]] levels

==== Tactics ====
  * Use speed advantage to position and attack safely
  * Fire or holy damage typically effective against undead
  * Area attacks can be useful against groups of zombies
  * Be careful of being surrounded by multiple zombies
  * Ranged attacks can be effective to maintain distance

==== Variants ====
  * [[rpd:gnoll_zombie|Gnoll Zombie]] - A specific type of zombie

==== Related ====
  * Part of the [[rpd:undead|Undead]] category
  * Often found in groups in undead-themed areas
  * Less dangerous individually than other undead like [[rpd:wraith|Wraiths]]

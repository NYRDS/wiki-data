====== Dreamweed Plant ======

{{ rpd:images:levelObject_Dreamweed.png|Dreamweed Plant }}

==== Description ====
The Dreamweed plant causes any creature that touches it to fall asleep. When a character or enemy steps on the tile containing a Dreamweed plant, they are put to sleep temporarily.

==== Effects ====
  * Puts the creature that touches it to sleep temporarily
  * Provides a control effect that neutralizes threats
  * The sleep effect lasts for a duration
  * The plant disappears after being touched

==== Mechanics ====
  * Dreamweed plants can be found growing in the dungeon
  * They can also be grown by planting [[rpd:dreamweed_seed_item|Dreamweed Seeds]]
  * The sleep effect is beneficial for avoiding combat
  * Sleeping creatures cannot act while asleep

==== Strategy ====
  * Dreamweed plants are useful for bypassing difficult enemies
  * Can be used to gain time in dangerous situations
  * Effective against enemies that would otherwise be difficult to fight
  * Consider using to pass through areas with dangerous stationary enemies

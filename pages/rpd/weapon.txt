====== Weapon ======

{{ rpd:images:short_sword_sprite.png|Generic Weapon }}

A **weapon** is a piece of equipment used to attack enemies in Remixed Dungeon. Weapons are essential for combat and come in various types and tiers, each with different properties and special abilities.

==== Weapon Categories ====
  * **Melee Weapons:** Used in close combat
  * **Ranged Weapons:** Used to attack enemies from a distance
  * **Throwing Weapons:** One-time use weapons thrown at enemies

==== Weapon Properties ====
  * **Damage:** Physical damage dealt per hit
  * **Durability:** How many hits the weapon can take before breaking
  * **Speed:** Attack speed modifier
  * **Accuracy:** Hit chance bonus
  * **STR:** Strength requirement to wield effectively
  * **Tier:** Quality level (1-5 for regular weapons, 6 for special weapons)

==== Weapon Types ====
  * [[rpd:melee_weapons|Melee Weapons]] - Close combat weapons
  * [[rpd:ranged_weapons|Ranged Weapons]] - Distance combat weapons
  * [[rpd:throwing_weapons|Throwing Weapons]] - Single-use thrown weapons

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Upgrading:** Weapons can be upgraded with [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade|Scroll of Upgrade]] and [[rpd:scroll_of_weapon_upgrade|Scroll of Weapon Upgrade]]
  * **Enchantments:** Special magical properties that affect weapon behavior
  * **Class Bonuses:** Some classes get bonuses with specific weapon types

==== Acquisition ====
Weapons can be found in:
  * Chests throughout the dungeon
  * Enemy drops
  * [[rpd:shops|Shops]] - Purchased from shopkeepers
  * [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] - Created through transmutation
  * [[rpd:crafting|Crafting]] - Created using various materials

==== Strategy ====
  * Choose weapons that complement your class and playstyle
  * Consider the trade-offs between damage, speed, and durability
  * Upgrade weapons when you have the resources and opportunity
  * Consider enchantments that complement your build

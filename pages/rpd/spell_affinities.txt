====== Spell Affinities ======

**Spell Affinities** in Remixed Dungeon determine which classes are particularly adept at using certain types of spells, providing bonuses or special abilities related to those magic types. Each hero class has an innate affinity with one or more types of magic.

==== Affinity Types ====

==== Combat Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Warrior
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced combat spells like Smash, Die Hard

==== Elemental Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Mage
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced fire, ice, and nature-based spells (e.g., Ignite, Root Spell)

==== Necromancy Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Necromancer
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced undead and death-related spells (e.g., Raise Dead, Exhumation)

==== Rogue Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Rogue
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced stealth and agility spells (e.g., Backstab, Haste)

==== Witchcraft Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Gnoll
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced magic and manipulation spells (e.g., Lightning Bolt, Roar)

==== Huntress Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Huntress
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced nature and charm spells (e.g., Calm, Charm)

==== Elf Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Elf
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced natural magic spells (e.g., Magic Arrow, Sprout)

==== Priest Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Priest
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced healing and sanctity spells (e.g., Heal, Order)

==== PlagueDoctor Affinity ====
  * **Classes:** Doctor
  * **Benefits:** Enhanced disease and toxic spells (e.g., Plague, GasBomb)

==== Mechanics ====
  * Classes with an affinity for a spell type typically receive benefits such as:
    * Reduced mana costs
    * Increased effectiveness
    * Special bonuses when casting related spells
    * Additional abilities tied to the magic type

==== Strategy ====
  * Choose spells that match your class's affinities for optimal effectiveness
  * Consider subclass abilities that complement your class's magic affinities
  * Some spells may be less effective if they don't match your class's affinities

====== Spell Affinities ======

Spell affinities in Remixed Pixel Dungeon are categories that group spells based on their magical properties and the classes that can use them. Each hero class has a primary affinity that determines which spells they can effectively use.

==== Affinity Types ====
  * [[rpd:common|Common Affinity]] - Basic spells available to most classes
  * [[rpd:elemental_mob|Elemental Affinity]] - Fire, ice, and nature-based spells
  * [[rpd:necromancy|Necromancy Affinity]] - Undead and death-related spells
  * [[rpd:combat|Combat Affinity]] - Offensive and defensive spells
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue Affinity]] - Stealth and agility spells
  * [[rpd:witchcraft|Witchcraft Affinity]] - Magic and manipulation spells
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress Affinity]] - Nature and charm spells
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf Affinity]] - Natural magic spells
  * [[rpd:priest_class|Priest Affinity]] - Healing and sanctity spells
  * [[rpd:plague_doctor_mob|Plague Doctor Affinity]] - Disease and toxic spells

==== Elf Affinity ====

The **Elf Affinity** is exclusive to the [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] class and encompasses natural magic spells.

===== Associated Spells =====
  * [[rpd:magic_arrow_spell|Magic Arrow Spell]] - Core spell for the Elf class
  * [[rpd:sprout_spell|Sprout Spell]] - Nature-based spell
  * [[rpd:hide_in_grass_spell|Hide in Grass Spell]] - Stealth spell for grassy terrain
  * [[rpd:shoot_in_eye_spell|Shoot in Eye Spell]] - Attack spell
  * [[rpd:root_spell|Root Spell]] - Control spell
  * Other nature-based and elven-themed spells

==== Affinity Mechanics ====
  * Each hero class has a primary affinity which determines spell effectiveness
  * Spells used outside of one's primary affinity may be less effective
  * The level of magic affinity can affect spell power and mana costs
  * Some classes can gain access to additional affinities through talents or special means

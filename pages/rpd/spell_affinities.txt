====== Spell Affinities ======

This page describes the various magic affinities in Remixed Dungeon, which determine which classes, subclasses, or heroes can use certain spells.

==== Affinities ====

==== Common ====
  * Available to most hero classes
  * Examples: [[rpd:town_portal_spell|Town Portal]]

==== Combat ====
  * For warrior-type classes
  * Examples: [[rpd:die_hard_spell|Die Hard]], [[rpd:dash_spell|Dash]]

==== Rogue ====
  * For rogue-class heroes
  * Examples: [[rpd:cloak_spell|Cloak]], [[rpd:backstab_spell|Backstab]]

==== Witchcraft ====
  * Related to magical damage and effects
  * Examples: [[rpd:healing_spell|Heal]], [[rpd:lightning_bolt_spell|Lightning Bolt]]

==== Priest ====
  * Healing and support magic
  * Examples: [[rpd:healing_spell|Heal]], [[rpd:calm_spell|Calm]]

==== Huntress ====
  * Related to huntress abilities
  * Examples: [[rpd:calm_spell|Calm]], [[rpd:charm_spell|Charm]]

==== Elf ====
  * Specialized for elf class mechanics
  * Examples: [[rpd:magic_arrow_spell|Magic Arrow]], [[rpd:nature_armor_spell|Nature Armor]]

==== Necromancy ====
  * Dark magic and undead control
  * Examples: [[rpd:raise_dead_spell|Raise Dead]], [[rpd:exhumation_spell|Exhumation]]

==== Plague Doctor ====
  * Disease and healing focused spells
  * Examples: [[rpd:healing_spell|Heal]], [[rpd:order_spell|Order]]

==== Elemental ====
  * Magic related to elemental damage
  * Examples: [[rpd:wind_gust_spell|Wind Gust]] (used by air elementals)

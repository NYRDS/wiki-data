====== Snail ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_Snail.png|Snail }}

Snail is a mob found in the early levels of Remixed Dungeon.

==== Description ====
The Snail is a slow-moving creature that appears in the early levels of the dungeon. It's one of the weakest enemies in the game but can still pose a threat to unprepared heroes.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 6
  * **Attack Skill:** 6
  * **Defense Skill:** 2
  * **Damage:** 1-3
  * **EXP for Kill:** 1
  * **Defense Rate (DR):** 1
  * **Speed:** 0.5x (very slow)
  * **Level Range:** Up to level 2
  * **View Distance:** 3 tiles

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Sewers area (levels 1-2)

==== Combat ====
  * Low damage and health
  * Very slow movement (0.5x speed)
  * Generally not dangerous to experienced players
  * Can be made into a pet (using Capturer item)

==== Drops ====
  * Low loot chance (0% base chance)

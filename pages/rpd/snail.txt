====== Snail ======

{{ rpd:images:snail_mob.png|Snail }}

==== Description ====
The **Snail** is a small, slow-moving creature found in the early levels of the dungeon. It's one of the weakest enemies in the game and often serves as a basic challenge for new players or as a food source.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 6
  * **Attack Skill:** 6
  * **Defense Skill:** 2
  * **Damage:** 1-3
  * **Armor (DR):** 1
  * **Speed:** 0.5 (Very Slow)
  * **EXP:** 1
  * **Max Level:** 2
  * **Loot Chance:** 0%
  * **Type:** Creature

==== Abilities ====
  * **Slow:** Moves at half normal speed
  * **Harmless:** Generally poses minimal threat to players
  * **Pet Potential:** Can be turned into a pet by certain magic

==== Location ====
  * Found in early dungeon levels
  * Common creature in the sewers and prison levels
  * Often appears as a weak enemy for character progression

==== Strategy ====
  * Easy target for gaining experience at low levels
  * No special tactics required
  * Can be safely engaged while building up strength
  * Generally not threatening to properly equipped characters

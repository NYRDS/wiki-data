====== Snail ======

{:rpd:images:snail_sprite.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:snail_sprite.png|Snail }}

Snails are rare enemies found in the earliest levels of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in Sewers (level 1)
  * **HP**: Very Low
  * **Attack**: Very Low
  * **Defense**: Very Low
  * **EXP**: Very Low

==== Description ====
Snails are extremely rare creatures found only on the very first level of the dungeon. They are so slow and weak that they're more of a curiosity than an actual threat.

==== Behavior ====
  * Moves extremely slowly
  * Poses virtually no threat to the player
  * Rare spawn on level 1

==== Drops ====
Snails typically drop:
  * Small amount of gold
  * Very rarely minor items

==== Special Notes ====
  * Extremely rare enemy
  * Only found on level 1 of the dungeon
  * Virtually harmless
  * Most players will never encounter one
  * Considered an easter egg of the game

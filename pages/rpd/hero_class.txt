====== Hero Classes ======

{{ rpd:images:warrior_hero.png|Hero Classes }}

**Hero Classes** determine the starting equipment and special abilities of the [[rpd:hero|hero]] in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Available Classes ====
  * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] - Strong and durable with bonus [[rpd:health|health]]
  * [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] - Powerful spells and [[rpd:wands|wands]], with [[rpd:food|food]] that recharges [[rpd:wands|wands]]
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - Stealthy with [[rpd:invisibility_buff|invisibility]] and bonus [[rpd:gold|gold]]
  * [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] - Ranged combat specialist with [[rpd:bow_item|bow]] and [[rpd:arrows_item|arrows]]
  * [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] - Magical abilities with [[rpd:mana|mana]] system and special subclass options

==== Class Mechanics ====
Each hero class starts with:
  * Unique [[rpd:weapon_item|weapon]]
  * Unique [[rpd:armor|armor]]
  * Special ability that activates under specific conditions
  * Unique [[rpd:food|food]] items

==== Subclasses ====
Advanced players can unlock [[rpd:hero_subclasses|subclasses]] (mastery paths) after winning with a class:
  * Warrior: [[rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]] or [[rpd:brawler_subclass|Brawler]]
  * Mage: [[rpd:battlemage_subclass|Battle Mage]] or [[rpd:warmage_subclass|Warmage]] 
  * Rogue: [[rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]] or [[rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]]
  * Huntress: [[rpd:spearmaid_subclass|Spearmaid]] or [[rpd:sniper_subclass|Sniper]]
  * Elf: [[rpd:shaman_subclass|Shaman]] or [[rpd:druid_subclass|Druid]]

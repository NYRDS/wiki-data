===== Mechanics =====
  * Applies [[rpd:blindness_buff|blindness]] effect to the target
  * Interrupts some of the target's actions
  * Temporarily limits the target's perception
  * Duration of the effect depends on the spell's strength

===== Acquisition =====
  * The spell can be acquired through:
    * [[rpd:spell_book|Spellbook]]
    * [[rpd:scroll|Scrolls]] of specific [[rpd:spell|spells]]
    * [[rpd:artifact|Artifacts]] related to [[rpd:spell|spells]]

===== Strategy =====
  * Useful for blinding strong enemies for a short time
  * Can help avoid strong attacks from the enemy
  * Effective when combined with other [[rpd:spell|spells]] or [[rpd:potion|potions]]
  * Useful in fighting enemies that rely on sight

===== Comparison =====
  * Unlike physical attacks, doesn't deal direct damage
  * Focused on enemy control
  * Has limited utility but effective at the right moment

====== Yog's Heart ======

{{ rpd:images:yogsheart_sprite.png|Yog's Heart Sprite }}

==== Description ====
**Yog's Heart** is one of the four Yog-Dzewa organs that appear during the final boss fight against Yog-Dzewa. It represents the heart of the ancient chaos god and is a formidable opponent with powerful abilities.

==== Stats ====
* **HP:** 450
* **HT:** 450
* **Defense Skill:** 40
* **Attack Skill:** 26
* **Damage:** 35-45
* **DR (Damage Reduction):** 22
* **EXP for Kill:** 12
* **Level:** Guts level (26-30)

==== Special Abilities ====
* **Beckoning:** When damaged, calls all other mobs on the level to its location
* **Larva Summoning:** When defending against attacks, has a chance to spawn a Larva on a nearby cell
* **Healing:** Each turn, has a chance to heal another random mob on the level by 20% of its max HP
* **Immunities:** Immune to Toxic Gas, Paralysis, Amok, Sleep, Terror, and Burning

==== Behavior ====
* Spawns at the beginning of the Yog-Dzewa fight along with 2-3 other Yog organs
* Actively moves to engage the player
* Works in coordination with other Yog organs (Yog's Brain, Yog's Eye, Yog's Teeth, Rotting Fist, Burning Fist)

==== Drops ====
* Drops various valuable items upon defeat (specific drops determined by game mechanics)

==== Strategy ====
* Should be prioritized as a target due to its healing ability
* The beckoning effect can quickly overwhelm players, so it's best to isolate it if possible
* The spawning of Larva can create additional threats during the fight
* Use area-effect abilities carefully as they might affect other Yog organs as well

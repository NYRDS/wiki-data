====== Ice Guardian Core ======

{{ rpd:images:ice_guardian_core_sprite.png|Ice Guardian Core }}

Ice Guardian Core is a boss in Remixed Dungeon, found in the Ice Caves area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: End of Ice Caves (levels ice4)
  * **HP**: Very High
  * **Attack**: High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: Very High

==== Description ====
The Ice Guardian Core is the central power source and controller of the Ice Caves. It utilizes the freezing elements of this area to defend itself against intruders with devastating ice-based attacks.

==== Behavior ====
  * Uses freezing attacks that can immobilize the player
  * Controls the environmental hazards of the Ice Caves
  * Has powerful ice-based area effects
  * May summon ice-related entities or constructs

==== Drops ====
Ice Guardian Core typically drops:
  * **Ice Key** - Used to access special areas
  * Ice-related magical items
  * Powerful equipment
  * Large amount of gold
  * Unique items specific to this fight
==== Special Notes ====
  * Boss of the Ice Caves area
  * Central power source of the Ice Caves
  * Defeating it grants access to special areas with the Ice Key
  * Uses freezing mechanics during the fight
  * Unique to the Ice Caves expansion

====== Ice Guardian Core ======
==== Description ====
The core of an Ice Guardian, a powerful artifact from ancient kobold engineering. Power stored within allows Ice guardians to reconstruct themselves.
==== Special Properties ====
  * **Reconstruction:** Contains energy for self-repair
  * **Ice Magic:** Infused with powerful ice magic
  * **Ancient Technology:** Created by ancient kobolds
  * **Energy Source:** Provides power for Ice Guardian abilities
==== Uses ====
  * Crafting ingredient for ice-based items
  * Power source for cold-based magic items
  * Component for construction of ice-related equipment
==== Notes ====
Ice Guardian Cores are valuable artifacts representing the pinnacle of ancient kobold engineering, containing concentrated ice energy that can be used for various purposes.

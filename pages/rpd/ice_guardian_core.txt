====== Ice Guardian Core ======

The **Ice Guardian Core** is a powerful ice-themed boss enemy found at the end of the [[rpd:ice_caves|Ice Caves]] (ice5, depth 19).

==== Description ====
The Ice Guardian Core is the boss of the Ice Caves, a powerful entity of ice and cold energy that commands the frozen depths. It represents one of the major challenges in the ice-themed branch dungeon.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Very High
* Defense: High
* Attack: Very High, with freezing effects
* Speed: Moderate
* Special: Controls surrounding ice and cold

==== Tactics ====
Fighting the Ice Guardian Core requires good fire resistance and healing capabilities. The battle should be approached with fire-based weapons or spells to exploit its weakness to heat. The hero should also have ways to deal with freezing effects and cold damage during the encounter.

====== Witchdoctor ======

{{ rpd:images:witchdoctor_sprite.png|Witchdoctor }}

The **Witchdoctor** is a mastery path available for the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class in Remixed Dungeon. This subclass combines necromantic powers with healing abilities.

==== Subclass Overview ====
  * **Parent Class:** [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]]
  * **Unlock Condition:** Via Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss (Tengu)
  * **Magic Affinity:** Necromancy with healing abilities
  * **Special Ability:** Combines necromantic summoning with healing magic

==== Unlock Requirements ====
  * Defeat the second boss (Tengu)
  * Acquire and use the [[rpd:tome_of_mastery|Tome of Mastery]] to unlock the subclass
  * Only available to players of the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Combines the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]]'s undead summoning with healing and nature magic
  * May have unique interactions with both necromantic and natural magic items
  * Could potentially have mechanics that bridge life and death through natural means

==== Advantages ====
  * Access to both necromantic power and healing abilities
  * Greater versatility than standard [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]]
  * Potentially better survival capabilities through healing abilities
  * Combines two different magical disciplines for strategic options

==== Disadvantages ====
  * May not specialize as deeply in either necromancy or healing
  * Complexity of managing two different magical systems
  * Possible balance trade-offs between necromancy and healing abilities

==== Strategy ====
  * Balance necromantic summoning with healing for sustained dungeon progression
  * Leverage both nature and undead synergies where possible
  * Use healing abilities to support summoned undead
  * Consider equipment that benefits both magical disciplines

==== Class Comparisons ====
  * Similar to the [[rpd:plague_doctor|Plague Doctor]] in combining healing with specialized mechanics, but with nature magic instead
  * Contrasts with the [[rpd:lich|Lich]] mastery which focuses purely on necromancy

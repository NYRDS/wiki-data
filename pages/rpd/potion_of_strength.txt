====== Potion of Strength ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_strength_sprite.png|Potion of Strength }}

The **Potion of Strength** is a powerful potion in Remixed Dungeon that permanently increases the drinker's strength by 1 point.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Potion
  * **Rarity:** Rare (high-value potion)
  * **Special Effect:** Permanently increases strength by 1 point
  * **Usage:** Single use item (consumed upon drinking)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Permanent Increase:** Increases the hero's Strength (STR) attribute by 1 point permanently
  * **Character Application:** Only affects heroes (Hero class instances), not other characters
  * **Identification:** Becomes identified when consumed or when seen in inventory after being identified
  * **Arrow Moistening:** When used to moisten arrows, converts them to Amok Arrows

==== Strategy ====
  * The most valuable permanent enhancement potion in the game
  * Best saved for when STR becomes critical for equipment usage
  * Particularly valuable for classes that rely heavily on melee combat
  * Should generally be saved rather than used early in a run
  * Allows use of higher-level weapons and armor that require more strength
  * Provides a permanent damage bonus for all melee attacks

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found rarely throughout dungeon levels
  * May appear in special treasure rooms
  * Sometimes available in shops at high prices
  * Can be given as rewards from certain NPCs (e.g., Plague Doctor quest)

==== Effect ====
When consumed, the Potion of Strength permanently increases your strength by one point, allowing you to carry heavier armor and use more powerful weapons, and increases your melee damage output.

====== Potion Item ======

**Potion Item** is a base item class in Remixed Dungeon that represents all potion types in the game.

==== Description ====
Potion items are consumable items that provide various effects when thrown or drunk by the player character. Each potion has specific effects and uses.

==== Base Properties ====
  * **Weight:** 1 (unless specified otherwise)
  * **Class:** Upgradable
  * **Usage:** Can be thrown or drunk
  * **Inventory Space:** Takes 1 slot
  * **Container:** Can be stored in regular inventory

==== Common Potion Types ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Restores health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]] - Increases hero level
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] - Freezes enemies and terrain
  * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]] - Provides temporary invisibility
  * [[rpd:potion_of_levitation_item|Potion of Levitation]] - Allows flying over terrain and traps
  * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]] - Permanently increases strength and health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] - Creates paralyzing gas clouds
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] - Neutralizes harmful gases
  * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] - Permanently increases strength
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]] - Creates toxic gas clouds

==== Mechanics ====
  * Potions can be identified to reveal their effects
  * Some potions have different effects when thrown vs drunk
  * [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] allows combining potions with other items
  * Potions can interact with [[rpd:moisten_mechanic|moisten mechanic]]

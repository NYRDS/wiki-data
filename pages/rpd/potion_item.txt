====== Potions ======

Potions in Remixed Dungeon are consumable items that provide various effects when quaffed. Potions are identified through use or by Scrolls of Identify.

==== Common Potions ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]]: Restores health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]]: Grants experience points
  * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]]: Permanently increases strength
  * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]]: Increases strength and health permanently
  * [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]]: Creates fire damage in an area
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]]: Creates paralyzing gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]]: Creates poisonous gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]]: Makes you temporarily invisible
  * [[rpd:potion_of_mind_vision_item|Potion of Mind Vision]]: Reveals monsters and items on the level
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]]: Neutralizes harmful gases and effects
  * [[rpd:potion_of_blessing_item|Potion of Blessing]]: Provides temporary combat buffs
  * [[rpd:potion_of_overhealing_item|Potion of Overhealing]]: Heals above maximum health

==== Potion Mechanics ====
  * Potions are consumed automatically and provide immediate effects
  * unidentified potions have randomized appearances
  * Some potions require careful timing for effective use
  * Potions can be thrown at enemies to provide effects to them

==== Thrown Potions ====
Some potions gain different effects when thrown rather than drunk:
  * **Liquid Flame**: Can ignite enemies and terrain when thrown
  * **Toxic Gas**: Creates area-effect damage when thrown
  * **Paralytic Gas**: Can paralyze enemies in an area when thrown

==== Identification ====
  * Potions must be identified to know their effects
  * Can be identified by drinking, using Scroll of Identify, or Crystal Ball
  * Once identified, all potions of the same type show their effects

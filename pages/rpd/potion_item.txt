====== Potion Item ======

Potions are consumable items in Remixed Dungeon that provide various beneficial or harmful effects when consumed. Each potion has a unique effect and can be identified through various means.

==== Description ====
Potions are single-use consumable items that provide instant effects when drunk by the hero. There are various types of potions in the game, each with unique effects. Potions start unidentified and must be identified to know their effect before use.

==== Types of Potions ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Restores health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]] - Provides experience points
  * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]] - Permanently increases strength
  * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] - Alternative name for Potion of Might
  * [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]] - Creates fire in an area
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] - Creates paralyzing gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] - Neutralizes harmful gases
  * [[rpd:potion_of_mind_vision_item|Potion of Mind Vision]] - Reveals nearby secrets
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] - Creates freezing area
  * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]] - Provides temporary invisibility
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]] - Creates toxic gas cloud
  * [[rpd:potion_of_holy_water_item|Potion of Holy Water]] - Cleanses curses

==== Identification ====
Potions start unidentified and appear as "potion of unknown" with a random color. They can be identified by:
  * Drinking them (risky!)
  * Using a [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]]
  * Throwing them at walls or enemies
  * Using certain [[rpd:ring_of_detection_item|Ring of Detection]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Always carry identification scrolls to safely identify potions
  * Some potions like [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] can save your life
  * Others like [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] can be used tactically against enemies
  * [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] allows creating potions from ingredients

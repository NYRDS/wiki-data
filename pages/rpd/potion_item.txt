====== Potion Item ======

[[rpd:images:potion_red.png|Potion]]

==== Description ====
Potions are consumable items that provide beneficial effects to the player when used. Each potion has a unique effect and can be identified through various means.

==== Common Potions ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Restores health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]] - Provides experience points
  * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] - Increases strength
  * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]] - Permanently increases strength and health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]] - Creates fire in an area
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] - Freezes and damages enemies
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] - Releases paralyzing gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]] - Releases toxic gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_holy_water_item|Potion of Holy Water]] - Uncurses items
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] - Clears harmful gases
  * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]] - Provides invisibility
  * [[rpd:potion_of_mind_vision_item|Potion of Mind Vision]] - Allows seeing through walls
  * [[rpd:potion_of_shielding_item|Potion of Shielding]] - Provides temporary damage reduction
  * [[rpd:potion_of_overhealing_item|Potion of Overhealing]] - Provides healing and temporary health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_blessing_item|Potion of Blessing]] - Provides various positive effects

==== Mechanics ====
  * Potions can be identified by using them, throwing them, or through certain other items/abilities
  * Throwing a potion will activate its effect at the location it breaks
  * Identified potions can be used directly for their effect
  * Most potions become "Unclassified" when the player has not seen them before
  * Potions have random colors when unidentifed

==== Identification ====
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]] - Identifies a single item
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_mind_vision_item|Scroll of Mind Vision]] - Reveals items on the level
  * Throwing the potion - Reveals its effect through the result
  * [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] - Can be used to identify potions through brewing

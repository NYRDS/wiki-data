====== Plague Doctor Subclass ======

{{ rpd:images:plague_doctor_subclass.png|Plague Doctor }}

==== Description ====
The Plague Doctor is a subclass for the Doctor hero class in Remixed Dungeon. Plague Doctors specialize in disease and toxic magic, focusing on ailments and debilitations.

==== Abilities ====
  * **Disease Mastery:** Enhanced effects from disease and toxic spells
  * **Toxic Magic:** Greater effectiveness of poison and gas-based attacks
  * **Ailment Focus:** Abilities that debilitate or weaken enemies
  * **Poison Resistance:** May have natural resistance to toxic effects

==== Specialization ====
  * Focuses on debuffing and weakening enemies
  * Utilizes toxic and disease-based mechanics
  * Often has abilities to exploit enemy weaknesses

==== Lore ====
The Plague Doctor subclass represents the dark healers who turned to disease and toxins as their area of expertise, becoming masters of affliction instead of healing.

==== Acquisition ====
The Plague Doctor subclass can be unlocked by reading a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss, Tengu. It's only available to the Doctor hero class.

==== Equipment ====
  * Special armor: [[rpd:doctor_armor|Doctor Armor]] can be obtained using an [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor
  * Preferred items: Poison potions, toxic gas items, disease-related equipment

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on weakening and debuffing enemies
  * Utilize toxic and disease-based items effectively
  * Exploit enemy vulnerabilities
  * Build toward an ailment-focused gameplay style

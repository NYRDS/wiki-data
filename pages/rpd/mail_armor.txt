====== mail armor ======

{{ rpd:images:mailarmor_sprite.png|Mail Armor Sprite }}

==== Description ====
**Mail Armor** is a medium-tier armor in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. Interlocking metal links make for a tough but flexible suit of armor.

==== Stats ====
  * **Armor Tier**: Medium
  * **Durability**: Good
  * **Speed Penalty**: Moderate
  * **Protection**: Better than leather armor

==== Mechanics ====
  * Balances protection with flexibility
  * Constructed from interlocking metal rings
  * Offers better protection than leather armor with a moderate mobility trade-off

==== Availability ====
  * Found in shops
  * Can appear as loot in various dungeon levels

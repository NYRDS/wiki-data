====== Halls ======

The Halls (Demon Halls) is a dungeon area located beneath the Dwarven Metropolis, characterized by demon inhabitants and challenging magical content.

==== Description ====
In the past these levels were the outskirts of Metropolis. After the costly victory in the war with the old god dwarves were too weakened to clear them of remaining demons. Gradually demons have tightened their grip on this place and now it's called Demon Halls. Very few adventurers have ever descended this far...

==== Level Characteristics ====
  * **Dungeon Depth:** Levels 22-25 of the main dungeon (approximately)
  * **Difficulty:** Very challenging, end-game difficulty
  * **Environment:** Eerie, supernatural halls with magical properties
  * **Enemies:** Demons, magical creatures, and powerful supernatural beings
  * **Layout:** Complex layouts with magical architecture

==== Enemies ====
  * [[rpd:succubus|Succubi]] - Alluring demon creatures
  * [[rpd:evil_eye|Evil Eyes]] - Powerful magical watchers
  * [[rpd:shadow_lord|Shadow Lord]] - The area's boss inhabitant
  * [[rpd:spawner|Spawners]] - Creatures that create other monsters
  * [[rpd:bat|Bats]] - Enhanced, more dangerous versions
  * [[rpd:brute|Demon Brutes]] - Powerful demon warriors
  * Various other supernatural entities

==== Features ====
  * **Magical Architecture:** Supernatural construction
  * **Demon Magic:** Environmental effects related to demon presence
  * **Supernatural Hazards:** Unique magical dangers
  * **Boss Arena:** Special arena for [[rpd:shadow_lord|Shadow Lord]] encounter
  * **Limited Resources:** Scarce standard consumables

==== Special Characteristics ====
  * **Extreme Difficulty:** Very challenging enemies and environment
  * **Magical Focus:** Enemies with powerful magical abilities
  * **Supernatural Environment:** Non-physical challenges and puzzles
  * **Boss Level:** Features the [[rpd:shadow_lord|Shadow Lord]] boss encounter
  * **End-Game Content:** Requires significant preparation and power

==== Strategy ====
  * Ensure maximum equipment and item preparation before entering
  * Focus on magical resistance and protection items
  * Prepare for enemies with complex magical abilities
  * Bring substantial healing and restoration supplies
  * Prepare specifically for the [[rpd:shadow_lord|Shadow Lord]] boss encounter
  * Consider your character's magical preparation and resistances

====== Wandmaker NPC ======

**Wandmaker** is an NPC in Remixed Dungeon who specializes in magical equipment.

==== Role ====
  * Provides a special quest on the Dungeon level
  * Rewards players with a high-quality wand upon completion
  * Specializes in magical equipment and knowledge

==== Quest Requirements ====
  * Find the three Rotberry seeds
  * Bring them to the Wandmaker in the Dungeon level
  * Receive a powerful wand as a reward

==== Quest Rewards ====
  * One of the following wands based on class:
    * [[rpd:wand_of_disintegration|Wand of Disintegration]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_lightning|Wand of Lightning]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_blink|Wand of Blink]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_regrowth|Wand of Regrowth]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_slowness|Wand of Slowness]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_telekinesis|Wand of Telekinesis]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_avalanche|Wand of Avalanche]]
    * [[rpd:wand_of_flock|Wand of Flock]]

DM-300 is the second boss in Remixed Dungeon, found in the Caves area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Level 14 (end of Caves)
  * **HP**: Very High
  * **Attack**: High
  * **Defense**: Very High
  * **EXP**: Very High

==== Description ====
DM-300 is a large robot construct that was designed to maintain and clean the caves. Over time, it has become corrupted and now attacks any living thing it encounters. It's one of the most mechanically complex bosses in the game.

==== Behavior ====
  * Uses various mechanical attacks including laser beams
  * Deploys mechanical assistants during the fight
  * Has powerful area-of-effect attacks
  * Immune to many status effects due to mechanical nature

==== Drops ====
DM-300 typically drops:
  * **Spectral Prison Key** - Unlocks special areas
  * Powerful mechanical items or components
  * Large amount of gold
  * Unique items specific to this fight

==== Special Notes ====
  * Second boss, located at the end of the Caves level
  * Mechanical construct boss
  * Defeating DM-300 unlocks the next area of the dungeon
  * The fight requires dealing with mechanical assistants as well as the main body
  * Immunities to many status effects make it unique among bosses

DM-300 is a powerful mechanical boss enemy found in the Caves area of the dungeon. It's a massive construct built by the dwarves for mining operations.

==== Description ====
DM-300 (Dwarf Machine Model 300) was originally built by the dwarves for mining operations in the deep caves. However, after the decline of the Dwarven Metropolis, it has become a guardian of the caves that attacks any intruders. It's an enormous mechanical construct with devastating attacks.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Very high for a boss
  * **Attack:** Multiple powerful mechanical attacks
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Rocket launcher that fires across the room
    * Rapid laser beam attack
    * Self-repair when not taking damage
    * Powerful physical melee attacks
  * **Mechanics:** Mechanical nature with special resistances

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Self-Repair:** Gradually heals when not taking damage during the fight
  * **Rocket Attacks:** Launches rockets that can hit multiple tiles
  * **Laser Beam:** Powerful sweeping laser attack
  * **Durability:** High resistance to certain types of damage

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Caves area (level 15)
  * Appears in a boss arena designed for mechanical combat

==== Preparation ====
  * **Equipment:** Weapons effective against mechanical enemies
  * **Consumables:** Bring plenty of healing and utility items
  * **Strategy:** Plan for a lengthy fight that requires consistent damage
  * **Positioning:** Understand how to avoid rocket and laser attacks

==== Tactics ====
  * Deal consistent damage to prevent self-repair
  * Avoid standing in the path of rocket attacks
  * Dodge the sweeping laser beam attack
  * Be prepared to heal during the fight
  * Attack from range when possible to avoid melee attacks

==== Rewards ====
  * Access to the next dungeon area (City)
  * Unique mechanical or technological equipment
  * Valuable experience and treasure

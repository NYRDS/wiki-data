====== Spider Queen ======

The Spider Queen is the boss creature that appears in the final Spider Nest level (depth 10). She is a formidable opponent with various spider-related abilities.

==== Description ====
The Spider Queen is the matriarch of the spider nest areas. She is a large, powerful spider with magical abilities and a commanding presence over other spider creatures.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Very high, as a boss creature
  * **Attack:** Powerful melee attacks and special spider abilities
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Web entanglement attacks
    * Possible summoning of other spider creatures
    * Poison attacks or web-related effects
  * **Tactics:** The battle likely takes place in a web-filled environment

==== Location ====
  * Final boss of the Spider Nest dungeon (depth 10s)
  * Appears in [[rpd:spider_nest|Spider Nest]] levels

==== Rewards ====
Defeating the Spider Queen typically yields:
  * Special spider-themed artifacts
  * Unique equipment or items
  * Access to new areas or abilities
  * Boss-level loot

==== Strategy Tips ====
  * Be prepared for a challenging fight
  * Fire-based attacks may be effective against web-heavy areas
  * Expect to face multiple spider creatures during the battle
  * Bring healing items and utility scrolls

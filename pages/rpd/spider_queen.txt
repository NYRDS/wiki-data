====== Spider Queen ======

{{ rpd:images:spiderqueen_sprite.png|Spider Queen Sprite }}

The **Spider Queen** is a boss mob in Remixed Dungeon. A hybrid creature of a beautiful woman and a hideous spider, she rules over the spider lair with an iron fist.

==== Description ====
The Spider Queen is a formidable boss character with the body of a beautiful woman crossed with a hideous spider. Every living being in this lair is her servant. Meeting this extraordinary lady may turn out to be a deadly experience because you are trespasser!

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 120
  * **HT:** 120 (Max HP)
  * **Attack Skill:** 21
  * **Defense Skill:** 18
  * **Damage:** 12-20
  * **Damage Reduction:** 10
  * **EXP for Kill:** 11
  * **Level:** Spider level (6s-10s)

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Poison Attack:** On successful hit, has a 50% chance to inflict [[rpd:poison|Poison]] for 7-9 turns
  * **Spider Spawning:** Randomly spawns Spider Eggs in adjacent cells approximately every 20 turns
  * **Phase 2 Behavior:** When HP drops below 50%, she will retreat from enemies when they're within 5 tiles
  * **Melee Attack Only:** Can only attack adjacent enemies (when HP is above 50%)
  * **Fire Immunity:** Completely immune to fire damage and burning effects
  * **Spider Immunity:** Immune to [[rpd:web|Web]] effects

==== Drops ====
The Spider Queen drops the following items:
  * **Guaranteed Drop:** [[rpd:skeleton_key|Skeleton Key]] (100% chance)
  * **Random Drop:** One of the following (equal probability):
    * [[rpd:chaos_crystal|Chaos Crystal]] (33.33% chance)
    * [[rpd:spider_charm|Spider Charm]] (33.33% chance)
    * [[rpd:spider_armor|Spider Armor]] (33.33% chance)

==== Location ====
  * Found in the spider lair (Spider Level), typically at depths with 6s-10s in the level name
  * Part of the sub-levels of the dungeon

==== Strategy ====
  * Keep your distance when she's at full health since she can only attack adjacent enemies
  * Be prepared for poison damage with antidotes like [[rpd:potion_of_healing|Potion of Healing]] or healing abilities
  * Be aware that she will retreat when below 50% health, potentially making it harder to finish the fight
  * Fire or burning attacks are ineffective due to her immunity
  * Be ready to deal with spawned Spider Eggs during the fight
  * Consider using ranged attacks or spells to maintain distance
  * Bring sufficient healing to handle the poison effects over time

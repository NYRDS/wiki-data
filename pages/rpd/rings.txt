====== Rings ======

Rings are accessory items in Remixed Dungeon that provide special ongoing bonuses when worn. The hero can wear up to two rings at once.

==== Types of Rings ====
  * [[rpd:ring_of_haste|Ring of Haste]] - Increases movement speed
  * [[rpd:ring_of_shadows|Ring of Shadows]] - Improves stealth capabilities
  * [[rpd:ring_of_regeneration|Ring of Regeneration]] - Gradually restores health
  * [[rpd:ring_of_wealth|Ring of Wealth]] - Increases quality of random drops
  * [[rpd:ring_of_accuracy|Ring of Accuracy]] - Improves attack accuracy
  * [[rpd:ring_of_elements_item|Ring of Elements]] - Resistance to elemental effects
  * [[rpd:ring_of_evasion|Ring of Evasion]] - Increases defense
  * [[rpd:ring_of_force|Ring of Force]] - Increases melee damage
  * [[rpd:ring_of_haggler|Ring of Haggler]] - Reduces shop prices

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Identification:** Like all rings, starts unidentified and must be worn to discover its properties
  * **Quality:** Ring effectiveness varies based on its quality level
  * **Upgrading:** Rings can be upgraded to improve their effects
  * **Durability:** Rings wear out over time and eventually break
  * **Dual Wielding:** Two rings can be worn simultaneously for combined effects

==== Strategy ====
  * **Early Game:** Focus on rings that improve survival
  * **Mid Game:** Prioritize rings that complement your build
  * **Late Game:** Choose rings that maximize your strengths
  * **Combination:** Some ring combinations can be very powerful

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in treasure rooms
  * Available in shops in deeper levels
  * Dropped by various enemies
  * Created through special game mechanics

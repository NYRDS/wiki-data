====== Rings ======

{{ rpd:images:ring_of_evasion_item.png|Ring}}

**Rings** are [[rpd:accessories|accessory]] items that provide persistent effects to the [[rpd:hero|hero]].

==== Types of Rings ====
  * [[rpd:ring_of_evasion_item|Ring of Evasion]] - Increases chance to dodge attacks
  * [[rpd:ring_of_strength_item|Ring of Strength]] - Increases [[rpd:strength|strength]]
  * [[rpd:ring_of_shielding_item|Ring of Shielding]] - Provides periodic shielding
  * [[rpd:ring_of_mending_item|Ring of Mending]] - Gradually heals
  * [[rpd:ring_of_haste_item|Ring of Haste]] - Increases movement speed
  * [[rpd:ring_of_power_item|Ring of Power]] - Increases [[rpd:wands|wand]] damage
  * [[rpd:ring_of_wealth_item|Ring of Wealth]] - Improves item drops
  * [[rpd:ring_of_accuracy_item|Ring of Accuracy]] - Improves hit chance
  * [[rpd:ring_of_elements_item|Ring of Elements]] - Resistance to elemental damage
  * [[rpd:ring_of_detection_item|Ring of Detection]] - Improved detection
  * [[rpd:ring_of_satiety_item|Ring of Satiety]] - Slows hunger
  * [[rpd:ring_of_foresight_item|Ring of Foresight]] - Improved trap detection

==== Ring Mechanics ====
Rings are [[rpd:accessories|accessories]] that:
  * Require one ring slot to wear
  * Are affected by [[rpd:curse|curses]]
  * Can be [[rpd:enchantments|enchantments|enchanted]] to improve their effects
  * Usually scale their effects based on their level

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found in [[rpd:chests|chests]] throughout dungeon levels
  * Available in [[rpd:shopkeeper_npc|shops]]
  * Dropped by various [[rpd:mobs|mobs]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Each ring provides a different beneficial effect
  * Can be crucial for survival in specific situations
  * Consider the ring's effect in combination with other equipment
  * Cursed rings can be dangerous if not identified

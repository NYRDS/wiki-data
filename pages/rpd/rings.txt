====== Rings ======

{{ rpd:images:ring_example.png|Ring Types }}

Rings are wearable accessories in Remixed Dungeon that provide various passive bonuses to the hero. Each ring has unique effects and must be identified to reveal its properties.

==== Ring Types ====

=== Combat Enhancement ===
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_accuracy|Ring of Accuracy]]**: Increases hit chance in combat
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_damage|Ring of Damage]]**: Increases physical damage dealt
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_evasion|Ring of Evasion]]**: Increases dodge chance
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_force|Ring of Force]]**: Allows wielding weapons as if having higher strength
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_haste_item|Ring of Haste]]**: Increases movement speed
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_might_item|Ring of Might]]**: Increases maximum HP and strength
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_thorns_item|Ring of Thorns]]**: Reflects damage back to attackers
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_shadows_item|Ring of Shadows]]**: Provides stealth bonus against enemies

=== Defensive ===
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_elements|Ring of Elements]]**: Provides resistance to elemental and magical damage
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_haggler_item|Ring of Haggler]]**: Reduces shop prices (requires special handling)
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_herbalism_item|Ring of Herbalism]]**: Increases chances of seed drops from grass
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_satiety|Ring of Satiety]]**: Reduces hunger rate

=== Utility ===
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_regeneration|Ring of Regeneration]]**: Increases health regeneration rate
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_detection|Ring of Detection]]**: Improves detection of secrets and traps
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_wealth|Ring of Wealth]]**: Increases chances of finding valuable items
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_stone_walking|Ring of Stone Walking]]**: Allows movement through walls (with limitations)

==== Identification ====
  * Rings must be identified to reveal their properties
  * Identified rings show their specific enchantment
  * Effects are active regardless of identification status

==== Mechanics ====
  * Rings must be equipped in one of the ring slots
  * Each hero has two ring slots
  * Effects stack if multiple rings with same effect are worn
  * Rings can be upgraded using a Scroll of Upgrade
  * Level bonuses improve ring effectiveness

==== Upgrading ====
  * Each upgrade level provides increased bonuses
  * Higher-level rings provide progressively stronger effects
  * Some rings have special properties at +3 or higher
  * Ring levels are not capped

==== Strategy ====
  * Early game: Ring of Might or Ring of Satiety are generally helpful
  * Mid game: Ring of Accuracy or Ring of Evasion for combat
  * Late game: Ring of Elements for defense against powerful enemies
  * Class-specific: Choose rings that complement your build

==== Crafting ====
  * Can be created at an alchemy pot using metal and other components
  * Recipe varies depending on the desired ring type
  * Higher level rings require more expensive components

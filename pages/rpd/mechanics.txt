The game has some interesting game mechanics, that give the player new experience and ways to enhance the gameplay. Here are listed all game mechanics unique for [[start|Remixed Pixel Dungeon]]:

  * [[rpd:moisten|Moistening]]
  * [[rpd:rotten_food|Food can rot]]
  * [[rpd:sub_levels|Branches]]
  * [[rpd:seeds|Seeds can be eaten]]
  * [[Rat king]] can be challenged
  * Spear and Glaive - can hit up to distance 2 ( since remix.19.2 )
  * [[Kusarigama|Kusarigama]]
  * [[rpd:chaos|Chaos Set]]
  * [[Black Skull]]

====== Warrior ======

{{ rpd:images:warrior_sprite.png|Warrior }}
The Warrior is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is a melee-focused character with slightly higher strength than other classes.
==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 11 (vs 10 for other classes)
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * Short Sword (identified)
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Body Armor spell (in quickslot)
    * Potion of Strength (known item)
  * **Magic Affinity**: Combat
==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Warrior starts with a Short Sword instead of an unarmed class, making him immediately effective in melee combat.
  * The Warrior begins with a Potion of Strength already identified, allowing for an early strength boost.
  * The Body Armor spell is available in the quickslot for immediate use.
==== Advantages ====
  * Higher starting strength provides better damage and lifting capacity
  * Immediate melee effectiveness with the short sword
  * The Body Armor spell provides a useful defensive option
==== Disadvantages ====
  * Lacks ranged options at the start
  * Relies more heavily on direct combat than other classes
==== Subclasses ====

  * [[rpd:berserker|Berserker]]

The **Warrior** is one of the starting playable classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is the melee specialist with high health and excellent starting equipment.
==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Stats:** Strength and Health
  * **Starting Strength:** 11 (highest among all classes)
  * **Health:** Highest among all classes
  * **Special Ability:** The ability to use the Short Sword and Round Shield effectively
==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Weapon:** Short Sword (+1 damage boost) - A unique short sword that can be "reforged" to upgrade another melee weapon
  * **Armor:** Cloth Armor with 15 points of damage absorption
  * **Food:** Additional ration of food
  * **Potions:** Identified Potion of Strength (unique to Warrior)
  * **Quickslot:** Body Armor spell
==== Class Perks ====
  * **11 Strength:** Starts with 11 points of Strength
  * **High Health:** Starts with 20 HP (highest among all classes)
  * **Missile Disadvantage:** Less proficient with missile weapons
  * **Food Healing:** Any piece of food restores some health when eaten
  * **Identified Strength Potions:** Potions of Strength are identified from the beginning
==== Class Abilities ====
The Warrior has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Gladiator:** Successful attacks with melee weapons allow the Gladiator to start a combo, increasing damage with each consecutive hit
  * **Berserker:** When severely wounded, enters a state of wild fury significantly increasing damage output
  * **Warlock:** After killing an enemy, consumes its soul to heal wounds and satisfy hunger
==== Class Advantages ====
  * Best starting equipment among all classes
  * Highest health pool
  * Can use heavy weapons and armor early
  * Highest base strength
  * Access to shield usage for defense
  * Food provides additional healing
==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * No ranged combat options
  * Relies heavily on melee attacks
  * Less magical abilities than other classes
  * More dependent on good equipment for effectiveness
  * Less proficient with missile weapons
==== Class Armor ====
Warriors have a special class armor that allows them to perform a powerful combo attack that can damage and knock back enemies.
==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on close combat and taking advantage of high health
  * Invest in good armor and weapons early
  * Use shields effectively for defense
  * Take advantage of the extra strength to use better equipment sooner
  * Aggressive playstyle works well with high stats
  * Consider choosing a mastery path based on your playstyle preferences

====== Warrior ======

{{ rpd:images:warrior_sprite.png|Warrior }}

The Warrior is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is a melee-focused character with slightly higher strength than other classes.

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 11 (vs 10 for other classes)
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * Short Sword (identified)
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Body Armor spell (in quickslot)
    * Potion of Strength (known item)
  * **Magic Affinity**: Combat

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Warrior starts with a Short Sword instead of an unarmed class, making him immediately effective in melee combat.
  * The Warrior begins with a Potion of Strength already identified, allowing for an early strength boost.
  * The Body Armor spell is available in the quickslot for immediate use.

==== Advantages ====
  * Higher starting strength provides better damage and lifting capacity
  * Immediate melee effectiveness with the short sword
  * The Body Armor spell provides a useful defensive option

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Lacks ranged options at the start
  * Relies more heavily on direct combat than other classes

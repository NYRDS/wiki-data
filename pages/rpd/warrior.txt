====== Warrior ======

{{ :rpd:warrior.png?nolink&50|}}

The **Warrior** is one of the starting playable classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is the melee specialist with high health and excellent starting equipment.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Stat:** Strength and Health
  * **Strength:** 10 (highest among all classes)
  * **Health:** Highest among all classes
  * **Special Ability:** The ability to use the Short Sword and Round Shield effectively

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Weapon:** Short Sword (+1 damage boost)
  * **Armor:** Armor with 15 points of damage absorption
  * **Food:** Additional ration of food
  * **Potions:** Potion of Strength (unique to Warrior)

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **High Strength:** Starts with maximum strength, allowing immediate use of stronger equipment
  * **High Health:** Naturally higher health points than other classes
  * **Shield Proficiency:** Can effectively use shields
  * **Melee Mastery:** Optimized for close combat

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Best starting equipment among all classes
  * Highest health pool
  * Can use heavy weapons and armor early
  * Highest base strength
  * Access to shield usage for defense

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * No ranged combat options
  * Relies heavily on melee attacks
  * Less magical abilities than other classes
  * More dependent on good equipment for effectiveness

==== Class Armor ====
Warriors have a special class armor that allows them to perform a powerful combo attack that can damage and knock back enemies.

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on close combat and taking advantage of high health
  * Invest in good armor and weapons early
  * Use shields effectively for defense
  * Take advantage of the extra strength to use better equipment sooner
  * Aggressive playstyle works well with high stats

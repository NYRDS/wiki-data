====== Buffs and Debuffs ======

{{ rpd:images:buffs_illustration.png|Buffs and Debuffs }}

**Buffs and Debuffs** are temporary status effects that modify character abilities and stats in Remixed Dungeon. Buffs provide positive effects while debuffs impose negative effects.

==== Common Buffs ====
  * **Healing**: Gradually restores health over time
  * **Invisibility**: Makes the hero invisible to enemies
  * **Regeneration**: Faster health recovery
  * **Haste**: Increases movement and attack speed
  * **Shield**: Provides temporary damage absorption
  * **Barkskin**: Reduces all incoming damage
  * **Light**: Reveals dark areas of the dungeon
  * **Fury**: Increases damage output temporarily
  * **Awareness**: Enhances detection of secrets and traps
  * **Levitation**: Allows movement over traps and special terrain

==== Common Debuffs ====
  * **Poison**: Gradually reduces health over time
  * **Bleeding**: Deal damage over time with damage increasing each turn
  * **Burning**: Takes fire damage and some items may be destroyed
  * **Paralysis**: Prevents all actions for a period
  * **Blindness**: Prevents vision beyond immediate square
  * **Slow**: Reduces movement and action speed
  * **Weakness**: Reduces strength temporarily
  * **Doom**: Prevents healing for a period
  * **Charm**: Causes enemies to fight for you temporarily
  * **Amok**: Makes enemies attack allies aggressively
  * **Terror**: Forces characters to flee in fear

==== Buff Duration ====
  * Buffs last for a specific number of turns
  * Duration may scale with hero level or effect strength
  * Some buffs stack with themselves (like healing potions)
  * Others overwrite when applied again

==== Removal of Debuffs ====
  * **Potion of Purity**: Removes all harmful effects
  * **Cleansing items**: Special items that remove specific debuffs
  * **Time**: Most buffs and debuffs expire after a certain duration
  * **Water**: Can remove some debuffs like burning
  * **Resting**: Some temporary effects may end when resting

==== Beneficial Applications ====
  * **Healing**: Use when health is low or during safe moments
  * **Invisibility**: Escape dangerous situations or bypass enemies
  * **Regeneration**: For extended combat situations
  * **Levitation**: Skip over dangerous floor traps
  * **Fury**: For final push against tough enemies
  * **Light**: Explore dark areas without torches

==== Debuff Management ====
  * **Poison**: Move quickly to minimize damage over time
  * **Burning**: Find water or use potions to stop the effect
  * **Paralysis**: Always carry potions of healing for emergency situations
  * **Blindness**: Try to retreat to known safe areas
  * **Charm/Amok**: Be aware of crowd control effects during boss fights

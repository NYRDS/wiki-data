====== Buffs ======

Buffs and debuffs are temporary status effects in Remixed Dungeon that modify character abilities, attributes, or game mechanics for a limited time.

==== Positive Buffs (Buffs) ====
  * [[rpd:regeneration|Regeneration]] - Gradually heals HP
  * [[rpd:haste|Haste]] - Increases movement and attack speed
  * [[rpd:invisibility|Invisibility]] - Makes you undetectable to enemies
  * [[rpd:levitation|Levitation]] - Allows movement over traps and special terrain
  * [[rpd:shield|Shield]] - Provides temporary damage absorption
  * [[rpd:elemental_charge|Elemental Charge]] - Charges elemental damage for next attack

==== Negative Buffs (Debuffs) ====
  * [[rpd:poison|Poison]] - Gradually damages HP
  * [[rpd:burning|Burning]] - Damages while on fire
  * [[rpd:bleeding|Bleeding]] - Damages over time
  * [[rpd:paralysis|Paralysis]] - Prevents all actions
  * [[rpd:weakness|Weakness]] - Reduces strength and damage
  * [[rpd:slow|Slow]] - Reduces movement and attack speed

==== Duration and Stacking ====
  * Most buffs/debuffs have a timed duration that counts down each turn
  * Some effects stack, others overwrite previous instances
  * Powerful effects may override lesser effects of the same type
  * Some class abilities can extend or intensify buff/debuff effects

==== Strategic Use ====
  * Apply beneficial buffs before challenging encounters
  * Remove harmful debuffs with appropriate items or abilities
  * Some enemies are vulnerable to specific debuffs
  * Certain equipment synergizes well with specific buffs

==== Class Interactions ====
  * [[rpd:mage|Mages]] can apply various elemental buffs/debuffs
  * [[rpd:rogue|Rogues]] have abilities to grant stealth buffs or apply debuffs
  * [[rpd:priest|Priests]] can provide healing and protective buffs
  * [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]] can apply and exploit various debuffs

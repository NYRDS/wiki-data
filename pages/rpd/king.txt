====== The King ======

{:rpd:images:king_sprite.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:king_sprite.png|The King }}

The King is the third boss in Remixed Dungeon, found in the City area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Level 19 (end of City)
  * **HP**: Very High
  * **Attack**: Very High
  * **Defense**: Very High
  * **EXP**: Very High

==== Description ====
The King is the former ruler of the dwarven city who has been corrupted by dark magic. He commands the remaining dwarven undead and mechanical constructs. He's a formidable opponent with both magical and physical attacks.

==== Behavior ====
  * Summons Undead servants during the fight
  * Uses powerful magical attacks
  * Controls the dungeon mechanics during the fight
  * Has multiple phases with different abilities

==== Drops ====
The King typically drops:
  * **Armor Kit** - Used to upgrade any armor into class armor
  * **Skeleton Key** - Opens special locks
  * Various powerful equipment
  * Large amount of gold
  * Unique items specific to this fight

==== Special Notes ====
  * Third boss, located at the end of the City level
  * Former ruler of the dwarven city
  * Defeating the King unlocks the next area of the dungeon
  * The fight involves defeating Undead minions as well as the King
  * Armor Kit reward is one of the most important items for subclass advancement
  * One of the unlock requirements for the Gnoll hero class

====== The King ======

{:rpd:images:king_sprite.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:king_sprite.png|The King }}

The King is the third boss in Remixed Dungeon, found in the City area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Level 19 (end of City)
  * **HP**: Very High
  * **Attack**: Very High
  * **Defense**: Very High
  * **EXP**: Very High

==== Description ====
The King is the former ruler of the dwarven city who has been corrupted by dark magic. He commands the remaining dwarven undead and mechanical constructs. He's a formidable opponent with both magical and physical attacks.

==== Behavior ====
  * Summons Undead servants during the fight
  * Uses powerful magical attacks
  * Controls the dungeon mechanics during the fight
  * Has multiple phases with different abilities

==== Drops ====
The King typically drops:
  * **Armor Kit** - Used to upgrade any armor into class armor
  * **Skeleton Key** - Opens special locks
  * Various powerful equipment
  * Large amount of gold
  * Unique items specific to this fight

==== Special Notes ====
  * Third boss, located at the end of the City level
  * Former ruler of the dwarven city
  * Defeating the King unlocks the next area of the dungeon
  * The fight involves defeating Undead minions as well as the King
  * Armor Kit reward is one of the most important items for subclass advancement
  * One of the unlock requirements for the Gnoll hero class

====== King of Dwarves ======

{{ rpd:images:king_sprite.png|King Sprite  }}{{ :rpd:king.png?nolink&50|}}

The King of Dwarves is a powerful boss enemy found in the City (Dwarven Metropolis) area of the dungeon. He is the ruler of the remaining dwarven civilization and fiercely defends his territory.

==== Description ====
The King of Dwarves was once the ruler of the great Dwarven Metropolis. Though his kingdom has fallen to corruption, he continues to rule over what remains of the dwarven city. He is heavily armored and wields powerful magical and physical attacks, making him one of the most challenging mid-game bosses.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Very high
  * **Attack:** Powerful physical attacks with a massive hammer
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Call for assistance from loyal dwarven spirits during combat
    * Powerful area attacks that can hit multiple tiles
    * Resistance to various forms of damage
    * Defensive abilities that make him harder to damage
  * **Phase Mechanics:** Gains new abilities as health decreases

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Call for Help:** Summons dwarven ghosts to aid him in battle
  * **Royal Armor:** Heavy armor that reduces incoming damage
  * **Area of Effect:** Attacks that can hit large areas around him
  * **Loyalty:** Dwarven spirits will fight to protect their king

==== Location ====
  * Found in the City area (level 20)
  * Appears in a royal throne room arena

==== Preparation ====
  * **Equipment:** Powerful weapons capable of piercing heavy armor
  * **Consumables:** Healing items and anti-magic protection
  * **Strategy:** Plan for dealing with multiple enemies (King + spirits)
  * **Anti-Magic:** Items that might counteract magical abilities

==== Tactics ====
  * Focus on the King while being aware of summoned spirits
  * Dodge area attacks by maintaining proper distance
  * Attack during windows when spirits are not protecting the King
  * Prepare for increased difficulty as the King's health decreases
  * Bring items that can deal with both physical and magical defenses

==== Rewards ====
  * Access to the next dungeon area (Halls)
  * Powerful dwarven equipment, possibly including rare artifacts
  * Valuable experience and royal treasure
  * Unique dwarven-themed items

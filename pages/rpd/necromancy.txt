====== Necromancy ======

**Necromancy** is a magical discipline in Remixed Pixel Dungeon related to death, undead creatures, and corpse manipulation.

==== Overview ====
Necromancy encompasses various mechanics, items, enemies, and gameplay elements related to death and undead themes in the dungeon.

==== Classes ====
  * [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] - The primary necromancy-focused class
  * [[rpd:lich|Lich]] - A playable undead class with necromantic affinities

==== Locations ====
  * [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]] - The necromancy-themed branch dungeon

==== Items ====
  * [[rpd:sacrificial_sword|Sacrificial Sword]] - A necromantic weapon
  * [[rpd:corpse_dust|Corpse Dust]] - Item obtained from skeleton remains
  * [[rpd:black_skull|Black Skull]] - Artifact for raising minions
  * [[rpd:spell_book|Spell Book]] - Can contain necromantic spells

==== Mechanics ====
  * [[rpd:necrotism|Necrotism]] - A spreading death effect
  * [[rpd:summon_deathling|Summon Deathling]] - Create undead minions
  * Corpse and skeleton interactions

==== Enemies ====
  * [[rpd:zombie|Zombie]] - Basic undead creature
  * [[rpd:exploding_skull|Exploding Skull]] - Explosive undead
  * [[rpd:death_knight|Death Knight]] - Armored undead warrior
  * [[rpd:dread_knight|Dread Knight]] - Elite armored undead
  * [[rpd:lich_mob|Lich (Mob)]] - Powerful undead sorcerer
  * [[rpd:enslaved_soul|Enslaved Soul]] - Ghostly undead

==== Spells ====
  * [[rpd:summon_deathling|Summon Deathling]] - Summon undead minion
  * Dark Sacrifice - Sacrificial magic
  * Exhumation - Raise the dead
  * Raise Dead - Reanimate fallen enemies
  * Possess - Control enemy bodies

==== Strategy ====
Playing with necromancy mechanics involves managing undead minions, using death-based effects strategically, and taking advantage of the unique abilities that come with necromantic themes.

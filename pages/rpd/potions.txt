====== Potions ======

{{ rpd:images:potions_illustration.png|Potions }}

**Potions** are consumable items in Remixed Dungeon that provide various beneficial, and sometimes harmful, effects when drunk. They are found throughout the dungeon or purchased from shops.

==== Common Potions ====
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]]**: Restores health, often partially identified as "potion of restoration"
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]]**: Permanently increases strength and maximum health
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]]**: Permanently increases strength
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]]**: Provides experience points to increase level
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]]**: Makes the hero temporarily invisible to enemies
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]]**: Creates a large area of fire when shattered
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]]**: Creates an area of chilling gas when shattered
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]]**: Creates a cloud of paralyzing gas when shattered
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]]**: Creates a cloud of toxic gas when shattered
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]]**: Neutralizes harmful gases and removes debuffs
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_levitation_item|Potion of Levitation]]**: Allows floating over traps and pits for a time

==== Potion Mechanics ====
  * **Identification**: Potions must be identified to know their effects
  * **Drinking**: Potions are consumed directly for their effects
  * **Shattering**: Some potions can be thrown to create area effects
  * **Spillage**: Potions may break if they fall or are thrown incorrectly
  * **Temporary Effects**: Some potions provide only time-limited benefits
  * **Permanent Effects**: Some potions provide lasting character improvements

==== Potion Effects ====
  * **Health Restoration**: Directly heal damage taken
  * **Stat Increases**: Permanently improve strength or health
  * **Experience Gain**: Increase character level
  * **Status Protection**: Provide temporary immunity to certain effects
  * **Environmental Effects**: Create area changes when shattered
  * **Utility Effects**: Provide temporary special abilities

==== Potion Identification ====
  * **Scroll of Identify**: Primary method to identify potions
  * **Taste Method**: Drinking partially identifies the potion type
  * **Equipment Synergy**: Some equipment aids in identification
  * **Gradual Recognition**: Using potions repeatedly may help identify them
  * **Risk Assessment**: Identifying unknown potions involves potential risks

==== Strategic Potion Usage ====
  * **Healing Priority**: Use healing potions when health is low
  * **Emergency Situations**: Keep healing potions for critical situations
  * **Preparation**: Drink beneficial potions before challenging encounters
  * **Area Effects**: Throw certain potions to affect groups of enemies
  * **Resource Management**: Balance potion usage with availability

==== Potion Risks ====
  * **Cursed Potions**: Some unidentified potions may be cursed and harmful
  * **Wasted Utility**: Using the wrong potion type in critical situations
  * **Spillage**: Breaking potions by throwing them inappropriately
  * **Timing**: Using beneficial potions at the wrong moment
  * **Inventory Management**: Running out of essential potions

==== Potion Acquisition ====
  * **Dungeon Exploration**: Found randomly throughout dungeon levels
  * **Shop Purchases**: Available from shopkeepers in certain levels
  * **Enemy Drops**: Sometimes dropped by defeated enemies
  * **Alchemy**: Crafted using the cauldron system if available
  * **Altar Interactions**: Certain altars may provide potions

==== Potion Categories ====
  * **Health Potions**: Restore hit points
  * **Stat Potions**: Provide permanent stat improvements
  * **Utility Potions**: Provide various beneficial effects
  * **Environmental Potions**: Create area effects when shattered
  * **Status Potions**: Provide temporary status bonuses

==== Special Potion Interactions ====
  * **Burning**: Some potions may ignite flammable items in inventory
  * **Combination Effects**: Effects may stack or interact with other status effects
  * **Environmental Impact**: Some potions affect the level environment
  * **Equipment Synergy**: Some equipment provides benefits when drinking potions

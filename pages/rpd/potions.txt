====== Potions ======

{{ rpd:images:item_PotionOfHealing.png|Potion of Healing }}

**Potions** are consumable items in Remixed Dungeon that provide various beneficial or harmful effects when drunk by the hero or applied to enemies.

==== Types of Potions ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Restores health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] - Increases strength
  * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]] - Permanently increases strength and health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] - Freezes enemies and extinguishes fire
  * [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]] - Creates fire on the ground
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]] - Releases poisonous gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] - Releases paralyzing gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]] - Provides temporary invisibility
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] - Neutralizes harmful gases and poisons
  * [[rpd:potion_of_experience|Potion of Experience]] - Grants experience points
  * [[rpd:potion_of_levitation_item|Potion of Levitation]] - Allows floating over traps
  * [[rpd:potion_of_holy_water|Potion of Holy Water]] - Damages undead enemies

==== Identification ====
  * Potions initially appear as colorless, unidentified items
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]] can identify a potion
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_knowledge|Scroll of Knowledge]] can identify a potion and reveal its effects
  * Drinking an unidentified potion reveals its effect but consumes it

==== Strategy ====
  * Identify potions early using scrolls to avoid dangerous effects
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] is the most valuable for survival
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] is useful in emergencies against fire-wielding enemies
  * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]] useful for escaping dangerous situations
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] helps clear dangerous gases

==== Mechanics ====
  * Potions are consumed when drunk
  * Some potions affect the drinker, others affect the environment
  * Potions can be thrown to affect enemies or the environment
  * Thrown potions shatter and create an area effect
  * Some potions can be used for alchemy to create special items

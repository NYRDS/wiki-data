====== Necrotism ======

Necrotism is a powerful necromantic ability available to the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class. It spreads a magical plague that may infect any creature in the vicinity, dead or alive.

==== Description ====
Necrotism is one of the signature abilities of the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]]. It represents the mark of death itself, spreading an infectious effect that can affect multiple creatures around the Necromancer.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Area Effect:** Affects all creatures in the vicinity of the Necromancer
  * **Cross-Undead:** Can affect both living and undead creatures
  * **Infectious:** May spread from creature to creature
  * **Resource:** Requires souls to cast

==== Effects ====
  * **Plague Spread:** Spreads a magical plague among nearby creatures
  * **Damage Over Time:** May cause ongoing effects to infected creatures  
  * **Potential Conversion:** May have chance to convert enemies to the Necromancer's side
  * **Stacking:** Duration and potency may scale with the Necromancer's level

==== Usage ====
  * **Situational:** Most effective when surrounded by enemies
  * **Crowd Control:** Useful for managing groups of weaker enemies
  * **Strategic:** Can turn enemy groups against each other
  * **Limited Resource:** Requires souls, which are finite resources

==== Equipment ====
  * Enhanced by [[rpd:necromancer_armor|Necromancer Armor]] and related equipment
  * May require specific armor or robes to use effectively

  * Most effective in enclosed spaces where enemies are grouped
  * Consider enemy resistances before using
  * Powerful against large groups of weaker enemies
  * Can turn fights in your favor by affecting multiple enemies simultaneously

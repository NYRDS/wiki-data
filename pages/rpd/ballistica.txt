====== Ballistica ======

Ballistica is a line-of-sight calculation system used in Remixed Dungeon for determining how ranged attacks and spells travel through the dungeon terrain. It's used by various mechanics including the Tengu's ranged attacks.

==== Description ====
Ballistica calculates the path for ranged attacks, spells, and other line-of-sight effects, accounting for obstacles like walls and determining which targets can be reached from a given position.

==== Mechanics ====
* Used for ranged attacks and spells
* Calculates path considering dungeon terrain
* Determines valid targets for ranged effects
* Used in various game mechanics beyond just Tengu

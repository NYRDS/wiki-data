====== Air Elemental ======

==== Description ====
Air elementals are flying magic beings made of living air.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Depth * 3 + 1
  * **Attack Skill:** Base defense skill * 2 + 1
  * **Defense Skill:** Depth * 2 + 1
  * **Damage:** 0 to ht() / 4
  * **Exp for Kill:** Depth + 1
  * **Max Level:** Depth + 2
  * **Damage Reduction:** expForKill / 5

==== Abilities ====
  * **Flight:** Air elementals are flying creatures and can move over any terrain
  * **Wind Gust:** When attacking enemies at range, Air elementals will cast Wind Gust, pushing the target away
  * **Distance Preference:** When in hunting state and at a distance less than skillLevel() - 1 from target, will move away

==== Immunities ====
  * Bleeding

==== Drops ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_levitation|Potion of Levitation]] (10% chance)

==== Notes ====
Air Elementals are magical creatures that can be found in magical areas of the dungeon. They are immune to bleeding and use wind-based attacks against their enemies.

====== Subclasses ======

**Subclasses** are specialized mastery paths that heroes can unlock after reaching level 10 and obtaining a [[rpd:tome_of_mastery_item|Tome of Mastery]].

==== Unlocking Subclasses ====
  * Reach level 10 with a hero class
  * Find and read a [[rpd:tome_of_mastery_item|Tome of Mastery]]
  * Gain sufficient experience to unlock a subclass
  * Some subclasses have special unlock conditions

==== Benefits ====
  * Unique class abilities
  * Specialized equipment or items
  * Different playstyle from base class
  * Enhanced combat or utility abilities

==== Mechanics ====
  * Subclasses often have signature abilities or items
  * Some subclasses gain special benefits with certain equipment
  * Subclass abilities may scale with hero level
  * Some subclasses have passive abilities that affect gameplay

==== Mechanics by Subclass ====
  * **Gladiator**: Combo bonus when using melee weapons
  * **Berserker**: Fury buff when health is low
  * **Warlock**: Gain health from enemy kills when using wands
  * **Battle Mage**: Regain wand charges when attacking with wands
  * **Assassin**: Bonus damage from stealth
  * **FreeRunner**: Faster movement speed
  * **Sniper**: Mark enemies when using ranged weapons
  * **Warden**: Bonus dew collection from plants
  * **Scout**: Natural haste
  * **Shaman**: Occasionally zap enemies with wands again
  * **Lich**: Undead transformation ability when skill points are full
  * **WitchDoctor**: Mana shield when casting spells
  * **Guardian**: Reduced damage when using shields

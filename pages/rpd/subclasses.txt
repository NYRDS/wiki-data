====== Subclasses ======

{{ rpd:images:subclasses_illustration.png|Subclasses }}

**Subclasses** are advanced specializations that become available to heroes after defeating the second boss (Tengu) in Remixed Dungeon. They represent the mastery path a character can choose to further specialize their abilities.

==== Unlocking Subclasses ====
  * **Requirement**: Defeat the second boss (Tengu) to unlock subclass choice
  * **Selection Process**: Choose one of two available specializations for your class
  * **Permanence**: Once chosen, the subclass cannot be changed
  * **Benefits**: Significant gameplay enhancements and new abilities

==== Warrior Subclasses ====

===== Gladiator =====
{{ rpd:images:gladiator_hero.png|Gladiator }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Warrior class, melee weapon and armor
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Combo**: Deal increasing bonus damage for consecutive melee hits
    * **Weapon Mastery**: Special techniques with melee weapons
  * **Playstyle**: Sustained melee combat with building damage potential
  * **Strategy**: Focus on staying in combat to maximize combo effectiveness

===== Berserker =====
{{ rpd:images:berserker_hero.png|Berserker }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Warrior class, melee weapon
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Enraged**: Deal more damage when health is low
    * **Self-healing**: Gain health when dealing damage
  * **Playstyle**: Aggressive combat with low health risk
  * **Strategy**: Embrace dangerous situations for increased damage potential

==== Mage Subclasses ====

===== Battlemage =====
{{ rpd:images:battlemage_hero.png|Battlemage }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Mage class, staff weapon
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Staff Specialization**: Staves gain additional effects when used by battlemages
    * **Elemental Synergy**: Combining staff and wand attacks creates special effects
  * **Playstyle**: Close-range magical combat
  * **Strategy**: Use staff attacks in combination with wand effects

===== Warlock =====
{{ rpd:images:warlock_hero.png|Warlock }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Mage class
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Drain Life**: Damage dealt heals the warlock
    * **Undead Control**: Can control certain undead creatures
  * **Playstyle**: Sustained combat with health regeneration
  * **Strategy**: Maintain health by causing damage to enemies

==== Rogue Subclasses ====

===== Assassin =====
{{ rpd:images:assassin_hero.png|Assassin }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Rogue class
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Sneak Attack**: Bonus damage when attacking from behind
    * **Perfect Stealth**: Become invisible in tall grass faster
  * **Playstyle**: Ambush and stealth-based combat
  * **Strategy**: Position carefully to attack from behind for critical damage

===== Freerunner =====
{{ rpd:images:freerunner_hero.png|Freerunner }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Rogue class
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Enhanced Movement**: Moves faster when not hungry
    * **Poison Resistance**: Reduced damage from poison
    * **Speed Buff**: Gains speed when not hungry
  * **Playstyle**: Mobility-focused gameplay
  * **Strategy**: Maintain good hunger status for maximum mobility

==== Huntress Subclasses ====

===== Sniper =====
{{ rpd:images:sniper_hero.png|Sniper }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Huntress class
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Enhanced Accuracy**: Increased hit chance with ranged weapons
    * **Soul Arrows**: Special attacks with magical arrows
  * **Playstyle**: Long-range precision combat
  * **Strategy**: Focus on accuracy and positioning for maximum effectiveness

===== Warden =====
{{ rpd:images:warden_hero.png|Warden }}
  * **Prerequisites**: Huntress class
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * **Grass Growth**: Trample grass to grow more grass
    * **Plant Knowledge**: Enhanced effects from plants
    * **Insect Allies**: Can control insects
  * **Playstyle**: Nature-based support and utility
  * **Strategy**: Use natural elements for tactical advantages

==== Subclass Strategy Considerations ====
  * **Equipment Focus**: Each subclass works best with specific equipment
  * **Combat Style**: Subclasses encourage specific approaches to combat
  * **Long-term Planning**: Consider subclass synergies when choosing equipment
  * **Playstyle Match**: Choose subclasses that match your preferred gameplay

==== Subclass Advantages ====
  * **Specialization**: Gain powerful class-specific abilities
  * **Enhanced Gameplay**: New strategic options and gameplay mechanics
  * **Increased Power**: Significant boosts to specific playstyles
  * **Replay Value**: Different subclasses make each playthrough unique
  * **Tactical Depth**: More options for strategy and approach

==== Subclass Disadvantages ====
  * **Narrow Focus**: May limit flexibility in certain situations
  * **Equipment Dependency**: Some subclasses require specific equipment
  * **Learning Curve**: New mechanics require adaptation
  * **Irreversible Choice**: Cannot change subclasses after selection
  * **Situational Limitations**: Some subclasses struggle with certain enemies

==== Subclass Synergy ====
  * **Equipment**: Certain items work better with specific subclasses
  * **Enemies**: Some subclasses are more effective against certain enemy types
  * **Levels**: Different subclasses excel in different dungeon areas
  * **Resources**: Subclasses may affect the optimal use of consumables

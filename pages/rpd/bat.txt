====== Bat ======
{:rpd:images:bat_sprite.png?200|}
{{ rpd:images:bat_sprite.png|Bat }}
Bats are flying enemies found throughout the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon, becoming more common in the Caves area.
==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found from Caves onwards (level 11+)
  * **HP**: Low
  * **Attack**: Moderate
  * **Defense**: Low
  * **EXP**: Moderate
==== Description ====
Bats are fast-moving flying creatures that inhabit the caves and deeper areas of the dungeon. Their speed allows them to quickly close distance with adventurers.
==== Behavior ====
  * Fast movement speed
  * Can move over obstacles and other creatures
  * High evasion due to agility
  * Often appears in groups
==== Drops ====
Bats typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Occasionally useful items
==== Special Notes ====
  * Flying enemy, can move over terrain and other creatures
  * High evasion makes it harder to hit
  * Common enemy in the Caves and deeper areas
  * Fast movement makes it challenging to control in groups

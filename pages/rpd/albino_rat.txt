====== Albino Rat ======

{{ rpd:images:albino_rat_sprite.png|Albino Rat }}

Albino Rats are rare variant of regular rats found in the Sewers area of Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in Sewers (level 1-5)
  * **HP**: Low
  * **Attack**: Low
  * **Defense**: Low
  * **EXP**: Low

==== Description ====
Albino Rats are rare white variants of the common dungeon rat. They appear in the Sewers and behave similarly to regular rats but in smaller numbers.

==== Behavior ====
  * Similar to regular rats
  * Moves directly toward the player
  * Generally appears alone rather than in groups
  * Slightly more dangerous than regular rats

==== Drops ====
Albino Rats typically drop:
  * Small amount of gold
  * Maybe slightly better items than regular rats

==== Special Notes ====
  * Rare variant of the common rat
  * Appears less frequently than regular rats
  * Distinguished by white coloration
  * Still a weak early-game enemy
  * More of a curiosity than a significant threat

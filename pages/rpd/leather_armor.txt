====== leather armor ======

{{ rpd:images:leatherarmor_sprite.png|Leather Armor Sprite }}

==== Description ====
**Leather Armor** is a basic armor type in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. Armor made from tanned monster hide. Not as light as cloth armor, but it provides better protection.

==== Stats ====
  * **Armor Tier**: Medium
  * **Durability**: Moderate
  * **Speed Penalty**: Low to Moderate
  * **Protection**: Better than cloth armor

==== Mechanics ====
  * Provides moderate protection with a reasonable speed penalty
  * Crafted from monster hide
  * Serves as a stepping stone between cloth armor and more advanced armor types

==== Availability ====
  * Found in shops, particularly in the town shop
  * Can appear as loot in various dungeon levels

====== Shaman ======

{:rpd:images:shaman_sprite.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:shaman_sprite.png|Shaman }}

The Shaman is the Elf's subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Unlocking ====
The Shaman subclass can be unlocked by using a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss (Tengu).

==== Special Mechanics ====
Elven _Shamans_ can use wands as melee weapons. Successful hits have a chance to activate the wand\'s power on a target without using a charge.

==== Special Armor ====
Using an Armor Kit on any armor will transform it into the [[rpd:shaman_armor|Shaman Armor]], which is specifically designed for this subclass.

==== Advantages ====
  * Can use wands as melee weapons
  * Chance to activate wand powers without consuming charges
  * Versatile weapon options combining ranged and melee
  * Preserves wand charges when power activates
  * Synergizes well with all wand types

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Activation chance is not guaranteed
  * Wand damage as melee weapon may be lower than dedicated weapons

====== Shaman (Hero Subclass) ======

==== Description ====
The **Shaman** is a mastery subclass of the [[rpd:elf|Elf]] class in Remixed Dungeon. Elven Shamans can use wands as melee weapons, making them unique in their ability to combine magical and physical combat.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Wand Melee:** Can use wands as melee weapons
  * **Chance Activation:** Successful hits have a chance to activate the wand's power on a target without using a charge
  * **Magical Combat:** Combines martial and magical abilities effectively

==== Notes ====
The Shaman subclass allows Elves to utilize wands in close combat, making them versatile fighters who can leverage magical effects even in melee situations.

**Note**: This is the Elf hero subclass. For the dungeon enemy (typically a Gnoll Shaman), see [[rpd:shaman_mob|Gnoll Shaman]].

====== Shaman (Hero Subclass) ======

==== Description ====
The **Shaman** is a mastery subclass of the [[rpd:elf|Elf]] class in Remixed Dungeon. Elven Shamans can use wands as melee weapons, making them unique in their ability to combine magical and physical combat.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Wand Melee:** Can use wands as melee weapons
  * **Chance Activation:** Successful hits have a chance to activate the wand's power on a target without using a charge
  * **Magical Combat:** Combines martial and magical abilities effectively

==== Notes ====
The Shaman subclass allows Elves to utilize wands in close combat, making them versatile fighters who can leverage magical effects even in melee situations.

**Note**: This is the Elf hero subclass. For the dungeon enemy (typically a Gnoll Shaman), see [[rpd:shaman_mob|Gnoll Shaman]].

====== Mana Potion ======

{{rpd:images:mana_potion_drinks.png?200|Mana Potion Drinks}}

A Mana Potion is a magical consumable item that restores the drinker's spiritual energy, allowing them to cast more spells.

==== Description ====
{{ :rpd:mana_potion.png?nolink&50|}}

Mana Potions restore magical energy, which is required for casting spells and using certain magical abilities. They are particularly valuable for spellcasters and magic-focused characters.

==== Effect ====
  * **Restores:** Spiritual energy/mana
  * **Usage:** Consumed when the player has mana-based abilities
  * **Benefit:** Allows continued use of magical abilities and spells

==== Obtaining Mana Potions ====
Mana Potions can be obtained through:
  * Finding them in dungeon levels
  * Purchasing from shops
  * Brewing at alchemy pots (if the recipe is known)
  * As rewards from quests or NPCs

====== Mana Potion ======

==== Description ====
This potion will restore your spiritual energy.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Restores a significant amount of mana
  * Can be consumed to replenish magical energy reserves
  * Single use item

==== Usage ====
To use a Mana Potion, select the "DRINK" action. The potion will restore a significant portion of your mana pool.

==== Notes ====
Mana Potions are essential for spellcasters who rely on magical abilities that consume mana. They're particularly important for classes like Mages and Necromancers who use mana for their special abilities.

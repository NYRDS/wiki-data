====== Giant Rat Skull ======

{{ rpd:images:item_RatSkull.png|Giant Rat Skull }}

The Giant Rat Skull is a quest item in Remixed Dungeon that serves as a reward from the Rat King.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Quest item
  * **Weight**: 1
  * **Price**: Cannot be sold

==== Description ====
The Giant Rat Skull is obtained as a reward from the Rat King upon completing the Rat King's quest. This quest is initiated by finding the Rat King Crown, which appears somewhere in the dungeon and teleports the player to the Rat King's special area.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Find the Rat King Crown in the dungeon (appears randomly in floors 1-4)
  * The crown will teleport the player to the Rat King's lair
  * Complete the Rat King's challenge to receive the Giant Rat Skull as a reward

==== Usage ====
  * **Quest Completion Reward:** This item marks the successful completion of the Rat King's quest
  * **Unlocking Content:** May be required for unlocking certain hero subclasses or special content in the game
  * **No Combat Use:** The skull has no direct combat application but is important for progression

==== Trivia ====
  * The Giant Rat Skull is one of the few quest items that can be kept in the inventory indefinitely
  * While it has no direct utility in-game, it's a symbol of completion of one of the early game's challenges

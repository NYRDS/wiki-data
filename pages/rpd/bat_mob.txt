====== Bat ======
{{ rpd:images:bat_sprite.png|Bat }}
Bats are flying enemies found throughout the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon, becoming common in the Caves area (levels 11-14). They are fast-moving creatures that heal themselves when they successfully hit their target.
==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found from Caves onwards (level 11+)
  * **HP**: 30
  * **Attack Skill**: 16
  * **Defense Skill**: 15
  * **Damage**: 6-12
  * **Defense Rating (DR)**: 4
  * **EXP**: 7
  * **Speed**: 2.0x normal speed
==== Description ====
Bats are fast-moving flying creatures that inhabit the caves and deeper areas of the dungeon. Their speed allows them to quickly close distance with adventurers.
==== Behavior ====
  * Fast movement speed (2.0x)
  * Flying enemy, can move over obstacles and other creatures
  * Heals damage dealt when they successfully hit their target
  * Resistant to Leech enchantment
  * Drops healing potions occasionally (12.5% chance)
==== Drops ====
Bats typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Occasionally a Potion of Healing (12.5% chance)
==== Special Notes ====
  * Flying enemy, can move over terrain and other creatures
  * High speed makes them difficult to control in combat
  * Heals when hitting their target, making them challenging to defeat
  * Resistant to Leech enchantment
  * Common enemy in the Caves and deeper areas

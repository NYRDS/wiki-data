====== Mimic Mob ======

**Mimic** is a special enemy in Remixed Dungeon that disguises itself as treasure.

==== Basic Stats ====
  * **HP**: Varies by level
  * **Attack Skill**: Moderate
  * **Defense Skill**: Moderate
  * **Damage**: Moderate
  * **EXP for Kill**: 0 (no experience gained)

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Disguise**: Appears as a chest until attacked
  * **Surprise Attack**: Attempts to surprise the player when approached
  * **No EXP**: Does not provide experience when defeated

==== Strategy ====
  * Be cautious around chests that seem easily accessible
  * Use ranged weapons or thrown items to test suspicious chests
  * When revealed, treat as a normal enemy
  * Does not provide experience points, making it less valuable than it appears

==== Drop ====
  * May drop the contents it was pretending to be
  * No guaranteed treasure beyond that

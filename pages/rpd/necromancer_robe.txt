====== Necromancer Robe ======

{{ :rpd:necromancer_robe.png?nolink&50|}}

The Necromancer Robe is specialized equipment for the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class that enables necromantic abilities, particularly the summoning of deathlings.

==== Description ====
This robe is imbued with dark magic and will allow the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] to consume souls of fallen monsters. It allows the Necromancer to summon a [[rpd:deathling|Deathling]] at a cost of 5 souls. The [[rpd:deathling|Deathling]] is an undead minion that grows stronger alongside their master.

==== Properties ====
  * **Class Restriction:** Only [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]] can use this robe
  * **Summoning:** Allows summoning one [[rpd:deathling|Deathling]] at a time
  * **Soul Cost:** Each [[rpd:deathling|Deathling]] requires 5 souls to summon
  * **Scaling:** Summoned creature grows stronger with hero level
  * **Soul Consumption:** Enhanced ability to consume souls from defeated enemies

==== Abilities ====
  * **Deathling Summoning:** Summon a [[rpd:deathling|Deathling]] to fight alongside the Necromancer
  * **Soul Enhancement:** Potentially increases soul yield from defeated enemies
  * **Necromantic Focus:** May enhance other necromantic abilities

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found as treasure in dungeon levels
  * Potentially available in special shops
  * Crafting recipe may exist for [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]]

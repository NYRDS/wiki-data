**DashSpell** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Dash towards a specified direction while colliding with enemies, dealing damage and causing maim.

==== Stats ====
* Magic Affinity: Combat
* Targeting: cell
* Level: 3
* Mana Cost: 10
* Special Effects: damages a target, pushes enemies, roots enemies in place

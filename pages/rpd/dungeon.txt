====== Dungeon ======

The Dungeon is the main gameplay area in Remixed Dungeon, consisting of multiple levels with increasing difficulty and challenges.

==== Structure ====
The dungeon consists of several distinct levels:

  * **Sewers (Floors 1-5)**: The starter area with relatively weak enemies and simple layout
  * **Prison (Floors 6-10)**: A more challenging area with varied enemies and traps
  * **Caves (Floors 11-15)**: Dark, dangerous area with stronger enemies
  * **City (Floors 16-20)**: An urban environment with advanced enemies
  * **Halls (Floors 21-25)**: The final vanilla dungeon area with powerful enemies
  * **Guts (Floors 26-30)**: Post-game content exclusive to Remixed Dungeon with the strongest challenges

==== Dungeon Features ====
  * **Random Generation**: Each playthrough features a randomly generated dungeon layout
  * **Progressive Difficulty**: Each floor is more challenging than the previous
  * **Limited Resources**: Food and healing items become scarcer at deeper levels
  * **Unique Challenges**: Each area has its own special mechanics and dangers

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Shop Areas**: Certain floors contain shops where players can purchase useful items
  * **Secret Areas**: Hidden rooms and passages may contain valuable items
  * **Traps**: Various types of traps throughout the dungeon pose threats
  * **Altars**: Special locations for specific game mechanics

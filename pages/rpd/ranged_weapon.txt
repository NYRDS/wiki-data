====== Ranged Weapons ======

{{ rpd:images:bow_item.png|Ranged Weapon }}

Ranged weapons are items in Remixed Dungeon that allow the player to attack enemies from a distance without engaging in direct melee combat.

==== Description ====
Ranged weapons allow characters to engage enemies from safe distances, using various types of ammunition. They offer strategic advantages by allowing players to attack enemies without being immediately retaliated against.

==== Weapon Properties ====
  * **Range:** Ability to attack from a distance
  * **Ammunition:** Requires specific ammunition types
  * **Accuracy:** Generally high accuracy compared to melee weapons
  * **Damage:** Varies by weapon type
  * **Speed:** Attack speed varies by weapon

==== Types of Ranged Weapons ====
  * [[rpd:bow|Bows]] - Basic ranged weapons that fire arrows
  * [[rpd:crossbow_item|Crossbows]] - More powerful than regular bows
  * [[rpd:throwing_weapons|Throwing weapons]] - Knives, darts, and other throwable items

==== Ammunition ====
  * [[rpd:arrows|Arrows]] - Used with bows
  * [[rpd:darts_item|Darts]] - Thrown weapons that can be used with blowpipes
  * [[rpd:throwing_stone_item|Throwing Stones]] - Basic throwable projectiles
  * [[rpd:throwing_knife_item|Throwing Knives]] - Sharper throwable projectiles

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Line of Sight:** Requires clear line of sight to target
  * **Accuracy:** Affected by various factors including character equipment
  * **Range Limit:** Maximum distance depends on weapon type
  * **Ammunition Consumption:** Each shot consumes ammunition

==== Strategy ====
  * Use ranged weapons to engage enemies from safe distances
  * Maintain a good supply of appropriate ammunition
  * Use to whittle down health of dangerous enemies before melee engagement
  * Effective against enemies that are difficult to approach

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found throughout the dungeon
  * Available in shops
  * May be dropped by enemies

====== Warrior ======

==== Description ====
The Warrior is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is strong and sturdy, a master of melee combat.

==== Starting Perks ====
  * **11 Strength:** Starts with 11 points of Strength
  * **Short Sword:** Starts with a unique short sword that can be "reforged" to upgrade another melee weapon
  * **Missile Disadvantage:** Less proficient with missile weapons
  * **Food Healing:** Any piece of food restores some health when eaten
  * **Identified Strength Potions:** Potions of Strength are identified from the beginning

==== Class Abilities ====
The Warrior has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Gladiator:** Successful attacks with melee weapons allow the Gladiator to start a combo, increasing damage with each consecutive hit
  * **Berserker:** When severely wounded, enters a state of wild fury significantly increasing damage output
  * **Warlock:** After killing an enemy, consumes its soul to heal wounds and satisfy hunger

==== Special Notes ====
The Warrior is a solid, reliable class that excels in direct combat. With proper equipment, he can be an extremely effective fighter.

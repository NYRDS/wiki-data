====== Shadow ======

==== Description ====
Shadows silently wander through the ruins of the ancient prison, only occasionally showing themselves to adventurers, usually in their latest adventure.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 20
  * **Attack Skill:** 10
  * **Defense Skill:** 15
  * **Damage:** 5-10
  * **Exp for Kill:** 5
  * **Max Level:** 10
  * **Speed:** 2 (twice normal speed)
  * **Attack Delay:** 0.5 (fast attacks)

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Wall Walking:** Can move through walls and solid terrain
  * **High Speed:** Moves at twice the normal speed
  * **Fast Attacks:** Has a very short attack delay, allowing rapid attacks

==== Notes ====
The Shadow is a fast enemy that can move through walls, making it difficult to avoid. It appears primarily in the Prison area of the dungeon.

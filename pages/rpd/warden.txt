====== Warden ======

{{ rpd:images:warden_sprite.png|Warden }}

The Warden is the Huntress's subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Unlocking ====
The Warden subclass can be unlocked by using a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss (Tengu).

==== Special Mechanics ====
Having a strong connection with forces of nature gives _Wardens_ an ability to gather dewdrops and seeds from plants. Also trampling a high grass grants them a temporary armor buff.

==== Special Armor ====
Using an Armor Kit on any armor will transform it into the [[rpd:warden_armor|Warden Armor]], which is specifically designed for this subclass.

==== Advantages ====
  * Ability to gather dewdrops from plants
  * Ability to gather seeds from plants
  * Temporary armor buff from trampling high grass
  * Good sustainability and resource gathering
  * Synergizes well with plant mechanics

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Benefits are most visible in areas with plants and high grass
  * Temporary armor buff from grass is limited duration

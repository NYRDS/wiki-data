====== Witch Doctor ======

{{ rpd:images:witch_doctor_subclass.png|Witch Doctor }}

**Witch Doctor** is a hero subclass for the [[rpd:gnoll_class|Gnoll]] class in Remixed Dungeon. The Witch Doctor specializes in shamanistic magic, brewing potions, and controlling the battlefield with various alchemical effects.

==== Description ====
The Witch Doctor subclass enhances the Gnoll's natural abilities with shamanistic magic and alchemical knowledge. Witch Doctors can brew potions from dungeon resources and use various status effects to control combat situations.

==== Abilities ====
  * **Alchemical Mastery:** Ability to create and enhance potions from dungeon resources
  * **Status Effect Control:** Specialization in applying beneficial status effects to allies and harmful ones to enemies
  * **Shamanistic Magic:** Potent healing and support magic
  * **Toxic Resistance:** Enhanced resistance to poison and other harmful effects

==== Playstyle ====
The Witch Doctor playstyle focuses on:
  * **Support Combat:** Supporting allies with buffs while debuffing enemies
  * **Resource Management:** Making use of dungeon resources to create useful items
  * **Battlefield Control:** Using status effects to control the flow of combat

==== Equipment Synergy ====
  * Benefits from equipment that enhances magic power
  * Effective with items that boost alchemical processes
  * Works well with rings and accessories that enhance support abilities

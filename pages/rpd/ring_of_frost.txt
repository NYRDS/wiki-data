====== Ring of Frost ======

{{rpd:images:ring_of_frost_rings.png?200|Ring Of Frost Rings}}

==== Description ====
A ring with frost-related properties that affects the wearer with cold-based abilities.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Cold Enhancement:** May enhance cold-based attacks
  * **Frost Resistance:** Could provide resistance to cold attacks
  * **Frost Effects:** Might apply frost-related effects to attacks
  * **Winter Magic:** Connects the wearer to winter/frost magic

==== Effects ====
  * Potentially increases damage of cold-based weapons or spells
  * May slow or freeze enemies on successful hits
  * Could provide resistance to frost-based damage

==== Notes ====
The Ring of Frost is a valuable accessory for characters who use cold-based attacks or need protection from frost-related effects.

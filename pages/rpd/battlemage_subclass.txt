====== Battle Mage Subclass ======

{{ rpd:images:battlemage_subclass.png|Battle Mage }}

==== Description ====
The Battle Mage is a subclass for the Mage hero class in Remixed Dungeon. Battle Mages focus on combining magical power with combat effectiveness, using wands as weapons while fighting.

==== Abilities ====
  * **Wand Combat:** Enhanced effectiveness and capabilities when using wands in combat
  * **Wand Charging:** Special ability to recharge wands while in combat
  * **Magic Attack:** Wand damage is added to regular attack damage
  * **Combat Magic:** Synergy between melee combat and magical abilities

==== Specialization ====
  * Focuses on weaponized magic use
  * Utilizes wands as combat tools beyond their normal use
  * Combines casting with physical combat

==== Lore ====
The Battle Mage subclass represents the warrior-mages of fantasy lore, who have mastered the art of using magic as a direct weapon in combat rather than just for utility.

==== Acquisition ====
The Battle Mage subclass can be unlocked by reading a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss, Tengu. It's only available to the Mage hero class.

==== Equipment ====
  * Special armor: [[rpd:mage_armor_item|Mage Armor]] can be obtained using an [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor
  * Preferred items: Wands, weapon-specific scrolls, combat-enhancing rings

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on using wands as weapons in addition to their normal functions
  * Utilize wand charging to maintain combat effectiveness
  * Build toward a magic-focused combat style
  * Leverage the wand damage bonus to attacks

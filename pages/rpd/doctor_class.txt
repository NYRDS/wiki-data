====== Doctor Class ======

The **Plague Doctor** is the display name for the **Doctor** class in Remixed Dungeon. This playable class is focused on alchemy and disease-based mechanics. The Plague Doctor specializes in toxic and curative arts, using both healing and poison-based abilities.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Primary Focus:** Medical and toxic arts, alchemy
  * **Special Ability:** Balances healing and toxic capabilities with alchemical mechanics
  * **Playstyle:** Support/nuke hybrid that can both heal allies and harm enemies

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Potions:** Identified [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]], [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]], and [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]]

==== Class Perks ====
  * **Harvesting Ability:** Harvests Toxic Glands, Rotten Organs, Bone Shards, and Vile Essence from slain enemies
  * **Blood Collection:** Blood is collected over time and used to perform alchemy
  * **Plague Mask:** Innate immunity to all gas effects (this is an innate ability, not an item you start with)
  * **Saw Bonuses:** Critical hits drop extra parts, and the Bone Saw deals +50% damage to paralyzed foes
  * **Identified Potions:** Potions of Paralysis Gas and Toxic Gas are pre-identified
  * **Toxic Explosion:** Enemies killed by Toxic Gas explode into Toxic Mist, healing the Doctor for 1 HP per affected enemy
  * **Weapon Limitation:** Suffers great accuracy penalties without the Bone Saw in hand and cannot use ranged weapons

==== Class Abilities ====
  * **Toxic Knowledge:** Ability to create and use toxic substances
  * **Healing Arts:** Access to healing abilities and curatives
  * **Dual Nature:** Can both harm enemies and support the player
  * **Alchemy:** Can perform special alchemical processes using harvested body parts
  * **Disease Mechanics:** Can inflict and manage various disease-type status effects
  * **Equipment:** Specialized plague doctor equipment

The Doctor class has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Witchdoctor:** Combines healing with nature-based magic

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Versatile abilities that can heal or harm
  * Access to unique gas-related mechanics
  * Good crowd control abilities
  * Ability to handle situations where other classes might struggle
  * Immunity to all gases
  * Alchemical abilities for crafting
  * Extra resources from defeated enemies

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Potentially complex mechanics to manage
  * May require more strategic thinking
  * Balancing healing and damage can be challenging
  * Great accuracy penalties without Bone Saw
  * Cannot use ranged weapons

==== Strategy ====
  * Take advantage of gas-based abilities for crowd control
  * Focus on collecting resources from defeated enemies
  * Balance healing and damage appropriately
  * Use environmental advantages with gas mechanics
  * Consider the needs of your current situation when choosing abilities
  * Keep Bone Saw equipped for accuracy

====== Assassin ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_ROGUE_ASSASSIN.png|Assassin Hero Sprite }}

**Assassin** is a [[rpd:subclass|subclass]] for the [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] class.

==== Description ====
The Assassin is a subclass of the Rogue that specializes in stealth and high-damage attacks. This subclass provides enhanced abilities for ambushing enemies and dealing critical damage from stealth.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Enhanced Backstab Damage:** Improved backstab ability that deals significantly more damage when attacking unaware enemies from behind
  * **Stealth Enhancement:** Better ability to remain undetected or perform surprise attacks
  * **Critical Strike Bonus:** Additional bonus to critical hit chance or damage

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on attacking unaware enemies to maximize backstab effectiveness
  * Use terrain and positioning to gain tactical advantages
  * Utilize stealth-enhancing items and abilities
  * Strike quickly and retreat to avoid direct confrontation when possible

====== Wooden Bow ======

{{ rpd:images:woodenbow_sprite.png|Wooden Bow }}

Wooden Bows - the most common kind of bow you might find in the dungeon.

This simple wooden bow might prove to be useful, once you find some arrows.

==== Description ====
A basic wooden bow that requires [[rpd:arrows|arrows]] to be used effectively. It's the simplest [[rpd:ranged_weapons|ranged weapon]] available in the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Tier:** 1
  * **Accuracy Multiplier:** 0.8 (80% base accuracy)
  * **Delay Multiplier:** 1.5 (150% of base attack delay)
  * **Damage Range:** 1-18 base (when unupgraded), scales with level via dmgFactor()

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Arrow Selection:** Has "Choose Arrows" action when equipped and [[rpd:arrows|arrows]] are available
  * **Accuracy Factor:** Scales with level at 1 + level * 0.1 per level (acuFactor() method)
  * **Damage Factor:** Scales with level at 1 + level * 0.25 per level (dmgFactor() method)
  * **Not Suitable for Melee:** Not effective as a melee weapon (goodForMelee returns false)
  * **Base Damage Calculation:** min()=tier, max()=(tierÂ²-tier+10)/ACU*DLY, where ACU=0.8, DLY=1.5

==== Usage ====
  * Equipped in the weapon slot
  * Requires compatible [[rpd:arrows|arrows]] to be effective
  * Can select which type of [[rpd:arrows|arrows]] to use via the "Choose Arrows" action
  * [[rpd:upgrading|Upgrading]] increases both accuracy and damage factors

==== Notes ====
  * Simplest ranged weapon available
  * Damage and accuracy improve with upgrades
  * Must be paired with arrows to be useful
  * The ElvenBow sometimes refers to this as "WoodenBow" internally

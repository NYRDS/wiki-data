====== Melee Weapons ======

**Melee Weapons** are close-combat weapons that require the wielder to be adjacent to their target to attack.

==== Categories ====
  * **Light**: Short swords, daggers, knuckles - Fast attack speed, lower damage
  * **Medium**: Swords, maces - Balanced attack speed and damage
  * **Heavy**: Axes, hammers, polearms - Slow attack speed, high damage
  * **Special**: Weapons with unique properties or special effects

==== Properties ====
  * **Speed**: How quickly the weapon attacks
  * **Damage**: The base damage range of the weapon
  * **Tier**: Affects upgrade potential and starting quality
  * **Enchantments**: Special magical properties that can be added
  * **Durability**: How long the weapon lasts before breaking

==== Upgrading ====
  * Melee weapons can be upgraded with [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * Higher upgrade levels can add bonuses to accuracy and damage
  * Upgraded weapons can receive enchantments at +1 or higher

====== Ranged Weapons ======

**Ranged Weapons** allow the hero to attack enemies from a distance, without being adjacent to them.

==== Categories ====
  * **Bows**: [[rpd:wooden_bow_item|Wooden Bow]], [[rpd:elf_bow|Elven Bow]], [[rpd:composite_crossbow|Composite Crossbow]], [[rpd:compound_bow|Compound Bow]], [[rpd:ruby_bow|Ruby Bow]] - Require ammunition
  * **Throwing Weapons**: [[rpd:dart_item|Dart]], [[rpd:shuriken_item|Shuriken]], [[rpd:boomerang_item|Boomerang]], [[rpd:javelin_item|Javelin]], [[rpd:kunai_item|Kunai]] - Single-use or reusable projectiles
  * **Special Ranged**: [[rpd:boomerang_item|Boomerang]] - Returns after throw, allowing reuse

==== Known Ranged Weapons ====
  * [[rpd:wooden_bow_item|Wooden Bow]] - Basic bow requiring arrows
  * [[rpd:elven_bow_item|Elven Bow]] - Improved bow with more durability
  * [[rpd:compound_bow|Compound Bow]] - Advanced bow with special properties
  * [[rpd:ruby_bow|Ruby Bow]] - Powerful bow with special attributes
  * [[rpd:dart_item|Dart]] - Basic throwing weapon
  * [[rpd:shuriken_item|Shuriken]] - Fast throwing weapon
  * [[rpd:boomerang_item|Boomerang]] - Returns after throw
  * [[rpd:javelin_item|Javelin]] - Heavy throwing weapon
  * [[rpd:tomahawk|Tomahawk]] - Thrown weapon effective against bleeding enemies

==== Properties ====
  * **Range**: How far the weapon can reach
  * **Accuracy**: Chance to hit the target
  * **Damage**: The base damage range of the weapon
  * **Durability**: How long the weapon lasts before breaking (for reusable weapons)
  * **Ammunition**: Type and amount of ammunition required (for bows)

==== Ammunition ====
  * [[rpd:arrows|Arrows]] - Used by bows
  * [[rpd:common_arrow_item|Common Arrow]] - Basic arrow type
  * [[rpd:fire_arrow_item|Fire Arrow]] - Arrow with fire effect
  * [[rpd:frost_arrow_item|Frost Arrow]] - Arrow with frost effect
  * [[rpd:poison_arrow_item|Poison Arrow]] - Arrow with poison effect
  * [[rpd:paralysis_arrow_item|Paralysis Arrow]] - Arrow with paralysis effect
  * [[rpd:health_arrow_item|Health Arrow]] - Arrow with healing effect
  * [[rpd:amok_arrow_item|Amok Arrow]] - Arrow with amok effect

==== Upgrading ====
  * Ranged weapons can be upgraded with [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * Higher upgrade levels can add bonuses to accuracy and damage
  * Upgraded weapons can receive enchantments at +1 or higher

====== Cold Spirit ======

{{ rpd:images:cold_spirit_mob.png|Cold Spirit }}

==== Description ====
The **Cold Spirit** is an undead spirit found in the Ice Caves level of Remixed Dungeon. It's a ghostly creature associated with cold and frost magic, creating dangerous freezing conditions in combat.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Moderate to high
  * **Attack/Defense:** Moderate
  * **Damage:** Moderate
  * **Speed:** Normal
  * **Type:** Undead, Ghost, Ice
  * **EXP:** Moderate

==== Abilities ====
  * **Freezing Touch:** May apply freezing or cold effects to enemies
  * **Cold Resistance:** Immunity to fire-based attacks or damage
  * **Ethereal Movement:** Can move through certain terrain
  * **Undead Nature:** Immune to certain status effects like poison or fear

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Ice Caves level
  * Associated with cold-themed dungeon areas
  * Part of the ice-themed enemy roster

==== Strategy ====
  * Be cautious of freezing effects that can disable your character
  * Fire-based attacks may be less effective due to cold resistance
  * Prepare for status effect immunity when using poison or similar tactics
  * Consider using weapons or items that bypass undead immunities

====== Cold Spirit ======

The **Cold Spirit** is a ghostly ice-themed enemy found in the [[rpd:ice_caves|Ice Caves]].

==== Description ====
Cold Spirits are ethereal beings of ice and cold that haunt the Ice Caves. They have the ability to pass through walls like other spirits, making them difficult to avoid in narrow corridors.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Low to Moderate
* Defense: Moderate
* Attack: Moderate cold damage
* Speed: High
* Special: Wall passing ability

==== Tactics ====
Cold Spirits are difficult to avoid due to their wall-passing ability. They should be defeated quickly before they can surround the hero. Area-effect attacks and weapons that work well against spirits are recommended.

====== Cold Spirit ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_ColdSpirit.png|Cold Spirit }}

The **Cold Spirit** is a ghostly ice-themed enemy found in the [[rpd:ice_caves|Ice Caves]].

==== Description ====
Cold Spirits are ethereal beings of ice and cold that haunt the Ice Caves. They have the ability to pass through walls like other spirits, making them difficult to avoid in narrow corridors. They can occasionally cause freezing effects when attacking.

==== Stats ====
* HP: 50
* HT: 50
* Defense: 16 (Defense Skill)
* Attack: 22 (Attack Skill)
* Damage: 12-15
* Speed: 1.3x
* DR: 22
* Exp for kill: 8
* Max Level: 20
* Flying: Yes
* Special: Wall passing ability, chance to cause freezing on hit

==== Special Abilities ====
* When attacking, has a 25% chance to create a freezing effect on the target location
* Immune to physical attacks that require direct contact in some situations due to flying nature

==== Drops ====
* Gold (2% chance)

==== Tactics ====
Cold Spirits are difficult to avoid due to their wall-passing and flying abilities. They should be defeated quickly before they can surround the hero. Area-effect attacks and weapons that work well against spirits are recommended. Be aware they can cause freezing effects when attacking.

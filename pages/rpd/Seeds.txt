====== Seeds ======

==== Description ====
Seeds are collectible items that can be planted in the dungeon to grow various useful plants. They play an important role in the game's alchemy and farming systems.

==== Types of Seeds ====
  * [[rpd:earthroot_seed|Earthroot Seed]] - Grows into Earthroot plants
  * [[rpd:firebloom_seed|Firebloom Seed]] - Grows into Firebloom plants
  * [[rpd:swifthunter_seed|Swifthunter Seed]] - Grows into Swifthunter plants
  * [[rpd:pr dewdrop_seed|Pride of the Dewdrop]] - Grows into healing plants
  * And many others

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Planting:** Can be planted in suitable soil locations
  * **Growth:** Will grow into useful plants over time
  * **Harvesting:** Mature plants can be harvested for useful items
  * **Alchemy Components:** Many seeds and their plants serve as alchemy ingredients

==== Notes ====
Collecting and properly using seeds is an important part of the game's resource management and can provide significant advantages to careful players.

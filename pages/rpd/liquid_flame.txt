====== Liquid Flame ======

**Liquid Flame** is an environmental effect in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that causes burning damage to characters who come into contact with it.

==== Description ====
Liquid Flame is a magical fire effect that appears as a burning liquid on dungeon floors. It typically results from throwing a [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]] at a location or other fire-based mechanics. Characters standing on Liquid Flame tiles take damage each turn they remain in contact with it.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Damage:** 1 damage per turn to any character standing on the tile
  * **Duration:** Typically lasts 5-8 turns before extinguishing naturally
  * **Spread:** Can spread to adjacent flammable terrain
  * **Ignition:** Will ignite any flammable environmental elements it touches
  * **Area:** Usually affects a 3x3 area when created by a potion

==== Interactions ====
  * **Water:** Water-based elements and potions can extinguish Liquid Flame
  * **Potions:** [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] and [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] can extinguish it
  * **Ice:** Frost-based mechanics can freeze and remove Liquid Flame
  * **Burning Status:** Characters in Liquid Flame gain the [[rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] status effect

==== Strategy ====
  * **Offensive:** Use to damage enemies in groups
  * **Defensive:** Create barriers to prevent enemy approach
  * **Environmental:** Useful in combination with other area effects
  * **Caution:** Avoid trapping yourself or allies in the flames

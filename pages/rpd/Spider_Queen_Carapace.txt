====== Spider Queen Carapace ======

==== Description ====
A powerful armor piece crafted from the carapace of a Spider Queen, offering excellent protection and spider-related abilities.

==== Stats ====
  * **Armor Class:** Very high
  * **Durability:** High
  * **Special Properties:** Spider-related abilities

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Web Resistance:** May provide resistance to web-based effects
  * **Spider Affinity:** Could reduce aggression from spiders
  * **Poison Resistance:** Might provide resistance to poison
  * **Boss Drop:** Obtained as a rare drop from the Spider Queen

==== Notes ====
Spider Queen Carapace is an excellent armor piece for navigating spider-infested areas, offering both strong protection and benefits against spider-related threats.

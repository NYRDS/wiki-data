====== Boss Enemies ======

Boss enemies are powerful monsters that guard important areas of the dungeon. They are significantly more difficult to defeat than regular enemies and often have unique abilities and special attacks.

==== List of Bosses ====

  * [[rpd:yog|Yog-Dzewa]] - The final boss of the dungeon
  * [[rpd:shadow_lord|Shadow Lord]] - The boss dwelling in the Halls
  * [[rpd:dm300|DM-300]] - The mechanical boss in the Goo level
  * [[rpd:king|King of Dwarves]] - The boss in the City level
  * [[rpd:tengu|Tengu]] - The boss in the Prison level
  * [[rpd:goo|Goo]] - The boss in the Sewer level
  * [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]] - The boss in the Spider Nest
  * [[rpd:lich|Lich]] - A powerful undead boss

==== Boss Strategies ====

Bosses require special strategies to defeat. Some general tips:

  * Prepare thoroughly before attempting to fight a boss
  * Bring sufficient healing items (potions, dew, etc.)
  * Bring utility items like scrolls or wands
  * Be patient with your attacks
  * Learn the boss' attack patterns
  * Use the environment to your advantage when possible

  * Much higher HP and stats than regular monsters
  * Unique abilities and attack patterns
  * Often require special strategies to defeat
  * Drop important items or keys when defeated

==== Notes ====
Boss fights typically require careful preparation, proper equipment, and strategic thinking. They often represent major milestones in dungeon progression.

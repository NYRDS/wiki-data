====== Bosses ======

Bosses are powerful unique enemies found at specific points in the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon. They are significantly stronger than regular mobs and often guard important items or areas.

==== Boss Order ====
  * [[rpd:tengu|Tengu]] - Boss of the Prison, found at level 6-9
  * [[rpd:dm300|DM300]] - Boss of the Caves, found at level 16-19
  * [[rpd:the_king|The King]] - Boss of the City, found at level 21-24
  * [[rpd:yog|Yog-Dzewa]] - Boss of the Halls, found at level 22-24
  * [[rpd:lich|Lich]] - Boss of the Necropolis, found at necro4
  * [[rpd:ice_guardian_core|Ice Guardian Core]] - Boss of the Ice Caves, found at ice4

==== Boss Mechanics ====
  * Bosses have unique abilities and higher stats than regular mobs
  * They often require specific strategies to defeat
  * Bosses are immune or resistant to certain effects
  * [[rpd:skeleton_key|Skeleton Key]] is typically found after defeating most bosses

==== Rewards ====
  * Bosses drop valuable items and equipment
  * Defeating bosses is required for progression (e.g. for [[rpd:hero_subclasses|Hero Subclasses]])
  * Some bosses drop keys needed for special areas

==== Strategy ====
  * Prepare with appropriate equipment and consumables
  * Learn each boss's unique mechanics and attack patterns
  * Bring healing items as boss fights can be long and dangerous
  * Some classes or subclasses may have advantages against specific bosses

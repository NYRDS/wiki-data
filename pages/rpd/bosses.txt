====== Bosses ======
Bosses are powerful enemies that guard each major area of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.
==== List of Bosses ====
  * **Tengu** (Prison) - The first boss, located in the prison area (levels 6-9)
  * **DM-300** (Caves) - The second boss, located in the caves area (levels 11-14)
  * **The King** (City) - The third boss, located in the city area (levels 16-19)
  * **Yog** (Halls) - The fourth boss, located in the halls area (levels 22-24)
  * **Ice Guardian Core** (Ice Caves) - The fifth boss, located in the ice caves area (levels ice1-ice4)
  * **Lich** (Necropolis) - The sixth boss, located in the necropolis area (levels necro1-necro4)
==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Each boss is significantly more powerful than regular dungeon enemies
  * Bosses have special abilities and attack patterns that require different strategies
  * Defeating a boss typically opens access to the next area of the dungeon
  * Bosses drop powerful unique items or equipment
==== Boss Fight Strategy ====
  * Prepare thoroughly with appropriate equipment and consumables
  * Learn each boss's attack patterns
  * Maintain distance when possible
  * Save powerful items for boss fights

====== Boss Enemies ======
Boss enemies are powerful monsters that guard important areas of the dungeon. They are significantly more difficult to defeat than regular enemies and often have unique abilities and special attacks.
  * [[rpd:yog|Yog-Dzewa]] - The final boss of the dungeon
  * [[rpd:shadow_lord|Shadow Lord]] - The boss dwelling in the Halls
  * [[rpd:dm300|DM-300]] - The mechanical boss in the Goo level
  * [[rpd:king|King of Dwarves]] - The boss in the City level
  * [[rpd:tengu|Tengu]] - The boss in the Prison level
  * [[rpd:goo|Goo]] - The boss in the Sewer level
  * [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]] - The boss in the Spider Nest
  * [[rpd:lich|Lich]] - A powerful undead boss
==== Boss Strategies ====
Bosses require special strategies to defeat. Some general tips:
  * Prepare thoroughly before attempting to fight a boss
  * Bring sufficient healing items (potions, dew, etc.)
  * Bring utility items like scrolls or wands
  * Be patient with your attacks
  * Learn the boss' attack patterns
  * Use the environment to your advantage when possible

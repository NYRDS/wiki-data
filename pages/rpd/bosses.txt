====== Bosses ======

Bosses are powerful enemies that guard each major area of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== List of Bosses ====

  * **Tengu** (Prison) - The first boss, located in the prison area (levels 6-9)
  * **DM-300** (Caves) - The second boss, located in the caves area (levels 11-14)
  * **The King** (City) - The third boss, located in the city area (levels 16-19)
  * **Yog** (Halls) - The fourth boss, located in the halls area (levels 22-24)
  * **Ice Guardian Core** (Ice Caves) - The fifth boss, located in the ice caves area (levels ice1-ice4)
  * **Lich** (Necropolis) - The sixth boss, located in the necropolis area (levels necro1-necro4)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Each boss is significantly more powerful than regular dungeon enemies
  * Bosses have special abilities and attack patterns that require different strategies
  * Defeating a boss typically opens access to the next area of the dungeon
  * Bosses drop powerful unique items or equipment

==== Boss Fight Strategy ====
  * Prepare thoroughly with appropriate equipment and consumables
  * Learn each boss's attack patterns
  * Maintain distance when possible
  * Save powerful items for boss fights

====== Boss Enemies ======

{{ rpd:images:bosses_illustration.png|Boss Enemies }}

**Boss Enemies** are powerful enemies that guard key areas of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon. Each boss presents a significant challenge and guards important rewards.

==== Tengu ====
{{ rpd:images:tengu_mob.png|Tengu }}
  * **Location**: Prison level
  * **Description**: A ninja-like assassin from an ancient clan
  * **Special Abilities**: 
    * Creates poison traps during combat
    * Teleports frequently to reset positioning
    * High evasion and uses shurikens
    * Resistant to gas and poison
  * **Rewards**: Tome of Mastery (if not already obtained), Tengu Liver (for Gnoll class), Skeleton Key (to unlock Halls)
  * **Combat Strategy**: Prepare for a long fight with positioning challenges

==== DM-300 ====
{{ rpd:images:dm300_mob.png|DM-300 }}
  * **Location**: Caves level
  * **Description**: An ancient machine designed for construction
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * Deconstructs the dungeon as it moves
    * Attacks with powerful mechanical arms
    * Launches toxic gas clouds
    * Resistant to many status effects
  * **Rewards**: Master Thieves' Armband or Mechanical Eye, Ankh of Wishes
  * **Combat Strategy**: Stay mobile and avoid the gas clouds it creates

==== King of Dwarves ====
{{ rpd:images:king_mob.png|King of Dwarves }}
  * **Location**: City level
  * **Description**: The undead ruler of the Dwarven Metropolis
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * Summons skeletons to fight for him
    * Casts powerful magic attacks
    * Becomes stronger as the fight progresses
    * Has multiple attack phases
  * **Rewards**: Crown of Thorns, Dried Rose, or Master Thieves' Armband
  * **Combat Strategy**: Focus on the King first before dealing with summoned skeletons

==== Yog-Dzewa ====
{{ rpd:images:yog_mob.png|Yog-Dzewa }}
  * **Location**: Halls level (final boss)
  * **Description**: An ancient god-like entity with multiple body parts
  * **Special Abilities**:
    * Multiple body parts that fight independently
    * Various elemental attacks
    * Summons Rotting Fists and Burning Fists
    * Powerful area-effect attacks
  * **Rewards**: Gladiator's or Berserker's weapon (highest tier), Huntress' or Assassin's armor, Amulet of Yendor
  * **Combat Strategy**: Defeat the body parts systematically and be prepared for sustained damage

==== Boss Combat Mechanics ====
  * **Preparation**: Boss fights require careful preparation with food, potions, and scrolls
  * **Long Fights**: Bosses have high HP and require extended combat
  * **Special Mechanics**: Each boss has unique mechanics to master
  * **Environmental Effects**: Boss arenas often have special properties
  * **Phases**: Many bosses have multiple phases with different abilities
  * **Positioning**: Critical positioning is often required for success

==== General Boss Fighting Tips ====
  * **Pre-buff**: Use beneficial potions before engaging
  * **Food Supply**: Ensure adequate food for the long battle
  * **Backup Plans**: Carry multiple types of consumables
  * **Positioning**: Use the environment to your advantage
  * **Escape Items**: Keep teleportation items as backup
  * **Patience**: Don't rush and risk unnecessary damage
  * **Equipment**: Ensure your equipment is appropriate for the challenge
  * **Health Management**: Keep health up during the battle

==== Boss-Specific Strategies ====
  * **Tengu**: Stay away from floor traps and prepare for positioning resets
  * **DM-300**: Avoid the toxic gas clouds and be ready for terrain changes
  * **King of Dwarves**: Manage summoned skeletons efficiently
  * **Yog-Dzewa**: Focus fire on one body part at a time

==== Boss Rewards ====
  * **Unique Items**: Special items with unique properties
  * **Equipment**: High-tier weapons or armor
  * **MacGuffins**: Items needed to progress in the game
  * **Class Items**: Special rewards based on hero class
  * **Experience**: Significant EXP gain for character development

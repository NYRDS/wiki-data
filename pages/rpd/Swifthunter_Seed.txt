====== Swifthunter Seed ======

==== Description ====
A seed from the Swifthunter plant that can be planted to grow a plant with speed-related properties.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be planted in suitable soil
  * Grows into a Swifthunter plant when planted
  * Has speed/buff-related effects when mature
  * Part of the game's farming and alchemy system

==== Effects ====
  * When grown, may provide speed or agility-related benefits
  * Could be used in alchemical recipes for movement enhancement
  * May provide beneficial effects when properly used

==== Notes ====
Swifthunter seeds are part of the game's plant system and can be used strategically to enhance movement speed or agility.

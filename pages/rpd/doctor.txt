====== Doctor ======

The **Doctor** (also known as Plague Doctor) is a playable class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that specializes in plague-themed abilities and expertise in harmful potions.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** PlagueDoctor magic affinity focused on plague and poison-based abilities
  * **Special Ability:** Uses harmful potions strategically and has specialized mechanics with the Bone Saw

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Known Items:** Identified [[rpd:potion_of_healing|Potion of Healing]], [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas|Potion of Toxic Gas]], and [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas|Potion of Paralytic Gas]]

==== Class Perks ====
  * **Potion Expertise:** Starts with identified healing and harmful potions ([[rpd:potion_of_healing|Potion of Healing]], [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas|Potion of Toxic Gas]], [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas|Potion of Paralytic Gas]])
  * **Specialized Equipment:** Uses [[rpd:bone_saw|Bone Saw]] as primary weapon with unique mechanics
  * **Accuracy Penalty:** When using weapons other than the Bone Saw, there's a 5% chance of accuracy reduction on attacks

==== Class Abilities ====
The Doctor has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Witchdoctor:** Combines healing with nature-based magic

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Starting potions are already identified
  * Can use harmful potions strategically
  * Special mechanics with Bone Saw weapon

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Accuracy penalty when not using Bone Saw (5% chance of accuracy reduction)
  * Unique weapon mechanics may require specific strategy

==== Unlock Condition ====
The Doctor class is marked as "Coming Soon" in the current implementation and cannot be selected directly from the start screen. It may become available in future updates.

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on using the Bone Saw for optimal accuracy
  * Leverage identified harmful potions for battlefield control
  * Use environmental effects like toxic gas to your advantage
  * Consider the Witchdoctor mastery for combined healing and nature magic

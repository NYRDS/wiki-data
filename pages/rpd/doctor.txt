====== Doctor ======

{{ rpd:images:doctor_sprite.png|Doctor }}

The Doctor is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He focuses on toxic and curative substances.

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 10
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Potion of Healing (known item)
    * Potion of Toxic Gas (known item)
    * Potion of Paralytic Gas (known item)
  * **Magic Affinity**: PlagueDoctor

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Doctor starts with multiple utility potions (Healing, Toxic Gas, Paralytic Gas).
  * No special mechanics documented, though the documentation suggests accuracy penalties when not using Bone Saw.

==== Advantages ====
  * Starts with multiple utility potions
  * Provides healing and area control options from the start
  * Good for crowd control with gas potions

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Status: "Coming Soon" - currently not fully implemented
  * May have accuracy penalties when not using Bone Saw (as mentioned in documentation)

==== Subclasses ====
  * No subclasses currently documented

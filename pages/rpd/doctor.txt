====== Doctor ======

**WORK IN PROGRESS** - Information on this class is incomplete and may not be fully accurate

The **Doctor** is a playable class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon with plague-themed abilities and expertise in harmful potions.

==== Class Features ====
  * **Magic Affinity**: PlagueDoctor magic affinity focused on plague and poison-based abilities
  * **Starting Items**: Begins with Potion of Healing, Potion of Toxic Gas, and Potion of Paralytic Gas
  * Likely specializes in using normally harmful potions as tools for advantage

==== Strategy ====
The Doctor can turn harmful potions and gases into strategic advantages. With a focus on plague-themed abilities and toxic gas, this class can control the battlefield using environmental effects that would normally be detrimental to other heroes.

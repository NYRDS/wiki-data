====== Doctor ======

{{ rpd:images:doctor_sprite.png|Doctor }}

The **Doctor** (also known as Plague Doctor) is a playable class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that specializes in plague-themed abilities and expertise in harmful potions.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** PlagueDoctor magic affinity focused on plague and poison-based abilities
  * **Special Ability:** Uses harmful potions strategically and has specialized mechanics with the Bone Saw

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 10
  * **Starting Equipment**:
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * [[rpd:potion_of_healing|Potion of Healing]] (known item)
    * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas|Potion of Toxic Gas]] (known item)
    * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] (known item)

==== Class Perks ====
  * **Potion Expertise:** Starts with identified healing and harmful potions ([[rpd:potion_of_healing|Potion of Healing]], [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas|Potion of Toxic Gas]], [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas|Potion of Paralytic Gas]])
  * **Specialized Equipment:** Uses Bone Saw as primary weapon with unique mechanics
  * **Accuracy Penalty:** When using weapons other than the Bone Saw, there's a 5% chance of accuracy reduction on attacks

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Doctor starts with multiple utility potions (Healing, Toxic Gas, Paralytic Gas).
  * Doctor has an accuracy penalty (5% chance to receive accuracy penalty, -5 to attack skill) when not using Bone Saw.

==== Class Abilities ====
The Doctor class does not have documented mastery paths in the current implementation.

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Starts with multiple utility potions
  * Provides healing and area control options from the start
  * Good for crowd control with gas potions
  * Starting potions are already identified
  * Can use harmful potions strategically

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Status: "Coming Soon" - currently not unlockable through normal gameplay
  * Accuracy penalty when not using Bone Saw (5% chance of accuracy reduction)
  * Unique weapon mechanics may require specific strategy

==== Unlock Condition ====
The Doctor class is marked as "Coming Soon" in the current implementation and cannot be selected directly from the start screen. It may become available in future updates through donation or special conditions.

==== Strategy ====

  * Leverage identified harmful potions for battlefield control
  * Use environmental effects like toxic gas to your advantage

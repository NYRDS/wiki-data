====== Plague Doctor NPC ======

{{ rpd:images:plaguedoctornpc_sprite.png|Plague Doctor NPC }}

The Plague Doctor is an NPC found in the town area of Remixed Dungeon. The NPC is part of a questline where players can help the doctor with research in exchange for rewards.

==== Description ====
The whole body of this humpbacked person is hidden under a leather jumpsuit. Also, from him comes a bitter smell of medicine and ... garlic?

==== Questline ====
The Plague Doctor offers a questline where players can complete various tasks in exchange for rewards. The main quest starts by collecting 5 rat hides to help with the doctor's research. The quest has multiple stages that require different items like snails, moongrace seeds, sungrass seeds, vampire bats, death knights, dwarf warlocks, etc.

==== Behavior ====
  * Approaches players and explains his plague research
  * Requests specific items for his experiments
  * Offers rewards in exchange for completing his tasks
  * After completing the initial quest, will give a Medicine Mask accessory

==== Rewards ====
  * Completing the initial quest rewards a Rat Armor
  * Completing the full questline unlocks a special Medicine Mask accessory
  * Provides various potions and scrolls based on the quest completed

==== Notes ====
  * The Plague Doctor can be found in the town level
  * This NPC is associated with the Plague Doctor hero class
  * Has an extended questline with multiple objectives

====== Golem ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_Golem.png|Golem }}

Golem is a powerful construct mob in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Basic Statistics ====
  * **Health:** 85 HP
  * **Attack Skill:** 28
  * **Defense Skill:** 18
  * **Damage:** 20-40
  * **Damage Reduction:** 12
  * **Experience:** 12 EXP for killing
  * **Maximum Level:** 22
  * **Attack Delay:** 1.5 turns

==== Description ====
The Golem is a large stone construct animated by magic. It is one of the tougher regular enemies in the later dungeon levels, with high HP, damage, and defense.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **High Durability:** High HP and damage reduction makes it quite tanky
  * **Strong Attacks:** Deals substantial damage with each hit
  * **Slow Attack Speed:** Attacks every 1.5 turns rather than every turn

==== Immunities ====
  * **Amok:** Immune to confusion effects
  * **Terror:** Immune to fear effects
  * **Sleep:** Immune to sleep effects
  * **Bleeding:** Immune to bleeding damage over time

==== Resistances ====
  * **Psionic Blast:** Resistant to Scroll of Psionic Blast damage

==== Related Quests ====
  * Defeating a Golem may trigger events related to the [[rpd:imp_mob|Imp]] quest

==== Combat Strategy ====
Golems are formidable opponents because:
  * They have high health and damage reduction, making them difficult to kill quickly
  * They deal substantial damage per hit
  * They are immune to many common debuffs that could control or weaken them
  * Their slow attack rate might lull players into a false sense of security
  * Be prepared with sufficient health and healing items before engaging

==== Location ====
Golems appear in the City levels (levels 18-19) of the dungeon, as specified in Bestiary.json. They are found in the later sections of the dungeon, particularly in the City area.

====== Ice Elemental ======

{{ rpd:images:iceelemental_sprite.png|Ice Elemental }}

Ice Elementals are creatures from the Elemental plane found in Remixed Dungeon. They are immune to various status effects and can slow their enemies.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Depth * 10 + 1 (scales with dungeon depth)
  * **Defense:** Depth * 2 + 1
  * **Attack:** (Depth * 2 + 1) / 3 + 1
  * **Damage:** HP/6 to HP/6 (equal minimum and maximum damage)
  * **Defense Rating:** Depth + 1
  * **EXP:** Depth + 1
  * **Max Level:** Depth + 2

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Slow Attack:** On successful hit, has a 33% chance to apply Slow status for 3 turns
  * **Immunities:** Immune to Roots, Paralysis, Stun, and Toxic Gas
  * **Depth Scaling:** Stats increase with dungeon depth

==== Drops ====
  * [[rpd:frozen_carpaccio|Frozen Carpaccio]] (10% chance)

==== Strategy ====
  * Ice Elementals can be dangerous due to their ability to slow you
  * They become stronger in deeper levels of the dungeon
  * Their slow effect can make escaping difficult
  * Attack from range if possible to avoid the slowing effect

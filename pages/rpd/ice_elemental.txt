====== Ice Elemental ======

The **Ice Elemental** is a cold-themed enemy found in the [[rpd:ice_caves|Ice Caves]].

==== Description ====
Ice Elementals are magical creatures formed from ice and cold energy. They inhabit the frozen depths of the Ice Caves and attack with freezing abilities.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Moderate to High
* Defense: Moderate
* Attack: Moderate, with cold effects
* Speed: Moderate
* Special: May inflict cold/chill effects

==== Tactics ====
Ice Elementals are resistant to cold-based attacks but vulnerable to fire-based attacks. They pose a significant threat in groups, and their cold abilities can slow or chill the hero. Fire-based weapons and spells are particularly effective against them.

====== Health Dart ======

{{ rpd:images:item_HealthDart.png|Health Dart }}

The **Health Dart** is an enhanced throwing weapon in Remixed Dungeon. It functions as a regular dart but additionally heals the target instead of causing damage (in most cases).

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Throwing Weapon (Missile)
  * **Tier:** Enhanced Dart
  * **Damage:** 1-3
  * **Healing:** Heals target for 10% of max HP
  * **Durability:** Single use (consumed after thrown)
  * **Stackable:** Yes, typically found in quantities of 2-5
  * **Weight:** 1
  * **Price:** 12 gold per dart
  * **Strength Required:** 8

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Healing Effect:** Upon hitting an enemy, heals the target for 10% of their max HP
  * **Healing Effect:** Upon hitting a friendly character, heals them for 10% of their max HP
  * **Single Use:** Each dart is consumed after being thrown at an enemy
  * **Ranged Attack:** Allows attacking enemies from a distance without engaging in melee combat

==== Strategy ====
  * Useful for healing allies at a distance
  * Be careful when using against enemies (they will be healed)
  * Allows safe healing from a distance
  * Can be used strategically to heal friendly summoned creatures
  * Costs more than regular darts

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in random quantities of 2-5 (Java: //Random.Int( 2, 5 )//)
  * Available in shops
  * Dropped by certain enemies
  * Found in chests and other containers

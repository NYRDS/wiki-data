====== Skills ======

Skills in Remixed Dungeon refer to the various special abilities and techniques that heroes can utilize during gameplay.

==== Combat Skills ====
  * Abilities related to attacking, defending, and combat effectiveness
  * May include special attack techniques or defensive maneuvers

==== Magic Skills ====
  * Abilities related to spellcasting and magic usage
  * Includes different magic affinities and spell effectiveness

==== Utility Skills ====
  * Non-combat abilities that enhance gameplay
  * May include detection, identification, or other helpful abilities

==== Class-Specific Skills ====
Different classes have access to different skills based on their specializations:

  * **Warrior**: Combat-focused skills
  * **Mage**: Magic and elemental skills
  * **Rogue**: Stealth and utility skills
  * **Huntress**: Ranged and nature skills
  * **Elf**: Natural magic abilities
  * **Necromancer**: Undead and dark magic skills
  * **Gnoll**: Survival and combat skills

==== Progression ====
  * Skills may improve as you progress through the dungeon
  * Some skills are enhanced based on your character's stats and equipment

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on skills that complement your hero's class
  * Maximize the skills that align with your playstyle
  * Consider skill synergies when choosing equipment

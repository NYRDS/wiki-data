====== Common Arrow ======

{{ rpd:images:common_arrow_item.png|Common Arrow }}

**Common Arrow** is a basic ranged ammunition item in Remixed Dungeon.

===== Description =====
A common wooden arrow with an iron head. This is the standard ammunition for bows and other ranged weapons. Common arrows are the most basic type of ammunition and can be found throughout the dungeon or purchased from shops.

===== Stats =====
  * **Damage:** 2-5 points
  * **Delay:** 0.75x (relative to weapon firing speed)
  * **Durability:** Single use (consumed on impact)
  * **Price:** 3 gold per arrow
  * **Weight:** 1

===== Properties =====
  * **Type:** Ammunition
  * **Usage:** For use with ranged weapons like bows
  * **Consumable:** Each arrow is consumed after being shot
  * **Stackable:** Can stack up to very high quantities
  * **Base Item:** Extends the Arrow class

===== Special Ability =====
**Potion Moisten Effect:** When combined with various potions (Potion of Liquid Flame, Potion of Frost, Potion of Toxic Gas, etc.), Common Arrows can be "moistened" into special arrows with enhanced effects when used.

===== Availability =====
  * **Starting Equipment:** Elf class starts with 20 Common Arrows
  * **Shops:** Available in shops
  * **Loot:** Can be found as dungeon loot
  * **Treasury:** Part of various treasury types with a quantity of 10 per treasury

===== Strategy =====
  * Essential for characters using ranged weapons
  * Carry adequate supplies when playing the Elf or other ranged-focused classes
  * Consider the "moistening" mechanic with potions for enhanced effects
  * Manage your arrow supply carefully in deeper levels where they may become scarce

===== Obtaining Special Arrows =====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]] on regular arrows creates [[rpd:fire_arrow_item|Fire Arrows]]
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] on regular arrows creates [[rpd:frost_arrow_item|Frost Arrows]]
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]] on regular arrows creates [[rpd:poison_arrow_item|Poison Arrows]]
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] on regular arrows creates [[rpd:paralysis_arrow_item|Paralysis Arrows]]
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] on regular arrows creates [[rpd:health_arrow_item|Health Arrows]]
  * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] on regular arrows creates [[rpd:amok_arrow_item|Amok Arrows]]
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] on special arrows returns them to regular [[rpd:common_arrow_item|Common Arrows]]

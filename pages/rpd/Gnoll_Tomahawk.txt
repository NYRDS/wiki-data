====== Gnoll Tomahawk ======

==== Description ====
A distinctive weapon used by gnolls, particularly the Gnoll Hero class.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** 3-17 points
  * **Strength Required:** 15
  * **Durability:** Normal

==== Special Properties ====
  * Unique starting weapon for the [[rpd:gnoll_hero|Gnoll Hero]] class
  * Thrown weapon with good range
  * Can be upgraded like other weapons

==== Notes ====
The Gnoll Tomahawk is the signature weapon of the Gnoll Hero class, providing a powerful ranged option for these hyena-like warriors.

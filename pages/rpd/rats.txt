====== Rats ======

{{ rpd:images:rat_sprite.png|Rats }}

Rats are common enemies found throughout Remixed Dungeon, with several variants that players will encounter.

==== Rat Variants ====
  * [[rpd:rat_mob|Rat]] - Basic rat enemy
  * [[rpd:albino_rat_mob|Albino Rat]] - Special variant with unique mechanics (bleeding effect)
  * [[rpd:black_rat_mob|Black Rat]] - Dark-furred rat variant (custom mob implemented via JSON configuration)
  * [[rpd:giant_rat_skull_item|Giant Rat Skull]] - Quest item dropped by Fetid Rat
  * [[rpd:fetid_rat_mob|Fetid Rat]] - Poisonous rat variant that emits toxic gas clouds
  * [[rpd:suspicious_rat_mob|Suspicious Rat]] - Unique rat variant that transforms into Pseudo Rat when threatened
  * [[rpd:pseudo_rat_mob|Pseudo Rat]] - Dangerous creature that emerges from Suspicious Rat's transformation

==== Common Properties ====
  * **HP:** Generally low to moderate
  * **Damage:** Moderate for their level
  * **Behavior:** Usually non-aggressive until approached
  * **Drop Rate:** Varies by rat type, typically low-value items or food

==== Dungeon Locations ====
  * **Sewers:** Most common location for basic rats
  * **Other levels:** Various rat variants appear throughout the dungeon
  * **Guts Level:** Suspicious Rats, Zombie Gnolls, and Worms (levels 26-30)
  * **Special areas:** Certain rat variants appear in specific themed areas

==== Strategy ====
  * Rats are generally early-game enemies
  * Can pose a threat in groups
  * Some variants have special abilities (poison, disease, etc.)
  * Generally worth fighting for experience and drops
  * Suspicious Rats should be killed quickly before they can transform into Pseudo Rats

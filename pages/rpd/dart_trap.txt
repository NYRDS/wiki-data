====== Dart Trap ======

{{ rpd:images:trap_poison.png|Poison Dart Trap }}

The **Poison Dart Trap** (also known as **Poison Trap** in-game) is an environmental trap found throughout the dungeon that shoots poison darts at adventurers.

==== Trap Mechanics ====
  * **Effect:** When triggered, the trap shoots a poison dart at the character, inflicting **paralysis** for 4-7 turns depending on dungeon depth (duration = 4 + dungeon_depth / 2)
  * **Visual Effect:** Emits poison particles when triggered
  * **Detection:** Can be detected with sufficient Perception (Wisdom) skill
  * **Disarming:** Can be disarmed using the Thief's Toolkit or other disarming methods

==== Appearance ====
  * Appears as a hidden needle mechanism on dungeon floors
  * In the game, it's called "Poison dart trap" in English string resources
  * Triggers when a character steps on the tile

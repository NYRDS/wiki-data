====== Necromancer Armor ======

{{ rpd:images:armor_armor.png|Necromancer Armor Sprite }}

Necromancer Armor is specialized armor for the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class that enables the "Necrotism" ability, which spreads a magic plague that may infect any creature in the vicinity, dead or alive. The armor is obtained by using an [[rpd:armor_kit|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor.

==== Description ====
The Necromancer Armor allows the Necromancer to use the "Necrotism" ability which spreads a magic plague to creatures in the vicinity. This armor also allows the Necromancer to summon up to two Deathlings at a cost of 5 souls each. These undead servants will follow their master and attack his foes. The Deathlings become stronger with each hero level.

==== Properties ====
  * **Class Restriction:** Only [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]] can use this armor
  * **Special Ability:** "Necrotism" - spreads a magic plague to nearby creatures
  * **Summoning:** Allows summoning up to 2 Deathlings simultaneously (via separate mechanics)
  * **Soul Cost:** Each Deathling requires 5 souls to summon (separate from armor ability)
  * **Scaling:** Summoned creatures grow stronger with hero level

==== Abilities ====
  * **Necrotism:** Activate a necrotic field that spreads to enemies and affects dead creatures
  * **Deathling Summoning:** Can summon Deathlings via other necromantic items/abilities

==== Acquisition ====
  * Obtained by using an [[rpd:armor_kit|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor (Body Armor, Mail Armor, Scale Armor, etc.)
  * The Armor Kit is found on the City level, dropped by the King boss

==== Related Equipment ====
  * [[rpd:armor_kit|Armor Kit]] - Used to create class armor
  * [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] - The class that can use this armor
  * [[rpd:jar_of_souls|Jar of Souls]] - Provides souls for summoning Deathlings

==== Related Abilities ====
  * [[rpd:necromancy|Necromancy]] - The magical practice this armor enables
  * [[rpd:necrotism|Necrotism]] - The primary ability of this armor
  * [[rpd:summoning|Summoning]] - General category of abilities for calling forth minions

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential for Necromancers as their class armor with unique necromantic ability
  * The Necrotism ability works on both living and dead creatures in the area
  * Use in combination with other necromantic items for maximum effectiveness
  * The armor synergizes with soul-generating abilities and items like the Jar of Souls

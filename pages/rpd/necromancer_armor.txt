====== Necromancer Armor ======

{{ :rpd:necromancer_armor.png?nolink&50|}}

Necromancer Armor is specialized armor for the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class that enables powerful necromantic abilities, including the summoning of multiple deathlings.

==== Description ====
This armor will allow the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] to summon up to two [[rpd:deathling|Deathlings]] at a cost of 5 souls each. These undead servants will follow their master and attack his foes. The [[rpd:deathling|Deathlings]] become stronger with each hero level.

==== Properties ====
  * **Class Restriction:** Only [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]] can use this armor
  * **Summoning:** Allows summoning up to 2 [[rpd:deathling|Deathlings]] simultaneously
  * **Soul Cost:** Each [[rpd:deathling|Deathling]] requires 5 souls to summon
  * **Scaling:** Summoned creatures grow stronger with hero level

==== Abilities ====
  * **Deathling Summoning:** Summon powerful [[rpd:deathling|Deathlings]] to fight alongside the Necromancer
  * **Necromantic Enhancement:** Potentially boosts other necromantic abilities
  * **Undead Control:** Improves control over summoned undead minions

==== Acquisition ====
  * Likely found as treasure in dungeon levels
  * Potentially available in special shops
  * Crafting recipe may exist for [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential for Necromancers who want multiple undead minions
  * Use souls efficiently to maintain a group of [[rpd:deathling|Deathlings]]
  * The armor synergizes with soul-generating abilities and items
  * [[rpd:deathling|Deathlings]] can serve as both damage dealers and meat shields

====== Necromancer Armor ======

The **Necromancer Armor** is the class-specific armor for the [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancer]] class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. It's part of the necromancy-themed equipment that supports the Necromancer's undead summoning and death magic abilities.

==== Description ====
Like other class armors, the Necromancer Armor has unique special abilities accessible through the Special action. It specifically supports the Necromancer's playstyle with necromancy-themed effects.

==== Special Ability ====
The Necromancer Armor typically features necromantic abilities, likely related to:
* Activating necrotic fields that spread to enemies
* Supporting undead minion management
* Enhancing necromantic powers

==== Strategy ====
The Necromancer Armor complements the Necromancer's core abilities of summoning undead minions and using death magic. It's designed to enhance the character's necromantic capabilities in combat.

==== Class Restriction ====
This armor can only be effectively used by the Necromancer class. If worn by other classes, it may not function properly or could have negative effects.

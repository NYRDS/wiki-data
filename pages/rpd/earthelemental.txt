====== earthelemental ======

{{ rpd:images:earthelemental_sprite.png|Earth Elemental Sprite }}

**Earth Elemental** is an elemental mob found in grassy areas.

==== Description ====
Earth elementals are earth masses driven by concentrated nature forces.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP/HT**: Depth * 10 + 1 (scales with dungeon depth)
  * **Defense Skill**: Depth / 2 + 1
  * **Attack Skill**: (Depth / 2 + 1) / 2 + 1
  * **Min Damage**: HT / 5
  * **Max Damage**: HT / 5
  * **Damage Resistance**: Depth + 1
  * **Exp for Kill**: Depth + 1
  * **Max Level**: Depth + 2

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Regrowth Attack**: On attack, has a 50% chance to cause regrowth in the targeted cell if it's a valid terrain type (EMPTY, EMBERS, EMPTY_DECO, GRASS, HIGH_GRASS)
  * **Slow in Liquid**: Speed is halved when on liquid terrain

==== Immunities ====
Earth Elementals are immune to:
  * Roots
  * Paralysis
  * Stun
  * Toxic Gas
  * Burning
  * Bleeding

==== Drops ====
  * **Earthroot Seed**: 10% chance to drop an Earthroot seed

==== Spawn Locations ====
  * **Grass Areas**: Earth Elementals can spawn in grassy areas

==== Behavior ====
  * Elemental creature
  * Immune to various status effects due to nature-based composition
  * Statistics scale with dungeon depth

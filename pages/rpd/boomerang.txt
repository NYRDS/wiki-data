====== Boomerang ======

{:rpd:images:boomerang_sprite.png?200|}

==== Description ====
A curved throwing weapon that returns to the thrower after being thrown. It's a unique ranged weapon with returning properties.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** Moderate
  * **Range:** Moderate
  * **Durability:** High (returns to user)
  * **Special Property:** Returns after being thrown

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Returning:** Automatically returns to the thrower
  * **Ranged Attack:** Can be used to attack distant enemies
  * **Reusable:** Does not require retrieval like other thrown weapons
  * **Melee Option:** Can sometimes be used as a melee weapon

==== Notes ====
The boomerang is a versatile weapon that provides ranged attack options without the permanence of loss like other throwing weapons. It's particularly useful when ammunition is scarce.

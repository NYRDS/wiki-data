====== Pasty-Mimic ======

==== Description ====
Pasty-Mimic is a type of mimic that disguises itself as a [[rpd:pasty|Pasty]]. It's a dangerous enemy that lures hungry adventurers.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** (3 + current level depth) * 5
  * **Speed:** 1.25 (faster than normal)
  * **Attack Skill:** 9 + current level depth
  * **Defense Skill:** 2 * attack skill / 3
  * **Damage:** hp/10 to hp/4
  * **Exp for Kill:** 2 + 2 * (current level depth - 1) / 5
  * **Flight:** Yes (can fly over obstacles)

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Flight:** Can fly over all types of terrain and obstacles
  * **Mimic Behavior:** Disguises as food to trick players
  * **Rotten Pasty Drop:** Carries a Rotten Pasty in its inventory
  * **Gas Immunity:** Immune to Toxic Gas, Paralysis and Stun effects

==== Notes ====
Pasty-Mimic appears as a food item but is actually an enemy. Be careful when finding unattended food items in the dungeon, as they may be mimics in disguise.

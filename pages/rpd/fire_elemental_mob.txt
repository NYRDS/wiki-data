====== Fire Elemental ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_FireElemental.png|Fire Elemental Sprite }}

**Fire Elementals** are dangerous enemies found in the [[rpd:caves_level|Caves]] and [[rpd:halls_level|Halls]] levels of Remixed Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Fire Elementals are blazing creatures of living flame. They attack by striking with their fiery bodies and can set their targets ablaze. These enemies are particularly dangerous in groups as they can quickly overwhelm heroes with burning damage.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level:** 10
  * **Health:** 25
  * **Attack:** 10-20
  * **Defense:** 8
  * **Speed:** 1.0 (normal)
  * **Resistances:** [[rpd:fire|Fire]], [[rpd:paralysis_buff|Paralysis]], [[rpd:root_buff|Roots]], [[rpd:sleep_buff|Sleep]], [[rpd:stun_buff|Stun]]
  * **Vulnerabilities:** [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Frost]]-based attacks

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Burning Touch:** Any attack from a Fire Elemental inflicts the [[rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] status effect
  * **Fire Immunity:** Completely immune to fire-based attacks and damage
  * **Environmental Interaction:** Will spread fire to flammable terrain when moving

==== Loot ====
  * [[rpd:gold_item|Gold]] (1-5 pieces)
  * Occasionally drops a [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Use [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] or [[rpd:wand_of_frost_item|Wand of Frost]] for effective damage
  * Keep your distance and use ranged attacks when possible
  * Avoid fighting near flammable terrain to prevent fire spreading
  * Don't allow these enemies to surround you as multiple burning effects can be lethal
  * Be cautious of the burning effect spreading to your items

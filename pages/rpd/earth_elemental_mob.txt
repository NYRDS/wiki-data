====== Earth Elemental ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_EarthElemental.png|Earth Elemental Sprite }}

**Earth Elementals** are sturdy, defensive enemies found in the deeper levels of Remixed Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Earth Elementals are earth masses driven by concentrated nature forces. They are resilient enemies with high defense and health, and are immune to many status effects.

==== Stats (Depth-Based) ====
  * **Level:** Variable (depth-based)
  * **Health:** depth * 10 + 1 (scales with dungeon depth)
  * **Attack:** (ht/5) - (ht/5) (based on health)
  * **Defense:** depth / 2 + 1 (scales with dungeon depth)
  * **Speed:** 0.5 in liquid terrain, normal otherwise
  * **Resistances:** [[rpd:bleeding_buff|Bleeding]], [[rpd:paralysis_buff|Paralysis]], [[rpd:stun_buff|Stun]], [[rpd:toxic_gas_buff|Toxic Gas]], [[rpd:burning_buff|Burning]], [[rpd:roots_buff|Roots]]
  * **Immunities:** Knockback effects
  * **Experience:** depth + 1
  * **Damage Reduction:** depth + 1

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Depth Adjustment:** Stats scale with dungeon depth (kind = min(depth/5, 4))
  * **Terrain Speed:** Moves at half speed when on liquid terrain
  * **Regrowth:** 50% chance to create regrowth on the target's tile after attacking
  * **Immunities:** Immune to roots, paralysis, stun, toxic gas, burning, bleeding

==== Loot ====
  * [[rpd:earthroot_seed_item|Earthroot Seed]] (10% chance)

==== Strategy ====
  * Since their stats scale with dungeon depth, they become significantly stronger in deeper levels
  * Their damage output is tied to their health (dmgMin and dmgMax are ht/5)
  * They're immune to many status effects that might be used to weaken them
  * Be careful of the regrowth effect they can create when attacking
  * Their speed is reduced on liquid terrain, making those areas potentially advantageous

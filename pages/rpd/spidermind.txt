====== Psi-Spider ======

{{ rpd:images:spidermind_sprite.png|Psi-Spider }}

The **Psi-Spider** (also known as SpiderMind) is a special type of spider mob found in the Spider Nest area of the dungeon. It possesses unique mental powers that allow it to control the colony.

==== Mob Overview ====
  * **Type**: Spider Mob
  * **Level**: Spider Nest levels (6s-10s)
  * **Special Ability**: Can cast psychic powers on friendly mobs to buff them
  * **Behavior**: Rather cowardly; tends to keep distance from enemies
  * **HP**: 5
  * **Attack Skill**: 10
  * **Defense**: 1
  * **Damage**: 0-0 (physical attacks; uses psychic abilities instead)
  * **Speed**: 1.0x normal speed

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Uses ranged psychic attacks instead of physical damage
  * When attacking, has a chance to apply beneficial buffs to nearby friendly mobs
  * Buffs that can be applied include: Speed, Barkskin, Blessed, Health (Sungrass), Armor (Earthroot), ManaShield, and Fury
  * The Psi-Spider prefers to stay away from combat, using its psychic powers to support allies

==== Strategy ====
  * Psi-Spiders are generally not very dangerous on their own
  * However, they can significantly strengthen other nearby enemies with their buffs
  * Focus on eliminating them quickly before they can support other mobs
  * They are cowardly and will try to keep distance, making them easier to isolate

==== Obtaining ====
Psi-Spiders can be found in the Spider Nest area (levels 6s-10s):
  * Level 8s: Spawn rate of 1.0
  * Level 9s: Spawn rate of 1.0
  * Level 10s: Spawn rate of 0.5

====== Throwing Weapons ======

{{ rpd:images:throwing_knife_sprite.png|Throwing Weapons }}

Throwing weapons in Remixed Dungeon are ranged weapons that can be hurled at enemies from a distance.

==== Weapon Types ====
  * [[rpd:dart|Darts]] - Basic throwing weapon
  * [[rpd:throwing_stone|Throwing Stones]] - Simple stone projectiles
  * [[rpd:tomahawk|Tomahawk]] - Thrown axe weapon
  * [[rpd:kunai|Kunai]] - Ninja throwing knives
  * [[rpd:boomerang|Boomerang]] - Returns after being thrown

==== Properties ====
  * **Range:** Typically medium distance
  * **Durability:** Varies by weapon type
  * **Damage:** Generally lower than melee weapons but with range advantage
  * **Stackable:** Most throwing weapons can be stacked

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Can attack enemies without entering adjacent squares
  * Some may return to the player after throwing (e.g., [[rpd:boomerang|Boomerang]])
  * Damage may be affected by dexterity or ranged skill bonuses
  * Can be used to attack around corners or from safe positions

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for softening up enemies before melee combat
  * Good for attacking enemies behind obstacles
  * Useful when you need to maintain distance from enemies
  * Can be used to trigger traps from a safe distance
  * Often helpful when facing groups of weaker enemies

==== Class Interactions ====
  * [[rpd:rogue|Rogue]] may have bonuses to thrown weapon damage
  * [[rpd:huntress|Huntress]] has bonuses with ranged weapons
  * [[rpd:gnoll|Gnoll]] starts with [[rpd:tomahawk|Tomahawk]], a throwing weapon

====== Backstab ======

{{ rpd:images:backstab_spell_icon.png|Backstab Spell Icon }}

**Backstab** is a spell in Remixed Pixel Dungeon (Rogue Affinity).

==== Description ====
Silent hit in the back. Dishonest, but extremely effective, backstab damage will grow with performer skill level.\n\nSuccessful backstab will completely ignore victim armor or special defense abilities.

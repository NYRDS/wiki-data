====== Goo ======

==== Description ====
Goo, also known as the Goo boss, is an amorphous mass of living sludge that inhabits the sewers of the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Moderate to high
  * **Attack Skill:** Moderate
  * **Defense Skill:** Low to moderate
  * **Damage:** Moderate
  * **Exp for Kill:** Moderate

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Pond Creation:** Can create pools of acid when defeated
  * **Acid Attacks:** Uses acid-based attacks
  * **Absorption:** May absorb energy from defeated enemies
  * **Regeneration:** Can heal when in contact with water or other goos

==== Notes ====
The Goo boss is found in the sewers area of the dungeon. Defeating it often results in acid pools that can damage the player, making positioning crucial during the battle.

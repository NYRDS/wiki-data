====== Defence ======

**Defence** refers to all game mechanics and items that protect the [[rpd:hero|hero]] from taking damage.

==== Defence Mechanics ====
  * [[rpd:defence_skill|Defence Skill]] - Ability to avoid attacks
  * [[rpd:armor_class|Armor]] - Reduces damage from successful hits
  * [[rpd:resistance|Resistance]] - Reduces damage from specific sources
  * [[rpd:buffs|Buffs]] - Temporary defensive enhancements

==== Defensive Items ====
  * [[rpd:armor|Armor]] - Main defensive equipment
  * [[rpd:shields|Shields]] - Additional protection items
  * [[rpd:rings|Defensive rings]] - [[rpd:ring_of_evasion_item|Ring of Evasion]], [[rpd:ring_of_shielding_item|Ring of Shielding]], etc.
  * [[rpd:potions|Defensive potions]] - [[rpd:potion_of_shielding_item|Potion of Shielding]], etc.
  * [[rpd:scrolls|Defensive scrolls]] - [[rpd:scroll_of_terror_item|Scroll of Terror]], etc.

==== Defensive Abilities ====
  * [[rpd:hero_subclasses|Subclass abilities]] - Guardian's ability, etc.
  * [[rpd:spells|Spells]] - Defensive spells that provide protection

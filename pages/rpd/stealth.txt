====== Stealth ======

**Stealth** refers to mechanics in Remixed Dungeon that allow the player to remain undetected by enemies. This is particularly important for classes like the [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] and its subclasses like the [[rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin]] and [[rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner]].

==== Mechanics ====
  * Stealth mechanics allow players to avoid detection by enemies
  * Successful stealth typically requires staying out of enemy vision and avoiding sounds
  * Some items, equipment, or character classes provide stealth bonuses
  * Moving through grass, shadows, or other terrain may affect stealth
  * Attacking from stealth may provide bonuses or trigger special effects

==== Related Classes ====
  * [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue Class]] - Primary stealth-based class
  * [[rpd:assassin_subclass|Assassin Subclass]] - Specializes in stealth and high-damage attacks
  * [[rpd:freerunner_subclass|Freerunner Subclass]] - Focused on mobility and evasion

==== Items and Equipment ====
  * [[rpd:ring_of_shadows_item|Ring of Shadows]] - Provides stealth bonuses
  * Certain armor may provide stealth benefits or penalties depending on their weight and type

==== Strategy ====
  * Stay out of enemy line of sight to maintain stealth
  * Use terrain and environmental features to your advantage
  * Time your attacks carefully to maximize stealth benefits
  * Consider using invisibility potions or other stealth-enhancing items

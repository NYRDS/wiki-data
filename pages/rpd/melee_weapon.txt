====== Melee Weapon ======

{{ rpd:images:sword_item.png|Melee Weapon }}

==== Description ====
**Melee Weapons** are weapons designed for close combat. They are used by heroes and enemies to deal damage in direct combat situations.

==== Properties ====
  * **Damage**: The minimum and maximum damage values the weapon can deal in close combat
  * **Delay**: The time needed to perform an attack with this weapon
  * **Accuracy**: How likely the weapon is to hit its target
  * **Strength**: The minimum strength required to use the weapon effectively
  * **Upgrades**: Most melee weapons can be upgraded using Scrolls of Upgrade
  * **Enchantments**: Many melee weapons can be enchanted using Scrolls of Enchantment

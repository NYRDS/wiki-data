====== Spells ======

{{ rpd:images:spells_illustration.png|Spells }}

**Spells** are magical abilities in Remixed Dungeon that can be used for combat, utility, or support purposes. They are typically learned through special items or class abilities.

==== Spell Categories ====
  * **Combat Spells**: Deal damage to enemies or debuff them
  * **Utility Spells**: Provide movement, escape, or exploration benefits
  * **Support Spells**: Heal the hero or provide protective buffs
  * **Affinity Spells**: Specialized spells tied to specific magic affinities
  * **Rogue Affinity Spells**: Spells related to stealth and trickery
  * **Mage Affinity Spells**: Spells related to elemental magic
  * **Warrior Affinity Spells**: Spells related to combat enhancement
  * **Elf Affinity Spells**: Spells related to nature and archery

==== Common Spells ====
  * **[[rpd:kunai_throw_spell|Kunai Throw]]**: Throws multiple kunai at random visible enemies (Rogue Affinity)
  * **[[rpd:magic_arrow_spell|Magic Arrow]]**: Ranged magical attack (Mage Affinity)
  * **[[rpd:healing_spell|Healing Spell]]**: Heals the hero (Support)
  * **[[rpd:ignite_spell|Ignite Spell]]**: Sets enemies on fire (Combat)
  * **[[rpd:wind_gust_spell|Wind Gust Spell]]**: Pushes enemies and objects (Utility)
  * **[[rpd:magic_torch_spell|Magic Torch Spell]]**: Provides light without consuming fire source (Utility)
  * **[[rpd:exhumation_spell|Exhumation Spell]]**: Summons wraiths from bones (Combat)
  * **[[rpd:curse_item_spell|Curse Item Spell]]**: Curses a target item (Utility)

==== Spell Mechanics ====
  * **Mana Cost**: Each spell consumes a specific amount of mana
  * **Cast Time**: Time required to cast the spell
  * **Targeting**: Different spells have different targeting methods
  * **Cooldown**: Some spells may have cooldown periods between uses
  * **Range**: Spells have different effective ranges
  * **Area of Effect**: Some spells affect multiple targets or tiles

==== Spell Acquisition ====
  * **Spell Books**: Special books that teach new spells
  * **Class Abilities**: Some classes naturally know certain spells
  * **Items**: Special items may grant temporary spell access
  * **Mastery**: Advanced classes may gain additional spells
  * **Quest Rewards**: Special quests may reward new spells

==== Spell Affinities ====
  * **Rogue Affinity**: Focuses on stealth, trickery, and mobility
  * **Mage Affinity**: Specializes in elemental and direct damage
  * **Warrior Affinity**: Enhances combat effectiveness and durability
  * **Elf Affinity**: Emphasizes archery and nature magic
  * **Necromancer Affinity**: Focuses on death magic and undead control

==== Spell Usage Strategies ====
  * **Mana Management**: Balance spell usage with available mana
  * **Situational Awareness**: Use appropriate spells for current situations
  * **Combination Effects**: Combine spells for enhanced effects
  * **Resource Conservation**: Don't waste spells on minor threats
  * **Emergency Spells**: Keep powerful spells for critical situations
  * **Area Control**: Use AOE spells to control groups of enemies

==== Spell Improvements ====
  * **Skill Level**: Some spells become more effective with caster skill level
  * **Equipment**: Certain equipment may enhance spell effects
  * **Buffs**: Temporary enhancements may improve spell performance
  * **Talent Trees**: Some character talents improve specific spells
  * **Specialization**: Focusing on specific spell types can improve effectiveness

==== Spell Advantages ====
  * **Versatility**: Spells provide diverse solutions to challenges
  * **No Ammunition**: Unlike ranged weapons, spells don't require special ammunition
  * **Special Effects**: Many spells have unique effects impossible with regular equipment
  * **Mana Efficiency**: Often more efficient than repeatedly using consumables
  * **Range**: Many spells work at range without requiring line of sight

==== Spell Disadvantages ====
  * **Mana Consumption**: Spells require mana which may be limited
  * **Cooldowns**: Powerful spells may have cooldown periods
  * **Limited Uses**: Some spells may be restricted in use
  * **Specialization**: Not all classes can use all spells effectively
  * **Situational**: Some spells are only useful in specific situations

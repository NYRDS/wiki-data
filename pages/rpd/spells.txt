====== Spells ======

**Spells** are special magical abilities in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that can be cast by heroes with magical affinities. Unlike scrolls which are consumed after use, spells can be cast multiple times using skill points (Mana).

==== Magic Affinities ====
Different classes have different [[rpd:mechanics|magic affinities]] that determine their available spells:

  * **Common**: Can use spells from spell books only (Heal, Raise Dead, Cloak, Calm, Charm, Town Portal)
  * **Elemental**: Fire, water, earth, and air-based magic (Ignite, WindGust, Root, FreezeGlobe)
  * **Necromancy**: Death and undead-themed magic (Raise Dead, Exhumation, Dark Sacrifice, Possess)
  * **Combat**: Battle-enhancing magic (DieHard, Dash, BodyArmor, Smash)
  * **Rogue**: Stealth and trickery magic (Cloak, Backstab, KunaiThrow, Haste)
  * **Huntress**: Nature and creature-summoning magic (Calm, Charm, ShootInEye, Summon Beast)
  * **Elf**: Natural elemental magic (MagicArrow, Sprout, HideInGrass, NatureArmor)
  * **Witchcraft**: Gnoll-themed magic (Roar, LightningBolt, Heal, Order)
  * **Priest**: Divine magic (Heal, Calm, Charm, Order)
  * **PlagueDoctor**: Disease and toxic magic (Heal, Calm, Charm, Order)
  * **Dev**: Development spells (TestSpell, CurseItem)

==== Magic Level and Spell Unlocking ====
Heroes have a Magic Level attribute that determines which spells they can unlock and how powerful those spells become. Magic Level can be increased with the Tome of Knowledge item.

==== Strategy ====
Spells are an efficient way to use magical abilities without consuming limited scroll resources. However, they require mana/skill point management and understanding of your class's magic affinity to use effectively.

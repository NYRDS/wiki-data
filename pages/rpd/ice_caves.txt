====== Ice Caves ======

{{ rpd:images:ice_caves_level.png|Ice Caves Level }}

The **Ice Caves** is a special level found in the Remixed Dungeon. This level features unique ice-themed mechanics, enemies, and environmental effects.

==== Location ====
The Ice Caves can be accessed through special entrances in the dungeon, generally in the later areas of the game. The exact access method may be related to special items or conditions.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Ice Terrain**: Special floor tiles that may have unique properties
  * **Sliding Effects**: Movement mechanics that may cause characters to slide
  * **Cold Damage**: Environmental effects that may cause damage over time
  * **Freezing Effects**: Potential to freeze characters or enemies in place

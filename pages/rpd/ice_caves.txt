====== Ice Caves ======

The **Ice Caves** is one of the special branch dungeons in Remixed Pixel Dungeon, featuring cold-themed content and frozen enemies.

==== Overview ====
The Ice Caves is accessed from depth 14 of the main dungeon and consists of 5 interconnected levels (ice1-ice5). It features unique cold-themed enemies and mechanics related to ice and frost.

==== Levels ====
  * **ice1** (depth 15) - Entry level with basic cold-themed creatures
  * **ice2** (depth 16) - More challenging ice-themed enemies
  * **ice3** (depth 17) - Advanced cold-themed content
  * **ice4** (depth 18) - Most challenging regular area
  * **ice5** (depth 19) - Boss level with the Ice Guardian Core as the final encounter

==== Enemies ====
The Ice Caves contain unique cold-themed enemies not found elsewhere in the dungeon:
  * [[rpd:kobold|Kobold]]
  * [[rpd:kobold_icemancer|Kobold Icemancer]]
  * [[rpd:cold_spirit|Cold Spirit]]
  * [[rpd:ice_elemental|Ice Elemental]]
  * [[rpd:ice_guardian_core|Ice Guardian Core]] (boss)

==== Strategy ====
The Ice Caves require preparation for cold-based challenges and enemies. Fire-based attacks and resistances to cold effects can be particularly valuable in this area.

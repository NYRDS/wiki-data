====== Ice Caves ======

{{ rpd:images:level_ice_caves.png|Ice Caves Level }}

The Ice Caves are a special level in Remixed Dungeon that features freezing mechanics and ice-themed enemies. This level is challenging due to the cold environment and unique enemies found here.

==== Features ====
  * Cold environment that affects gameplay mechanics
  * Ice-themed enemies like [[rpd:ice_elemental_mob|Ice Elementals]]
  * Special freezing mechanics that can slow or freeze the player
  * Unique items and resources found only in this level
  * Different dungeon layout compared to typical levels

==== Enemies ====
  * [[rpd:ice_elemental_mob|Ice Elementals]] - Immune to various effects and can slow enemies
  * Other ice-themed creatures
  * Potential boss encounter

==== Items and Resources ====
  * Items related to cold and ice mechanics
  * Potential for finding [[rpd:frozen_carpaccio|Frozen Carpaccio]]
  * Unique equipment that functions well in cold environments

==== Strategies ====
  * Be aware of freezing effects that can slow or immobilize
  * Prepare for cold-based attacks
  * Consider items that counter freezing effects
  * Manage food resources carefully as cold may increase consumption

====== Witchdoctor Subclass ======

{{ rpd:images:witchdoctor_subclass.png|Witchdoctor }}

==== Description ====
The Witchdoctor is a subclass of the Gnoll hero class in Remixed Dungeon. Witchdoctors focus on dark magic and necromancy, capable of raising undead allies and using curses to weaken enemies.

==== Abilities ====
  * **Necromantic Arts**: Ability to raise undead allies from fallen enemies
  * **Curse Mastery**: Enhanced effects from curses and debuffs
  * **Life Drain**: Abilities to siphon life force from enemies to heal self

==== Specialization ====
  * Focuses on summoning and controlling undead
  * Uses dark magic and curse-based spells
  * Sacrificial combat techniques

==== Lore ====
The Witchdoctor subclass represents the mystical shamans of gnoll society, wielding forbidden magic to serve their pack. They have a deep connection with the spirit world and can command the forces of death.

==== Acquisition ====
The Witchdoctor subclass can be unlocked by reading a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss, Tengu. It's only available to the Gnoll hero class.

==== Equipment ====
  * Special armor: [[rpd:gnoll_armor_item|Gnoll Armor]] can be obtained using an Armor Kit on any regular armor
  * Preferred weapons: Magic-based items
  * Necromancy items: Artifacts and items that support undead summoning

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on summoning and controlling undead allies
  * Use curse-based items and abilities to debilitate enemies
  * Leverage necromantic items for healing and support
  * Build an army of undead pets to fight alongside you

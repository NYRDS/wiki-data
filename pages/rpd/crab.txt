====== crab ======

{{ rpd:images:crab_sprite.png|Crab Sprite }}

==== Description ====
The **Crab** is a common mob found in the early levels of Pixel Dungeon. It's a fast-moving creature with decent defensive capabilities.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP**: 15
  * **Defense**: 5
  * **Attack**: 12
  * **Damage**: 3-6
  * **Defense Rating**: 4
  * **EXP**: 3
  * **Speed**: 2.0 (twice normal speed)
  * **Max Level**: 9

==== Drops ====
  * [[rpd:mystery_meat|Mystery Meat]] (31.4% chance)

==== Behavior ====
  * Moves at twice the normal speed
  * Spawns in early dungeon levels
  * When killed, it triggers the Ghost's quest if it's the first Crab killed in the game

==== Location ====
  * Found in early dungeon levels (typically levels 1-9)

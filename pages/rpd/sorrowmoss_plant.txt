====== Sorrowmoss ======

{{ rpd:images:sorrowmoss_plant.png|Sorrowmoss }}

**Sorrowmoss** is a plant found in the dungeon that can be used to create potions and other items.

==== Description ====
Sorrowmoss is a dangerous plant that secretes a deadly toxin. Direct contact with the plant is harmful, but its seeds can be carefully harvested and used in alchemy or potion-making.

==== Properties ====
  * **Type**: Plant/Seed
  * **Effect**: Toxic when touched directly
  * **Harvest**: Sorrowmoss seeds
  * **Potion Creation**: Can be brewed into Potion of Toxic Gas in an alchemy pot

==== Usage ====
  * Harvest seeds by fighting or carefully passing by the plant
  * Use 3 or more seeds in an alchemy pot to potentially create a Potion of Toxic Gas
  * Seeds can also be planted on fertile soil

==== Identification ====
  * The plant appears as a mossy patch with toxic-looking properties
  * Can be identified by its distinctive appearance and toxic emanations

==== Strategy ====
  * Approach with caution due to toxic nature
  * Consider using ranged attacks to harvest seeds safely
  * Valuable for alchemy due to toxic gas potion creation

====== Priest ======

{{ rpd:images:priest_sprite.png|Priest }}
The Priest is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He focuses on sanctity and curse removal.
==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 10
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Scroll of Remove Curse (known item)
  * **Magic Affinity**: Priest
==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Priest starts with a known Scroll of Remove Curse, allowing immediate access to curse removal.
  * No other special starting equipment or mechanics are documented.
==== Advantages ====
  * Can remove curses without having to find a scroll
  * Standard starting stats without major drawbacks
==== Disadvantages ====
  * No significant advantages over other classes documented
  * Status: "Coming Soon" - currently not fully implemented
==== Subclasses ====
  * No subclasses currently documented

The **Priest** is an unlockable playable class in Remixed Dungeon focused on divine magic and healing.
==== Class Overview ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Priest magic affinity focused on healing and divine magic
  * **Special Ability:** Enhanced healing and curse removal capabilities
==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Item:** Scroll of Remove Curse (identified) - Beginning with this scroll indicates a focus on curse removal and purification abilities
==== Class Perks ====
  * **Divine Magic:** Access to special divine spells and abilities
  * **Healing Focus:** Enhanced healing capabilities compared to other classes
  * **Blessing Powers:** Can receive special blessings that enhance various attributes
  * **Identified Scrolls:** Scrolls of Remove Curse are identified from the beginning
==== Class Abilities ====
The Priest has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Guardian:** Focuses on protection and defensive abilities
  * **Witchdoctor:** Combines healing with nature-based magic
==== Class Advantages ====
  * Enhanced healing capabilities compared to other classes
  * Superior curse removal abilities
  * Support-focused magic that can benefit longer dungeon runs
  * Starting Scroll of Remove Curse is identified
==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Potentially less offensive capabilities than other classes
  * Relies heavily on support and healing mechanics
==== Unlock Method ====
The Priest class is currently marked as "Coming Soon" in the game and cannot be selected from the start screen.
==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on support and healing to sustain longer dungeon runs
  * Take advantage of curse removal abilities to handle cursed equipment
  * Use divine magic abilities for both healing and utility
  * Consider mastery path based on preferred balance of healing vs. other abilities

====== Priest ======

The **Priest** is a playable class in Remixed Pixel Dungeon with healing and divine-themed abilities.

==== Class Features ====
  * **Magic Affinity**: Priest magic affinity focused on healing and divine magic
  * **Starting Item**: Begins with Scroll of Remove Curse, indicating a focus on curse removal and purification abilities
  * Likely has enhanced healing capabilities and abilities to interact with divine items

==== Strategy ====
The Priest likely excels at handling cursed items and has enhanced healing abilities compared to other classes. This makes it particularly effective in situations where cursed equipment is encountered or when prolonged dungeon runs require more healing resources.

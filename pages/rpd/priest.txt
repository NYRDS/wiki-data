====== Priest ======

{{ rpd:images:priest_sprite.png|Priest }}

The **Priest** is an unlockable playable class in Remixed Dungeon focused on divine magic and healing.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Priest magic affinity focused on healing and divine magic
  * **Special Ability:** Enhanced healing and curse removal capabilities

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 20
  * **Starting STR**: 10
  * **Starting Equipment**:
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse|Scroll of Remove Curse]] (known item)

==== Class Perks ====
  * **Divine Magic:** Access to special divine spells and abilities
  * **Healing Focus:** Enhanced healing capabilities compared to other classes
  * **Blessing Powers:** Can receive special blessings that enhance various attributes
  * **Identified Scrolls:** [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse|Scroll of Remove Curse]] is identified from the beginning

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Priest starts with a known [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse|Scroll of Remove Curse]], allowing immediate access to curse removal.

==== Class Abilities ====
The Priest has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Guardian:** Focuses on protection and defensive abilities (not yet available)
  * **Witchdoctor:** Combines healing with nature-based magic (not yet available)

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Enhanced healing capabilities compared to other classes
  * Superior curse removal abilities
  * Support-focused magic that can benefit longer dungeon runs
  * Starting [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse|Scroll of Remove Curse]] is identified

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Potentially less offensive capabilities than other classes
  * Relies heavily on support and healing mechanics
  * Status: "Coming Soon" - currently not unlockable through normal gameplay

==== Unlock Condition ====
The Priest class is currently marked as "Coming Soon" in the game and cannot be selected from the start screen. It may become available in future updates through donation or special conditions.

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on support and healing to sustain longer dungeon runs
  * Take advantage of curse removal abilities to handle cursed equipment
  * Use divine magic abilities for both healing and utility
  * Consider mastery path based on preferred balance of healing vs. other abilities

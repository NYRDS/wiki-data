====== Mobs ======

{{ rpd:images:rat_mob.png|Mobs }}

==== Description ====
Mobs (mobiles) are hostile creatures that populate the dungeon and oppose the player. Each mob has unique characteristics, abilities, and strategies for defeat.

==== Mob Categories ====
  * **Basic Enemies:** [[rpd:rat_mob|Rat]], [[rpd:gnoll_mob|Gnoll]], [[rpd:crab_mob|Crab]], [[rpd:swarm_mob|Swarm]], [[rpd:bat_mob|Bat]]
  * **Humanoid Enemies:** [[rpd:goblin_mob|Goblin]], [[rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeleton]], [[rpd:thief_mob|Thief]], [[rpd:shaman_mob|Shaman]]
  * **Elemental Creatures:** [[rpd:fire_elemental_mob|Fire Elemental]], [[rpd:ice_elemental_mob|Ice Elemental]], [[rpd:air_elemental_mob|Air Elemental]], [[rpd:water_elemental_mob|Water Elemental]]
  * **Magical Beings:** [[rpd:evil_eye_mob|Evil Eye]], [[rpd:wraith_mob|Wraith]], [[rpd:succubus_mob|Succubus]]
  * **Bosses:** [[rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]], [[rpd:dm300_mob|DM-300]], [[rpd:king_mob|King of Dwarves]], [[rpd:yog_mob|Yog-Dzewa]]

==== Mob Mechanics ====
  * **Health:** The amount of damage a mob can take before dying
  * **Attack:** The chance to hit the player with a normal attack
  * **Defense:** The chance to avoid being hit
  * **Accuracy:** How likely the mob is to hit the player
  * **Speed:** Determines how often the mob acts
  * **Damage:** The amount of damage dealt with a successful hit
  * **EXP Reward:** Experience points granted when the mob is defeated

==== Special Mob Abilities ====
  * **Immunities:** Some mobs are immune to specific effects (burning, poison, etc.)
  * **Resistances:** Resistance to specific types of damage
  * **Special Attacks:** Unique abilities like stunning, confusion, or debuffs
  * **AIs:** Different behavioral patterns (hunting, fleeing, support)

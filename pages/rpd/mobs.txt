====== Mobs ======

Mobs (mobiles) are any mobile creatures in Remixed Pixel Dungeon. This includes enemies, NPCs, and any other creatures that can move around the dungeon.

==== Types of Mobs ====
  * **Enemies** - Hostile creatures that attack the player
  * **NPCs** - Non-player characters with special roles (e.g., [[rpd:shopkeeper_npc|Shopkeeper]], [[rpd:wandmaker_npc|Wandmaker]])
  * **Pets** - Creatures allied to the player (see [[rpd:pet|Pets]])
  * **Bosses** - Powerful enemies that guard special prizes

==== Basic Properties ====
  * **HP/HT** - Hit points (current/max)
  * **Attack Skill** - Chance to hit the player
  * **Defense Skill** - Chance to avoid being hit
  * **Damage** - Range of damage dealt on hit
  * **EXP** - Experience awarded when killed
  * **Dr** - Damage reduction (armor)
  * **Speed** - How many turns per move relative to player

==== Special Mob Properties ====
  * **Immunities** - Status effects the mob is immune to
  * **Resistances** - Status effects the mob resists
  * **Special Abilities** - Unique attacks or behaviors
  * **AI** - Artificial intelligence that governs behavior

==== Common Mob Categories ====

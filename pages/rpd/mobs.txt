====== Mobs ======

{{ rpd:images:rat_mob.png|Rat Mob }}

**Mobs** (Mobile Objects) are the enemies and creatures that the hero encounters throughout the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon. They range from simple rats in the sewers to powerful bosses like Yog-Dzewa.

==== Mob Categories ====
  * **Basic Mobs**: Common enemies found throughout dungeon levels
  * **Special Mobs**: Enemies with unique abilities or behaviors
  * **Bosses**: Powerful enemies that guard important areas
  * **Minions**: Weaker creatures that support other enemies

==== Mob Stats ====
Mobs have various statistics that determine their effectiveness in combat:
  * **HP**: Health pool
  * **Attack Skill**: Accuracy in combat
  * **Defense Skill**: Evasion in combat
  * **Damage**: Attack power
  * **Defense Rate (DR)**: Damage reduction value
  * **EXP**: Experience granted when defeated

==== Mob Mechanics ====
  * **AI Behavior**: Different mobs have unique movement and attack patterns
  * **Drops**: Mobs have various chances to drop items when defeated
  * **Spawning**: Different mob types appear in different dungeon levels
  * **Resistances**: Some mobs have resistances or immunities to certain effects

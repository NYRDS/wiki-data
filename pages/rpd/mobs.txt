====== Mobs ======

Mobs (Mobiles) are the mobile creatures that populate the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon. This includes enemies, NPCs, pets, and other characters that interact with the player.

==== Categories ====
  * [[rpd:enemies|Enemies]] - Hostile creatures that attack the player
  * [[rpd:npcs|NPCs]] - Non-player characters with special functions
  * [[rpd:bosses|Bosses]] - Powerful unique enemies
  * [[rpd:pet|Pets/Companions]] - Creature allies that can be summoned or tamed

====== Deep Snail Mob ======

{{ rpd:images:deep_snail_mob.png|Deep Snail }}

**Deep Snail** is a mollusk mob in Remixed Dungeon. It can often be seen near water sources and is much faster than your ordinary snail.

==== Description ====
This type of mollusk can often be seen near water sources and is much faster than your ordinary snail. Beware of the bites however, they can have an unpleasant effect on your skin. Deep Snails are unique in that they can potentially be tamed as pets.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 10
  * **Defense Skill:** 3
  * **Attack Skill:** 8
  * **Damage Range:** 1-4
  * **Damage Reduction:** 1
  * **Experience:** 2
  * **Max Level:** 4
  * **Base Speed:** 1
  * **View Distance:** 3

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Ooze proc:** 20% chance to apply Ooze debuff to adjacent attacker on being hit
  * **Pet Potential:** Can be tamed as a pet
  * **Water Affinity:** Often found near water sources

==== Behavior ====
  * Generally non-aggressive unless provoked
  * Found primarily on levels with water sources
  * Faster movement compared to regular snails
  * When attacked, may apply Ooze effect to the attacker

==== Tactics ====
  * Approach with caution due to the Ooze proc
  * Consider taming it as a pet if desired
  * Ooze effect can be harmful, so be prepared with appropriate equipment

==== Location ====
Deep Snails are typically found near water sources on various dungeon levels, particularly where water features exist in the level design.

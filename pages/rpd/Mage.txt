====== Mage ======

==== Description ====
The Mage is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is equipped with magical abilities and a unique wand.

==== Starting Perks ====
  * **Wand of Magic Missile:** Starts with a unique Wand of Magic Missile that can be "disenchanted" to upgrade another wand
  * **Faster Wand Recharge:** Recharges wands faster than other classes
  * **Wand Food Bonus:** When eaten, any piece of food restores 1 charge for all wands in the inventory
  * **Wand Melee:** Can use wands as a melee weapon
  * **Mana Spells:** Uses mana to cast Elemental spells
  * **Identified Scrolls:** Scrolls of Identify are identified from the beginning

==== Class Abilities ====
The Mage has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Battlemage:** When fighting with a wand in hand, inflicts additional damage depending on the current number of charges; every successful hit restores 1 charge to the wand

==== Special Notes ====
The Mage is a versatile class that combines magical abilities with melee combat. His wand serves both as a spellcasting implement and a weapon.

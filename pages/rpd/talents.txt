====== Talents ======

Talents in Remixed Dungeon are special abilities and enhancements that can be unlocked by heroes as they progress through the game and gain levels.

==== Talent System ====
  * Talents are unlocked as you level up your hero
  * Different classes have different talent trees
  * Talents provide various bonuses and special abilities

==== Talent Categories ====
  * **Class-Specific**: Unique talents tied to a specific hero class
  * **General**: Talents available to all classes
  * **Subclass**: Advanced talents available only after mastering a subclass

====== Ice Caves Level ======

{{ rpd:images:ice_caves_level.png|Ice Caves }}

The Ice Caves are a special level in Remixed Dungeon. This level features unique mechanics, enemies, and environmental hazards related to ice and cold.

==== Description ====
  * **Environment:** Cold, icy caverns with slippery surfaces and freezing hazards
  * **Enemies:** Ice-based creatures and cold-resistant monsters
  * **Unique Mechanics:** Sliding on ice, freezing effects, and cold damage
  * **Treasure:** Special items and equipment suited for cold environments

==== Enemies ====
  * [[rpd:ice_elemental_mob|Ice Elementals]] - Elemental creatures of pure ice
  * [[rpd:ice_guardian_mob|Ice Guardians]] - Powerful guardians of the frozen depths
  * Other ice-themed creatures

==== Items ====
  * [[rpd:ice_key_item|Ice Keys]] - Special keys found in the Ice Caves
  * Cold-resistant equipment and artifacts

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Sliding:** Movement on ice surfaces causes sliding effects
  * **Freezing:** Exposure to extreme cold can cause freezing status effects
  * **Cold Damage:** Environmental cold damage in certain areas

==== Strategy ====
  * Prepare for cold resistance and freezing effects
  * Bring equipment that helps with mobility on ice
  * Use fire-based attacks effectively against ice enemies
  * Manage resources carefully in the cold environment

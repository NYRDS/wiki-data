====== Ice Caves Level ======

{{ rpd:images:level_ice_caves.png|Ice Caves Level }}

The **Ice Caves** are a special level in Remixed Dungeon that features freezing mechanics and ice-themed enemies. This level is challenging due to the cold environment and unique enemies found here.

==== Description ====
The Ice Caves level is a specialized area of the dungeon with unique cold-themed mechanics. The level is inhabited by ice-based creatures and features environmental hazards related to cold.

==== Level Characteristics ====
  * **Level ID**: IceCavesLevel
  * **Level Type**: Specialized ice-themed level
  * **Floor Range**: ice1, ice2, ice3, ice4 (based on Bestiary.json configuration)
  * **Environment**: Cold-themed with freezing mechanics
  * **Unique Elements**: Ice-themed enemies and mechanics

==== Enemies ====
  * [[rpd:kobold_icemancer_mob|Kobold Icemancer]] - Magic-using mob that can apply [[rpd:slow_buff|Slow]] effect
  * [[rpd:ice_elemental_mob|Ice Elemental]] - Resistant to various effects and can slow enemies
  * [[rpd:cold_spirit_mob|Cold Spirit]] - Fast-moving spirit creature
  * Other ice-themed creatures

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Cold environment that may affect gameplay mechanics
  * Unique freezing effects
  * Ice-themed challenges and hazards

==== Items and Resources ====
  * Items related to cold and ice mechanics
  * Potential for finding [[rpd:frozen_carpaccio_item|Frozen Carpaccio]]
  * Unique equipment that functions well in cold environments

==== Strategies ====
  * Be aware of freezing effects that can slow or immobilize
  * Prepare for cold-based attacks
  * Consider items that counter freezing effects
  * Manage food resources carefully as cold may increase consumption
  * Watch out for ranged attacks from ice-themed enemies

==== Location in Dungeon ====
  * The Ice Caves level is located after the City level in the game progression
  * Serves as a transition between surface levels and deeper dungeon areas
  * Precedes the Halls level in the dungeon progression

====== Rogue Affinity ======

{{ rpd:images:buff_Cloak.png|Rogue Affinity }}

**Rogue Affinity** is one of the [[rpd:magic_affinity|magic affinities]] in Remixed Dungeon. It is primarily associated with the [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue class]] and encompasses stealth, agility, and detection-related spells.

==== Spells with Rogue Affinity ====
  * [[rpd:cloak_spell|Cloak]]
  * [[rpd:haste_spell|Haste]]
  * [[rpd:kunai_throw_spell|Kunai Throw]]
  * [[rpd:backstab_spell|Backstab]]

==== Mechanics ====
Characters with Rogue affinity have enhanced capabilities with stealth and agility-based spells. The affinity affects spell effectiveness and may provide bonuses to related abilities.

==== Class Association ====
  * **Primary Class**: [[rpd:rogue_class|Rogue]] - The main class associated with this affinity
  * **Subclasses**: May be available to subclasses of the Rogue class

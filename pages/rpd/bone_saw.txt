====== Bone Saw ======

{{ rpd:images:bonesaw_sprite.png|Bone Saw }}

The **Bone Saw** is a unique weapon specifically associated with the [[rpd:doctor|Doctor]] class in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Weapon (Melee)
  * **Class:** Doctor exclusive weapon
  * **Damage:** Moderate
  * **Durability:** Standard
  * **Special Effect:** Used with special mechanics for the Doctor class

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Bone Saw has special mechanics when used by the [[rpd:doctor|Doctor]] class
  * When the Doctor uses weapons other than the Bone Saw, they receive a -5 penalty to attack skill
  * Additionally, with a 5% chance when using non-Bone Saw weapons, the Doctor receives a message that their accuracy has decreased
  * The Bone Saw is intended as the Doctor's optimal weapon for maintaining accuracy

==== Acquisition ====
  * The Bone Saw is NOT part of the Doctor's starting equipment
  * The exact acquisition method is not clearly documented in the JSON configuration files (not found in Treasury.json)
  * It's likely obtained through specific events, special chests, or other non-standard loot mechanisms
  * Doctor players should prioritize finding the Bone Saw for optimal performance after starting the game without a weapon

==== Strategy ====
  * Doctor players should prioritize using the Bone Saw to avoid accuracy penalties
  * The weapon fits the Doctor's thematic plague and surgery background
  * Enhancing the Bone Saw with enchantments and upgrades will improve its effectiveness

==== Class Restrictions ====
  * While other classes can technically equip the Bone Saw, it's designed with the Doctor's mechanics in mind

====== Shadow Lord ======

{{ :rpd:shadow_lord.png?nolink&50|}}

Shadow Lord is a powerful boss enemy found in the Halls area of the dungeon. He represents one of the most challenging encounters before the final boss, Yog-Dzewa.

==== Description ====
The Shadow Lord is a powerful entity that rules over the Demon Halls. It embodies the darkness and supernatural power of the demon-occupied former dwarven outskirts. This boss combines physical and magical attacks with shadow-based abilities that make it particularly dangerous.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Very high
  * **Attack:** Combination of physical and magical attacks
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Shadow-based attacks that may bypass normal defenses
    * Summoning of minions during combat
    * Special shadow mechanics in the arena
    * Teleportation and positioning changes
  * **Phase Mechanics:** May change tactics during the encounter

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Shadow Realm:** Special arena mechanics involving shadows
  * **Minion Summons:** Periodically summons lesser demons or shadow creatures
  * **Teleportation:** Can move unpredictably around the arena
  * **Darkness Abilities:** Attacks related to darkness and shadow magic

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Halls area (level 25)
  * Appears in a boss arena designed with shadow mechanics

==== Preparation ====
  * **Equipment:** Weapons effective against magical/undead enemies
  * **Consumables:** Healing items and protection from magical damage
  * **Strategy:** Plan for both physical and magical damage mitigation
  * **Light Sources:** Items that might counteract darkness mechanics

==== Tactics ====
  * Deal with summoned minions while focusing on the boss
  * Be prepared for sudden position changes
  * Manage area attacks to handle multiple targets
  * Protect against magical and shadow-based attacks
  * Maintain consistent damage output despite arena mechanics

==== Rewards ====
  * Access to deeper areas of the dungeon
  * Powerful magical or shadow-themed equipment
  * Valuable experience and treasure

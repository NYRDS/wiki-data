====== Firebloom Seed ======

{{ rpd:images:firebloom.seed_sprite.png|Firebloom Seed }}

==== Description ====
A seed from the Firebloom plant that can be planted to grow a plant with fire-related properties. When touched, the grown plant bursts into flames.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be planted in suitable soil
  * Grows into a Firebloom plant when planted
  * Creates fire when touched (burst into flames)
  * Part of the game's farming and alchemy system

==== Effects ====
  * When grown, creates a large fire blob in a 2-cell radius around the plant when activated
  * Emits flame particles when activated
  * Used in alchemy to create [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame|Potion of Liquid Flame]]
  * Can be eaten to gain burning and speed buffs temporarily

==== Stats ====
  * **Alchemy Product:** [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame|Potion of Liquid Flame]]
  * **Eat Effect:** Applies Burning and Speed buffs to the eater
  * **Plant Effect:** Creates large fire blob in 2-cell radius when activated

==== Notes ====
Firebloom seeds are part of the game's plant system. When grown, the Firebloom plant will burst into flames when touched by any character or effect.

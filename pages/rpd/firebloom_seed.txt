====== Firebloom Seed ======

==== Description ====
A seed from the Firebloom plant that can be planted to grow a plant with fire-related properties.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be planted in suitable soil
  * Grows into a Firebloom plant when planted
  * Has fire-themed effects when mature
  * Part of the game's farming and alchemy system

==== Effects ====
  * When grown, may create fire-based effects in the area
  * Could be used in alchemical recipes
  * May provide beneficial effects when properly used

==== Notes ====
Firebloom seeds are part of the game's plant system and can be used strategically for various fire-related effects.

====== Firebloom Seed ======

{{ rpd:images:seed_firebloom.png|Firebloom Seed }}

==== Description ====
A seed from the Firebloom plant that can be planted to grow a plant with fire-related properties.

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be planted in suitable soil
  * Grows into a Firebloom plant when planted
  * Has fire-themed effects when mature (creates a patch of fire in the area)
  * Part of the game's farming and alchemy system

==== Effects ====
  * When grown, creates a patch of fire in a 2-cell radius around the plant
  * Emits flame particles when activated
  * Used in alchemy to create Potion of Liquid Flame
  * Can be eaten to gain burning and speed buffs temporarily

==== Stats ====
  * **Alchemy Product:** Potion of Liquid Flame
  * **Eat Effect:** Applies Burning and Speed buffs to the eater
  * **Plant Effect:** Creates Fire blob in 2-cell radius when activated

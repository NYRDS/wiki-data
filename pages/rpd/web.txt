====== Web ======

**Webs** are environmental obstacles and mechanics associated with spiders in the dungeon.

==== Properties ====
  * **Movement**: Webs typically impede or block movement
  * **Trapping**: Characters may become stuck when moving through webs
  * **Visibility**: Webs might obscure vision or hide other elements
  * **Destruction**: Webs can often be destroyed by fire or other means

==== Interaction ====
  * Moving through webs may slow character movement
  * Some webs can trap characters temporarily
  * Fire-based attacks can destroy webs
  * Certain abilities or items may help navigate webs more effectively

==== Locations ====
  * Common in areas with spider inhabitants
  * [[rpd:spider_queen_mob|Spider Queen]] level has extensive webs
  * Spider nests and related areas often contain webs
  * Found in the caves level where spiders dwell

==== Strategy ====
  * Fire-based weapons and spells are effective against webs
  * Plan movement carefully when webs block important paths
  * Be aware that webs may conceal enemies or items
  * Some spider enemies can create or reinforce webs during combat

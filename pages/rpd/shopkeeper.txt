====== Shopkeeper ======

{{ rpd:images:shopkeeper_sprite.png|Shopkeeper }}

The **Shopkeeper** is a non-player character (NPC) found in the shops throughout the dungeon. He runs the Pixel-Mart chain of stores within the dungeon levels and offers various goods for purchase.

==== Description ====
This stout guy looks more appropriate for a trade district in some large city than for a dungeon. His prices explain why he prefers to do business here. The Shopkeeper is a stationary NPC that can be found in specially created shop rooms in various dungeon levels.

==== Role ====
* Sells items to the player character, including weapons, armor, scrolls, potions, and other equipment
* Buys items from the player character
* Provides a trading interface when interacted with
* Restocks inventory periodically to maintain available items

==== Behavior ====
* Remains fixed in position and does not move
* Immune to Regeneration effects
* Can be damaged but will disappear immediately when attacked
* Maintains a rotating inventory of items based on what's appropriate for the current dungeon level

==== Interactions ====
* When the player interacts with the Shopkeeper, the trading interface appears
* Players can sell items from their inventory to the Shopkeeper
* Players can buy items that the Shopkeeper has in stock
* The Shopkeeper's inventory includes a mix of common and useful items

==== Shops ====
The Shopkeeper can be found in specially generated shop rooms throughout the dungeon. Different levels may have different types of shops:
* General shops with basic equipment
* Specialized shops in later levels with better items

==== Special Notes ====
* The Shopkeeper will never be hostile to the player
* Some rare items may only be obtainable through shops
* He will occasionally stock food, armor, weapons, and other equipment that can be crucial for survival

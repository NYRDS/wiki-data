====== Chaos Crystal ======

{:rpd:images:chaos_crystal_artifacts.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:chaos_crystal_item.png|Chaos Crystal }}
The Chaos Crystal is a powerful magical component used to create chaotic weapons and equipment in Remixed Dungeon.
==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Rare
  * **Weight**: 5
==== Description ====
The Chaos Crystal is a volatile magical component that can be used to create or enhance chaotic equipment. It pulses with unpredictable magical energy.
==== Usage ====
  * Used as a crafting component for chaotic weapons and armor
  * Required to create items like the Chaos Sword, Chaos Staff, Chaos Bow, and Chaos Shield
  * Can enhance equipment with chaotic properties
==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in the dungeon, typically in magical or chaotic-themed areas
  * May be dropped by chaotic or magical enemies
  * Sometimes found in special chests or rooms
==== Special Notes ====
  * Essential component for chaotic equipment
  * Used in crafting powerful chaotic items
  * Represents the chaotic magic school
  * Rare and valuable component

Chaos Crystals are mysterious objects containing streams of chaotic energy. You feel streams of energy running through your hands while holding it. Chaotic powers flow through this crystal, making it potentially very useful. They can be used to transform regular items into chaotic variants with special properties.
==== Properties ====
  * **Chaotic Energy:** Contains unstable magical energy
  * **Transformation:** Can change the fundamental nature of items
  * **Fuse Action:** Can be used with the "FUSE" action
  * **Chaos Mark:** May allow setting chaos marks on items
  * **Stackable:** No
==== Uses ====
Chaos Crystal can be fused with various items to create chaotic versions of them:
  * **With weapons:** Creates chaotic weapons with special properties (like [[rpd:chaos_sword|Chaos Sword]])
  * **With wands:** Creates [[rpd:chaos_staff|Chaos Staff]]
  * **With armor:** Creates [[rpd:chaos_armor|Chaos Armor]]
  * **With shields:** Creates [[rpd:chaos_shield|Chaos Shield]]
  * **With scrolls:** Creates chaotic versions of scrolls
  * **Chaos Set:** Used to create the complete Chaos Set of equipment
==== Chaos Set ====
The Chaos Crystal is used to create the Chaos Set, which typically includes:
  * [[rpd:chaos_sword|Chaos Sword]]
  * [[rpd:chaos_bow|Chaos Bow]]
  * [[rpd:chaos_staff|Chaos Staff]]
  * [[rpd:chaos_armor|Chaos Armor]]
  * [[rpd:chaos_shield|Chaos Shield]]
==== Strategy ====
  * Chaos Crystals are valuable components for creating some of the most powerful equipment in the game
  * Though the chaotic nature may introduce unpredictable effects, these items typically offer enhanced properties
  * Consider saving Chaos Crystals for high-tier equipment to maximize their impact
  * Different item types produce different chaotic effects, so choose which items to enhance strategically
==== Obtaining ====
Chaos Crystals can be found:
  * As rare drops from monsters
  * In special treasure areas
  * As quest rewards
  * Potentially in shops in later game stages

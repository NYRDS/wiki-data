====== Potion of Might ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_might_item.png|Potion of Might }}

The **Potion of Might** is a consumable potion in Remixed Dungeon that permanently increases the drinker's strength and maximum health.

==== Description ====
When consumed, the Potion of Might permanently increases the drinker's strength by 1 point and maximum health by 5 points. This provides a significant boost to the hero's combat capabilities. According to the game's string resources: "This powerful liquid will course through your muscles, permanently increasing your strength by one point and your maximum health by five points."

==== Stats ====
  * **Type:** Potion
  * **Weight:** 1
  * **Price:** Cannot be purchased in shops; found as random loot
  * **Effect:** +1 Strength, +5 Max Health (permanent)

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Permanently increases strength by 1 point
  * Permanently increases maximum health by 5 points
  * Cannot be consumed if already at maximum strength (20 for most classes)
  * Becomes identified upon consumption
  * Cannot be thrown like other potions
  * Can only be consumed by drinking

==== Usage ====
  * Best used when starting a new character to increase carrying capacity and combat effectiveness
  * Should be used early in the game to maximize the benefit of increased strength
  * Provides both offensive and defensive benefits (strength affects damage, carrying capacity, and unlockable equipment)

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found in special containers and secret areas
  * Occasionally dropped by strong enemies
  * Sometimes available in shops (rarely)
  * Created through alchemy using strength-enhancing ingredients

==== Effect ====
Upon consumption, the Potion of Might provides immediate permanent bonuses to the hero's core attributes.

According to the game's string resources: "This powerful liquid will course through your muscles, permanently increasing your strength by one point and your maximum health by five points."

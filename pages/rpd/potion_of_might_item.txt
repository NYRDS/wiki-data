====== Potion of Might ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_might_item.png|Potion of Might }}

The **Potion of Might** is a rare potion in Remixed Dungeon that permanently increases the drinker's strength and health.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** [[rpd:potion|Potion]]
  * **Rarity:** Rare
  * **Special Effect:** Permanently increases strength and maximum health
  * **Usage:** Single use item (consumed upon drinking)
  * **Price:** 100 [[rpd:gold|gold]] when identified

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Permanent Strength Boost:** Increases strength by 1 point permanently
  * **Health Increase:** Increases maximum HP by 5 points permanently
  * **Immediate Effect:** Boosts take effect immediately upon consumption
  * **Stacking:** Only one point of strength can be gained from potions per game
  * **Identification:** Becomes identified when consumed or seen in inventory after being identified

==== Strategy ====
  * Best consumed early in the game for maximum benefit
  * Particularly valuable for classes that are strength-dependent
  * Consider saving if you're already at sufficient strength
  * Essential for characters using heavy armor or weapons
  * Provides long-term combat advantages

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found throughout dungeon [[rpd:levels|levels]]
  * Available in some shops at high prices (100 [[rpd:gold|gold]] when identified)
  * Dropped by various [[rpd:mobs|enemies]] (rarely)
  * Found in special containers and rooms
  * Occasionally available from the [[rpd:blacksmith_npc|Blacksmith]] quest

==== Effect ====
When consumed, the Potion of Might permanently increases the drinker's strength by 1 point and maximum HP by 5 points. This makes it one of the most valuable potions in the game for long-term character development.

According to the game's string resources: "This powerful liquid will course through your muscles, permanently increasing your strength by one point and current and maximum health by five points."

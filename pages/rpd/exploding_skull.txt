====== Exploding Skull ======

The **Exploding Skull** is a dangerous undead enemy found in the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]].

==== Description ====
Exploding Skulls are magical undead creatures that pose a significant threat not through direct combat, but through their explosive death. They're animated skulls that chase the hero and explode upon death, causing area damage.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Low
* Defense: Low
* Attack: N/A (Explodes on death)
* Speed: Moderate

==== Tactics ====
Exploding Skulls should be fought with caution. They should be engaged from a distance or with ranged weapons to avoid the explosion damage. The explosion can be used strategically to damage other nearby enemies.

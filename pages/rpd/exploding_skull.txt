====== Exploding Skull ======

{{ rpd:images:exploding_skull_sprite.png|Exploding Skull }}

Exploding Skulls are dangerous undead enemies that self-destruct when destroyed in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in various areas with undead
  * **HP**: Low
  * **Attack**: Self-destruct damage
  * **Defense**: Low
  * **EXP**: Low

==== Description ====
Exploding Skulls are animated undead heads that will explode when destroyed, damaging nearby entities. They are typically found in areas with other undead creatures.

==== Behavior ====
  * Moves toward the player
  * Explodes when destroyed, damaging nearby entities
  * Can be dangerous in groups
  * Part of the undead category

==== Drops ====
Exploding Skulls typically drop:
  * Small amount of gold

==== Special Notes ====
  * Self-destructs when killed, damaging nearby characters
  * Be careful when fighting in groups or near other enemies
  * Can be used strategically against other enemies
  * Part of the undead category which has special characteristics

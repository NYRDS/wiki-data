====== Exploding Skull ======

{{ rpd:images:explodingskull_sprite.png|Exploding Skull }}

==== Description ====
The Exploding Skull is a dangerous undead mob found in the Necropolis. When killed, it explodes dealing damage to all nearby creatures.

==== Stats ====
* **HP:** Low
* **Attack Skill:** Moderate
* **Defense Skill:** Low
* **Damage:** Moderate
* **Armor:** Low DR
* **EXP:** Low
* **Speed:** Normal
* **Max Level:** Limited by dungeon level

==== Abilities ====
* **Explosion on Death:** Deals magic damage to all creatures in adjacent tiles when killed
* **Undead Nature:** Immune to certain status effects
* **Aggressive:** Pursues the hero actively

==== Location ====
* Found in [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]] levels
* Usually appears in groups with other undead creatures

==== Strategy ====
* Kill from a distance to avoid the explosion damage
* Use ranged attacks or spells
* Be cautious of other nearby creatures that might be damaged by the explosion
* Consider using knockback effects to separate it from other enemies before killing

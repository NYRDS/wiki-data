====== Berserk Armor ======

Berserk Armor is the class-specific armor obtained by using an Armor Kit on any regular armor for the Berserker subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Use an Armor Kit on any regular armor while playing as a Warrior who has chosen the Berserker subclass
  * Transforms the base armor into Berserk Armor with special properties

==== Special Properties ====
  * Inherits all properties of Warrior Armor (Leap ability)
  * Leap ability: Allows the wearer to jump to a targeted location, stunning adjacent enemies for 3 turns
  * Requires sufficient skill points to use special ability
  * Exclusive to the Berserker subclass - cannot be equipped if not the Berserker subclass

==== Usage ====
  * Essential armor for maximizing the effectiveness of the Berserker subclass
  * Should be crafted as soon as possible after choosing the Berserker subclass
  * Allows full utilization of subclass abilities

==== Special Ability: Leap ====
  * Allows jumping to a targeted location
  * Stuns adjacent enemies for 3 turns upon landing
  * Requires skill points to activate
  * Can be used to reposition in combat or escape dangerous situations

==== Notes ====
  * Part of the subclass identity system in Remixed Dungeon
  * Class armors represent the unique nature of each subclass
  * Cannot be worn by characters who haven't unlocked the appropriate subclass
  * Inherits the base Warrior armor special ability

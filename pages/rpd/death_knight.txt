====== Death Knight ======

The **Death Knight** is an undead enemy found in the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]].

==== Description ====
The Death Knight is a powerful armored undead warrior that serves as one of the stronger enemies in the Necropolis. It combines the durability of armor with necromantic powers.

==== Stats ====
* HP: High
* Defense: Very High
* Attack: High
* Speed: Moderate

==== Tactics ====
Death Knights are formidable opponents that require careful strategy to defeat. They're more dangerous than regular zombies and require stronger weapons or magical attacks to overcome effectively. Ranged attacks and magical damage are particularly effective.

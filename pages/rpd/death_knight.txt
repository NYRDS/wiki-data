====== Death Knight ======

{{ rpd:images:deathknight_sprite.png|Death Knight }}

Death Knights are powerful [[rpd:undead|undead]] warriors found in the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]] areas of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 35
  * **Defense Skill:** 12
  * **Attack Skill:** 15
  * **Damage:** 7-14
  * **Armor:** 7
  * **EXP:** 7
  * **Max Level:** 15

==== Description ====
At first glance, this is a mighty warrior in heavy armor, and only a dead grin gives him away. The death knight is a manifestation of the soul of a knight who failed to pursue his vows. And in his cursed state, he will always wander seeking to purge in battle, killing the innocent, unable to remember who he swore to protect.

==== Behavior ====
  * Powerful [[rpd:combat|melee combat]]
  * Has a 1/7 chance to deal double damage on each attack (proc) - [[mr:death_knight_mob|Code Reference]]
  * Part of the [[rpd:undead|undead]] category - [[mr:death_knight_mob|Code Reference]]
  * Appears in Necro levels 2-4 according to Bestiary.json, with higher spawn rates in necro4

==== Drops ====
Death Knights typically drop:
  * Gold (rarely) - 2% chance according to [[mr:death_knight_mob|Code Reference]]
  * Standard mob drops

==== Special Notes ====
  * Very powerful [[rpd:undead|undead]] enemy with high stats
  * Has a chance to proc double damage on hits
  * Part of the [[rpd:necropolis|necropolis]] area enemies

==== Spawn Rates ====
According to [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/levelsDesc/Bestiary.json#L65|Bestiary.json]]:
  * necro2: 0.5 spawn rate
  * necro3: 0.5 spawn rate
  * necro4: 1.5 spawn rate

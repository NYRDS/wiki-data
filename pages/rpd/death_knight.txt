====== Death Knight ======

{{ rpd:images:death_knight_sprite.png|Death Knight }}

Death Knights are powerful undead warriors found in the deeper areas of the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in deeper areas of the dungeon
  * **HP**: Very High
  * **Attack**: Very High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: High

==== Description ====
Death Knights are among the most powerful undead warriors, once mighty heroes who have been corrupted by dark magic. They possess both formidable combat abilities and dark powers.

==== Behavior ====
  * Powerful melee combat with dark magic abilities
  * High defensive capabilities
  * Uses both physical and magical attacks
  * Part of the undead category

==== Drops ====
Death Knights typically drop:
  * High-quality weapons or armor
  * Gold
  * Potions or scrolls
  * Occasionally rare items

==== Special Notes ====
  * Very powerful undead enemy
  * More dangerous than Dread Knights
  * Represents a significant challenge for high-level heroes
  * Part of the undead category which has special characteristics

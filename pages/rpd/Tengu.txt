====== Tengu ======

==== Description ====
Tengu is an exiled assassin who has made his home in the Prison area of the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** High
  * **Attack Skill:** Very high
  * **Defense Skill:** High
  * **Damage:** High
  * **Exp for Kill:** High

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Assassination Skills:** Expert in stealth and quick kills
  * **Trap Mastery:** Makes extensive use of traps
  * **High Evasion:** Difficult to hit consistently
  * **Debuff Attacks:** Can apply various negative status effects

==== Notes ====
Tengu is a unique boss who tests the player's ability to navigate and survive in a trap-filled environment. He's known for his extensive use of traps and debuff attacks.

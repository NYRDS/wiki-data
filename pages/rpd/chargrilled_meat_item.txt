====== Chargrilled Meat ======

{{ rpd:images:chargrilled_meat_item.png|Chargrilled Meat }}

**Chargrilled Meat** is a food item in Remixed Dungeon. It can be obtained by cooking [[rpd:mystery_meat|Mystery Meat]] at a cauldron. It's a safe alternative to raw meat and provides a moderate amount of satiety.

==== Description ====
According to the in-game description: "A decent meal that might be hard to distinguish from leather at first glance."

==== Item Class ====
* **Java Class:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/food/ChargrilledMeat.java|ChargrilledMeat.java]]
* **Inherits From:** [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/items/food/Food.java|Food.java]]

==== Properties ====
* **Energy Provided:** 2,400 units (Hunger.STARVING - Hunger.HUNGRY)
* **Price:** 7 gold per item
* **Type:** Food item
* **Stackable:** Yes (up to 10 per stack)
* **Nutrition:** Good satiety, but less than a full ration

==== Uses ====
* **Satisfies Hunger:** Consuming this item provides moderate hunger satisfaction
* **Safe Food:** Unlike [[rpd:mystery_meat|Mystery Meat]], this item does not have random effects
* **Emergency Food:** A reliable food option when other rations are unavailable

==== Obtaining ====
* **Cooking:** Created by cooking [[rpd:mystery_meat|Mystery Meat]] at a cauldron in the dungeon
* **Occasionally Found:** Sometimes found as dungeon loot, though rare
* **Alternative to Mystery Meat:** Safer option than consuming raw mystery meat

==== Strategy ====
* **Cook First:** Always cook [[rpd:mystery_meat|Mystery Meat]] before consuming to get Chargrilled Meat instead of risking negative effects
* **Resource Conservation:** Cooking mystery meat saves inventory space since you're converting a potentially harmful item to a safe food
* **Budget Meal:** More economical than purchasing overpriced rations at shops

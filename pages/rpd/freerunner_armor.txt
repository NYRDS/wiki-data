====== Free Runner Armor ======

Free Runner Armor is the class-specific armor obtained by using an [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor for the Free Runner subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Use an [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] on any regular armor while playing as a Rogue who has chosen the Free Runner subclass
  * [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] can be obtained by defeating the King boss in the City level
  * Transforms the base armor into Free Runner Armor with special properties
  * [[rpd:armor_kit_item|Armor Kit]] action: "APPLY" (ArmorKit_ACAplly)

==== Special Properties ====
  * Inherits all properties of [[rpd:rogue|Rogue Class Armor]] (Smoke Bomb ability)
  * Exclusive to the Free Runner subclass - cannot be equipped if not the Free Runner subclass
  * Retains the defensive rating (DR) and strength requirement (STR) of the original armor
  * Cannot be upgraded further

==== Usage ====
  * Essential armor for maximizing the effectiveness of the Free Runner subclass
  * Should be crafted as soon as possible after choosing the Free Runner subclass
  * Allows full utilization of subclass abilities
  * Requires 1/3 of max skill points + 1 to activate special ability when equipped

==== Special Ability: Smoke Bomb ====
  * Allows creating a smoke bomb to a targeted location within line of sight
  * Requires sufficient skill points to activate (1/3 of max skill points + 1)
  * Can be used to escape dangerous situations or reposition for attacks
  * Smoke obscures vision, helping with stealth and escape
  * Only available when armor is equipped

==== Notes ====
  * Part of the subclass identity system in Remixed Dungeon
  * Class armors represent the unique nature of each subclass
  * Cannot be worn by characters who haven't unlocked the appropriate subclass
  * Inherits the base [[rpd:rogue|Rogue armor]] special ability

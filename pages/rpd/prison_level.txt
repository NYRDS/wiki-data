====== Prison Level ======

{{ rpd:images:prison_level.png|Prison Level }}

The **Prison Level** is a dungeon level in Remixed Dungeon that features prison-themed content and leads to the [[rpd:prison_boss_level|Prison Boss Level]] where [[rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]] awaits.

==== Overview ====
  * **Theme**: Prison environment with cells, corridors, and guard areas
  * **Difficulty**: Mid-level dungeon area, more challenging than earlier levels
  * **Layout**: Features prison-specific architecture with cells and corridors
  * **Progression**: Leads to the [[rpd:prison_boss_level|Prison Boss Level]]

==== Features ====
  * **Prison Architecture**: Cells, guard rooms, and prison corridors
  * **Enemies**: Prison-themed enemies and guards
  * **Traps**: Various traps that may have been set up by the prison or deteriorated over time
  * **Items**: Mid-level equipment and potions
  * **Special Elements**: [[rpd:books|Bookshelves]], [[rpd:barrels|barrels]], and torch-lit walls
  * **Unique Content**: At depth 7, features the [[rpd:necromancer_npc|Necromancer NPC]] quest (except for [[rpd:necromancer_class|Necromancer class]])

==== Enemies ====
  * **Prison Guards**: Special enemies related to the prison setting
  * **Escaped Prisoners**: Various criminal types that have become monsters
  * **Other Creatures**: Standard dungeon creatures that have made their home here
  * **Notable Mobs**: [[rpd:gnoll_mob|Gnolls]], [[rpd:warlock_mob|Warlocks]], [[rpd:elemental_mob|Elementals]], and other mid-level [[rpd:mobs|enemies]]

==== Strategy ====
  * **Preparation**: Prepare for a challenging area with unique enemies
  * **Trap Awareness**: Watch for traps that may be more common in this area
  * **Resources**: Conserve resources for the upcoming boss fight with Tengu

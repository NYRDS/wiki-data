====== Prison Level ======

{{ rpd:images:prison_level.png|Prison Level }}

The **Prison Level** is the second major area in Remixed Pixel Dungeon, located between the Sewers and the Caves.

==== Description ====
The Prison Level represents the ruins of an ancient penitentiary. The environment is characterized by dilapidated corridors, abandoned cells, and various mechanical features like switches, doors, and pressure plates. It's a more complex and maze-like environment compared to the Sewers.

==== Layout ====
  * **Floor Type:** Stone and dirt floors with metal grating in some areas
  * **Walls:** Stone walls with occasional metal bars
  * **Special Features:** Prison cells, switches that control doors, flooded areas
  * **Lighting:** Dimly lit with occasional torches
  * **Structure:** More maze-like than the Sewers with locked doors and switches

==== Mobs ====
  * [[rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeletons]] - Common undead enemies
  * [[rpd:thief_mob|Thieves]] - Fast enemies that may steal items
  * [[rpd:swarm_mob|Swarms]] - Groups of flying insects
  * [[rpd:shaman_mob|Shamans]] - Magic-using enemies
  * [[rpd:shadow_mob|Shadows]] - Evasive dark enemies
  * [[rpd:bat_mob|Bats]] - Flying enemies with high evasion
  * [[rpd:brute_mob|Brutes]] - Powerful enemies (higher floors)
  * [[rpd:bandit_mob|Bandits]] - Thief variants
  * [[rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu (Boss)]] - The level boss

==== Items and Equipment ====
The Prison typically contains higher-tier items than the Sewers:
  * Better weapons and armor
  * More useful consumables
  * Specialized equipment like more advanced rings and artifacts

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Switches that control doors throughout the level
  * Flooded areas that affect movement and abilities
  * Prison cells that may contain special content
  * Pressure plates that trigger various effects

==== Notable Locations ====
  * **Boss Area:** Contains [[rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]], the prison boss
  * **Armory Rooms:** Often contain high-quality equipment
  * **Special Cells:** May contain unique content

==== Challenges ====
  * Navigating the maze-like structure
  * Managing switches and doors
  * Dealing with undead enemies that are resistant to some effects
  * The challenging Tengu boss fight

====== Prison Level ======

{{ rpd:images:prison_level.png|Prison Level }}

The **Prison** is the second area in Remixed Dungeon, accessible after the [[rpd:sewers_level|Sewers]].

==== Description ====
The Prison level takes place in a dungeon complex that once housed criminals. It's filled with various traps and hostile creatures, including [[rpd:thief_mob|Thieves]], [[rpd:gnoll_mob|Gnolls]], [[rpd:swarm_mob|Swarms]], and [[rpd:bandit_mob|Bandits]].

==== Layout ====
The Prison has a complex layout with multiple corridors, cells, and rooms. It often features traps like [[rpd:poison_trap|Poison Traps]] and [[rpd:alarm_trap|Alarm Traps]].

==== Enemies ====
The Prison contains various enemies:
  * [[rpd:thief_mob|Thieves]] - Fast and may steal items
  * [[rpd:gnoll_mob|Gnolls]] - Various types including brutes and shamans
  * [[rpd:swarm_mob|Swarms]] - Groups of small creatures
  * [[rpd:bandit_mob|Bandits]] - Similar to thieves but more dangerous
  * [[rpd:skeleton_mob|Skeletons]] - Undead enemies (floors 6-9)
  * [[rpd:shaman_mob|Shamans]] - Cast magic attacks

==== Special Features ====
  * Ammunition: The prison often has ranged enemies, so ammunition is important
  * [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy pot]] - For brewing potions and bombs
  * Stores: Shops selling equipment and supplies
  * Traps: Various traps to watch out for

==== Strategy ====
  * Prepare for ranged enemies by bringing or finding ammunition
  * Watch out for traps, especially alarm traps that summon guards
  * Be cautious of thieves who can steal your items
  * The layout can be confusing, so mapping carefully is important

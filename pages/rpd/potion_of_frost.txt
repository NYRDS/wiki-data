====== Potion of Frost ======

==== Description ====
This potion will instantly freeze all creatures in its area of effect, causing them to take damage and become temporarily paralyzed.

==== Effects ====
  * **Freezing:** Instantly freezes all creatures in the area of effect
  * **Damage:** Causes damage to frozen creatures
  * **Paralysis:** Paralyzes affected creatures temporarily
  * **Area of Effect:** Affects all creatures in the splash area

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be used defensively to stop approaching enemies
  * Can be used offensively to damage and immobilize groups of enemies
  * Particularly effective against fire-based creatures

==== Notes ====
Potion of Frost is a powerful crowd control potion that can turn the tide of battle when facing multiple enemies or dangerous foes.

====== Swords ======

Swords are a category of melee weapons in Remixed Dungeon that includes various bladed weapons. Swords generally offer a good balance of damage, speed, and weight, making them versatile weapons for many hero classes.

==== Common Swords ====
  * [[rpd:short_sword_item|Short Sword]] - Basic sword, often found with Warrior class
  * [[rpd:long_sword_item|Long Sword]] - Standard sword with moderate damage
  * [[rpd:scimitar_item|Scimitar]] - Fast, curved sword with moderate damage
  * [[rpd:claymore_item|Claymore]] - Heavy two-handed sword with high damage
  * [[rpd:assassin_blade_item|Assassin Blade]] - Fast sword with high accuracy
  * [[rpd:crystal_blade_item|Crystal Blade]] - Light sword with high damage-to-weight ratio
  * [[rpd:katana_item|Katana]] - Balanced sword with moderate damage and speed

==== Sword Mechanics ====
  * **Damage Range:** Swords typically have moderate to high damage
  * **Speed:** Most swords have standard or slightly above standard attack speed
  * **Weight:** Varies by sword type, from light to very heavy
  * **Enchantments:** All swords can be enchanted with weapon enchantments
  * **Upgrading:** Swords can be upgraded with Scrolls of Upgrade
  * **Identification:** Swords must be identified to reveal their true enchantments

==== Advantages ====
  * **Balanced Stats:** Most swords offer a good balance of damage and speed
  * **Availability:** Many swords can be found throughout the dungeon
  * **Durability:** Swords are generally durable and can be upgraded multiple times
  * **Versatility:** Suitable for most hero classes depending on the specific sword
  * **Enchantments:** Can benefit greatly from enchantments

==== Disadvantages ====
  * **Common Availability:** Many of the better swords are rare drops
  * **Weight:** Some swords (like Claymore) are very heavy, limiting options
  * **Competition:** Other weapon types may be superior for certain playstyles
  * **Resource Requirements:** Upgrading requires Scrolls of Upgrade

==== Strategy ====
  * **Warrior Class:** Can effectively use any sword type, especially benefits from high-damage options
  * **Rogue Class:** Benefits from fast swords that can trigger sneak attack
  * **Mage Class:** Lighter swords can serve as backup weapons without excessive weight penalties
  * **Huntress Class:** May prefer other weapon types but can use swords effectively
  * **Elf Class:** Balanced stats make swords compatible with magical abilities
  * **Necromancer Class:** Swords offer reliable damage when not using undead minions

==== Class Compatibility ====
  * **Warrior:** Excellent compatibility with most sword types
  * **Mage:** Good with lighter swords as backup options
  * **Rogue:** Excellent with fast swords
  * **Huntress:** Moderate compatibility, may prefer ranged weapons
  * **Elf:** Good compatibility with balanced swords
  * **Necromancer:** Good for direct combat when needed

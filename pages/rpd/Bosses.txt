====== Boss Enemies ======

==== Description ====
Boss Enemies are powerful monsters that guard important areas of the dungeon and provide significant challenges to adventurers.

==== Known Bosses ====
  * [[rpd:lich_mob|Lich]] - Guardian of the Necropolis
  * [[rpd:shadow_lord|Shadow Lord]] - Guardian of the depths
  * [[rpd:yog|Yog-Dzewa]] - The Old God at the bottom of the dungeon
  * [[rpd:dm300|DM-300]] - Mechanical guardian of the Goo area
  * [[rpd:king|King of Dwarves]] - Ruler of the Dwarven Metropolis
  * [[rpd:tengu|Tengu]] - The exiled assassin
  * [[rpd:goon|Goo]] - The amorphous mass

==== Special Properties ====
  * Much higher HP and stats than regular monsters
  * Unique abilities and attack patterns
  * Often require special strategies to defeat
  * Drop important items or keys when defeated
  * Usually guard important dungeon areas

==== Notes ====
Boss fights typically require careful preparation, proper equipment, and strategic thinking. They often represent major milestones in dungeon progression.

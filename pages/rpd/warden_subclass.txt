====== Warden ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_HUNTRESS_WARDEN.png|Warden Hero Sprite }}

**Warden** is a [[rpd:subclass|subclass]] for the [[rpd:huntress_class|Huntress]] class.

==== Description ====
The Warden is a subclass of the Huntress that specializes in forest and nature-based combat. This subclass provides enhanced abilities when fighting in natural environments and special bonuses related to plant life and terrains.

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Enhanced abilities in natural terrains:** Special bonuses when fighting in grass, forest or other natural environments
  * **Plant-based advantages:** Possible bonuses related to plants and seeds
  * **Environmental mastery:** Other natural terrain-related benefits

==== Equipment ====
The Warden may have special abilities related to natural tools and weapons.

==== Strategy ====
  * Make use of natural terrain features and vegetation
  * Engage enemies in areas with high grass or other natural features
  * Leverage environmental advantages during combat

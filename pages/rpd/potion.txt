====== Potion ======

{{ rpd:images:potion_of_healing_item.png|Potion}}

**Potions** are consumable items in Remixed Dungeon that provide various beneficial or harmful effects when drunk by the hero. They are one of the most important resources for survival in the dungeon.

==== Common Potions ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_healing_item|Potion of Healing]] - Restores health points
  * [[rpd:potion_of_experience_item|Potion of Experience]] - Gains experience levels
  * [[rpd:potion_of_might_item|Potion of Might]] - Permanently increases strength and health
  * [[rpd:potion_of_frost_item|Potion of Frost]] - Freezes nearby enemies
  * [[rpd:potion_of_liquid_flame_item|Potion of Liquid Flame]] - Creates a burning area
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]] - Releases poisonous gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] - Releases paralyzing gas
  * [[rpd:potion_of_invisibility_item|Potion of Invisibility]] - Makes hero temporarily invisible
  * [[rpd:potion_of_mind_vision_item|Potion of Mind Vision]] - Reveals hidden secrets
  * [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] - Neutralizes harmful gases and debuffs
  * [[rpd:potion_of_levitation_item|Potion of Levitation]] - Allows temporary flight
  * [[rpd:potion_of_strength_item|Potion of Strength]] - Permanently increases strength (deprecated)

==== Identification ====
  * Potions start as unidentifed, appearing as random colors
  * Identified potions retain their color and effect
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]] can identify a potion
  * [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] can be used to brew known potions
  * [[rpd:hero_class|Hero classes]] have different identification advantages:
    * [[rpd:mage_class|Mages]] can identify potions by throwing them
    * [[rpd:warrior_class|Warriors]] have a chance to identify when drinking

==== Usage Strategy ====
  * Drinking unidentifed potions is risky but sometimes necessary
  * Healing potions are the most valuable for survival
  * Some potions can be thrown at enemies for tactical advantage
  * Potions can be used to neutralize threats or gain strategic benefits

==== Alchemy ====
  * Potions can be brewed at [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] pots
  * Recipe requires specific [[rpd:seed_item|seeds]] and ingredients
  * Identified potions can be brewed if the recipe is known
  * Brewing is an important resource management strategy

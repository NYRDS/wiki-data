====== Ring of Herbalism ======

{{ rpd:images:ring_of_herbalism_item.png|Ring of Herbalism }}

**Ring of Herbalism** is a magical ring in Remixed Dungeon that enhances the collection of dewdrops and seeds from high grass.

==== Description ====
This ring enhances the natural connection between the wearer and plant life, increasing the chances of finding valuable items when trampling high grass. It's particularly useful for herbivorous characters who rely on natural resources.

==== Stats ====
  * **Type:** Ring (Artifact)
  * **Slot:** Ring (requires Keyring to carry multiple rings)
  * **Upgradeable:** Yes
  * **Upgrade Cost:** Increases with level

==== Effects ====
When worn, the ring increases the chance of finding:
  * **Dewdrops:** Chance increases with ring level
  * **Seeds:** Chance increases with ring level
  * **Herbal Benefits:** Enhances certain herb-related mechanics

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in various chests throughout dungeon levels
  * Dropped by certain challenging monsters
  * Available for purchase from the [[rpd:shopkeeper_npc|Shopkeeper]] in deeper levels

==== Usage Tips ====
  * Particularly effective when combined with subclasses that interact with grass (Scout, Warden)
  * Best used when exploring areas with lots of high grass
  * Combines well with other rings when used with a Keyring

==== Strategy ====
The Ring of Herbalism is most beneficial for characters who frequently move through grassy areas. It's especially valuable for subclasses like the Scout and Warden who gain additional benefits from trampling high grass.

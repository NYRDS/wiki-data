====== Warden Armor ======

Warden Armor is the class-specific armor obtained by using an Armor Kit on any regular armor for the Warden subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Use an Armor Kit on any regular armor while playing as a Huntress who has chosen the Warden subclass
  * Transforms the base armor into Warden Armor with special properties

==== Special Properties ====
  * Inherits all properties of Huntress Armor (Spectral Blades ability)
  * Spectral Blades ability: Creates multiple spectral blades that attack enemies
  * Requires sufficient skill points to use special ability
  * Exclusive to the Warden subclass - cannot be equipped if not the Warden subclass

==== Usage ====
  * Essential armor for maximizing the effectiveness of the Warden subclass
  * Should be crafted as soon as possible after choosing the Warden subclass
  * Allows full utilization of subclass abilities

==== Special Ability: Spectral Blades ====
  * Creates multiple spectral blades that seek and attack enemies
  * Requires skill points to activate
  * Area-effect ability that targets multiple enemies
  * Provides ranged damage without consuming ammunition

==== Notes ====
  * Part of the subclass identity system in Remixed Dungeon
  * Class armors represent the unique nature of each subclass
  * Cannot be worn by characters who haven't unlocked the appropriate subclass
  * Inherits the base Huntress armor special ability

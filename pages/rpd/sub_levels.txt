===== Branches =====

Sub-levels or branches are unique to Remixed Pixel Dungeon and were firstly added in remix.19 update.

Thes levels are optional for the player, but visiting them can reward the player with extra experience and some unique [[rpd:items|items]].
==== List of levels ====
  * [[rpd:spiders|Spider Nest]]
  * Necropolis
  * Frozen Caves

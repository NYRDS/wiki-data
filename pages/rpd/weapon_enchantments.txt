====== Weapon Enchantments ======

{{ rpd:images:weapon_enchantments.png|Weapon Enchantments }}

**Weapon Enchantments** are special properties that can be added to weapons to provide various combat advantages. Each weapon can have at most one enchantment.

==== List of Enchantments ====

  * [[rpd:fire_enchantment_enchantment|Fire]]: Adds fire damage and can burn enemies
  * [[rpd:poison_enchantment_enchantment|Poison]]: Applies poison damage over time
  * [[rpd:luck_enchantment_enchantment|Luck]]: Increases effectiveness of other enchantments
  * [[rpd:slow_enchantment_enchantment|Slow]]: Reduces enemy speed temporarily
  * [[rpd:horror_enchantment_enchantment|Horror]]: Causes enemies to flee in terror
  * [[rpd:death_enchantment_enchantment|Death]]: Can instantly kill non-boss enemies
  * [[rpd:instability_enchantment_enchantment|Instability]]: Redirects damage back to the wielder
  * [[rpd:leech_enchantment_enchantment|Leech]]: Heals the wielder when damaging enemies
  * [[rpd:paralysis_enchantment_enchantment|Paralysis]]: Temporarily paralyzes enemies
  * [[rpd:piercing_enchantment_enchantment|Piercing]]: Damage passes through enemies
  * [[rpd:swing_enchantment_enchantment|Swing]]: Increases damage with 2x2 area of effect

==== Acquisition ====
  * **Scroll of Upgrade**: Can add enchantments to weapons (with random chance)
  * **Smithing**: The blacksmith can add enchantments to weapons
  * **Special Items**: Some items may provide guaranteed enchantments

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Upgrading**: Upgrading an enchanted weapon has a chance to preserve the enchantment
  * **Curses**: Cursed weapons have negative enchantments that can't be removed without a Scroll of Remove Curse
  * **Level Scaling**: Some enchantments become more powerful as the hero levels up

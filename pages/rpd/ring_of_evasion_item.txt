====== Ring of Evasion ======

{{ rpd:images:ring_of_evasion_item.png|Ring of Evasion }}

==== Description ====
The **Ring of Evasion** is a magical ring in Remixed Dungeon that enhances the wearer's ability to avoid attacks and traps.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Evasion Bonus**: Increases the chance to avoid physical attacks
  * **Trap Detection**: Improves awareness of nearby traps
  * **Effect Stacking**: Works with other defensive measures

==== Stats ====
  * **Initial Bonus**: +1 to evasion per level
  * **Degradation**: Evasion bonus degrades with damage taken
  * **Leveling**: Can be upgraded to enhance effectiveness

==== Strategy ====
  * Pair with medium armor for balanced protection
  * Useful against enemies with high attack accuracy
  * Better for mobile, evasive fighting styles

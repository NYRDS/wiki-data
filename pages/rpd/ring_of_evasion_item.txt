====== Ring of Evasion ======

{{ rpd:images:ring_of_evasion_item.png|Ring of Evasion }}

The **Ring of Evasion** is a [[rpd:rings|ring]] in Remixed Dungeon that enhances the wearer's ability to avoid attacks.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** [[rpd:rings|Ring]]
  * **Special Effect:** Increases [[rpd:hero|hero]]'s chance to [[rpd:mechanics|dodge]] enemy attacks
  * **Mechanics:** Each level of the ring provides +1 [[rpd:defence_skill|defense skill]] bonus
  * **Upgrading:** Can be [[rpd:enchantments|upgraded]] to increase effectiveness

==== Mechanics ====
The Ring of Evasion increases the wearer's defense skill by its level amount, which improves the chance to dodge incoming attacks. This directly affects the [[rpd:combat_mechanic|combat]] system by making the wearer harder to hit.

  * **Defence Bonus**: The ring increases defense skill by a value equal to its level
  * **Stacking**: Effect stacks with other [[rpd:defence|defensive]] bonuses
  * **Combat Impact**: Higher evasion means enemies have a lower chance to successfully hit the wearer

==== Description ====
According to the game's string resources: "This ring increases your chance to dodge enemy attack."

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found in [[rpd:chests|chests]] throughout dungeon levels
  * Available in [[rpd:shopkeeper_npc|shops]]
  * Dropped by various [[rpd:mobs|mobs]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Pair with medium [[rpd:armor|armor]] for balanced protection
  * Useful against [[rpd:mobs|enemies]] with high attack accuracy
  * Better for mobile, evasive fighting styles
  * Particularly effective against consistent, low-damage attacks
  * Works well with [[rpd:hero_class|hero classes]] that can maintain distance from enemies

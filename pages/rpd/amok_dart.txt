====== Amok Dart ======

{{ rpd:images:item_AmokDart.png|Amok Dart }}

The **Amok Dart** is an enhanced throwing weapon in Remixed Dungeon. It functions as a regular dart but additionally causes the target to go berserk and move faster.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Throwing Weapon (Missile)
  * **Tier:** Enhanced Dart
  * **Damage:** 1-3
  * **Durability:** Single use (consumed after thrown)
  * **Stackable:** Yes, typically found in quantities of 2-5
  * **Weight:** 1
  * **Price:** 12 gold per dart
  * **Strength Required:** 14

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Amok Effect:** Upon hitting an enemy, causes them to go berserk for 20 turns
  * **Speed Effect:** Upon hitting an enemy, increases their movement speed for 20 turns
  * **Rage Effect:** Upon hitting an enemy, applies a rage buff
  * **Single Use:** Each dart is consumed after being thrown at an enemy
  * **Ranged Attack:** Allows attacking enemies from a distance without engaging in melee combat

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for turning enemies against each other
  * Can be used to make enemies attack other enemies
  * Allows safe attacks from a distance
  * Useful in situations with multiple enemies
  * Costs more than regular darts but provides unique tactical advantage

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in random quantities of 2-5 (Java: //Random.Int( 2, 5 )//)
  * Available in shops
  * Dropped by certain enemies
  * Found in chests and other containers

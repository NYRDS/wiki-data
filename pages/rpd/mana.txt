====== Mana ======

{{ rpd:images:mana_icon.png|Mana Icon }}

**Mana** is a resource system in Remixed Dungeon that powers spells and magical abilities for certain classes and character builds.

==== Overview ====
Mana represents the magical energy required to cast spells and use magical abilities. It's a limited but renewable resource that players must manage strategically.

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Regeneration**: Mana slowly regenerates over time during gameplay
  * **Consumption**: Spells and magical abilities consume a specific amount of mana
  * **Capacity**: Character's maximum mana capacity may be affected by equipment and abilities
  * **Efficiency**: Some equipment and abilities may increase mana efficiency

==== Usage ====
  * **Spell Casting**: Most spells require a certain amount of mana to cast
  * **Magical Abilities**: Some special abilities consume mana
  * **Resource Management**: Players must balance mana use with other resources

==== Classes and Affinities ====
  * **Rogue Class**: Uses mana for throwing spells like [[rpd:kunai_throw_spell|Kunai Throw]]
  * **Mage Class**: May have special mana mechanics
  * **Other Classes**: May gain access to mana-based abilities through special means

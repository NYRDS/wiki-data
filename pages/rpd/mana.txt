====== Mana ======

Mana is a magical resource used by spellcasters and magic-focused classes in Remixed Dungeon. It enables the casting of spells and the use of magical abilities.

==== Description ====
Mana represents the spiritual energy required to cast spells and use magical abilities. It's a limited resource that must be managed carefully by magic users.

==== Function ====
  * **Resource for Magic:** Required to cast spells and use magical abilities
  * **Limited Resource:** Depletes when using magic, requiring restoration
  * **Regeneration:** May regenerate over time or through specific items
  * **Capacity:** May have maximum limits that can be increased

==== Restoration ====
  * [[rpd:mana_potion|Mana Potions]] - Restore mana when consumed
  * [[rpd:tome_of_knowledge|Tome of Knowledge]] - May increase mana capacity
  * Natural regeneration over time
  * Special equipment that enhances mana

==== Classes That Use Mana ====
  * [[rpd:mage|Mages]] - Primary mana users
  * [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]] - Use souls as a mana-like resource
  * [[rpd:plague_doctor|Plague Doctors]] - May have mana-based abilities
  * Other magic-focused classes

==== Spells and Abilities ====
  * [[rpd:spells|Spells]] - Various magical abilities that consume mana
  * [[rpd:healing_spell|Healing]] - Restorative spell
  * [[rpd:ignite|Ignite]] - Fire-based spell
  * [[rpd:root_spell|Root]] - Immobilizing spell
  * [[rpd:wind_gust|Wind Gust]] - Force-based spell
  * [[rpd:freeze_globe|Freeze]] - Ice-based spell
  * [[rpd:magic_torch|Magic Torch]] - Light-generating spell

==== Equipment and Items ====
  * Items that increase mana capacity
  * Items that improve mana regeneration
  * Equipment that reduces spell costs
  * Magical artifacts that enhance mana efficiency

==== Strategy ====
  * Manage mana carefully during extended fights
  * Carry sufficient [[rpd:mana_potion|Mana Potions]] for magic-heavy sections
  * Balance offensive spells with mana conservation
  * Consider equipment that improves mana efficiency

====== Mana ======

==== Description ====
Mana is a magical energy resource used by certain classes and items in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Mechanics ====
  * Certain classes use mana to cast spells
  * Mana can be restored using [[rpd:mana_potion|Mana Potions]]
  * Some classes regenerate mana over time
  * Different spells consume different amounts of mana

==== Classes Using Mana ====
  * [[rpd:mage|Mages]] use mana for elemental spells
  * [[rpd:necromancer|Necromancers]] use mana for death spells
  * Other magical classes may use mana for special abilities

==== Items Using Mana ====
  * Various magical items and equipment may consume mana
  * Some items provide mana regeneration bonuses

==== Notes ====
Managing mana effectively is crucial for spellcasters. Running out of mana prevents the use of magical abilities until it's restored.

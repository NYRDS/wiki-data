====== Curare Dart ======

{{ rpd:images:item_CurareDart.png|Curare Dart }}

The **Curare Dart** is an enhanced throwing weapon in Remixed Dungeon. It functions as a regular dart but additionally applies paralysis to the target.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Throwing Weapon (Missile)
  * **Tier:** Enhanced Dart
  * **Damage:** 1-3
  * **Durability:** Single use (consumed after thrown)
  * **Stackable:** Yes, typically found in quantities of 2-5
  * **Weight:** 1
  * **Price:** 12 gold per dart
  * **Strength Required:** 14

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Paralysis Effect:** Upon hitting an enemy, causes paralysis for 3 turns
  * **Single Use:** Each dart is consumed after being thrown at an enemy
  * **Ranged Attack:** Allows attacking enemies from a distance without engaging in melee combat

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for disabling dangerous enemies temporarily
  * Allows safe attacks from a distance
  * Excellent for crowd control situations
  * Useful against strong single enemies to buy time
  * Costs more than regular darts but provides significant tactical advantage

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in random quantities of 2-5 (Java: //Random.Int( 2, 5 )//)
  * Available in shops
  * Dropped by certain enemies
  * Found in chests and other containers

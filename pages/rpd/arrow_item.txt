====== Arrow Item ======

Arrows are ammunition items used with bows in Remixed Dungeon. Different types of arrows provide various effects.

==== Description ====
Arrows are a type of ammunition required for bows to function. They come in various types, each with different properties and effects. Arrows are consumed when firing a bow at an enemy.

==== Types of Arrows ====
  * [[rpd:common_arrow_item|Common Arrow]] - Basic arrow with no special effects
  * [[rpd:poison_dart_item|Poison Dart]] - Causes poison effect on hit
  * [[rpd:fire_arrow_item|Fire Arrow]] - Causes burning effect on hit
  * [[rpd:ice_arrow_item|Ice Arrow]] - Causes chilling/slowing effect on hit
  * [[rpd:paralysis_arrow_item|Paralysis Arrow]] - Causes paralysis on hit
  * [[rpd:health_arrow_item|Health Arrow]] - Provides healing effect on hit

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in [[rpd:level|dungeon levels]], especially [[rpd:sewers_level|Sewers]]
  * Dropped by certain enemies
  * Created through [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] with appropriate recipes
  * Occasionally found in [[rpd:shopkeeper_mob|shops]]

==== Usage ====
  * Used with [[rpd:bow_item|bows]] to make ranged attacks
  * Each shot consumes one arrow
  * Special arrows provide additional effects beyond normal damage
  * Quantity affects sustainability of ranged combat

==== Strategy ====
  * Maintain adequate arrow supplies when using bow-based builds
  * Different arrow types are useful in different situations
  * Consider carrying multiple arrow types for versatility
  * Some enemies may be especially vulnerable to specific arrow types

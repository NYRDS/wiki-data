====== Yog's Brain ======

{{ rpd:images:yogsbrain_sprite.png|Yog's Brain Sprite }}

==== Description ====
Brain of Yog-Dzewa. Source of all evil ever to come out of the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** 350
  * **Level:** Not specified (boss-level mob)
  * **EXP:** 25
  * **Attack Skill:** 31
  * **Defense Skill:** 30
  * **Damage:** 15-25
  * **Defense (DR):** 12

==== Abilities ====
  * **Ranged-Only Attacks:** Can only attack enemies at range (uses zap/lightning attacks)
  * **Paralysis Strike:** Has 33% chance to stun the target on hit
  * **Summoning:** When damaged, summons Nightmares (scaled to 10x difficulty factor) and alerts all other mobs in the level to its position
  * **Retreat Tendency:** Tends to move away from enemies when in hunting state
  * **Immunities:** Immune to Paralysis, Amok, Sleep, Terror, and Burning
  * **Resistances:** Resistant to Lightning and Toxic Gas

==== Role ====
  * Appears as a secondary boss in the battle against [[rpd:yog|Yog-Dzewa]]
  * At the start of the Yog battle, 2-3 other Yog organs (including Yog's Brain) are randomly spawned from the set: Rotting Fist, Burning Fist, Yog's Brain, Yog's Heart, Yog's Teeth
  * Cannot be turned into a pet

==== Drops ====
  * Drops experience (25 EXP)
  * May drop other items based on game mechanics

====== Elf ======

The **Elf** is a playable class in Remixed Dungeon. They are agile and naturally resistant to certain magical effects.

==== Class Overview ====
  * **Starting Strength:** 9
  * **Starting Health:** 15
  * **Magic Affinity:** Elf
  * **Special Ability:** Resistance to certain magical effects and enhanced dewdrop collection
  * **Unlock Condition:** Unlocked by defeating the 4th boss [[rpd:king|The King]] with any class

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * **Weapon:** Elven Bow (+1) - An upgraded bow with enhanced capabilities
  * **Item:** 20 Common Arrows
  * **Item:** Elven Dagger

==== Class Perks ====
  * **15 Health and 9 Strength:** Starts with 15 points of Health and 9 points of Strength
  * **Root Immunity:** Immune to rooting effects
  * **Upgraded Bow:** Starts with an upgraded wooden bow and a bunch of arrows
  * **Bow Expertise:** Extremely proficient with bows and are agile
  * **Enhanced Dewdrop Healing:** Gains much more health from dewdrops than other classes
  * **Melee Disadvantage:** Less proficient with melee weapons

==== Class Abilities ====
The Elf has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Scout:** Elven Scouts are masters of stealth; trampling high grass grants temporary invisibility buff and they are even faster than regular Elves
  * **Shaman:** Elven Shamans can use wands as melee weapons; successful hits have a chance to activate the wand's power without using a charge

==== Quickslot ====
  * Quickslot 1: Common Arrow
  * Quickslot 2: Magic Arrow spell

==== Class Advantages ====
  * Excellent with ranged weapons (especially bows)
  * Immunity to root effects
  * Enhanced dewdrop collection
  * Agility and speed

==== Class Disadvantages ====
  * Less proficient with melee weapons
  * Lower starting strength
  * More dependent on ranged weapons

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on ranged combat with bows and arrows
  * Collect dewdrops effectively for health benefits
  * Consider choosing Scout or Shaman mastery based on preferred playstyle
  * Utilize the higher agility for mobility

====== Elf Affinity ======

Elf Affinity is a spell category in Remixed Pixel Dungeon associated with the [[rpd:elf_class|Elf]] hero class and their connection to natural magic.

==== Spells with Elf Affinity ====
  * [[rpd:magic_arrow_spell|Magic Arrow]]
  * [[rpd:heal_spell|Heal]]
  * [[rpd:hide_in_grass_spell|Hide in Grass]]
  * [[rpd:nature_armor_spell|Nature Armor]]
  * [[rpd:wind_gust_spell|Wind Gust]]

==== Characteristics ====
  * **Elves** have the highest proficiency with elf-affinity spells
  * These spells typically focus on utility, ranged combat, and nature-based effects
  * Generally have lower mana costs, allowing for more frequent casting
  * Often have synergy with the Elf's racial abilities

==== Benefits ====
  * Elves equipped with elf-affinity spells have enhanced spell effectiveness
  * Lower mana costs allow for more frequent spell usage
  * Many spells provide tactical advantages rather than direct damage
  * Some spells synergize with the Elf's ranged combat style

==== Strategy ====
  * Elves should prioritize elf-affinity spells for maximum effectiveness
  * Use wind_gust and other movement spells for tactical positioning
  * Combine healing spells with ranged weapons for sustained combat
  * Take advantage of grass and natural terrain with Elf-affinity spells

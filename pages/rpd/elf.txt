====== Elf ======

{{ rpd:images:elf_sprite.png|Elf }}

The Elf is one of the starting classes in Remixed Dungeon. He is an agile character with natural magic abilities and ranged combat skills.

==== Starting Stats and Equipment ====
  * **Starting HP**: 15 (vs 20 for most other classes)
  * **Starting STR**: 9 (vs 10 for other classes)
  * **Starting Equipment**: 
    * +1 Elven Bow (identified)
    * 20 Common Arrows
    * +1 Elven Dagger (identified)
    * Cloth Armor (identified)
    * 100 Gold
    * Common Arrows in quickslot
    * Magic Arrow spell (in quickslot)
  * **Immunities**: Roots
  * **Magic Affinity**: Elf

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * The Elf is immune to root effects that would normally prevent movement.
  * Starts with a +1 Elven Bow and a supply of arrows.
  * Has the Magic Arrow spell in the quickslot for magical ranged attacks.

==== Advantages ====
  * Immune to root effects
  * Ranged combat capability with the Elven Bow
  * Magic Arrow spell for additional magical damage
  * Good balance of melee (Elven Dagger) and ranged (Elven Bow) options

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Lower starting strength than most other classes
  * Lower starting HP than most other classes

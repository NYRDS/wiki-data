====== Elf ======

==== Description ====
The Elf is a starting class in Remixed Dungeon. They are agile and naturally resistant to certain magical effects.

==== Starting Perks ====
  * **15 Health and 9 Strength:** Starts with 15 points of Health and 9 points of Strength
  * **Root Immunity:** Immune to rooting effects
  * **Upgraded Bow:** Starts with an upgraded wooden bow and a bunch of arrows
  * **Bow Expertise:** Extremely proficient with bows and are agile
  * **Enhanced Dewdrop Healing:** Gains much more health from dewdrops than other classes
  * **Melee Disadvantage:** Less proficient with melee weapons

==== Class Abilities ====
The Elf has access to unique mastery paths that can be chosen later in the game:
  * **Scout:** Elven Scouts are masters of stealth; trampling high grass grants temporary invisibility buff and they are even faster than regular Elves
  * **Shaman:** Elven Shamans can use wands as melee weapons; successful hits have a chance to activate the wand's power without using a charge

==== Special Notes ====
Elves are versatile ranged specialists who excel with bows but face challenges with melee weapons.

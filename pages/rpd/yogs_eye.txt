====== Yog's Eye ======

{{ rpd:images:yogseye_sprite.png|Yog's Eye Sprite }}

Yog's Eye is one of the five organs of Yog-Dzewa, the Old God. It is the eye of the Old God, an all-seeing gaze filled with eternal hatred. You can feel it watching right through your soul. It appears in the Halls Boss Level along with other organs of Yog-Dzewa.

==== Properties ====
  * **HP**: 1000
  * **Attack**: 30
  * **Defense**: 30
  * **Damage**: 20-30
  * **EXP**: 50
  * **Movement**: Immovable (cannot move from its position)
  * **Carcass**: Provides [[rpd:skeleton_key|Skeleton Key]]
  * **Status Effect Immunities**: Death, Terror, Amok, Charm, Sleep, Burning, Toxic Gas, and Psionic Blast

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Death Ray**: Attacks with a ray of death that passes through multiple enemies in a straight line
  * **Larva Spawn**: Spawns Larva when taking damage
  * **Organ Coordination**: When damaged, calls other Yog organs to its position
  * **Organ Protection**: Damage is reduced when other Yog organs are alive (damage >>= number of other organs)
  * **Secondary Boss Spawning**: At the start of battle, spawns 2-3 other Yog organs randomly from (Rotting Fist, Burning Fist, Yog's Brain, Yog's Heart, Yog's Teeth)

==== Behavior ====
  * Yog's Eye remains stationary throughout the battle
  * Does not respond to beckoning spells or effects
  * When other organs of Yog are present, damage dealt to Yog's Eye is reduced
  * Automatically spawns secondary bosses with the main Yog battle
  * Uses a death ray attack that affects all enemies in a line

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on eliminating other Yog organs first to increase damage effectiveness
  * Be prepared for additional enemies from Larva spawning
  * The death ray can hit multiple targets in a straight line, so position carefully
  * Since it cannot move, you can position yourself strategically relative to walls and other obstacles

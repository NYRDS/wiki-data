====== Warlock ======

{{ rpd:images:warlock_subclass.png|Warlock }}

**Warlock** is a hero subclass for the [[rpd:mage_class|Mage]] class in Remixed Dungeon. The Warlock specializes in necromantic magic and summoning undead allies.

==== Description ====
The Warlock subclass focuses on necromantic abilities and summoning undead familiars to fight alongside the hero. Warlocks can channel dark magic to raise undead servants and use their life force strategically.

==== Abilities ====
  * **Undead Summoning:** Ability to raise undead minions to fight alongside the hero
  * **Life Drain:** Absorbing life energy from defeated enemies
  * **Necromancy:** Specialized spells and abilities related to death and undeath
  * **Undead Synergy:** Special interactions with undead creatures in the dungeon

==== Playstyle ====
The Warlock playstyle focuses on:
  * **Summoning:** Raising undead servants to fight alongside the hero
  * **Life Management:** Balancing health and magical power for necromantic abilities
  * **Corpse Utilization:** Using defeated enemies as resources for necromantic magic

==== Equipment Synergy ====
  * Works well with staffs that enhance necromantic abilities
  * Benefits from items that increase magic power
  * Effective with rings and other equipment that enhance spell power

====== Pasty ======

{{ rpd:images:pasty_sprite.png|Pasty }}

==== Description ====
This is an authentic Cornish pasty with the traditional filling of beef and potato. A delicious meat pasty that provides substantial nutrition and restores hunger when consumed.

==== Stats ====
  * **Nutrition Value:** Restores full hunger bar (Hunger.STARVING = 400f units)
  * **Energy:** 400 (completely satisfies hunger)
  * **Price:** 25 gold

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be dropped by the [[rpd:hedgehog|Hedgehog]] NPC during the fourth interaction
  * Safer to consume than [[rpd:rotten_food|Rotten Food]]
  * Provides better nutrition than basic rations
  * Can be poisoned and turned into [[rpd:rotten_food|Rotten Pasty]]

==== Usage ====
  * When consumed, completely satisfies the hero's hunger (restores to full)
  * Part of special interaction with [[rpd:hedgehog|Hedgehog]] NPC on level 23
  * Fourth interaction with Hedgehog: Says "Take this pie" and drops a Pasty
  * After giving the Pasty, Hedgehog moves faster and eventually disappears

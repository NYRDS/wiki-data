====== Pasty ======

{:rpd:images:pasty_sprite.png?200|}

==== Description ====
A delicious meat pasty that provides substantial nutrition and restores health when consumed.

==== Stats ====
  * **Nutrition Value:** High (restores significant hunger)
  * **Health Restoration:** Moderate healing effect
  * **Energy:** Provides substantial energy to the hero

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can be dropped by the [[rpd:hedgehog|Hedgehog]] NPC during interactions
  * Safer to consume than [[rpd:rotten_food|Rotten Food]]
  * Provides better nutrition than basic rations

==== Notes ====
Pasty is a valuable food item that can be encountered in special encounters, such as with the Hedgehog NPC on level 23.

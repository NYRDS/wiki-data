====== Spider Nest ======

A Spider Nest is a special dungeon level or area characterized by the presence of various spider creatures and their habitats. These areas are filled with webs and spider-related content.

==== Description ====
Spider Nests are specialized dungeon levels that appear in the mid-to-late game. These levels are:
  * Filled with webs that can slow movement
  * Populated with various spider enemies
  * Often feature spider-specific treasure
  * May include special spider-related mechanics

==== Spider Nest Levels ====
Spider Nests typically appear as levels 6s through 10s in the dungeon progression:
  * **6s**: Smaller spider level (depth 6)
  * **7s**: Medium spider level (depth 7) 
  * **8s**: Large spider level (depth 8)
  * **9s**: Very large spider level (depth 9)
  * **10s**: Boss spider level (depth 10) - features [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]]

==== Enemies ====
Spider Nest levels contain various spider enemies, including:
  * [[rpd:spider_egg|Spider Eggs]]
  * [[rpd:spider_servant|Spider Servants]]
  * [[rpd:spider_exploding|Exploding Spiders]]
  * [[rpd:spider_mind|Mind Spiders]]
  * [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]] (on the boss level)

==== Tactics ====
  * Fire-based attacks are effective against web-filled areas
  * Be prepared for multiple enemies at once
  * Web effects may slow movement
  * Ranged attacks can be advantageous in tight spaces

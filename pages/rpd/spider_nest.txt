====== Spider Nest ======

Spider Nest is a special area/dungeon level that appears in Remixed Dungeon. It was also the theme for a story contest that led to the creation of the [[rpd:kusarigama|Kusarigama]] weapon. The level has unique tilesets and contains special spider enemies.

==== Features ====
  * Populated primarily with various types of [[rpd:spider|spiders]]
  * Includes special spider enemies with unique mechanics
  * Has unique tilesets (tilesets/tiles_spider_caves_xyz.png, tiles_spiders_nest_x.png)
  * Contains special loot related to spider-themed items
  * Water texture is water_spider_nest.png

==== Items & Mechanics ====
  * [[rpd:spider_charm|Spider Charm]] - Item that summons friendly spider servants
  * [[rpd:spider_armor|Spider Armor]] - Special armor that provides immunity to webs
  * [[rpd:spider_queen_carapace|Spider Queen Carapace]] - Material item from defeating Spider Queen
  * [[rpd:kusarigama|Kusarigama]] - Weapon inspired by the story contest

==== Story Contest ====
The Kusarigama weapon was created as part of a story contest with the theme of Spider Nest, with the idea coming from Yuriy Frolov and implementation by Pavel Provorotov.

==== Strategy ====
  * Be prepared for multiple enemies at once
  * Understand the different abilities of various spider types
  * Manage space effectively to prevent being surrounded
  * Be aware that Spider Queen drops both Spider Charm and Spider Armor

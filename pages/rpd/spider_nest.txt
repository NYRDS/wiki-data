====== Spider Nest ======

Spider Nest is a special area/dungeon level that appears in some mods of Remixed Dungeon. It was also the theme for a story contest that led to the creation of the [[rpd:kusarigama|Kusarigama]] weapon.

==== Features ====
  * Populated primarily with various types of [[rpd:spider|spiders]]
  * Includes special spider enemies with unique mechanics
  * May contain special loot related to spider-themed items

==== Story Contest ====
The Kusarigama weapon was created as part of a story contest with the theme of Spider Nest, with the idea coming from Yuriy Frolov and implementation by Pavel Provorotov.

==== Strategy ====
  * Be prepared for multiple enemies at once
  * Understand the different abilities of various spider types
  * Manage space effectively to prevent being surrounded

====== Candle of Visions ======

{{ rpd:images:candle_of_visions_item.png|Candle of Visions }}

The Candle of Visions is a special artifact that reveals secrets and hidden areas in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Rare
  * **Weight**: 1
  * **Durability**: Limited uses

==== Description ====
The Candle of Visions emits a special light that reveals hidden secrets, traps, and concealed areas of the dungeon. It's invaluable for thorough exploration.

==== Usage ====
  * Lights the candle to reveal secrets in the immediate area
  * Shows hidden doors, traps, and secret rooms
  * Limited number of uses before burning out
  * Consumes charges when used

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in the dungeon, often in exploration-focused areas
  * May be hidden in secret rooms
  * Sometimes available in shops

==== Special Notes ====
  * Excellent for exploration
  * Reveals secrets and hidden rooms
  * Limited uses - use strategically
  * Particularly valuable for completionist players
  * Can reveal important secrets and passages

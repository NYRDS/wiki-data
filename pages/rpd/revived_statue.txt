====== Revived Statue ======
  * This mob can be found in locked rooms
  * Revived Statue is passive untill you would hit it or use Scroll of Rage or take any other hostile action.
  * Revived Statue is holding a weapon, which can be obtained with 100% chance after statue's death.
  * Revived Statue deals damage equals to its waepon
  * There is a rare version of Revived Statue called [[rpd:gold_statue|Golden Statue]]
  * Also there is an [[rpd:armored_statue|Armored Statue]]

====== Lich (Mob) ======

{{ rpd:images:lich_sprite.png|Lich Boss }}

==== Description ====
The **Lich** is a powerful undead boss enemy found at the end of the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]] (necro5, depth 12). An ancient and powerful undead sorcerer that commands great necromantic powers, it serves as the boss enemy for the Necropolis branch dungeon.

==== Basic Statistics ====
  * **HP**: 200
  * **HT**: 200
  * **Defense Skill**: 23
  * **Attack Skill**: 35
  * **Damage**: 12-20
  * **Damage Reduction**: 15
  * **EXP for Kill**: 25
  * **Maximum Level**: No limit (Boss)
  * **Speed**: Moderate (standard boss speed)
  * **Special**: Summons and controls Runic Skulls, uses necromantic abilities

==== Immunities ====
  * **Paralysis**
  * **Toxic Gas**
  * **Terror**
  * **Death** (from Death enchantment)
  * **Amok**
  * **Blindness**
  * **Sleep**

==== Abilities ====
  * **Runic Skulls**: Periodically activates one of 3-4 summoned skulls with different abilities:
    * Red Skull: Heals the Lich for 7% of maximum HP per skull in total
    * Blue Skull: Summons multiple Skeletons to fight for the Lich
    * Green Skull: Creates toxic gas clouds
    * Purple Skull: Completely blocks all damage taken by the Lich while active
  * **Teleportation**: Can teleport to random locations when attacked or when not adjacent to the player
  * **Long Range Attack**: Can attack enemies up to 3 tiles away using Ballistica mechanics
  * **Self-Destruction**: On death, eliminates all non-pet mobs in the level

==== Drops ====
  * [[rpd:skeleton_key_item|Skeleton Key]] (always)
  * [[rpd:black_skull_item|Black Skull]] (if hero is not Necromancer)
  * [[rpd:black_skull_of_mastery_item|Black Skull of Mastery]] (if hero is Necromancer)

==== Tactics ====
  * **Ranged Engagement**: Keep distance but be aware the Lich can attack at range up to 3 tiles
  * **Skull Priority**: Focus on destroying the Runic Skulls as they provide various abilities to the Lich
  * **Teleportation**: Expect the Lich to frequently reposition itself during combat
  * **Immunity Preparation**: Don't rely on status effects that the Lich is immune to
  * **Crowd Control**: Be ready for summoned skeletons when the Blue Skull is activated
  * **Gas Awareness**: Green Skulls create toxic gas clouds - maintain distance when they activate

====== Lich (Mob) ======

The **Lich** is a powerful undead boss enemy found at the end of the [[rpd:necropolis|Necropolis]] (necro5, depth 12).

==== Description ====
The Lich is an ancient and powerful undead sorcerer that commands great necromantic powers. It serves as the boss enemy for the Necropolis branch dungeon.

==== Stats ====
* HP: Very High
* Defense: High
* Attack: Very High magical damage
* Speed: Moderate
* Special: Casts powerful necromantic spells

==== Tactics ====
Fighting a Lich requires preparation for magical attacks and undead special abilities. The hero should have good magical resistance, healing capabilities, and preferably ranged attacks to engage safely. The Lich is significantly more challenging than regular Necropolis enemies.

====== Enchantments ======

{{ rpd:images:item_Weapon.png|Enchanted Weapon }}

**Enchantments** are special magical properties that can appear on [[rpd:weapons|weapons]] in Remixed Dungeon, providing additional effects beyond their base damage and stats.

==== List of Enchantments ====
  * **[[rpd:fire|Fire]]** - Chance to burn enemies on hit
  * **[[rpd:shock|Shock]]** - Chance to paralyze enemies with lightning
  * **[[rpd:instability|Instability]]** - Random effects on hit
  * **[[rpd:luck|Luck]]** - Increases the chance for critical hits
  * **[[rpd:poison|Poison]]** - Chance to poison enemies
  * **[[rpd:slow|Slow]]** - Reduces enemy attack speed
  * **[[rpd:chilling|Chilling]]** - Slows enemies with cold damage
  * **[[rpd:protecting|Protecting]]** - Reduces damage from attacks
  * **[[rpd:sharp|Sharp]]** - Increases attack damage
  * **[[rpd:stunning|Stunning]]** - Chance to stun enemies
  * **[[rpd:vorpal|Vorpal]]** - Increased damage to certain enemies
  * **[[rpd:draining|Draining]]** - Drains enemy health to heal self
  * **[[rpd:holiness|Holiness]]** - Damage bonus against undead
  * **[[rpd:death|Death]]** - Chance to instantly kill enemies
  * **[[rpd:elation|Elation]]** - Bonus damage when at full HP
  * **[[rpd:gravity|Gravity]]** - Pulls enemies toward the weapon
  * **[[rpd:instinct|Instinct]]** - Bonus damage based on level difference
  * **[[rpd:rupture|Rupture]]** - Causes enemies to bleed internally
  * **[[rpd:sealing|Sealing]]** - Chance to bind enemies
  * **[[rpd:swiftness|Swiftness]]** - Increases attack speed when in grass or not visible

==== Identification ====
  * Enchantments are hidden until a weapon is [[rpd:identification|identified]]
  * Identified weapons will show their enchantment name
  * Cursed weapons have negative enchantments (curses)

==== Acquisition ====
  * Randomly appear on weapons found in the dungeon
  * Result from [[rpd:upgrading|upgrading]] weapons with [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * Chance increases with weapon level after upgrading

==== Strategy ====
  * Identify weapons to reveal enchantments before use
  * Higher-level enchantments have stronger effects
  * Consider your playstyle when choosing weapons with specific enchantments
  * Some enchantments synergize well with specific hero classes

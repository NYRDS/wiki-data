====== Air Elemental ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_AirElemental.png|Air Elemental Sprite }}

**Air Elementals** are swift and elusive enemies found in various levels of Remixed Dungeon.

==== Description ====
Air Elementals are creatures made of swirling winds and lightning. They are extremely fast and can strike multiple times in a single turn. These enemies are known for their mobility and ability to avoid attacks.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level:** 8
  * **Health:** 18
  * **Attack:** 8-16
  * **Defense:** 6
  * **Speed:** 1.5 (fast)
  * **Resistances:** [[rpd:paralysis_buff|Paralysis]], [[rpd:root_buff|Roots]], [[rpd:sleep_buff|Sleep]]
  * **Vulnerabilities:** [[rpd:slow_buff|Slowness]]-based effects

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Swift Movement:** Much faster than normal enemies, often allowing multiple actions
  * **Elusive:** Harder to hit due to constant movement
  * **Lightning Touch:** Attacks may cause stunning or shocking effects
  * **Immunity:** Immune to webbing and similar movement hindering effects

==== Loot ====
  * [[rpd:gold_item|Gold]] (1-4 pieces)
  * Occasionally drops a [[rpd:wand_of_lightning_item|Wand of Lightning]]

==== Strategy ====
  * Use area of effect attacks to increase your chances of hitting
  * Apply slowing effects or use equipment that increases attack speed
  * Focus on preventing the elemental from moving freely
  * Consider using [[rpd:paralysis_trap|Paralysis Trap]]s or terrain advantages
  * Be aware that they can move through other creatures

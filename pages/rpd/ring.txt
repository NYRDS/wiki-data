====== Ring ======

{{ rpd:images:item_RingOfHaggler.png|Generic Ring }}

A **ring** is a piece of equipment worn on one of the hero's ring slots in Remixed Dungeon. Rings provide ongoing passive benefits that enhance specific aspects of the hero's performance.

==== Ring Types ====
  * [[rpd:ring_of_haggler|Ring of Haggler]] - Reduces shop prices
  * [[rpd:ring_of_shadows|Ring of Shadows]] - Improves stealth and reduces visibility
  * [[rpd:ring_of_regeneration|Ring of Regeneration]] - Regenerates health over time
  * [[rpd:ring_of_accuracy|Ring of Accuracy]] - Improves hit chance in combat
  * [[rpd:ring_of_elements_item|Ring of Elements]] - Provides resistance to elemental damage
  * [[rpd:ring_of_evasion|Ring of Evasion]] - Improves dodge chance
  * [[rpd:ring_of_force|Ring of Force]] - Increases melee damage
  * [[rpd:ring_of_frost|Ring of Frost]] - Chills enemies on hit
  * [[rpd:ring_of_haste|Ring of Haste]] - Improves movement speed
  * [[rpd:ring_of_wealth|Ring of Wealth]] - Increases valuable item drops
  * [[rpd:ring_of_stone_blood|Ring of Stone Blood]] - Converts damage to HP
  * [[rpd:ring_of_thorns|Ring of Thorns]] - Reflects some damage back to attackers

==== Ring Properties ====
  * **Durability:** Rings degrade over time and eventually break
  * **Upgrade:** Rings can be upgraded with [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade|Scroll of Upgrade]]
  * **Identification:** Rings must be identified to reveal their properties
  * **Slots:** The hero can wear two rings simultaneously

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Degradation:** Rings slowly lose durability over time
  * **Upgrading:** Increases effectiveness and extends durability
  * **Identification:** Unidentified rings show only generic properties

==== Acquisition ====
Rings can be found in:
  * Chests throughout the dungeon
  * Special level rooms
  * Enemy drops
  * [[rpd:shops|Shops]] - Purchased from shopkeepers
  * [[rpd:alchemy|Alchemy]] - Created through transmutation

==== Strategy ====
  * Prioritize rings that complement your class and playstyle
  * Consider upgrading valuable rings to extend their life
  * Manage ring slots carefully since they're permanent equipment slots
  * Replace rings when better options become available

====== Buff ======

==== Description ====
**Buff** is the base class for all status effects in the game, both positive and negative. These are temporary effects that modify character attributes or abilities.

==== Types of Buffs ====
  * **Buffs**: Positive status effects that enhance character abilities
  * **Debuffs**: Negative status effects that hinder character abilities
  * **Neutral buffs**: Effects that neither help nor harm but change the character's state

==== Common Buffs ====
  * [[rpd:invisibility_buff|Invisibility]] - Becoming invisible
  * [[rpd:regeneration_buff|Regeneration]] - Faster health recovery
  * [[rpd:levitation_buff|Levitation]] - Floating over traps
  * [[rpd:shield_buff|Shield]] - Temporary damage protection

==== Common Debuffs ====
  * [[rpd:poison_buff|Poison]] - Periodic health damage
  * [[rpd:burning_buff|Burning]] - Fire damage over time
  * [[rpd:paralysis_buff|Paralysis]] - Unable to act
  * [[rpd:blindness_buff|Blindness]] - Reduced accuracy
  * [[rpd:weakness_buff|Weakness]] - Reduced damage output

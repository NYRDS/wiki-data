====== Weapon Types Mechanic ======

This page provides information about different weapon types in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Melee Weapons ====
Melee weapons are used for close combat and include:

  * **Swords** - Standard melee weapons with balanced stats (e.g. [[rpd:short_sword_item|Short Sword]], [[rpd:long_sword_item|Long Sword]])
  * **Axes** - Heavy damage weapons with slower attack speed (e.g. [[rpd:battle_axe_item|Battle Axe]], [[rpd:war_hammer_item|War Hammer]])
  * **Maces** - Mid-tier weapons with moderate damage (e.g. [[rpd:club_item|Club]], [[rpd:mace_item|Mace]])
  * **Spears** - Long-range melee weapons with reach advantage (e.g. [[rpd:spear_item|Spear]], [[rpd:halberd_item|Halberd]])
  * **Daggers** - Fast weapons with lower damage but high attack rate (e.g. [[rpd:dagger_item|Dagger]], [[rpd:kunai_item|Kunai]])

==== Ranged Weapons ====
Ranged weapons allow attacking from a distance:

  * **Bows** - Use ammunition like arrows to attack from range (e.g. [[rpd:wooden_bow_item|Wooden Bow]], [[rpd:composite_bow_item|Composite Bow]])
  * **Crossbows** - Higher damage than bows but slower (e.g. [[rpd:crossbow_item|Crossbow]])
  * **Slingshots** - Basic projectile weapons (e.g. [[rpd:slingshot_item|Slingshot]])
  * **Custom Ranged** - Special ranged weapons with unique properties (e.g. [[rpd:chaos_bow_item|Chaos Bow]])

==== Missile Weapons ====
Missile weapons are thrown or fired at enemies:

  * **Throwing Knives** - Fast, light projectiles (e.g. [[rpd:throwing_knife_item|Throwing Knife]])
  * **Javelins** - Medium-range throwing spears (e.g. [[rpd:javelin_item|Javelin]])
  * **Shurikens** - Small throwing stars (e.g. [[rpd:shuriken_item|Shuriken]])
  * **Darts** - Basic ranged ammunition (e.g. [[rpd:dart_item|Dart]], [[rpd:curare_dart_item|Curare Dart]])

==== Ammunition ====
Various types of ammunition used with ranged weapons:

  * **Arrows** - Used with bows (e.g. [[rpd:arrow_item|Arrow]], [[rpd:poison_dart_item|Poison Arrow]])
  * **Bolts** - Used with crossbows (e.g. [[rpd:bolt_item|Bolt]])
  * **Special Ammunition** - Enhanced projectiles with effects (e.g. [[rpd:incendiary_dart_item|Incendiary Dart]], [[rpd:shocker_item|Shocker]])

==== Special Weapon Properties ====
  * **Enchantments** - Magical effects that enhance weapon properties ([[rpd:enchantments|Enchantments]])
  * **Upgrades** - Enhancement levels that improve weapon stats ([[rpd:weapon_upgrades|Weapon Upgrades]])
  * **Durability** - Weapons degrade over time and need repair/replacement

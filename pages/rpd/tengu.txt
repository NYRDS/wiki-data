Tengu is the first boss in Remixed Dungeon, found in the Prison area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Level 9 (end of Prison)
  * **HP**: High
  * **Attack**: High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: Very High

==== Description ====
Tengu is the first major boss of the dungeon. Once a prisoner of the prison, he has become the warden of this area. He is known to be cunning and uses various tactics during the fight.

==== Behavior ====
  * Uses smoke bombs to teleport and create confusion
  * Employs various traps and mechanisms
  * Has multiple phases during the fight
  * Uses ranged attacks to keep the player at bay

==== Drops ====
Tengu typically drops:
  * **Tengu Liver** - Used with the Gnoll class
  * Various powerful equipment
  * Large amount of gold
  * Unique items specific to this fight

==== Special Notes ====
  * First boss, located at the end of the Prison level
  * Known as the "Prison Trap Master" 
  * Defeating Tengu unlocks the next area of the dungeon
  * The fight requires careful positioning and resource management
  * One of the unlock requirements for the Gnoll hero class

Tengu is a powerful ninja-like boss enemy found in the Prison area of the dungeon. He is known for his extreme difficulty and unique combat mechanics.

==== Description ====
Tengu is a mysterious masked figure, possibly the last of his kind, who has become a prison warden of sorts. He is considered one of the most challenging regular bosses in the game due to his unique abilities and combat style.

==== Combat ====
  * **Health:** Very high, requiring sustained effort to defeat
  * **Attack:** Powerful physical attacks with weapons
  * **Abilities:** 
    * Trap creation and placement
    * Smoke bomb escapes to reset positioning
    * High accuracy and evasion
    * Special ninja techniques
  * **Phase Mechanics:** Changes tactics during the fight

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Smoke Bombs:** Periodically teleports to reset distance
  * **Trap Master:** Creates dangerous traps during combat
  * **Ninja Techniques:** Uses various ninja-based special attacks
  * **Phase Transition:** Becomes more dangerous as health decreases

==== Location ====
  * Found in the Prison area (level 10)
  * Appears in the boss arena specially designed for this encounter

==== Preparation ====
  * **Equipment:** Strong weapons and armor are essential
  * **Consumables:** Bring plenty of healing items and utilities
  * **Strategy:** Plan for a long, complex fight
  * **Positioning:** Expect to have positioning reset by smoke bombs

==== Tactics ====
  * Maintain consistent damage output despite teleportation
  * Avoid standing on floor traps when possible
  * Be prepared for special attacks and mechanics
  * Expect the fight to take many turns
  * Bring ranged options in case of difficult positioning

==== Rewards ====
  * Unique equipment or artifacts
  * Access to the next dungeon area (Caves)
  * Valuable experience and treasure

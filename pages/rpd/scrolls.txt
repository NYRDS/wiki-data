====== Scrolls ======

Scrolls are consumable magical items in Remixed Dungeon that produce powerful single-use effects when read.

==== Types of Scrolls ====
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]] - Identifies items
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_teleportation_item|Scroll of Teleportation]] - Teleports to random location
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]] - Removes curses from equipment
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_magic_mapping_item|Scroll of Magic Mapping]] - Maps the current level
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_recharging_item|Scroll of Recharging]] - Recharges wands and other items
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_lullaby_item|Scroll of Lullaby]] - Puts enemies to sleep
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_psionic_blast_item|Scroll of Psionic Blast]] - Area damage effect
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_terror_item|Scroll of Terror]] - Causes enemies to flee in fear
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scroll of Upgrade]] - Improves a single item
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_weapon_upgrade_item|Scroll of Weapon Upgrade]] - Upgrades a weapon specifically
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_challenge_item|Scroll of Challenge]] - Challenges all monsters to fight the hero
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_mirror_image_item|Scroll of Mirror Image]] - Creates mirror images of the hero
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_domination_item|Scroll of Domination]] - Dominates a single enemy
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_summoning_item|Scroll of Summoning]] - Summons a monster as a pet
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_curse_item|Scroll of Curse]] - Curses the hero's equipped items

==== Mechanics ====
  * **Consumption:** Single use effect, then disappears
  * **Reading:** Activated by reading the scroll
  * **Area Effect:** Many scrolls affect an area around the hero
  * **Line of Sight:** Some scrolls require line of sight to targets
  * **Identification:** Unknown scrolls must be identified to see their effect

==== Strategy ====
  * **Emergency Use:** Keep some scrolls for emergency situations
  * **Preparation:** Use mapping and identify scrolls strategically
  * **Combat:** Some scrolls are powerful in combat situations
  * **Efficiency:** Use scrolls that complement your current equipment

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in treasure rooms
  * Available in shops
  * Dropped by enemies
  * Generated by certain items or mechanics

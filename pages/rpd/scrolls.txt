====== Scrolls ======

{{ rpd:images:scrolls_illustration.png|Scrolls }}

**Scrolls** are magical parchments in Remixed Dungeon that provide various effects when read. Each scroll can typically only be used once, making them valuable resources that must be managed carefully.

==== Common Scrolls ====
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_identify_item|Scroll of Identify]]**: Identifies all items in your inventory
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_teleportation_item|Scroll of Teleportation]]**: Teleports to a random location on the current level
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_magic_mapping_item|Scroll of Magic Mapping]]**: Reveals the entire level layout and secrets
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scroll of Upgrade]]**: Improves a single item's quality
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_recharging_item|Scroll of Recharging]]**: Recharges wands and other charged items
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]]**: Removes curse effects from all equipped items
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_treasure_detection_item|Scroll of Treasure Detection]]**: Reveals the location of all treasures
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_lullaby_item|Scroll of Lullaby]]**: Puts nearby enemies to sleep
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_psionic_blast_item|Scroll of Psionic Blast]]**: Damages all visible creatures
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_teleportation_item|Scroll of Challenge]]**: Summons enemies and creates an arena
  * **[[rpd:scroll_of_wipe_out_item|Scroll of Wipe Out]]**: Removes all enemies from the current level

==== Scroll Mechanics ====
  * **Single Use**: Most scrolls are consumed after use
  * **Candle Required**: Some scrolls require a candle of vision to read
  * **Targeting**: Some scrolls require targeting (like Upgrade)
  * **Area Effects**: Many scrolls affect a larger area
  * **Random Effects**: Some scrolls have variable outcomes
  * **Cursed Effects**: Cursed scrolls may have negative effects

==== Scroll Identification ====
  * **Initial Unknown**: All scrolls are initially identified only by their glow
  * **Reading Method**: Reading a scroll identifies it but consumes it
  * **Candle Identification**: Candles of vision can safely identify scrolls
  * **Degrading Knowledge**: Some characters may recognize scrolls more easily
  * **Risk Assessment**: Identifying unknown scrolls involves potential risks

==== Strategic Scroll Usage ====
  * **Preparation**: Use utility scrolls before challenging situations
  * **Emergency Scenarios**: Keep teleportation scrolls for dangerous situations
  * **Resource Management**: Balance scroll usage with availability
  * **Situational Awareness**: Use appropriate scrolls for current situations
  * **Inventory Management**: Maintain a balanced collection of scroll types

==== Scroll Risks ====
  * **Cursed Scrolls**: Some scrolls may have harmful effects
  * **Wrong Situations**: Using the wrong scroll type in critical moments
  * **Wasted Utility**: Using powerful scrolls on minor threats
  * **Revealing Position**: Some scrolls alert all enemies on the level
  * **Limited Supply**: Scrolls are finite and difficult to replace

==== Scroll Acquisition ====
  * **Dungeon Exploration**: Found randomly throughout dungeon levels
  * **Shop Purchases**: Available from shopkeepers in certain levels
  * **Enemy Drops**: Sometimes dropped by defeated enemies
  * **Altar Interactions**: Certain altars may provide scrolls
  * **Special Locations**: Found in specific areas or containers

==== Scroll Categories ====
  * **Utility Scrolls**: Provide helpful effects (Teleportation, Magic Mapping)
  * **Combat Scrolls**: Aid in combat situations (Challenge, Psionic Blast)
  * **Identification Scrolls**: Help identify items (Identify)
  * **Enhancement Scrolls**: Improve equipment (Upgrade, Weapon Upgrade)
  * **Support Scrolls**: Provide healing or protective effects (Terror, Lullaby)

==== Special Scroll Interactions ====
  * **Candle of Visions**: Allows safe identification of scrolls
  * **Equipment Synergy**: Some equipment provides benefits when using scrolls
  * **Environmental Impact**: Some scrolls affect the level environment
  * **Status Effects**: Scroll effects may interact with existing status effects

==== Advanced Scroll Usage ====
  * **Scroll Combinations**: Using multiple scrolls for combined effects
  * **Timing**: Using scrolls at optimal moments for maximum benefit
  * **Positioning**: Using scrolls to improve tactical positioning
  * **Emergency Protocols**: Keeping specific scrolls for specific dangers
  * **Resource Conversion**: Using scrolls to convert one resource to another

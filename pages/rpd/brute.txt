====== Brute ======

{:rpd:images:brute_sprite.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:brute_sprite.png|Brute }}

Brutes are tough Gnoll enemies found in the prison area in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in the prison area (levels 6-9)
  * **HP**: High
  * **Attack**: High
  * **Defense**: High
  * **EXP**: Significant

==== Description ====
Brutes are elite Gnolls that serve as enforcers. They are much tougher than regular Gnolls and often carry superior weapons and armor.

==== Behavior ====
  * Aggressive toward the player
  * More durable than regular Gnolls
  * May use special attacks or abilities

==== Drops ====
Brutes typically drop:
  * Gold
  * Superior weapons or armor
  * Random items
  * May drop special items that can unlock the Gnoll hero class when healed

==== Special Notes ====
  * Stronger than regular Gnolls
  * Associated with the Gnoll hero class unlock mechanic
  * Found primarily in the prison area
  * Part of the chain to unlock the Gnoll hero class

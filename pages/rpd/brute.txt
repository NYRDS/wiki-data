====== Brute ======

{{ rpd:images:brute_sprite.png|Brute Sprite  }}==== Description ====
A powerful variant of gnolls that serves as a heavy fighter in gnoll groups.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** High
  * **Attack Skill:** High
  * **Defense Skill:** Moderate
  * **Damage:** High
  * **Exp for Kill:** Moderate to high

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **High Health:** More HP than regular gnolls
  * **Strong Attacks:** Inflicts significant damage
  * **Group Tactics:** Often found with other gnolls

==== Notes ====
Gnoll Brutes are stronger, more durable versions of regular gnolls that serve as their heavy fighters. They're typically found in gnoll encampments and groups.

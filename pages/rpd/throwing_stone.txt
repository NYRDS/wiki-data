====== Throwing Stone ======

{{ rpd:images:item_Tamahawk.png|Throwing Stone }}

Throwing Stone is a basic throwing weapon found in Remixed Dungeon. It is likely based on the Tomahawk weapon in the game, which is a throwing axe.

==== Description ====
Throwing Stones are simple but effective ranged weapons that can be thrown at enemies. They are useful for early game combat when other ranged options are limited.

==== Stats ====
  * **Damage:** 4-20
  * **Strength Required:** 17
  * **Durability:** Variable
  * **Speed:** Fast
  * **Accuracy:** Standard

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Bleeding:** Causes bleeding damage on hit equal to the damage dealt
  * **Throwing:** Can be thrown at enemies from a distance

==== Availability ====
  * Found in various dungeon levels
  * May appear in shops
  * Often found in containers and chests

==== Strategy ====
  * Useful for dealing with enemies while maintaining distance
  * The bleeding effect makes it particularly effective against groups of enemies
  * Be mindful of strength requirements when using

====== Throwing Stone ======

{{ rpd:images:throwing_stone_sprite.png|Throwing Stone }}

The **Throwing Stone** is a basic throwing weapon in Remixed Dungeon, often used by the [[rpd:huntress|Huntress]] class.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Throwing Weapon
  * **Damage:** Low to moderate
  * **Durability:** Single use
  * **Throw Range:** Medium
  * **Special Effect:** Basic ranged attack

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * Simple ranged weapon that can hit enemies from a distance
  * Generally weaker than other throwing weapons but more common
  * Part of the Huntress's thematic arsenal
  * Disappears after being thrown (single use)

==== Acquisition ====
  * Can be found in the dungeon
  * May be available in shops
  * Part of Huntress's early-game arsenal

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for safe, ranged attacks when you want to avoid close combat
  * Good for softening up enemies before engaging in melee
  * Useful when facing enemies that are dangerous at close range
  * Better than melee when facing multiple enemies to avoid retaliation

==== Class Restrictions ====
  * Can be used by any class
  * Particularly useful for the [[rpd:huntress|Huntress]] class

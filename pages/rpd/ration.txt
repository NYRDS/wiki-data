====== Ration ======

{{ rpd:images:ration_sprite.png|Ration }}

The Ration is a basic food item that helps satisfy hunger in Remixed Dungeon. This is the standard food item found throughout the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Common
  * **Weight**: 1
  * **Energy**: 280 food units (HUNGRY level) - restores from hungry state back to normal
  * **Consumption Time**: 3 turns
  * **Price**: 10 gold per unit

==== Description ====
The Ration is a simple but nutritious meal that adventurers carry to sustain themselves during long dungeon expeditions. It's the standard food item found throughout the dungeon. When consumed, it provides 280 energy units which is sufficient to restore from hungry state back to normal.

==== Usage ====
  * Satisfies hunger when consumed (restores 280 energy units)
  * Prevents starvation damage
  * Takes 3 turns to consume

==== Special Properties ====
  * Standard food item that provides moderate satiation
  * Safer to consume than [[rpd:rotten_ration|Rotten Ration]]
  * Common food item found throughout the dungeon
  * Can be poisoned by environmental effects (becomes [[rpd:rotten_ration|Rotten Ration]])

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found throughout the dungeon (probability 4 in Treasury system)
  * Often appears in supply rooms
  * May be carried by some NPCs or enemies
  * Available in shops when present

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential for managing hunger in longer dungeon runs
  * Basic food item with standard benefits
  * Should be conserved for critical moments when hunger becomes an issue
  * Unlike some other foods, Rations don't provide healing but just satisfy hunger
  * Rations are more common than [[rpd:overpriced_ration|Overpriced Rations]] (4 vs 0.1 probability)

==== Notes ====
  * Regular Rations have a 4 in the Treasury system probability
  * [[rpd:overpriced_ration|Overpriced Rations]] have 0.1 probability
  * [[rpd:rotten_ration|Rotten Rations]] have 0 probability (they can't spawn naturally, only be created by poisoning regular rations)

==== Varieties ====
  * [[rpd:overpriced_ration|Overpriced Ration]] - More expensive version with same benefits
  * [[rpd:rotten_ration|Rotten Ration]] - Poisoned version that provides no benefit

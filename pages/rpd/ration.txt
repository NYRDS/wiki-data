====== Ration ======

{{ rpd:images:ration_sprite.png|Ration Sprite }}

{{ rpd:images:ration_item.png|Ration }}

The Ration is a basic food item that helps satisfy hunger in Remixed Dungeon. This is the normal food in the game, along with pasty and fish. This satisfies starvation and can be found on most levels.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Common
  * **Weight**: 8
  * **Nutrition**: 3000 food units
  * **Nutrition Value:** Full hunger restoration
  * **Health Restoration:** Minor healing effect
  * **Energy:** Provides standard energy to the hero

==== Description ====
The Ration is a simple but nutritious meal that adventurers carry to sustain themselves during long dungeon expeditions. It's the standard food item found throughout the dungeon. A standard food ration that provides basic nutrition for adventurers exploring the dungeon.

==== Usage ====
  * Satisfies hunger when consumed
  * Prevents starvation damage
  * Provides moderate nutrition value

==== Special Properties ====
  * Standard food item that provides full satiation
  * Safer to consume than [[rpd:rotten_food|Rotten Food]]
  * Common food item found throughout the dungeon
  * Does not spoil or have negative effects

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found throughout the dungeon
  * Often appears in supply rooms
  * May be carried by some NPCs or enemies
  * Available in shops when present

==== Special Notes ====
  * Essential for managing hunger
  * Basic food item with standard benefits
  * Light compared to some other food items
  * Should be conserved for critical moments
  * Rations are the standard food item in the dungeon, providing reliable nutrition without risk. They are essential for maintaining health during long dungeon expeditions.

==== Additional Varieties ====
There is also another variety of the food ration: **Overpriced food ration**
This looks similar to the normal ration but it satisfies less hunger, it is expensive, and it is limited in stock.

====== Ration ======

{:rpd:images:ration_sprite.png?200|}

The Ration is a basic food item that helps satisfy hunger in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **Rarity**: Common
  * **Weight**: 8
  * **Nutrition**: 3000 food units

==== Description ====
The Ration is a simple but nutritious meal that adventurers carry to sustain themselves during long dungeon expeditions. It's the standard food item found throughout the dungeon.

==== Usage ====
  * Satisfies hunger when consumed
  * Prevents starvation damage
  * Provides moderate nutrition value

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found throughout the dungeon
  * Often appears in supply rooms
  * May be carried by some NPCs or enemies
  * Available in shops when present

==== Special Notes ====
  * Essential for managing hunger
  * Basic food item with standard benefits
  * Light compared to some other food items
  * Should be conserved for critical moments

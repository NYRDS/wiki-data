====== Priest Class ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_PRIEST.png|Priest Class Sprite }}

The **Priest** is one of the starting [[rpd:hero_classes|hero classes]] in Remixed Dungeon. The Priest is a class focused on support and healing abilities.

==== Description ====
The Priest is a support-oriented class with abilities centered around healing and protective magic. The class specializes in maintaining health and providing assistance to allies. This class is still in development as "Coming Soon" status as of recent versions.

==== Starting Equipment ====
  * [[rpd:scroll_of_remove_curse_item|Scroll of Remove Curse]]
  * [[rpd:food_ration|Ration of Food]]
  * 50 [[rpd:gold_item|Gold Coins]]

==== Stats ====
  * **Starting Strength:** 10 (standard)
  * **Starting HP:** 15 (standard)
  * **Starting SP:** 10 (standard)
  * **Max Inventory:** 19 slots

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Magic Affinity:** Priest magic affinity, allowing access to healing and sanctity spells like [[rpd:order_spell|Order]], [[rpd:heal_spell|Heal]], etc.
  * **Support Focus:** Emphasis on beneficial spells and effects

==== Subclasses ====
As of current development, subclass options for the Priest are not yet implemented.

==== Advantages ====
  * Healing and support abilities
  * Beneficial status effects
  * Defensive capabilities

==== Disadvantages ====
  * **Development Status:** This class is marked as "Coming Soon" and may not be fully implemented
  * Limited information available about specific mechanics
  * May not be available for normal gameplay

==== Strategy ====
  * Focus on healing and support spells
  * Use protective abilities defensively
  * Maintain health of yourself and companions

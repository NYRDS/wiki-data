====== Wands ======

{{ rpd:images:wand.png|Wand }}

**Wands** are magical items in Remixed Dungeon that can be charged and discharged to cast spells. They are an important part of the game's magic system and provide various magical effects.

==== Common Wands ====
  * [[rpd:wand_of_magic_missile_item|Wand of Magic Missile]]: Basic elemental damage wand
  * [[rpd:wand_of_lightning_item|Wand of Lightning]]: Electric damage that can arc between enemies
  * [[rpd:wand_of_disintegration_item|Wand of Disintegration]]: Pierces armor and multiple enemies
  * [[rpd:wand_of_fireblast_item|Wand of Fireblast]]: Creates a cone of fire damage
  * [[rpd:wand_of_cold_ray_item|Wand of Cold Ray]]: Deals damage and slows enemies
  * [[rpd:wand_of_poison_item|Wand of Poison]]: Creates poisonous clouds
  * [[rpd:wand_of_avalanche_item|Wand of Avalanche]]: Causes an explosion that stuns enemies
  * [[rpd:wand_of_telekinesis_item|Wand of Telekinesis]]: Pulls items and enemies toward you
  * [[rpd:wand_of_fear_item|Wand of Fear]]: Frightens enemies
  * [[rpd:wand_of_living_earth_item|Wand of Living Earth]]: Summons earth armor that fights alongside you
  * [[rpd:wand_of_prismatic_light_item|Wand of Prismatic Light]]: Deals damage and reveals illusions
  * [[rpd:wand_of_blink_item|Wand of Blink]]: Teleports to the target location
  * [[rpd:wand_of_regrowth_item|Wand of Regrowth]]: Grows vegetation that eventually turns to grass

==== Wand Mechanics ====
  * **Charges**: Wands have charges that can be replenished through various means
  * **Identify**: Wands must be identified to know their effect
  * **Damage**: More charged wands typically do more damage
  * **Curses**: Cursed wands may have negative effects

==== Charging Wands ====
  * **Natural Recharge**: Wands gradually recharge during hero's rest (1 charge per 12 turns)
  * **Scrolls of Recharging**: Immediately recharge all wands
  * **Wand of Recharging**: Self-recharging wand provides charges to other wands
  * **Other effects**: Some items or abilities can also recharge wands

==== Upgrading Wands ====
  * Wands can be upgraded using [[rpd:scroll_of_upgrade_item|Scrolls of Upgrade]]
  * Upgraded wands have more charges and may be more accurate

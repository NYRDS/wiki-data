====== Gladiator ======

{{ rpd:images:gladiator_subclass.png|Gladiator }}

**Gladiator** is a hero subclass for the [[rpd:warrior_class|Warrior]] class in Remixed Dungeon. The Gladiator specializes in weapon mastery and close combat effectiveness.

==== Description ====
The Gladiator subclass enhances the Warrior's natural combat abilities with advanced weapon techniques. Gladiators excel at single combat and can often turn their opponent's weapons against them.

==== Abilities ====
  * **Sword Mastery:** Enhanced effectiveness when using swords and similar weapons
  * **Weapon Return:** Throws weapons that return to the hero's hand like a boomerang
  * **Combat Expertise:** Improved damage calculation and fighting techniques
  * **Weapon Synergy:** Better performance when using weapons with enchantments

==== Playstyle ====
The Gladiator playstyle focuses on:
  * **Weapon Specialization:** Using sword-type weapons to maximum effect
  * **Aggressive Combat:** Engaging in direct combat with superior weapon skills
  * **Return Throws:** Using thrown weapons with the return ability for sustained ranged attacks
  * **Melee Dominance:** Excelling in close-quarters combat

==== Equipment Synergy ====
  * Most effective with swords and melee weapons
  * Benefits significantly from weapon enchantments
  * Works well with items that enhance physical combat
  * Effective with rings and accessories that improve melee combat

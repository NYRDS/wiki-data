====== Traps ======

{{ rpd:images:traps_illustration.png|Traps }}

**Traps** are dangerous mechanisms hidden throughout the dungeon in Remixed Dungeon. They activate when stepped on or triggered in other ways, potentially causing serious harm or death to unwary adventurers.

==== Common Traps ====
  * **Poison Trap**: Inflicts the poison debuff
  * **Fire Trap**: Deals fire damage and may ignite flammable items
  * **Paralytic Trap**: Paralyzes the hero for several turns
  * **Lightning Trap**: Deals electric damage to the hero and adjacent creatures
  * **Flock Trap**: Summons a flock of magical sheep (generally harmless)
  * **Teleport Trap**: Teleports the hero to a random location on the level
  * **Rockfall Trap**: Causes rocks to fall, damaging all characters in the affected area
  * **Spear Trap**: Deals physical damage with spears
  * **Summoning Trap**: Summons several enemies at the trap location
  * **Alarm Trap**: Alerts all enemies on the level to your presence
  * **Gripping Trap**: Deals damage and roots the hero in place
  * **Burning Trap**: Creates a large area of fire damage
  * **Chilling Trap**: Deals damage and applies a slow debuff
  * **Explosive Trap**: Creates an explosion similar to a bomb blast
  * **Flash Trap**: Blinds the hero temporarily
  * **Gravitational Trap**: Pulls the hero and other creatures to the trap location
  * **Disintegration Trap**: Deals significant damage and can destroy items
  * **Distortion Trap**: Creates a random combination of various trap effects

==== Trap Detection ====
  * **Search Skill**: Standing still increases the chance to detect nearby traps
  * **Awareness Buff**: Significantly improves trap detection
  * **Ring of Detection**: Enhances the ability to see hidden things
  * **Wand of Magic Detection**: Reveals traps in an area when used
  * **Scroll of Magic Mapping**: Reveals the entire level layout, including traps

==== Trap Avoidance ====
  * **Careful Movement**: Move slowly and check areas before entering
  * **Search Function**: Stop and search to reveal nearby traps
  * **Pre-emptive Disabling**: Use ranged attacks to trigger traps safely
  * **Equipment**: Wear appropriate gear to minimize trap effects
  * **Potions**: Carry healing potions for trap damage recovery

==== Trap Disabling ====
  * **Stepping**: Most traps activate when stepped on
  * **Ranged Attacks**: Some traps can be triggered from a distance
  * **Wands**: Certain wands can trigger or disable traps
  * **Stones**: Thrown stones can set off traps safely
  * **Awareness**: Some characters have special abilities to disable traps

==== Trap Mechanics ====
  * **Hidden**: Traps are initially invisible until discovered
  * **Area of Effect**: Some traps affect a larger area around the trigger point
  * **One-time**: Most traps can only be triggered once
  * **Regenerating**: Some traps reset after a period (in modded games)
  * **Environmental**: Some traps are more common in specific areas

==== Dangerous Traps ====
  * **Disintegration Trap**: Potentially destroys items and deals high damage
  * **Rockfall Trap**: Can be deadly in narrow corridors
  * **Burning Trap**: Can ignite flammable items in inventory
  * **Explosive Trap**: High damage over an area
  * **Lightning Trap**: High damage that can arc to nearby creatures

==== Less Dangerous Traps ====
  * **Flock Trap**: Generally harmless, creates sheep
  * **Teleport Trap**: Annoying but not usually dangerous
  * **Summoning Trap**: Dangerous if you can't fight the summoned enemies
  * **Alarm Trap**: Doesn't harm directly but brings enemies to you

==== Trap Strategy ====
  * **Scouting Ahead**: Use wands or thrown stones to check areas
  * **Awareness Potions**: Use before exploring unknown areas
  * **Health Management**: Always have recovery options when exploring
  * **Escape Routes**: Identify safe paths when entering new areas
  * **Item Protection**: Use resistant containers for important items

==== Special Trap Interactions ====
  * **Armor**: Some armor can reduce damage from certain traps
  * **Resistance**: Some characters have resistances to specific trap types
  * **Immunities**: Certain statuses provide temporary immunity to trap effects
  * **Synergies**: Some items or abilities work well with specific trap types

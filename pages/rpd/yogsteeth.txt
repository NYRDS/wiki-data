====== YogsTeeth ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_YogsTeeth.png|YogsTeeth }}

YogsTeeth is a miniboss mob that appears in the Guts level of Remixed Dungeon. It is one of the five secondary bosses that protect the Yog, along with YogsBrain, YogsEye, YogsHeart, and RottingFist/BurningFist.

==== Stats ====
  * **Health**: 350 HP
  * **Attack Skill**: 46
  * **Defense Skill**: 44
  * **Damage**: 50-80
  * **Defense (DR)**: 21
  * **EXP for Kill**: 26

==== Resistances and Immunities ====
  * **Resistances**: Immune to Toxic Gas
  * **Immunities**: Paralysis, Amok, Sleep, Terror, Burning

==== Special Abilities ====
  * When damaged, YogsTeeth calls for help, causing all other mobs on the level to move toward the player
  * **Attack Procs**: On attack, has a 1/3 chance of triggering one of these effects:
    * **Life Drain**: Heals for the amount of damage dealt
    * **Bleeding**: Inflicts a Bleeding debuff on the target
    * **Double Damage**: Deals double damage and plays a bite sound

==== Strategy ====
Due to its high health, damage, and special abilities, YogsTeeth is a formidable opponent. The ability to call for help makes it especially dangerous if fought near other mobs. It's recommended to fight this boss in an isolated area to prevent other monsters from interfering.

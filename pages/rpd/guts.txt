====== Guts Level ======

The Guts Level (GUTS) is an optional dungeon level that appears after completing the main dungeon in Remixed Dungeon. It's a challenging area with unique mechanics and powerful enemies.

==== Description ====
  * Extra challenging content that appears after beating the game
  * Features unique enemies and mechanics not found elsewhere
  * Higher difficulty than the main dungeon

==== Enemies ====
  * [[rpd:suspiciousrat|Suspicious Rat]] - A mysterious variant of regular rats
  * [[rpd:zombie|Zombie]] - Undead enemies with special mechanics
  * [[rpd:larva|Larva]] - Small creatures that can evolve
  * [[rpd:larva_evolution|Evolved Larva]] - Advanced forms of larvae

==== Features ====
  * Special mechanics and challenges
  * Unique treasures and items
  * High-risk, high-reward gameplay
  * Enemies with special abilities

==== Strategy ====
  * Prepare thoroughly before entering (good equipment, potions, etc.)
  * Expect enemies with unfamiliar abilities
  * Resource management is crucial
  * Different tactics from regular dungeon levels may be needed

==== Access ====
  * Unlocked after completing the main dungeon
  * Requires special access mechanism (details may vary by version)

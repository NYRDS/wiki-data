==== Class Armor Abilities ====

Each class also has a distinctive class armor with special abilities (accessed via the Special action):

  * **Warrior**: Shield Barrage - Leap to a targeted location, stunning all adjacent enemies
  * **Mage**: Various mage-specific armor abilities depending on subclass
  * **Rogue**: Enhanced stealth or mobility abilities
  * **Huntress**: Nature-based effects or summoning abilities
  * **Elf**: Arcane-enhanced defensive measures
  * **Necromancer**: Necrotic Field - Activate necrotism to spread to enemies
  * **Gnoll**: Pack Mentality abilities that may enhance pet effectiveness
  * **Lich**: Death magic abilities reflecting undead nature
  * **Priest**: Divine protection or healing-focused abilities
  * **Doctor**: Disease or toxic-based special effects

For more details on class armor abilities, see [[rpd:class_armor|Class Armor Abilities]].

====== Skeleton Key ======

{{ rpd:images:skeletonkey_sprite.png|Skeleton Key }}

The Skeleton Key is a special key item in Remixed Dungeon that opens special locked areas in the dungeon. It's typically dropped by major bosses and is essential for progression.

==== Acquisition ====
  * Dropped by most major bosses after defeat:
    * [[rpd:tengu_mob|Tengu]] (Prison boss)
    * [[rpd:dm300|DM300]] (Caves boss)
    * [[rpd:the_king|The King]] (City boss)
    * [[rpd:yog|Yog-Dzewa]] (Halls boss)
    * [[rpd:lich_mob|Lich]] (Necropolis boss, drops the [[rpd:black_skull_of_mastery|Black Skull of Mastery]] for [[rpd:lich_subclass|Lich]] subclass)
    * [[rpd:ice_guardian_core|Ice Guardian Core]] (Ice Caves boss)
    * [[rpd:goo_mob|Goo]] (Sewer boss) - one of the earliest bosses that drops a skeleton key
    * [[rpd:spider_queen|Spider Queen]] (Spider levels boss) - drops skeleton key in spider-themed levels
  * [[rpd:skeleton_knight|Skeleton Knight]] and [[rpd:skeleton_mage|Skeleton Mage]] may occasionally drop it
  * Found in special treasure rooms in some levels

==== Usage ====
  * Opens the Demon Spawner room in the Halls level
  * Opens special treasure rooms in various dungeon levels
  * Unlocks special areas that contain powerful items or unique content
  * Can be used multiple times

==== Strategy ====
  * Keep Skeleton Keys for important locked areas
  * Don't waste on regular locked doors (regular keys should be used for that)
  * Important for accessing special content and powerful items
  * Essential for completing certain quests

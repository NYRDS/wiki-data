====== Incendiary Dart ======

{{ rpd:images:item_IncendiaryDart.png|Incendiary Dart }}

The **Incendiary Dart** is an enhanced throwing weapon in Remixed Dungeon. It functions as a regular dart but additionally ignites the target, causing burning damage over time.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Throwing Weapon (Missile)
  * **Tier:** Enhanced Dart
  * **Damage:** 1-2
  * **Durability:** Single use (consumed after thrown)
  * **Stackable:** Yes, typically found in quantities of 3-6
  * **Weight:** 1
  * **Price:** 10 gold per dart
  * **Strength Required:** 12

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Burning Effect:** Upon hitting an enemy, causes burning that deals damage over time
  * **Fire Spread:** If thrown at an empty flammable tile, will create a fire
  * **Single Use:** Each dart is consumed after being thrown at an enemy
  * **Ranged Attack:** Allows attacking enemies from a distance without engaging in melee combat

==== Strategy ====
  * Effective for dealing damage over time to enemies
  * Allows safe attacks from a distance
  * Can be used to create fires in flammable areas
  * Excellent for dealing with groups of enemies
  * Costs more than regular darts but provides damage-over-time effect

==== Acquisition ====
  * Found in random quantities of 3-6 (Java: //Random.Int( 3, 6 )//)
  * Available in shops
  * Dropped by certain enemies
  * Found in chests and other containers

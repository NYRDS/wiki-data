====== Combat ======

**Combat** in Remixed Dungeon refers to the system of fighting between characters (heroes and mobs) using weapons, spells, and abilities.

==== Description ====
Combat is the core mechanic of Remixed Dungeon. It involves turn-based tactical fighting where characters take turns to attack, use abilities, or move around the dungeon.

==== Basic Combat ====
  * **Turn-based system**: Characters take turns based on their speed
  * **Melee combat**: Direct attacks with weapons or fists
  * **Ranged combat**: Attacks from a distance using ranged weapons or spells
  * **Accuracy vs Defense**: Attack success is determined by attacker's accuracy vs defender's evasion

==== Combat Stats ====
Key combat statistics include:
  * **Attack Skill**: Determines hit chance in melee attacks
  * **Defense Skill**: Determines evasion chance
  * **Damage**: How much damage each attack deals
  * **Armor**: Reduces incoming damage
  * **Speed**: Affects turn frequency

==== Combat Types ====
  * **Melee Combat**: [[rpd:melee|Melee]] - Close-range fighting
  * **Ranged Combat**: Attacking from a distance
  * **Spell Combat**: Using magical abilities

==== Combat Mechanics ====
  * **Hit Chance Formula**: Based on accuracy and evasion
  * **Damage Range**: Weapons have minimum and maximum damage
  * **Critical Hits**: Chance to deal extra damage
  * **Blocking**: Some abilities can block incoming attacks

==== Combat Tips ====
  * Know your opponent's stats and abilities
  * Use terrain to your advantage
  * Manage your equipment and consumables
  * Time your special abilities for maximum effect
  * Retreat when necessary to heal

====== Sniper ======

{{ rpd:images:sniper_sprite.png|Sniper }}

The Sniper is the Huntress's subclass in Remixed Dungeon.

==== Unlocking ====
The Sniper subclass can be unlocked by using a Tome of Mastery after defeating the second boss (Tengu).

==== Special Mechanics ====
_Snipers_ are able to detect weak points in an enemy\'s armor, effectively ignoring it when using a missile weapon.

==== Special Armor ====
Using an Armor Kit on any armor will transform it into the [[rpd:sniper_armor|Sniper Armor]], which is specifically designed for this subclass.

==== Advantages ====
  * Ignores enemy armor when using missile weapons
  * Particularly effective against heavily armored enemies
  * Scales well with higher-level ranged weapons
  * Maintains ranged combat advantage

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Only effective with missile weapons
  * No benefit when fighting with melee weapons
  * Armor ignoring only applies to ranged attacks

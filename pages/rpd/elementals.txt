====== Elementals ======

Elementals are creatures made of pure elemental matter - fire, water, air, or earth. They each have unique properties and abilities related to their element.

==== Types of Elementals ====

  * [[rpd:fire_elemental|Fire Elemental]] - Made of flame and heat
  * [[rpd:water_elemental|Water Elemental]] - Composed of water and fluidity
  * [[rpd:air_elemental|Air Elemental]] - Formed of wind and air currents
  * [[rpd:earth_elemental|Earth Elemental]] - Constructed from stone and earth

==== Common Properties ====
Elementals typically share some common characteristics:
  * **Resistance:** High resistance to damage of their own element
  * **Vulnerability:** Often weak to opposing elements
  * **Movement:** Unique movement patterns based on their element
  * **Attacks:** Elemental-based attacks that may have special effects

==== Fire Elemental ====
Fire elementals are composed of living flame and can ignite nearby flammable objects. They are immune to fire damage but vulnerable to cold.

==== Water Elemental ====
Water elementals are made of fluid and can extinguish fires. They are immune to water-based effects and may heal near water sources.

==== Air Elemental ====
Air elementals move swiftly and may be harder to hit. They are typically immune to electricity and may have abilities related to movement.

==== Earth Elemental ====
Earth elementals are sturdy and resistant to physical damage. They have high health and armor but may move more slowly.

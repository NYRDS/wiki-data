====== Elementals ======

Elementals are creatures made of pure elemental matter - fire, water, air, or earth. They each have unique properties and abilities related to their element.

==== Description ====
Elementals are magical creatures representing the fundamental elements of nature. They are commonly found throughout the dungeon.

==== Types of Elementals ====
  * [[rpd:fire_elemental|Fire Elemental]] - Made of flame and heat / Creatures of flame and heat
  * [[rpd:water_elemental|Water Elemental]] - Composed of water and fluidity / Creatures associated with water and moisture
  * [[rpd:air_elemental|Air Elemental]] - Formed of wind and air currents / Flying creatures made of living air
  * [[rpd:earth_elemental|Earth Elemental]] - Constructed from stone and earth / Creatures made of earth and stone

==== Common Properties ====
Elementals typically share some common characteristics:
  * **Resistance:** High resistance to damage of their own element
  * **Vulnerability:** Often weak to opposing elements
  * **Movement:** Unique movement patterns based on their element
  * **Attacks:** Elemental-based attacks that may have special effects
  * **Magical Nature:** All elementals are magical beings
  * **Elemental Resistance:** Each type has resistances to opposing elements
  * **Environmental Affinity:** May be stronger in certain environments
  * **Drops:** Often drop useful reagents or potions

==== Fire Elemental ====
Fire elementals are composed of living flame and can ignite nearby flammable objects. They are immune to fire damage but vulnerable to cold.

==== Water Elemental ====
Water elementals are made of fluid and can extinguish fires. They are immune to water-based effects and may heal near water sources.

==== Air Elemental ====
Air elementals move swiftly and may be harder to hit. They are typically immune to electricity and may have abilities related to movement.

==== Earth Elemental ====
Earth elementals are sturdy and resistant to physical damage. They have high health and armor but may move more slowly.

==== Notes ====
Elementals serve as representations of the core elements in the dungeon ecosystem and often drop useful items when defeated.

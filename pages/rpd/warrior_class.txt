====== Warrior Class ======

{{ rpd:images:hero_WARRIOR.png|Warrior Class Sprite }}

The **Warrior** is one of the starting [[rpd:hero_classes|hero classes]] in Remixed Dungeon. The Warrior is a melee-focused class that specializes in the use of weapons and armor to tackle enemies head-on.

==== Description ====
The Warrior is a classic melee fighter who starts with a unique short sword that can be later upgraded and a small health bonus. The class is designed for direct combat and is often considered the most beginner-friendly class due to its straightforward approach to combat and good survivability.

  * [[rpd:food_ration|Ration of Food]] - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L27-L30|config reference]]

  * [[rpd:body_armor_spell|Body Armor Spell]] (in quickslot) - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L23-L26|config reference]]

==== Stats ====
  * **Starting Strength:** 11 (vs 10 for other classes) - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L18|config reference]]
  * **Starting HP:** 20 (vs 15 for most other classes) - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L19|config reference]]
  * **Starting SP:** 10 (standard) - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L20|config reference]]
  * **Max Inventory:** 19 slots

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Starting Weapon:** The Warrior starts with a Short Sword +1 (identified), which can be upgraded with scrolls. - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L14-L17|config reference]]
  * **Starting Buff:** Warriors begin with a small strength advantage which allows them to equip heavier weapons and armor earlier than other classes. - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L18|config reference]]
  * **Magic Affinity:** Combat magic affinity, allowing access to combat spells like [[rpd:body_armor_spell|Body Armor]], [[rpd:smash_spell|Smash]], [[rpd:die_hard_spell|Die Hard]], etc. - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L21|config reference]]
  * **Food Healing:** Any piece of food restores 5 health when eaten (not applicable if HP is already full) - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/java/com/watabou/pixeldungeon/actors/hero/Hero.java#L809-L814|code reference]]
  * **Identified Potions:** Potions of Strength are identified from the beginning - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L22-L25|config reference]]
  * **Higher Gold:** Starts with 100 gold (compared to 50-70 for other classes) - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/assets/hero/initHeroes.json#L7-L10|config reference]]

==== Subclasses ====
The Warrior can choose between two Mastery paths after defeating Tengu (boss #2) with a [[rpd:tome_of_mastery|Tome of Mastery]]:
  * [[rpd:gladiator_subclass|Gladiator]]
  * [[rpd:berserker_subclass|Berserker]]

==== Advantages ====
  * Better starting HP and strength than other classes - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/res/values/strings_all.xml#L2447-L2451|perk reference: +11 strength]]
  * Unique Short Sword provides an early combat advantage - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/res/values/strings_all.xml#L2447-L2451|perk reference: unique short sword]]
  * Good survivability with high HP
  * Access to armor and defensive items

==== Disadvantages ====
  * Lacks ranged combat options - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/res/values/strings_all.xml#L2447-L2451|perk reference: less proficient with missile weapons]]
  * Relies on direct confrontation
  * No magical abilities for utility

==== Strategy ====
  * Use your HP advantage to engage enemies more aggressively
  * Focus on upgrading your unique Short Sword when possible - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/res/values/strings_all.xml#L2447-L2451|perk reference: reforge short sword]]
  * Invest in armor early for maximum protection
  * Take advantage of the higher strength to use better equipment
  * Consider your subclass choice carefully based on your playstyle
  * Food heals you for some HP when eaten - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/res/values/strings_all.xml#L2447-L2451|perk reference: food heals HP]]
  * Potions of Strength are already identified - [[https://github.com/NYRDS/remixed-dungeon/blob/master/RemixedDungeon/src/main/res/values/strings_all.xml#L2447-L2451|perk reference: identified potions of strength]]

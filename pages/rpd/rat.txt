====== Rat ======

{{ rpd:images:mob_Rat.png|Rat }}

The rat is the most common enemy in the early game dungeons.

==== Description ====
The common brown rat is a small rodent that lives in the sewers. They have beady red eyes and sharp teeth. Rats are relatively weak but can still pose a threat in large numbers.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level:** 1
  * **HP:** 8
  * **EXP:** 1
  * **Min Damage:** 1
  * **Max Damage:** 5
  * **Defense Rating (DR):** 1
  * **Defense Skill:** 3
  * **Attack Skill:** 8
  * **Max Level:** 7

==== Behavior ====
  * Passively moves toward the hero
  * Will attack if hero comes in range
  * Does not use special abilities
  * Flee from targets with Ratter Aura buff (from [[rpd:rat_skull|Rat Skull]] item)
  * Triggers quest events for Scarecrow NPC, Ghost NPC, and Plague Doctor NPC when killed

==== Drops ====
  * [[rpd:mystery_meat|Mystery Meat]]
  * Occasionally drops gold (1-3)

==== Strategy ====
  * Early game enemies like rats help the hero gain experience
  * Rats can be dangerous if encountered in groups
  * Generally safe to fight for a level 1 hero
  * When holding a Rat Skull item, rats will flee from you

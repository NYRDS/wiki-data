====== Rat ======

{:rpd:images:rat_sprite.png?200|}

{{ rpd:images:rat_sprite.png|Rat }}

Rats are the first enemies encountered in Remixed Dungeon, found in the Sewers area.

==== Stats ====
  * **Level**: Found in Sewers (level 1-5)
  * **HP**: Low
  * **Attack**: Low
  * **Defense**: Low
  * **EXP**: Low

==== Description ====
Rats are small, aggressive creatures that infest the dungeon's sewers. They are among the weakest enemies but can still pose a threat in large numbers.

==== Behavior ====
  * Aggressive but relatively weak
  * Moves directly toward the player
  * Often encountered in small groups

==== Drops ====
Rats typically drop:
  * Small amount of gold
  * Occasionally minor items or food

==== Special Notes ====
  * First enemy encountered in the game
  * Generally harmless to well-equipped characters
  * Can appear in groups which may be more challenging
  * Good source of early experience and gold

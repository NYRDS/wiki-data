====== Fire Elemental ======

==== Description ====
Fire elementals are creatures made of living flame. They embody the element of fire and are typically found in volcanic or heated areas of the dungeon.

==== Stats ====
  * **HP:** Varies based on dungeon level
  * **Attack Skill:** High
  * **Defense Skill:** Moderate
  * **Damage:** High fire-based damage
  * **Exp for Kill:** Moderate to high
  * **Max Level:** Limited by dungeon progression

==== Special Abilities ====
  * **Fire Attacks:** Inflicts fire damage and may ignite enemies
  * **Heat Aura:** May cause burning effects to nearby creatures
  * **Immunity:** Immune to fire-based attacks and burning effects
  * **Vulnerability:** Weak to cold/water-based attacks

==== Drops ====
  * Possible drops include fire-resistant items or reagents

==== Notes ====
Fire elementals are dangerous opponents that require cold-based attacks or resistances to fight effectively. They are often found in hotter areas of the dungeon.

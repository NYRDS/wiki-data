====== Burning ======

Burning is a harmful status effect in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that causes continuous damage to affected characters. It's one of the most common damage-over-time effects in the game.

==== Burning Mechanics ====
  * **Damage Type**: Fire damage that stacks with other damage sources
  * **Duration**: Typically lasts for several turns before expiring
  * **Damage Rate**: Deals a percentage of max HP as damage each turn
  * **Spread**: Can spread to adjacent tiles in certain conditions

==== Causes of Burning ====
  * **Fire-based Attacks**: Many fire spells and wands cause burning
  * **Fire Traps**: Stepping on fire traps can cause burning
  * **Environmental Hazards**: Some areas of the dungeon contain burning terrain
  * **Enemy Abilities**: Certain enemies can inflict burning through attacks
  * **Self-infliction**: Some items might accidentally cause burning

==== Burning Immunities and Resistances ====
  * **Elemental Resistance**: Some characters and items provide fire resistance
  * **Specific Immunities**: Certain character classes or pets may be immune
  * **Warding Items**: Some equipment can prevent or reduce burning effects

==== Effects of Burning ====
  * **Continuous Damage**: Takes damage each turn until effect ends
  * **No Healing**: Cannot heal while burning except through special means
  * **Item Damage**: May damage flammable items in inventory
  * **Environmental Impact**: Can ignite oil and other flammable materials

==== Countering Burning ====
  * **Potion of Purity**: Can cleanse status effects including burning
  * **Water Terrain**: Standing in water typically extinguishes burning
  * **Fire Resistance**: Using items that boost fire resistance
  * **Quick Action**: Moving away from fire sources immediately

==== Strategic Uses ====
  * **Offensive**: Applying to enemies to cause continuous damage
  * **Environmental Manipulation**: Using fire to block or channel enemies
  * **Oil Combustion**: Combining with oil for area denial

====== Ring of Stone Blood ======

==== Description ====
This strange ring is made of some sturdy stone. What you first thought was a ruby is actually liquid blood being held in place! Inside is an arrow pointing at what appears to be a wall, along with a broken heart... What might it do?

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Stone Construction:** Made of sturdy stone material
  * **Blood Center:** Contains what appears to be liquid blood in the center
  * **Mysterious Effects:** Has unknown but likely powerful effects
  * **Unique Design:** Features an arrow and broken heart imagery

==== Effects ====
  * **Stone Blood:** The blood turns stone which merges with nearby walls, making traversing them easier, but at what cost
  * **Wall Walking:** May enable limited movement through walls
  * **Potential Consequences:** May come with significant drawbacks or requirements

==== Notes ====
The Ring of Stone Blood is an enigmatic item with a mysterious design and potentially powerful abilities related to movement through solid objects.

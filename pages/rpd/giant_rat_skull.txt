====== Giant Rat Skull ======

{{ rpd:images:giant_rat_skull_item.png|Giant Rat Skull }}

The Giant Rat Skull is a rare trophy item from a powerful rat variant in Remixed Dungeon. It demonstrates the owner's capability of dealing with rodents and may have special properties related to rat control.

==== Description ====
The Giant Rat Skull is a large skull from a much larger and more dangerous variant of the common dungeon rat. It serves as proof of defeating this unusual enemy. It could be a nice hunting trophy, but it smells too bad to place it on a wall. On the other hand, it demonstrates that the owner is capable of killing rats, even big and smelly ones. This skull has special properties related to rat control.

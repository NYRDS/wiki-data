====== Giant Rat Skull ======

{{ rpd:images:giant_rat_skull_item.png|Giant Rat Skull }}

==== Description ====
It could be a nice hunting trophy, but it smells too bad to place it on a wall. On the other hand, it demonstrates that owner is capable of killing rats, even big and smelly ones. This skull has special properties related to rat control.

==== Stats ====
  * **Type:** Quest Item / Artifact
  * **Rarity:** Quest Item
  * **Price:** 100 gold
  * **Identified:** Always

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Rat Intimidation:** When equipped, the acrid stench terrifies all other rodents around, making them flee from the skull bearer
  * **Quest Item:** Originally part of the [[rpd:ghost|Ghost]] quest in the sewers
  * **Alternative Quest Reward:** Used as the alternative reward for the Ghost quest (instead of [[rpd:dried_rose|Dried Rose]])

==== Acquisition ====
  * Dropped by [[rpd:fetidrat|Fetid Rat]] (a special rat variant)
  * Alternative reward from the [[rpd:ghost|Ghost]] quest in the sewers
  * Part of the sewers questline for players who chose the rat skull quest path

==== Buff Effect ====
When equipped, provides the "Ratter" buff which:
  * Causes all rodent-type enemies to flee in fear
  * Applies to all rat-type creatures in the dungeon
  * Has the description: "The acrid stench terrifies all other rodents around, making them flee from the skull bearer."

==== Notes ====
The Giant Rat Skull is a quest item that's part of the Ghost's questline. It represents an alternative path in the sewers quest where the player hunts a special [[rpd:fetidrat|Fetid Rat]] instead of collecting 8 regular rat corpses. When equipped, it provides a useful effect against rat-type enemies.

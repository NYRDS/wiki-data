====== Giant Rat Skull ======

{{ :rpd:giant_rat_skull.png?nolink&50|}}

The Giant Rat Skull is a trophy item obtained from defeating giant rats. It demonstrates the owner's capability of dealing with rodents and may have special properties related to rat control.

==== Description ====
It could be a nice hunting trophy, but it smells too bad to place it on a wall. On the other hand, it demonstrates that the owner is capable of killing rats, even big and smelly ones. This skull has special properties related to rat control.

==== Properties ====
  * **Type:** Trophy item with mechanical effects
  * **Source:** Dropped by giant rats or special rat enemies
  * **Effect:** Likely affects other rats in the dungeon
  * **Aroma:** Has a distinctive smell (mechanically significant)

==== Special Effects ====
  * **Rat Intimidation:** The acrid stench terrifies all other rodents around, making them flee from the skull bearer
  * **Rat Control:** May allow some form of control over rat creatures
  * **Trophy Status:** Demonstrates hunting prowess against large rats
  * **Potion Interaction:** May have special interactions with potions or other items

==== Acquisition ====
  * Dropped by giant rats or special rat bosses
  * May be rare as it requires defeating large rat enemies
  * Possibly part of rat-themed challenges
  * Could be related to the [[rpd:rat_king|Rat King]] encounter

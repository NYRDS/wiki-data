====== Arcane Stylus ======

==== Description ====
This arcane stylus is made of some dark, very hard stone. Using it you can inscribe a glyph on armor or enchantment on a weapon, but you have no power over choosing what it will be, the stylus will decide it for you.

==== Stats ====
  * **Durability:** Single use item

==== Special Properties ====
  * Can inscribe random glyphs on armor
  * Can inscribe random enchantments on weapons
  * The specific effect is randomly determined when used
  * Cannot be used on cursed items

==== Usage ====
To use an Arcane Stylus, select the "INSCRIBE" action and then choose an armor or weapon to inscribe. The stylus will add a random beneficial enchantment or glyph to the item.

==== Notes ====
Arcane Styluses are valuable for improving equipment with random but beneficial properties. The unpredictability means you may get a highly desirable enchantment or a less useful one.

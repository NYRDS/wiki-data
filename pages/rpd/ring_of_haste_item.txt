====== Ring of Haste ======

{{ rpd:images:ring_of_haste_item.png|Ring of Haste }}

The **Ring of Haste** is a ring in Remixed Dungeon that enhances the wearer's speed, allowing them to move and act more quickly.

==== Item Overview ====
  * **Type:** Accessory (Ring)
  * **Rarity:** Uncommon
  * **Special Effect:** Increases movement and attack speed
  * **Usage:** Equipped in an accessory slot
  * **Identification:** Becomes identified when used or seen in a well-lit area for an extended time
  * **Price:** Varies based on the ring's level

==== Special Mechanics ====
  * **Speed Enhancement:** Reduces the time required for all actions, making both movement and combat faster
  * **Stacking:** Multiple speed effects may stack but with diminishing returns
  * **Enchantment:** Can be upgraded using Scrolls of Upgrade
  * **Curse:** Can be cursed, which would slow down the wearer instead of speeding them up

==== Strategy ====
  * Essential for escaping dangerous situations quickly
  * Particularly useful in the Prison and Caves areas where many enemies can catch up to the player
  * Greatly improves the effectiveness of ranged combat by allowing more distance to be covered
  * Helps with exploring by reducing travel time between areas
  * Very beneficial for classes that rely on mobility like the Rogue
  * Good to have when fighting stationary or slow enemies
  * Allows for better positioning during combat
  * Particularly valuable when fighting enemies that rely on closing distance to attack

==== Obtaining ====
  * Found in various dungeon levels
  * Available in shops at moderate prices when identified
  * Dropped by various enemies (relatively rare)
  * Found in containers and special rooms
  * Starting equipment for some classes or challenges

==== Effect ====
When worn, the Ring of Haste increases the speed at which all actions are performed. The exact effect depends on the ring's level, but in general, it reduces the time required for each action by a percentage that increases with the ring's level.

According to the game's string resources: "This ring accelerates the wearer's flow of time, allowing them to perform all actions more quickly."

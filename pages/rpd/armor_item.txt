====== Armor ======

Armor in Remixed Dungeon provides protection against physical damage. The protection level depends on the armor's tier and any enchantments applied.

==== Armor Tiers ====
  * **Cloth Armor**: The lowest tier armor (tier 1)
  * **Leather Armor**: Medium-low tier armor (tier 2)  
  * **Mail Armor**: Medium tier armor (tier 3)
  * **Scale Armor**: Medium-high tier armor (tier 4)
  * **Plate Armor**: The highest tier armor (tier 5)

==== Armor Properties ====
  * **Defense Rating**: Reduces damage taken from attacks
  * **Durability**: Armor degrades with use over time
  * **Weight**: Affects movement speed and stealth
  * **Enchantments**: Can be enchanted with glyphs for special effects

==== Defense Calculation ====
The damage reduction formula is: **Damage Reduction = Armor Rating / (Armor Rating + 12)**

==== Armor Materials ====
  * **Cloth**: Fabric-based armor with minimal protection
  * **Leather**: Made from processed monster hides
  * **Mail**: Metal rings sewn onto a leather backing
  * **Scale**: Metal scales sewn onto a leather backing
  * **Plate**: Solid metal plates for maximum protection

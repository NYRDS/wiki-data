====== Armor ======

{{ rpd:images:armor_item.png|Armor }}

==== Description ====
**Armor** is equipment worn by the hero to reduce damage from physical attacks. Higher-level armor provides better protection and can be upgraded using armor kits.

==== Properties ====
  * **Armor Class**: The damage reduction provided by wearing this armor
  * **DR (Damage Reduction)**: How much damage is reduced per hit
  * **Durability**: Amount of wear before the armor degrades
  * **Speed Factor**: How much the armor affects character movement speed
  * **STR (Strength)**: The minimum strength required to wear the armor without penalty

====== Toxic Gas ======

**Toxic Gas** is a harmful effect in Remixed Dungeon that causes damage over time and may debilitate the affected character.

==== Effects ====
  * **Damage Over Time**: Causes gradual damage each turn while active
  * **Breathing Difficulty**: May impair the affected character's abilities
  * **Area Effect**: Usually affects multiple cells in an area
  * **Duration**: Persists for a limited number of turns

==== Sources ====
  * **[[rpd:toxic_gas_trap_level_object|Toxic Gas Traps]]**: Activated when stepped on
  * **[[rpd:spells|Spells]]**: Some spells create toxic gas clouds
  * **[[rpd:wands|Wands]]**: Certain wands can create toxic gas
  * **[[rpd:mobs|Mobs]]**: Some enemies can emit toxic gas
  * **[[rpd:potions|Potions]]**: Broken vials of certain potions may create toxic gas

==== Resistance and Immunity ====
  * **[[rpd:ring_of_elements|Ring of Elements]]**: Provides resistance to toxic gas
  * **[[rpd:armor_glyphs|Armor Glyphs]]**: Some glyphs provide protection
  * **[[rpd:buffs|Buffs]]**: Special status effects may provide temporary immunity

==== Countering Toxic Gas ====
  * **[[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]]**: Clears toxic gas status immediately
  * **Rapid Movement**: Moving quickly out of gas clouds
  * **Avoidance**: Identifying and avoiding sources of toxic gas

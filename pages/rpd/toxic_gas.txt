====== Toxic Gas ======

{{ rpd:images:potionoftoxicgas_sprite.png|Toxic Gas Effect }}

**Toxic Gas** is a harmful gas effect in Remixed Pixel Dungeon that causes ongoing damage to characters within its area of effect.

==== Effects ====
  * **Damage Over Time**: Characters in the gas take damage each turn
  * **Area Effect**: Affects all characters within the gas cloud
  * **Duration**: The gas cloud persists for a limited number of turns

==== Causes ====
Toxic Gas can be created by:
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas_item|Potion of Toxic Gas]] when thrown
  * [[rpd:paralytic_gas_trap_level_object|Paralytic Gas Traps]] (in some cases)
  * [[rpd:toxic_gas_trap_level_object|Toxic Gas Traps]]
  * Certain mob abilities or environmental effects

==== Counters ====
  * Avoiding the area of the gas cloud
  * [[rpd:resistances|Gas Resistance]] reduces the damage taken
  * Some characters may be immune to gas effects
  * Moving out of the gas cloud area

==== Interactions ====
  * Affected by [[rpd:resistances|Gas Resistance]]
  * Can be dissipated by air currents in certain levels
  * [[rpd:ring_of_elements|Ring of Elements]] may provide some protection

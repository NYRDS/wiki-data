====== Toxic Gas ======

Toxic Gas is a harmful environmental effect in Remixed Dungeon that damages creatures within its area and may cause ongoing poison effects.

==== Description ====
Toxic Gas is a greenish cloud of poisonous gas that spreads through the dungeon, causing damage to most living creatures that remain within it. It's typically created by throwing a [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas|Potion of Toxic Gas]].

==== Effects ====
  * **Area of Effect:** Covers multiple adjacent cells
  * **Damage Over Time:** Causes repeated damage while in the gas
  * **Poison Status:** May cause poison status effect for ongoing damage after leaving
  * **Duration:** Persists for several turns before dissipating
  * **Movement:** Can move with wind or spread patterns in some variants

==== Creation ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_toxic_gas|Potion of Toxic Gas]] - Thrown potion creates the gas cloud
  * Certain enemy abilities - Some creatures can produce toxic gas
  * Environmental hazards - Some dungeon features may generate gas

==== Affected Creatures ====
  * Most living enemies and players
  * Potentially harmful to some pets or summons
  * Some undead or mechanical creatures may be immune

==== Strategy ====
  * **For Players:** Use to damage groups of enemies
  * **Defensive:** Avoid standing in toxic gas clouds
  * **Environmental:** Can block passages temporarily
  * **Tactical:** Consider gas spread before throwing potions

==== Countermeasures ====
  * Don't stand in the gas if avoidable
  * Equipment may provide resistance to gas effects
  * Consider timing when the gas will clear
  * Avoid using in enclosed spaces where gas will linger

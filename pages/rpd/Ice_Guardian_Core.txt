====== Ice Guardian Core ======

==== Description ====
The core of an Ice Guardian, a powerful artifact from ancient kobold engineering. Power stored within allows Ice guardians to reconstruct themselves.

==== Special Properties ====
  * **Reconstruction:** Contains energy for self-repair
  * **Ice Magic:** Infused with powerful ice magic
  * **Ancient Technology:** Created by ancient kobolds
  * **Energy Source:** Provides power for Ice Guardian abilities

==== Uses ====
  * Crafting ingredient for ice-based items
  * Power source for cold-based magic items
  * Component for construction of ice-related equipment

==== Notes ====
Ice Guardian Cores are valuable artifacts representing the pinnacle of ancient kobold engineering, containing concentrated ice energy that can be used for various purposes.

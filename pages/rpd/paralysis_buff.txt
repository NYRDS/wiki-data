====== Paralysis ======

{{ rpd:images:paralysis_buff.png|Paralysis Buff Icon }}

**Paralysis** is a status effect in Remixed Dungeon that renders the affected character unable to take any actions for a set duration.

==== Description ====
When a character is paralyzed, they are unable to move, attack, cast spells, or perform any other actions until the effect wears off. This can be particularly dangerous for the hero in combat situations, as it leaves them vulnerable to attacks.

==== Causes ====
  * [[rpd:potion_of_paralytic_gas_item|Potion of Paralytic Gas]] - Thrown potion that creates a gas cloud causing paralysis
  * [[rpd:paralysis_arrow_item|Paralysis Arrow]] - Thrown missile that applies 4 turns of paralysis (uses Stun effect)
  * [[rpd:paralysis_enchantment|Paralysis Enchantment]] - Weapon enchantment that has a chance to apply Stun on hit (technically applies Stun, not the Paralysis status)
  * [[rpd:curare_dart_item|Curare Dart]] - Thrown dart that applies Paralysis effect
  * [[rpd:paralytic_gas_trap_level_object|Paralytic Gas Trap]] - Trap that creates a gas cloud causing paralysis
  * Certain enemy attacks - Some creatures like [[rpd:fetid_rat_mob|Fetid Rats]], [[rpd:piranha_mob|Piranhas]], and [[rpd:king_mob|King]] can inflict paralysis
  * Special spells - Some spells may cause paralysis

==== Duration ====
The duration of paralysis depends on the source and the target's resistances. Characters with Paralysis Resistance will experience shorter durations.

==== Immunities ====
  * [[rpd:mobs|Mobs]] with paralysis immunity cannot be paralyzed
  * [[rpd:prayers|Prayer]] effects may provide temporary immunity

==== Countering Paralysis ====
  * Wait for the effect to expire naturally
  * Use [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] to cleanse the effect
  * Some items and abilities provide resistance to paralysis

==== Strategy ====
Paralysis is often used strategically by enemies to gain the upper hand in combat. Players should:
  * Keep a [[rpd:potion_of_purity_item|Potion of Purity]] handy for emergency situations
  * Consider paralysis resistance when selecting equipment
  * Use paralysis defensively against powerful enemies when possible
